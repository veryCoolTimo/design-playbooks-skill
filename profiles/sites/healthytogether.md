## Overview
A light lavender base punctuated by full-bleed blocks of saturated electric blue-violet, hot magenta, and near-black. Despite the "dark-neon" tag in metadata, the screenshots show a predominantly light canvas (#F1EDFB) that borrows neon energy through color blocks and glossy 3D renders of phones and laptops with pink-to-purple gradient screens. Typography does the heavy lifting: huge, ultra-tight, extra-bold grotesque headlines in near-black on light, or white on the blue sections.

## Layout
Fixed light top nav: logo left, 4 text links right, single pill CTA ("Talk to Our Team") in blue-violet. Product-page heroes are left-aligned: small pink icon + pink eyebrow label ("Health Pass", "WIC MIS"), then a 2-line giant headline, a short right-offset paragraph, and a pill CTA pair (blue "Schedule a Demo" + black "Video Overview" with icon). Below the hero sits a large tilted 3D device render. Section rhythm alternates horizontal full-width color bands: pale lavender → lighter lilac (#DCD4F7-ish) → electric blue (#4740EE) → lavender → repeat; the home page adds black and photo sections. Every page ends with a rounded blue "Learn more about our solution." panel containing floating 3D gradient devices and abstract shapes, then a light multi-column footer with app-store badges.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped, no borders, no shadows; primary fill #5B4DF0 with white text, secondary near-black #17151F fill with white text and a leading icon. Cards/panels: very large radius (~24px), flat fills (blue CTA panel, white stat tiles, black quote tiles on home), no visible borders or drop shadows — color contrast does the separation. Feature rows: small purple/pink line icons above bold titles and short gray-ish body text, sometimes divided by thin vertical rules. Checklists use solid green circular check icons. Device mockups are photoreal 3D renders, often tilted, with soft cast shadows.

## Signature
- Alternating full-bleed horizontal bands: pale lavender / electric blue-violet #4740EE / black, with type flipping between near-black and white
- Floating 3D still-life clusters — laptop + phone with magenta-to-violet gradient screens plus abstract cones, spheres, and spiky pink shapes
- Extra-bold, very tightly tracked grotesque headlines ending in periods ("A fully integrated MIS for WIC.")
- Pink eyebrow label + tiny icon above every hero headline; pill-only buttons in blue or black

## Do
- Keep the canvas pale lavender, never pure white; reserve #4740EE for full-width bands and CTA panels
- Set headlines extra-bold with tight tracking and end sentences with a period
- Use pill buttons exclusively: blue-violet primary, near-black secondary with a leading icon
- Separate sections with flat color changes, not borders or shadows
- Use magenta/pink only as small accents: eyebrow labels, icons, gradient screens in 3D renders

## Don't
- Don't use outlined or square-cornered buttons — nothing on the site has a visible border
- Don't add drop shadows or card strokes; contrast comes from fill color
- Don't center product-page heroes — they are left-aligned with a right-offset supporting paragraph (only the home hero centers)
- Don't use pink as a button fill or large surface; it stays an accent
- Don't introduce serif or rounded-friendly typefaces; everything is one neo-grotesque family
