## Overview
Light, colorful SaaS marketing design anchored by a deep eggplant-purple brand color, with white body sections punctuated by pastel bands (periwinkle blue, lavender, mint, cream). Heroes and footers flip to saturated dark purple with magenta chat-bubble overlays, while mid-page content stays airy and white. Serif editorial headlines alternate with bold grotesque sans, giving a friendly, magazine-meets-product feel. Despite the "dark-neon" metadata label, only the hero/footer are dark; the dominant canvas is light.

## Layout
Slim white sticky nav: logo left, center links (Product/Solutions/Resources/Pricing), Sign in + pill "Try for free" (filled purple) and outlined "Request a demo" on the right. Home hero is full-bleed dark purple with left-aligned serif headline, dual pill CTAs, and photography of real people overlaid with floating magenta chat bubbles. Section rhythm alternates: white content band, tinted pastel band (light periwinkle, cream), dark purple feature band, then back to white. Large centered section headlines with generous vertical whitespace; 3–4 column card grids for features, stats, and articles. Product screenshots sit in rounded frames with small floating UI callout badges. Pages transition into the dark purple footer through a distinctive pixel-mosaic/glitch band of purple and periwinkle rectangles.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped throughout; primary filled deep purple with white text, secondary white/transparent with thin dark outline; small size, modest padding. Cards: 8–14px radius, flat pastel fills (lavender/periwinkle/mint trio for feature cards, cream for stat cards) or white with hairline gray borders (pricing tiers — highlighted tier gets a purple border and "Most popular" tag). Pricing compare table uses hairline row dividers, checkmark icons, and dot-scale ratings. Stats rendered as oversized serif numerals (6hr, 1.5x, 87%, 428%) on tinted card backgrounds. FAQ as bordered accordion rows with plus icons. Email capture: pill input with attached dark subscribe button. Testimonials as plain quote cards with small avatar and highlighted magenta phrases. No visible drop shadows; separation comes from color blocks and hairlines.

## Signature
- Pixel-mosaic "glitch" bands of purple/periwinkle rectangles used as section-to-footer transitions and in blog art.
- Deep purple hero + footer bookending a white body, with floating magenta chat-bubble UI vignettes over lifestyle photography.
- Pastel tri-color feature card sets (lavender, periwinkle, mint) with mini product-UI mockups inside.
- Serif editorial headlines (sometimes with italic emphasis or inline avatar clusters) mixed with bold grotesque section heads.

## Do / Don't
- Do bookend pages: dark eggplant-purple hero and footer, white/light everything in between.
- Do use pill buttons in pairs — filled purple primary next to outlined secondary — at every CTA moment.
- Do alternate white sections with flat pastel bands (periwinkle, lavender, mint, cream) instead of shadows or borders for rhythm.
- Do show the product as rounded screenshot frames with small floating status badges and chat bubbles.
- Do set oversized serif numerals for stats on tinted cards.
- Don't run neon glows, glassmorphism, or true dark-mode body sections — the "dark" here is confined to hero/footer blocks.
- Don't use heavy drop shadows or thick borders; separation is flat color and 1px hairlines.
- Don't square off buttons — everything interactive is a pill; card corners stay at medium radius.
- Don't introduce reds/oranges as accents; warmth comes only from cream/peach gradient backdrops, with magenta as the sole hot accent.
