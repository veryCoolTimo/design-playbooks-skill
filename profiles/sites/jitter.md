## Overview
Poster-scale big-type site: enormous tight-tracked black grotesque headlines on white carry every section, with product UI screenshots and bento card grids doing the showing. Color is rationed to three brand notes — soft lavender (#b493fe), neon yellow (#f4ff63), and a deep aubergine (#18082c) that fully inverts the customers page. Everything interactive is a pill; everything container-like is generously rounded.

## Layout
Top nav: wordmark left, four center links (Product, Customers, Templates, Pricing), "Log in" plus a black pill CTA right. Home hero is a centered stack — small "New" badge pill, 2-line giant headline, one lavender pill CTA — followed by a logo strip and a full-width product screenshot on a #f2f2f4 rounded band. Section rhythm alternates white and light-gray bands, each opened by a huge centered headline; product page runs long 2-column bento grids of feature cards. Giant pull-quote testimonials sit between sections with one word marker-highlighted in purple or yellow. Pages close with an oversized "Try Jitter today" CTA block on gray, a 5-column footer, then a black band (neon sticker logo + newsletter input) revealed under large rounded page corners. Templates page switches to a left sidebar filter + dense thumbnail grid.

## Components
Buttons: flat pills, no border, no shadow — black fill/white text (nav, footer CTA), lavender #b493fe fill/dark text (hero primary), neon yellow #f4ff63/dark text on dark surfaces. Segmented pill toggles for pricing period and customer filters (white active segment inside gray pill track). Cards: white, 16-24px radius, hairline or no border, flat; customer-story tiles are large color-field cards (lilac, neon-yellow gradient, indigo #2d0a6e–#330c7f gradient) with only a logo centered. Pricing plan cards are plain white with a plan-colored label and pill CTA; comparison table and FAQ use hairline gray dividers and checkmarks, no zebra fills. Newsletter input: dark rounded field with yellow pill submit.

## Signature
- Headlines set absurdly large and tight — near-black, centered, often the only content in a band
- Pill-everything: every button, badge, toggle, and filter is a full pill
- Lavender / neon-yellow / aubergine triad; customers page inverts to solid #18082c with yellow CTA
- Large rounded corners where light sections lift off the black footer band, like a sheet of paper over the page
- Marker-style animated highlight on a single word inside giant testimonial quotes

## Do
- Make the headline the section: bold grotesque, tight tracking, centered, black on white, far larger than feels safe
- Use flat pill buttons only — black by default, lavender #b493fe for the hero primary, neon yellow only on dark backgrounds
- Alternate #ffffff and #f2f2f4 bands and round their corners generously (16-24px+)
- Show the product with real UI screenshots and bento feature grids, not abstract illustration
- When inverting a page, go all the way to #18082c and switch the CTA to #f4ff63

## Don't
- Don't add shadows, gradients, or borders to buttons — fills are perfectly flat
- Don't set neon yellow text or buttons on white; on light pages it appears only as tiny badges
- Don't introduce blue/teal as brand color — cool tones show up only inside product artwork
- Don't loosen headline tracking or drop below heavy weight; airy light-weight display type breaks the look
- Don't frame content in heavy rules — separation is done with background shifts and hairline dividers
