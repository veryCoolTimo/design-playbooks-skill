## Overview
A Swiss-poster take on fintech: cool light-gray canvas, colossal black grotesque headlines, and a pixel-mosaic of flat color tiles (green, bubblegum pink, cyan, marigold, royal blue) snapped to a visible hairline grid. Long-scroll storytelling alternates the gray canvas with full-bleed dark green, pure black, and bright blue chapter sections. Real product UI screenshots carry the feature proof; decoration stays strictly geometric and flat — no gradients, no 3D, no soft shadows on brand elements.

## Layout
Sparse top nav: stacked wordmark left ("COM/PO/SER" broken across three lines), four text links plus a black pill Sign Up right. Hero is asymmetric: left-aligned type block ("Meet" on a green tile, giant "Composer" overlapping color tiles) over a faint 8-column hairline grid, with the tile mosaic filling the right half. Sections run in high-contrast bands — light gray → forest green → pure black → gray → bright blue (#1870DB) → gray → green card — each a distinct chapter with its own background. Stats row uses thin vertical dividers. Numbered "How do I get started?" steps pair small left-column text with large right-column app screenshots. Generous vertical padding; content sits on the grid with hard edges, not centered symmetry.

## Components
Primary CTAs are wide black pills with white label and a right arrow ("Get started", full-width "Sign up"); nav Sign Up is a small black pill. Product-UI screenshots act as cards: white, 8-14px radius, hairline borders, very soft diffuse shadows. In-product chips and buttons (Invest/Sell, if/then blocks) are small pill outlines in blue/green. Testimonial block is a large flat green rounded card with pink-highlighted headline text set like marker-highlighted lines ("What people are saying"). FAQ is a plain list with hairline dividers and chevrons. Footer team grid renders each person as a flat solid-color rectangle (green/blue/pink/orange/purple/red/yellow) with black text on black background.

## Signature
- Pixel-mosaic of flat square color tiles (green/pink/cyan/marigold/royal blue) overlapping gigantic black headline type on a hairline grid
- Poster-scale ultra-bold grotesque headlines that bleed across the layout ("Composer", "Built Better") with overlapping stacked words
- Hard chapter banding: gray → forest green → black → electric blue full-bleed sections down one page
- Pink highlight bars behind headline words on the green testimonial card; flat color-block team grid in the footer

## Do
- Set headlines in an ultra-heavy neo-grotesque at extreme sizes, left-aligned, and let them overlap decorative tiles or stack asymmetrically
- Keep every decorative element flat and rectangular, snapped to a visible hairline grid; square corners on tiles, pills only on buttons
- Alternate full-bleed section backgrounds (light gray, forest green #31805A, black, blue #1870DB) to chapter the scroll
- Use real product screenshots in white rounded cards as the only "illustrations" alongside the flat tile graphics
- Reserve black pill buttons with arrows for CTAs; use the accent palette for tiles, chips, and highlights, never for the primary CTA

## Don't
- Don't add gradients, glassmorphism, 3D renders, or soft blob shapes — the language is strictly flat and hard-edged
- Don't round the decorative color blocks or section bands; only buttons (pill) and screenshot cards (medium) get radius
- Don't center the hero; the composition is left-anchored type with the mosaic occupying the opposite half
- Don't use colored or gradient CTA buttons — the main CTA is always a black pill on any background
- Don't soften contrast between sections; adjacent bands jump from near-white to black or saturated green/blue without transitions
