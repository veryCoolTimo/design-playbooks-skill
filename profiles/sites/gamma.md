## Overview
Airy, optimistic light UI built on sky metaphors: white-to-powder-blue gradient backgrounds, soft illustrated clouds, sparkle glyphs, and a recurring "night sky" navy band. Deep indigo-navy carries all text and the primary CTA, while saturated pink, orange, and sunset-gradient artwork inject playfulness without touching the core chrome. Every page ends in the same signature synthwave footer scene with a giant translucent GAMMA wordmark.

## Layout
Sticky white top nav: left wordmark, center links (Products, Solutions, About, Pricing), right locale/Login plus one navy pill CTA. Heroes are split (left copy + CTA pair, right large product screenshot) on home/products/solutions, and centered on about/pricing. Section rhythm alternates: white/pale-blue gradient section, then a full-bleed dark navy gradient band (testimonial or careers CTA), back to light. Content sits in a narrow centered column (~1100px) with generous vertical whitespace; features run in 2x2 card grids, 3-up card rows, or alternating image/text zigzags. Pricing is a classic 3-column card row with the middle "Plus" card elevated and taller.

## Components
Buttons: solid navy pill for primary ("Start for free"); pricing CTAs use a navy-to-blue horizontal gradient pill; secondary is white pill with thin border; one pink pill and one orange pill appear as section-level accent CTAs on home. Cards: white fills, 8-14px radius, very soft diffuse shadows, thin or no borders; stat cards on about pair a small line icon with a huge colored number (blue/green/orange/magenta). Testimonial quote cards in pastel pink/peach/blue fills with bold-ink emphasis phrases. Accordions (principles, FAQ) are full-width rows with hairline dividers and +/chevron affordances; principle rows use pastel tinted fills with color-matched labels. Colorful small "tag pill" clusters (multicolor pastel chips) on the about page. Segmented monthly/yearly toggle as a white pill with an inset selected state.

## Signature
- Sky-to-space narrative: pastel clouds and sparkles up top, star-field navy gradient bands mid-page, and a fixed synthwave illustration footer (woman gazing up amid floating app windows) plus giant ghosted GAMMA letters on every page.
- Deep indigo-navy (#1B2A5E-ish) used for both headings AND primary buttons, so the brand reads blue-on-blue-sky.
- Rounded, friendly display type echoing the tubular rounded logo wordmark.
- Vivid illustrated product thumbnails (fox, rocket, headphones, mushrooms) as the only high-saturation elements against calm pastel chrome.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep backgrounds as soft vertical gradients (white → powder blue) rather than flat white; reserve pure white for cards.
- Use one navy pill CTA per view; let pink/orange pills appear only as occasional mid-page accents.
- Alternate light sections with full-bleed dark navy gradient bands for testimonials and recruiting/CTA moments.
- Show the product through colorful embedded screenshots and stylized illustration cards, not abstract stock art.
- Repeat the identical footer scene + ghost wordmark across all pages for continuity.

Don't:
- Don't use black text or gray-800 ink — everything reads deep navy/indigo.
- Don't square off buttons; every button on the site is a full pill.
- Don't apply hard borders or heavy shadows to cards — elevation is whisper-soft and diffuse.
- Don't let accent pink/orange/green leak into body text or nav; they live only in CTAs, tags, stats, and artwork.
- Don't crowd sections; each block gets a single idea with large vertical padding and a centered ~1100px column.
