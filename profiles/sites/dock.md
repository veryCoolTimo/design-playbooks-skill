## Overview
Minimal-light SaaS marketing page built on a pure white canvas with near-black geometric headlines and a single royal-blue CTA color. Each feature block sits inside a huge full-width rounded panel washed in a barely-there pastel tint (mint, pale green, ice blue), each containing a large realistic product screenshot. Two saturated dark-navy panels mid-page break the pastel rhythm before the page returns to white.

## Layout
Slim white sticky nav: logo left, center links with dropdown carets, "Talk to Sales / Log in" plus a filled blue pill-ish button right. Hero is centered text only — two-line bold headline, gray subhead, one blue CTA, G2 star-rating badge — followed by a logo strip of customer marks. Body rhythm is a stack of full-width tinted rounded panels, each with a small colored app-icon tile, centered heading, "Learn more →" link, a horizontal tab row, and a big embedded product UI screenshot with dark header chrome. A 3-column testimonial row and a 3-column template-card grid on white break up the panels. Page closes by repeating the exact hero headline + CTA over a 3D illustration, then a light multi-column footer.

## Components
Primary buttons: solid royal blue (#4466FF with a subtle lighter-blue gradient sheen), white semibold label, ~8-10px radius, no border. Secondary actions are plain text links; section links are colored "Learn more →" text with arrow. Tabs are text pills where the active one gets a soft tinted background. Cards (templates, testimonials) are white with hairline light-gray borders, large radius, faint shadow, screenshot on top and title/category text below. Product screenshots are framed with dark navy title bars, avatars, and a small blue Share button — they act as the illustration system.

## Signature
- Full-width, large-radius section panels in ultra-pale mint/green/ice tints alternating down the page, each hosting one oversized realistic product screenshot
- Small rounded app-icon tile (pastel square with glyph) floating above every section heading
- Heavy rounded geometric headline face against pure white, always center-aligned; hero headline repeated verbatim as the closing CTA section
- Dark indigo/navy interlude panels (integrations with floating logo orbs; video tour) for contrast mid-page

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep one CTA color only — royal blue fills; everything else is text links or tinted tabs
- Wrap each feature section in its own pale-tinted rounded container instead of using dividers
- Lead every section with an icon tile, a centered 2-line heading, and a "Learn more →" link before showing the product UI
- Use detailed, realistic product screenshots (with chrome, avatars, data) as the primary imagery
- Repeat the hero headline and CTA as the final section for a bookended page

Don't:
- Don't saturate the section tints — they stay near-white (#ECFFFD-level); reserve saturation for the navy panels and blue CTA
- Don't left-align hero or section headings; the page is relentlessly center-axis
- Don't add borders or heavy shadows to the tinted panels — they read as flat color fields
- Don't introduce warm colors (orange/red) into the UI; warmth appears only inside third-party logos and photos
- Don't use outline/ghost buttons — secondary actions are plain text
