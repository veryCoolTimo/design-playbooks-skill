## Overview
A scrapbook-collage landing page on a plain white canvas: serif headings smeared with hand-drawn yellow highlighter, candy-colored flat CTA buttons, and a chaotic fan of real user-made pages tilted at random angles. Decoration comes from photographed 3D objects (springs, cubes, croissant shapes), torn-paper collage imagery, purple marker doodles, and a fine-art painting sitting in grass at the footer. It deliberately reads handmade and campy rather than polished SaaS.

## Layout
Single narrow center column (~450px of content) on a wide white page; floating 3D props scatter into the empty margins at the top. Minimal pill nav top-center with just Login / Sign Up in a black capsule. Hero: small "BETA" scribble, huge serif wordmark over a highlighter swipe, sans subhead, then two full-width stacked CTA buttons. Sections are stitched together by a vertical dotted line running down the page spine; each section = highlighted serif heading + short gray sans paragraph + one big collage or screenshot cluster. Rhythm is generous vertical whitespace between vignettes, ending in a painted "Digital Paper" splash and a grass-strip footer with colorful social tiles.

## Components
Buttons: flat solid fills (purple, mint green), uppercase sans label with a trailing arrow glyph, slightly rounded corners (~4px), no border, no shadow, full column width. Showcase cards: user-page screenshots with thin dark borders, rounded corners, soft drop shadows, rotated 5-20 degrees like scattered polaroids. Headings sit on rough highlighter/brush-stroke marks instead of boxes. No visible inputs; nav is a black pill with tiny white/outline links. Inline UI callouts (the small "EDIT" chip) are tiny mint pills embedded in body text. Underlines and arrows are hand-drawn marker strokes.

## Signature
- Serif headlines on hand-swiped yellow highlighter marks, with a vertical dotted line threading all sections together
- Fanned, rotated screenshot cards of real user pages, like photos dumped on a table
- Photorealistic 3D doodads (springs, cubes, wedges) floating in the page margins
- Mixed-media collage: duct-taped banana, botanical illustration, glitch-holographic bust, classical painting in grass — deliberately "messy encouraged"

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas plain white and push all color into stickers, highlights, and CTAs
- Set section headings in a bold high-contrast serif over an irregular highlighter or brush mark
- Rotate cards and imagery a few degrees each so nothing sits on a rigid grid
- Use flat, saturated single-color buttons (purple primary, mint secondary) with uppercase labels and arrow glyphs
- Connect sections with a thin vertical dotted line and lots of vertical whitespace

Don't:
- Don't use gradients, glassmorphism, or big soft-shadow SaaS cards — surfaces here are flat and paper-like
- Don't align everything perfectly; straightened, grid-locked collages kill the scrapbook feel
- Don't set headings in the body sans — the serif/sans contrast carries the hierarchy
- Don't add a heavy multi-link navbar; chrome stays to a tiny centered pill
- Don't replace hand-drawn marks (underlines, arrows, scribbles) with clean vector rules
