## Overview
A soft lavender-pink pastel canvas topped with playful photo-collage illustration: floating islands, a toucan, hot-air balloons, and Star Trek/retro pop-culture cutouts. Elegant serif headlines with italicized emphasis words sit against friendly rounded sans body copy, and near-black charcoal blocks (Pro pricing card, footer) plus a signature chartreuse-lime give it bite. The mood is whimsical-editorial: a serious dev tool that refuses to look corporate.

## Layout
Slim top nav on the pastel canvas: lime "S" logo badge left, center-right dropdown links, account icon far right. Heroes are centered text stacks (serif headline, sans subhead, CTA pair) over a warm sunset-gradient collage; the home hero drops a large product screenshot below the fold. Sections alternate pastel-gradient and near-white backgrounds with generous vertical padding. Feature content runs in 2-3 column card grids (home control-panel grid, product page's long 3-col feature matrix grouped under serif section labels). Pricing is a classic 3-column tier row with the middle Pro tier as an elevated dark card overlapping its white neighbors. Footer is a full-width near-black block with monospace link columns and a newsletter input.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped throughout — dark charcoal fill with light text for primary CTAs, chartreuse #D6FB41 fill with dark text for the featured pricing CTA, and thin dark-outline pills on white for secondary ("Install Statamic", "Learn More"). Cards: white, 8-14px radius, hairline borders and very soft diffuse shadows, each embedding a mini product screenshot; the Pro card inverts to near-black. FAQ items are white rounded bars with left-caret disclosure triangles. Inputs: pill newsletter field in the dark footer with an inset white pill "Subscribe" button. Checklist rows use small green/lime check glyphs.

## Signature
- Photo-collage whimsy: toucan, floating islands, waterfalls, Enterprise starship, and celebrity cutouts pasted into pastel gradient scenes
- Serif headlines with *italic* emphasis words ("The *answer* to...") over rounded sans body
- Chartreuse-lime (#D6FB41) as the lone loud brand hit against lavender pastels and charcoal blocks
- Dark inverted panels for the money moments: featured pricing tier and the monospace-heavy footer

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Set headlines in an editorial serif and italicize one or two emphasis words per headline
- Keep buttons strictly pill-shaped; reserve the lime fill for the single highest-intent CTA and use charcoal or outline pills elsewhere
- Wash pages in soft lavender-to-pink gradients and let feature cards sit white with hairline borders and tiny embedded UI screenshots
- Invert to near-black for the featured plan and footer, switching link lists to monospace
- Sprinkle collaged photographic props (animals, sky, pop-culture objects) at hero tops and section seams

**Don't**
- Don't use lime as a text or background wash — on this site it only appears as button fill, logo badge, and small check/PRO badges
- Don't square off buttons or use heavy drop shadows; elevation here is hairline borders plus faint diffuse shadow
- Don't set headlines in bold sans — the serif/sans contrast is the core typographic identity
- Don't play the illustration straight; the collage is deliberately absurd, so generic stock photos or flat vector blobs break the voice
- Don't crowd sections — the rhythm relies on wide vertical whitespace between centered serif section intros and card grids
