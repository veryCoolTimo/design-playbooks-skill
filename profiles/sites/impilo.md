## Overview
A saturated indigo-violet (#413cd6) floods nearly every page as the literal canvas, with white text sitting directly on it and huge white rounded panels floating on top like paper sheets. Depth comes from a three-tone blue system: bright indigo canvas, near-black navy sections (#16165d), and dark navy cards (#23236a), punctuated by electric cyan (#30e9ff) for key words and data readouts. Fine white/cyan line illustrations of medical devices (blood pressure cuffs, cables, monitors) in an isometric wireframe style replace stock photography in product sections. The mood is confident and tech-forward but softened by a rounded typeface and pill shapes.

## Layout
- Nav: floating transparent bar on the indigo canvas — small white wordmark left, white text links with "→" arrow prefixes, white pill "Request Demo" button right. Same pattern repeats in the footer next to an oversized wordmark with an EKG-line motif over the "i".
- Hero: centered white display headline directly on indigo, sometimes with one word in cyan ("easy."), surrounded or followed by wireframe device illustrations and dark dashboard mockup cards.
- Section rhythm alternates full-bleed color slabs: indigo → white rounded-corner panel (inset from viewport edges with visible indigo margins) → near-black navy slab → back to indigo. The about page inverts it with a dark navy hero card inset on indigo.
- Home solutions story runs as a vertical sequence of dark navy cards, each pairing a short left-aligned text block (key phrases highlighted in cyan) with a wireframe illustration on the right.
- Blog: white rounded content panel on indigo; featured post on top, then a 3-column card grid; right sidebar with search, a dark navy newsletter card, and pill category tags.
- Generous vertical whitespace inside sections; centered headings with a small pill/EKG glyph ornament above them.

## Components
- Buttons: fully rounded pills. Primary CTA is inverted — solid white fill with dark navy text (on indigo and navy backgrounds). On white sections, buttons flip to white fill with a thin indigo border ("See Open Positions"). Subtle soft drop shadow on the nav CTA.
- Cards: large radius (roughly 24px+) everywhere — white content panels, dark navy feature cards, leadership photo cards, blog thumbnails. Dark cards on the white solutions page use thin light borders instead of shadows; elevation is mostly flat.
- Nav/footer links use a "→ Label" arrow-prefix convention; category tags and blog pills are outlined white capsules with trailing arrows.
- Inputs: rounded email field inside the dark newsletter card with a small square arrow submit button; pill search field with magnifier icon on the blog.
- Data UI mockups: dark navy dashboards with cyan line charts and big cyan numeric readouts ("131 / 83", "187").

## Signature
- One brand indigo (#413cd6) used as the actual page background across the whole site, not just accents — white type sits straight on it.
- EKG heartbeat line integrated into the logo wordmark and reused as a section divider/ornament motif.
- Isometric white/cyan wireframe illustrations of real medical hardware instead of photos in product sections.
- Inverted white pill CTAs — the "primary" button color is white, made loud by the saturated ground beneath it.
- Giant inset rounded panels (white or navy) that leave the indigo canvas showing at the edges.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Use #413cd6 as a full-bleed background for entire sections and set body copy in white directly on it.
- Reserve cyan #30e9ff for single emphasized words in headlines, inline keyword highlights, and chart/data readouts on dark navy.
- Make every button a pill; invert CTAs to solid white with dark navy labels on colored grounds.
- Draw product/hardware concepts as thin-line wireframe illustrations in white or cyan on indigo/navy.
- Prefix nav and tag links with a small "→" arrow.

**Don't**
- Don't introduce warm accent colors — the palette is strictly blues (indigo, navy, cyan) plus white; even certification badges render monochrome.
- Don't use heavy black display weights or condensed type; headings stay medium/semibold in a rounded grotesque.
- Don't fill CTAs with the brand indigo — indigo is the ground, never the button.
- Don't use sharp corners or small radii; nothing on the site is squarer than ~16px except full-bleed slabs.
- Don't rely on drop shadows for card depth on dark sections — use tone shifts (navy-on-indigo) and thin borders instead.
