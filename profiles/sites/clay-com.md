## Overview
Warm cream canvas with near-black type, punctuated by full-bleed jewel-tone sections (cobalt, berry magenta, forest green, marigold) that frame real product-UI screenshots. Playful illustration carries the brand: squiggly hand-drawn ribbon lines, cartoon flowers, and a signature 3D-rendered topiary landscape under a pink sky at every page's footer. The effect is a spreadsheet-serious product wrapped in a toy-like, crafted world.

## Layout
Sticky white pill-shaped nav bar with centered dropdown links, "Get a demo" ghost link, and a black pill "Sign up" on the right. Heroes are centered or left-aligned headlines on cream with doodle squiggles and small 3D props flanking the text; a logo strip ("Trusted by 300,000+ GTM teams") follows immediately. Section rhythm alternates cream/white editorial blocks with saturated full-bleed color bands, each containing a floating light-mode product screenshot plus a stat or quote. Feature grids run 3-4 columns of small bordered white cards. Every page closes the same way: centered "Turn your growth ideas into reality today" CTA over the 3D garden landscape, with the footer as a large rounded cream card floating on top of it.

## Components
Buttons: solid near-black pill with white label and trailing arrow (primary); white/ghost pill with thin dark border (secondary); small colored pill tags label sections. Cards: white with 1px light-gray border, 8-14px radius, flat — no drop shadows; testimonial cards are solid brand-color blocks (magenta, green, cobalt) with white text and avatar. Pricing table: pastel-tinted columns (lavender, cream-yellow, pink, light blue) with "Most popular"/"Best value" ribbon headers. Compliance badges rendered as cartoon flower medallions (SOC 2, GDPR, CCPA, ISO). Product screenshots shown as crisp light-UI tables with rounded corners floating on color fields. FAQ as thin-rule accordion rows.

## Signature
- 3D-rendered whimsical garden footer — sculpted green topiary hills, oversized flowers, rainbow path, pink sky — repeated on nearly every page.
- Hand-drawn multicolor squiggle ribbons winding around hero headlines.
- Full-bleed alternating jewel-tone sections (cobalt / berry / forest green / marigold) hosting real spreadsheet-style product UI.
- Flower-shaped compliance badges and small 3D props (watering can, toolbox) turning enterprise trust content playful.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the base warm cream (#F3EFE7) with near-black ink; reserve saturated color for full-bleed section bands and testimonial cards.
- Use black pill buttons with trailing arrows as the single primary CTA style everywhere, paired with thin-border ghost pills.
- Embed real, legible product-UI screenshots inside colored panels rather than abstract mockups.
- Rotate through the four accent hues (cobalt, magenta, green, marigold) across sections so no single color dominates a page.
- End pages with the illustrated landscape + floating rounded footer card for a consistent sign-off.

**Don't**
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients to cards — the system is flat, border-defined, and print-like.
- Don't use pure white as the page canvas for hero sections; the cream warmth is core to the look.
- Don't introduce a serif or script display face — everything is one grotesque sans family at varying weights.
- Don't render the illustration style as flat 2D vector; brand moments are dimensional, clay-like 3D or hand-drawn squiggles.
- Don't color the primary CTA with an accent hue — accents belong to backgrounds and tags, CTAs stay black.
