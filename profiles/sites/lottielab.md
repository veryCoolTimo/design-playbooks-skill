## Overview
Airy white pages built around a soft lavender-purple brand color, with playful two-tone headlines where the second line switches to an accent hue (purple, amber, or pink). Content is carried by rounded pastel tiles containing animated vector illustrations rather than photography. One full-width near-black indigo section on the home page provides dramatic contrast; everything else stays light, rounded, and friendly.

## Layout
Slim top nav: logo left, sparse text links right, ghost "Login" plus a small purple pill "Sign up". Centered hero with a two-line headline, one-sentence subhead, single pill CTA, then a large embedded product/editor screenshot. Feature sections alternate left/right: pastel illustration card on one side, heading with bulleted copy on the other, with generous vertical whitespace between them. Mid-page dark indigo band breaks the rhythm on home. Examples page is a dense masonry grid of square animation tiles in varied background colors. Pre-footer is a full-width purple gradient banner ("Bring your products to life") with a white or outlined pill CTA; footer is white with three link columns and pastel social tiles.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, flat fill in lavender purple (#8674F0), white text, no shadow; secondary CTAs appear as soft pastel pills (peach/pink tints) with darker text of the same hue; white pill on the gradient banner. Cards: very large radius (~20-24px), flat pastel fills (#EEF0FB lavender-gray, cream, mint, pink), no borders, no visible shadows — color alone separates them from the white canvas. Small floating UI chips (canvas-size input, easing panel) use white fills with tiny soft shadows and medium radius. Bullets are simple dots with bolded lead-in labels.

## Signature
- Two-tone headlines: dark indigo first line, accent-colored second line ("in a snap." in amber, "with ease and speed" in pink, "for product teams" in purple).
- Pastel rounded tiles filled with flat vector shapes and mini animation previews instead of photos or mockups.
- Purple-gradient full-width CTA banner with heavy corner rounding before the footer.
- Single dark indigo section amid an otherwise white page, hosting floating 3D-scattered device and shape illustrations.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas pure white and let flat pastel card fills do all the sectioning — no borders or dividers.
- Color the second line of every major headline with a rotating accent (purple, amber, pink, teal).
- Use pill buttons everywhere, with the lavender purple fill reserved for the main CTA.
- Round cards generously (16-24px) and pair each feature block with an illustration tile on the opposite side.
- Break long light pages with exactly one dark indigo or purple-gradient band.

Don't:
- Add drop shadows or outlines to buttons and cards; the style is flat except tiny floating UI chips.
- Use photography — all imagery is flat vector shapes, blob characters, and editor screenshots.
- Set headlines in a sharp or condensed face; the type is soft, rounded, and friendly throughout.
- Let accent colors bleed into body copy; paragraphs stay small, gray-indigo, and quiet.
- Stack multiple dark sections; the near-black band works because it is singular.
