## Overview
A candy-pastel, photo-driven SaaS look: white canvas broken into full-width rounded slabs of pastel pink, lavender, pale blue, cream, and mint, each holding lifestyle photography of real employees in rounded frames. Near-black ink and a single hot-magenta pill CTA cut through the softness. Section headings alternate between a characterful display serif and giant tight grotesque, giving an editorial, fashion-adjacent voice to an HR product.

## Layout
Slim white sticky nav: logo left, centered text links (Overview, Why Tedy?, Resources, Demo, Pricing), right side language switcher + magenta pill "Try for free" + black pill "Sign in". Home hero is a full-bleed photo with huge white serif headline overlaid and two pill CTAs, followed by a logo strip. Page rhythm is a stack of alternating full-width pastel color bands with generous vertical padding; inside them, centered headings over 3-column card grids or 2-column photo-plus-text splits. Product UI mockups float on pastel panels with small overlapping widget chips. Every page ends with the same dark near-black "Schedule a demo" footer block: rounded container, blurred glassy inner panel, glowing pink/purple/yellow/teal abstract shapes, giant white grotesque headline, magenta + white pill CTAs.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped, solid magenta with white label for primary, solid black or white pills for secondary, some with small arrow/play glyphs; no borders or shadows. Cards: white, large 16-24px radius, borderless and nearly shadow-free, sitting on pastel section backgrounds; pricing cards add a pastel pill plan-badge at top and colored check-circle feature lists (pink, purple, teal checks matching plan color); the featured plan gets a pink border + blush fill. Testimonial cards are full-width pastel slabs (rotating pink/blue/cream/lavender) pairing a quote with a rounded photo and small pill tags. FAQ is a hairline-divided accordion with black circular plus icons. Photos always get large rounded corners.

## Signature
- Rotating pastel section slabs (blush, lavender, sky, cream, mint) on a white canvas, each a full-width rounded band
- Hot-magenta pill CTA as the only saturated interactive color against pastels and black
- Serif/grotesque headline duet: quirky display serif for feature sections, oversized tight grotesque for hero-scale statements, with pastel highlighter marks behind key words
- Recurring dark glassy "Schedule a demo" footer with glowing blurred confetti shapes (pink circle, purple slab, yellow burst, teal triangle)

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate full-width pastel background bands between white sections to pace long pages; keep card fills white on top of them
- Reserve the saturated magenta strictly for pill CTAs and small check/tag details; let everything else stay pastel or black
- Mix the display serif and grotesque intentionally: serif for warm feature/benefit headings, giant grotesque for blunt statements (FAQs, Schedule a demo, pricing hero)
- Round everything generously — photos, cards, mockup panels — and float small UI widget chips over lifestyle photos
- Close every page with the dark rounded demo-CTA block with glowing abstract shapes

Don't:
- Add borders or heavy drop shadows to cards; separation comes from the pastel band behind them
- Use gradients on buttons or in content sections — glow/blur effects live only in the dark footer block
- Introduce a second saturated brand color for CTAs; purple, yellow, and teal appear only as decorative shapes and icon tints
- Square off corners on buttons (always pill) or photos (always heavily rounded)
- Set body copy in anything but the neutral grotesque, small and quiet, letting scale contrast with the oversized headings carry hierarchy
