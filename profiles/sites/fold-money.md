## Overview
A friendly, minimal-light fintech landing page built almost entirely from one rounded sans family in dark navy on white and cool-gray (#edeff3) bands. Restraint everywhere except deliberate moments of play: a hand-drawn collage above the hero headline, sketchy coin and key illustrations, and a wobbly "ANXIETY" ribbon. Real product UI (transaction cards, dashboard, phone mockups) does the selling instead of abstract graphics.

## Layout
Sparse top nav: tiny logo left, a handful of small pill links pinned to the corners; no heavy nav bar. Hero is fully centered: illustration cluster, giant two-line headline ("Be painfully aware."), short paragraph, then a gray QR-code download card and a large product screenshot. Sections alternate white and #edeff3 backgrounds in long vertical rhythm; most are two-column (stacked product-UI cards on one side, app icon + heavy headline + chips + paragraph on the other). One full-bleed dark-navy (#20284c) section breaks the light flow mid-page. Footer is a bento grid of rounded cards: solid blue QR card, team photo, privacy card, link list. Generous whitespace throughout; content column stays narrow.

## Components
Primary CTA is a solid #469bf7 blue rectangle with white label and long arrow ("Read our manifesto"), ~10-12px radius. Secondary actions are white pill buttons with thin borders ("Coming soon", "Team"). Feature chips are white pills with a hairline navy border and small icon ("No Email scraping"). Cards are white, 16px+ radius, with very soft diffuse shadows; transaction rows stack as overlapping rounded cards with category tags in light-gray pill badges. The browser-bar mockup and search input use large-radius fields with a visible navy outline on focus. Eyebrow labels are small all-caps navy text.

## Signature
- Single chubby rounded sans used at every scale, from 80px+ headlines to caption chips, always in the same dark navy
- Punchy, personality-driven copy as the visual centerpiece ("Be painfully aware.", "Never visit your Bank's website again.")
- Hand-drawn illustration accents (coins, key, food collage, kraft-paper ANXIETY ribbon) colliding with otherwise clinical UI screenshots
- Bento-style footer of rounded cards, including a solid #469bf7 QR card and a real team photo

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set headlines huge and heavy in the rounded sans, dark navy #22274c, sentence case with a period
- Alternate white and #edeff3 section backgrounds, and drop in one full-width #20284c navy section for contrast
- Sell with realistic product UI: stacked transaction cards, phone mockups, dashboard screenshots with soft shadows
- Use white pill chips with hairline borders for feature callouts, and reserve solid #469bf7 fills for the one real CTA per section
- Sprinkle hand-drawn/sketchy illustrations as section dividers and hero garnish

Don't:
- Don't introduce a second typeface or a serif; the whole page is one rounded family
- Don't use gradients, glassmorphism, or saturated multi-color palettes; color beyond navy/blue lives only inside illustrations and the logo green
- Don't build a heavy nav bar or mega-menu; navigation is a few corner pills
- Don't sharpen corners; nothing on the page is squarer than ~10px
- Don't make body copy corporate-neutral; the voice is casual and slightly cheeky, and the design leans on it
