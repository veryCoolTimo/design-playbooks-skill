## Overview
A near-black, dot-grid canvas that treats a painterly sunset-landscape illustration as the hero artifact, framed like a canvas print inside rounded corners. Chrome and pearl 3D spheres drift across sections, and thin colored spark-streaks fall through the dot grid. Mid-page the site inverts to a warm ivory (#f8f5ee) with blueprint-style line drawings on dark cards, then returns to the illustration under a giant cropped "micro" wordmark. Headlines alternate between a tight grotesque sans and an elegant transitional serif.

## Layout
Minimal top nav: wordmark left; "Login" text link and a black pill CTA with an iridescent gradient border right. Hero is a full-bleed rounded-corner illustration with the one-word headline "Organized." bottom-right in huge sans and a serif subline, CTA pill bottom-left. Sections are single centered columns with enormous vertical whitespace between them; rhythm alternates statement typography, floating product screenshots (framed like a monitor), a horizontally scrolling card rail, and an isometric board scene. A hard canvas flip from dark to ivory begins the "Built different" chapter, opened by an infinite marquee headline; feature cards there sit in a staggered two-column offset grid with numbered [01]-[03] captions. Footer: pill-shaped testimonial capsule, centered serif CTA line, black pill button, then the wordmark cropped over the illustration.

## Components
Buttons: pure pills, no shadow — ivory fill with near-black text on dark sections; black fill with white text on ivory; nav variant is black with a 1px multicolor gradient stroke. Cards: dark #14151a fills with medium radius, hairline borders, tiny teal-outlined status chips ("Demo Meeting", "Closed"), tag pills, and monospace metadata; feature cards on ivory hold thin-line gold/teal wireframe illustrations. Screenshots wrapped in dark rounded bezels with subtle drop shadow. Empty capsule and circle outlines used as decorative ghost shapes on the ivory canvas. No visible inputs.

## Signature
- Painterly sunset landscape (teal sky #51d4cc, pink-gold valley) recurring as hero, in-app greeting art, and footer backdrop behind a viewport-wide cropped "micro" wordmark.
- Photoreal chrome/pearl spheres floating over a dotted grid with thin falling spark-streaks in teal/orange.
- Sans-serif statement + serif subline pairing repeated for nearly every section heading; serif carries the emotional lines.
- Hard mid-page inversion from #0f1014 to ivory #f8f5ee, with blueprint line-art cards and a marquee "Micro is Built different."
- Monospace uppercase micro-labels ([ 01 ], AI-POWERED, CONTACT US) as quiet metadata.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep buttons strictly pill-shaped and monochrome (ivory-on-dark, black-on-ivory); reserve the gradient stroke for the single nav CTA.
- Frame illustrations and app screenshots inside large-radius containers so art reads as mounted objects, not backgrounds.
- Alternate grotesque sans and display serif within a heading pair; let the serif handle sublines and testimonials.
- Use teal #22baad only in small doses: chip outlines, status text, sort arrows, spark-streaks.
- Leave several viewport-heights of dotted-grid emptiness between sections; decorate it with spheres and ghost capsule outlines, not content.

Don't:
- Don't add saturated brand color to buttons or backgrounds — color lives exclusively in the illustration, wireframe strokes, and tiny chips.
- Don't use boxy or small-radius buttons; nothing interactive here has corners.
- Don't fill the ivory chapter with dense content — it stays airy with staggered dark cards and thin outlines.
- Don't set body copy in the serif; it is display-only, always centered.
- Don't drop the dot-grid texture on either canvas — flat solid backgrounds would break the page's atmosphere.
