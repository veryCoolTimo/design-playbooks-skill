## Overview
Big-type playground on a clean white canvas: enormous book-weight grotesque headlines wrapped in outlined lozenges and solid black highlight bars, floating over pastel color-block panels filled with sticker-like chat-UI vignettes. Feels like an annotated whiteboard — cursors, name tags, and comment threads are the illustration language, matching the product (collaboration SDK). Color is applied in flat candy fields (periwinkle, mint, coral, powder blue, butter yellow) against black-and-white typography.
## Layout
Slim top nav: tiny wordmark left, plain text links right, single pill "Get Started" CTA in periwinkle. Heroes are type-first — left-aligned or centered display headline in outline-pill + black-bar treatment, short paragraph, two small pill buttons. Sections alternate: full-width off-white (#F1EFE9) rounded panels holding product screenshots with a black caption chip and a purple pill CTA, then a 2- or 3-up mosaic of rounded pastel cards. Product page uses a strict zig-zag: pastel illustration card one side, eyebrow-label + heading + short copy + outlined pill button the other. Pricing is a classic 3-column table with colored top borders (green/purple/black) per tier plus a coral-ruled "Early Stage" strip. Footer flips to near-black with a giant white wordmark.
## Components
Buttons: pills throughout — filled periwinkle (#8B7BF5) for primary, tier-colored fills on pricing (green/purple/black), thin 1px black-outline pills with arrows for secondary. Cards: large-radius (16-24px) flat pastel or off-white blocks, no shadows, no borders. Pricing cards: white, 1px hairline border, 3-4px colored top edge. Feature cards on pricing: flat white tiles with small rounded-square icon chips. Headline decoration components: pill-shaped 1px outline around one line, solid black rounded bar with white text on the next. FAQ: hairline-divided accordion rows with chevrons. Screenshots sit as tilted sticker collages with hand-drawn cursors and name-tag chips.
## Signature
- Display headlines dressed in graphic containers: one line inside a thin outlined pill, the next reversed out of a solid black rounded bar.
- Flat candy-pastel color-block cards (periwinkle, mint, coral, powder blue, blush, butter) used as illustration stages, never as text backgrounds for long copy.
- Product UI rendered as playful stickers — floating comment threads, avatar stacks, "Viewing now" chips, oversized hand-drawn cursor doodles.
- Book-weight type at poster scale; hierarchy comes from size and the pill/bar devices, not from bold weights.
## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set headlines huge at regular weight and add hierarchy with the outline-pill + black-bar wrapper, not heavier fonts.
- Keep the base canvas white (or #F1EFE9 panels) and pour color only into large rounded illustration cards and small pill CTAs.
- Use pill buttons everywhere; reserve the periwinkle fill for the main CTA and thin black outlines for secondary actions.
- Illustrate with the product itself — chat bubbles, cursors, avatar chips arranged as tilted sticker collages.
- Color-code parallel items (pricing tiers, brand-name pills) with the accent set: green, periwinkle, coral, black.
Don't:
- Add drop shadows, gradients, or glassmorphism — every surface here is flat with hairline borders at most.
- Use bold or condensed display weights; the look depends on light, airy poster type.
- Put body copy on the pastel fields; long text always sits on white in near-black ink.
- Introduce photography — the site is 100% typography, flat color, and stylized UI vignettes.
- Shrink the headline devices; small pills and bars around modest type lose the big-type identity.
