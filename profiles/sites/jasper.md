## Overview
Warm off-white canvas with near-black ink, punctuated by one saturated electric purple that owns every primary CTA. Softness comes from pastel tint blocks (mint, lavender, peach, sky) and airy isometric line illustrations, while heavy near-black sections with faint perspective grid lines bookend each page. The home hero is the gradient-mesh moment: a sunset orange-to-purple sky over a field of 3D purple tiles. Note: the resources/customer-stories page runs a newer variant — serif display type, red-orange CTAs, lowercase "jasper" wordmark, collage photography on a sky-blue grid — treat it as an emerging rebrand layered on the same structure.

## Layout
Slim top nav: logo left, five center links (Platform, Solutions, Resources, Company, Pricing), Log In / Free Trial / purple "Get A Demo" pill-ish button right. Heroes are centered headline blocks; company page runs an oversized statement headline with inline pastel-highlighted words and tiny icon chips embedded mid-sentence. Sections alternate: white content band, pastel illustration grid, dark near-black band with thin perspective/grid linework. Every page ends the same way: a dark section containing a floating light editor-UI mockup ("Get started with Jasper today") surrounded by scattered 3D purple-edged folder icons, then a four-column dark footer. Content sits in a contained max-width column with wide margins; 3-4 column card grids carry features, stats, and resources.

## Components
Buttons: small-radius (4-6px) rectangles; primary is solid electric purple with white label, secondary is white/outlined with dark border; compact padding, no shadows. Cards: white or pastel-tinted fills, 1px light borders or borderless, 8-14px corners, flat (no drop shadows), often with a small line icon top-left and an arrow affordance bottom-right. Stat tiles: pastel background chips pairing a huge numeral with a small caption. Pricing: comparison table with thin hairline rows and checkmarks; three plan cards sit on a full-bleed purple gradient header. Accordions on solutions page use hairline dividers and small chevron squares. Testimonial cards mix photo tiles with colored quote tiles in a mosaic.

## Signature
- One electric purple (#8624F5) reserved almost exclusively for CTAs against a warm greige canvas — nothing else competes with it.
- Dark near-black closing sections with faint 3D perspective grid lines and floating isometric folder/document illustrations around a product-UI mockup.
- Pastel tint system (mint, lavender, peach, sky) used as card fills and inline text highlights inside headlines.
- Fine-lined isometric diagrams on cream tiles instead of screenshots for feature explanation.

## Do
- Reserve the purple fill for primary buttons only; render secondary actions as white/outlined buttons beside them.
- Alternate light cream bands with full-bleed near-black sections carrying thin grid/perspective linework.
- Use pastel background chips for stats and highlight strips inside display headlines.
- Explain features with monoline isometric illustrations on soft-tinted cards, arrow links in corners.
- End every page with the dark "editor mockup + floating folders" CTA section before the footer.

## Don't
- Don't add drop shadows or heavy borders — the system is flat, separated by tint and hairlines.
- Don't use pure white (#FFFFFF) as the page canvas; the base is warm off-white/greige.
- Don't round buttons into pills or oversize their padding; controls stay compact and near-square.
- Don't introduce more saturated hues alongside the purple in the core system (the red-serif look belongs only to the rebrand variant, not mixed in).
- Don't use photography as section backgrounds mid-page; photos live inside contained rounded tiles.
