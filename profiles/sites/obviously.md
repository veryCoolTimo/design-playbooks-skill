## Overview
Minimal-light SaaS marketing site that stays disciplined in structure but playful in decoration. White and pale lavender (#EDF1FB) sections alternate down the page, dark near-black grotesque type carries the message, and saturated blue/green/purple/yellow appear only inside cards, badges, and toy-like Memphis illustrations. The overall read is "serious data product, friendly personality."

## Layout
Sticky-feeling top nav: wordmark left, centered text links (active link in blue), single solid-blue Get Started button right. Heroes are centered: small badge or illustration cluster, 2-line display headline, short subcopy, blue primary + white secondary button pair, then a grayscale logo strip. Section rhythm alternates full-width white and #EDF1FB bands, with one dark plum (#3A3340) testimonial band for contrast. Content sits in a narrow centered column; features use 2-3 column card grids; case studies switch to a two-column layout (bordered fact card left, long-form prose right). Faint grid patterns, diamonds, and sparkles float in hero margins.

## Components
- Buttons: solid #2E7CF6 fill, white label, small 4-6px radius, compact padding; secondary is white with light gray border, same radius. Dark sections use white or green buttons.
- Cards: two families — soft cards (white or tinted fill, 8-14px radius, hairline border or none, barely-there shadow) and "sticker" cards with a solid 1-2px black border and hard offset shadow (case-study stat card, Success Stories badge, speech-bubble quotes).
- Feature tiles: fully color-blocked (blue, green, purple, yellow) with white text, small tag pill, and a flat illustration in the lower half.
- Pricing: three white columns on lavender, a green "Popular" pill, checklists with green check / gray x circles, blue CTAs top and bottom of each column.
- FAQ rows: tinted lavender pills with a blue circled plus icon.
- Small colored tag pills (yellow "Model Building", green "Step 4") label sections and steps.

## Signature
- Color-blocked capability cards (blue/green/purple) with flat toy-like illustrations and dotted-texture backgrounds.
- Yellow highlighter swipe on key headline words ("$1,000 /mo.", "Team").
- Black-outlined cards and speech bubbles with hard offset shadows — a comic/sticker accent inside an otherwise soft minimal page.
- Floating circular avatar chips with tiny name tags (Sarah, Scott) scattered around heroes and CTA banners.
- Full-width bright-blue CTA banner with circuit-line doodles before every footer.

## Do
- Alternate white and #EDF1FB section backgrounds; reserve the dark plum band for a single testimonial/social-proof section.
- Keep CTAs uniform: small-radius solid #2E7CF6 buttons with short labels ("Get Started"), paired with a quiet white secondary.
- Use saturated color only inside cards, pills, illustrations, and highlights — never as a whole-page background outside the CTA banner.
- Label sections and steps with small colored tag pills, and mark one headline word with a yellow highlight when emphasis is needed.
- Mix soft borderless cards with occasional black-bordered offset-shadow "stickers" for stats and quotes.

## Don't
- Don't use pill or 16px+ radius buttons; controls here are compact and nearly square-cornered.
- Don't set headlines in a serif or ultra-bold display face — everything is one grotesque family at medium/semibold.
- Don't apply heavy drop shadows or gradients to cards; depth comes from tint changes and hairline borders (gradient is allowed only on the blue CTA banner).
- Don't use photography — people appear only as small circular avatar photos; everything else is flat vector illustration.
- Don't color body text; prose stays near-black/gray with blue reserved for links and active nav.
