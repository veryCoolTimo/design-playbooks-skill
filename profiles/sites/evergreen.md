## Overview
Warm ivory canvas, near-black ink, and a single mint green (#BFEDC0) carry almost the whole page; black pill CTAs do the converting. Enormous high-contrast serif headlines are interrupted mid-sentence by photo cutouts of smiling people inside mint capsules, and hand-drawn leaf line-art frames the section breaks. The result reads editorial and organic rather than corporate SaaS, with one deep aubergine (#400C40) panel as the only saturated dark surface.

## Layout
Sticky-feel top nav on the cream canvas: serif wordmark left, plain text links right, one outlined pill ("Schedule a demo") as nav CTA. Hero is fully centered: two-line serif headline with inline photo capsules, short sans paragraph, single black pill CTA, then a product mock (recognition feed cards over a deep purple panel) with a stat banner above it. Sections alternate between the ivory canvas and a warm greige (#EDECE1) band, with hand-drawn leaf borders marking each transition. Mid-page sections switch to a two-column rhythm: UI mock or stat card left, heading plus icon-led bullet list right. Generous vertical whitespace throughout; content column stays narrow and centered. Footer returns to ivory with newsletter signup left and two link columns right.

## Components
- Buttons: primary is a solid black pill with bold white sans label ("Start 14 Day Trial"); secondary is a cream pill with a thin 1px black outline ("Schedule a demo"). Footer "Plant a tree" is solid black but nearly square-cornered, fused to the email input.
- Cards: white (#FFFFFF) recognition-feed cards with thin dark borders, 12-20px radius, soft or no shadow; testimonial cards with a circular mint-backed portrait overlapping the top edge; a deep aubergine #400C40 rounded panel behind the hero feed.
- Chips/pills: mint-outlined stat pills with a bold percentage inside; small mint tag chips ("Value: Grit") with thin borders; emoji reaction pills inside feed cards.
- Inputs: footer email field is a plain rectangle, thin black border, muted gray placeholder.
- Iconography: small leaf glyphs as list bullets; circled line-art social icons; third-party logos (Slack, Teams, G2) in full color.

## Signature
- Photo cutouts of people inside mint pill capsules embedded directly in the giant serif headline.
- One-green discipline: nearly everything is ivory/black/mint, so the deep purple hero panel and full-color logos pop.
- Hand-drawn leaf and vine line illustrations used as section dividers and margin decoration.
- Black pill CTAs on a warm cream page — no colored buttons anywhere.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas warm ivory (#FFFFF4) and alternate with greige #EDECE1 bands; never pure white page background.
- Set headlines in a heavy high-contrast serif at very large sizes with tight tracking; keep all UI/body text in the friendly sans.
- Use solid black pills for every primary CTA and thin-outlined cream pills for secondary actions.
- Reserve mint #BFEDC0 for capsules, chips, avatar backgrounds, and illustration fills — as tint, not as button color.
- Break section edges with hand-drawn leaf line art instead of hard dividers.

Don't:
- Don't introduce gradients, glassmorphism, or saturated blue/teal SaaS palettes — the page has none.
- Don't color the CTA green; conversion buttons are black on this site.
- Don't use stock-photo hero images; people appear only as small capsule cutouts and avatar circles.
- Don't add heavy drop shadows — cards rely on thin borders and flat fills.
- Don't use the aubergine #400C40 broadly; it appears on a single hero panel and matching chips.
