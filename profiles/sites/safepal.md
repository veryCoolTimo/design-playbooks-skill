## Overview
A dark-neon crypto landing that alternates between a starfield navy (#0D0B33) hero/footer and long stretches of saturated blue-violet (#4B21EE). Mint green (#7BF0C1) is the single energizing accent, carried by CTAs, illustration highlights, and teal uppercase eyebrows. Playful isometric 2.5D illustrations — outlined coins, vaults, phones, and a rainbow-mouthed mascot — keep the security story friendly rather than austere.

## Layout
Sticky-feel top nav: logo left, four center links, one white pill Download button right. Full-bleed hero with a giant two-line centered headline over a night-sky scene of floating isometric devices. Sections are long, center-aligned, single-column blocks with heavy vertical padding; a partner-logo marquee strip separates hero from body. One signature move: a huge dark-navy rounded panel (~40px radius) inset on the violet canvas holding the "Get more from your wallet" content. A light off-white (#F7F6FD) section near the bottom breaks the dark rhythm, then violet testimonials in a horizontal card rail, then a navy closing CTA block above a multi-column footer.

## Components
Buttons are all pills in three tiers: mint-green filled with near-black bold text (main CTAs, often with a soft concentric glow ring), white filled with dark text (nav Download, mid-page CTA), and ghost pills with a thin 1px white outline on violet (secondary Download / Buy Now). Cards: off-white rounded (~16-20px) testimonial cards with quote, avatar, name, and role on the violet background. Feature items are icon-over-text stacks with small outlined isometric icons, no card chrome. Section headings get a teal uppercase eyebrow line and a small white sparkle/x glyph floated beside the headline. No inputs visible.

## Signature
- Saturated #4B21EE violet fields bracketed by starfield-navy hero and footer, with tiny scattered star and sparkle glyphs throughout
- Mint-green pill CTAs with a soft glow halo against violet/navy
- Outlined isometric 2.5D illustrations (coins, vault, hardware wallet, mascot) in mint, navy, and white with halftone-dot textures
- Extra-bold centered headlines ending in periods ("Trusted by millions.") paired with teal uppercase eyebrows

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate deep navy (#0D0B33) and vivid violet (#4B21EE) full-bleed bands; use one light off-white band to reset the eye before the close
- Reserve mint #7BF0C1 for primary CTAs and illustration highlights only; add a subtle glow ring around key pills
- Center headlines, keep them huge and extra-bold, end them with a period, and top them with a teal all-caps eyebrow
- Use outlined isometric illustration and sprinkle small star/sparkle glyphs to build the "space" atmosphere
- Nest a large-radius dark panel inside a violet section to spotlight a content cluster

Don't:
- Use rectangular or small-radius buttons — every button here is a pill
- Introduce warm colors (orange/red/yellow) beyond tiny coin icons; the palette is strictly violet/navy/mint/white
- Left-align hero or section headings; the entire page rhythm is center-axis
- Use photography — product UI mockups and line-art isometric illustration carry all imagery
- Put mint text on the light section; on off-white, switch to near-black ink (#1E1E20) and keep mint for button fills
