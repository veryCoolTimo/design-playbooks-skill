## Overview
A high-energy Web3 landing system built on full-bleed color blocking: soft warm-gray heroes with glossy 3D renders alternate with saturated deep-indigo and electric-lime sections. Headlines in a friendly rounded grotesque sit centered over blob-shaped cards and playful 3D props (keys, locks, gems, chain links). Despite the loud palette, layouts stay airy and symmetric, giving a polished fintech feel rather than chaos.

## Layout
Slim pill-shaped floating nav bar (logo left, menu toggle right) that inverts color per section background. Hero: left-aligned headline + subcopy + single CTA beside a large 3D phone mockup on a light 3D-tile backdrop. Body is a strict rhythm of alternating full-width color slabs — gray → lime → indigo → gray → lime — each with huge rounded corners (40px+) where slabs meet, like stacked super-ellipse panels. Sections are mostly center-aligned headlines with content below; feature groups use asymmetric blob-card clusters connected by thin vertical/branching connector lines. Generous vertical whitespace inside every slab; subtle tonal watermark pattern (chain-link motif) textures the lime and indigo backgrounds.

## Components
Buttons: pill shape, solid deep-indigo fill with white label on light/lime backgrounds; lime-outline or lime-tinted pill variants on indigo; many carry a trailing arrow. Cards: irregular blob/speech-bubble shapes with one exaggerated corner, dark indigo fill with white text (on indigo sections) or translucent white fill (on light sections); no visible borders, soft or no shadows. Team grid on About: photo cards with medium-rounded corners on a rounded light-green container panel. Footer: white super-rounded panel containing a waitlist email input — pill input field with solid indigo pill "Sign up" button inline. Small lime pill eyebrow labels ("• ABOUT", "• SECURITY") tag section intros.

## Signature
- Tri-color slab rhythm: warm gray, electric lime (#B8F44C), and deep indigo (#2A1D85) stacked with giant rounded corners between sections
- Glossy 3D props everywhere — phones, keys, padlocks, gems, chain links — floating with soft reflections
- Blob/speech-bubble feature cards with one oversized corner instead of rectangular cards
- Rounded, slightly quirky display grotesque that keeps loud colors feeling friendly
- Thin connector lines linking cards and mockups into flow diagrams

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate full-width color slabs (gray / lime / indigo) and round their meeting edges 40px+
- Keep exactly one pill CTA per section, indigo fill on light, lime-accented on indigo
- Center section headlines and let 3D props or mockups carry the visual weight
- Texture large color fields with a faint tonal watermark pattern, never flat noise-free fills
- Invert text color per slab: indigo/dark ink on gray and lime, white plus lime highlights on indigo

Don't:
- Use rectangular sharp-cornered cards or buttons — everything here is pill or blob
- Add drop shadows or borders to cards; color contrast alone separates surfaces
- Introduce reds, oranges, or gradients as accents — the palette is strictly indigo/lime/lavender
- Use flat 2D illustration; imagery is glossy dimensional 3D renders
- Left-align section headlines mid-page; only heroes and feature-pair blocks break the centered rhythm
