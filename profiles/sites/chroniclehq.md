## Overview
Near-black, product-forward dark UI where the presentation tool itself is the hero imagery. Almost everything lives in grayscale — white headlines, gray body, charcoal cards — with violet reserved as a surgical accent for keywords, tag chips, and feature icons. The mood is quiet, dense, and premium, closer to a design-tool brand than a marketing site.

## Layout
Sticky minimal top nav: wordmark left, three text links center, single white pill CTA right. Hero is a centered-left headline block above a huge framed app screenshot floating on black. Sections alternate: small violet eyebrow label + large heading, then bento-style card grids (2-3 columns of charcoal panels showing product UI). Mid-page uses a full-width plain-text manifesto paragraph on pure black with generous whitespace. Pre-footer breaks the dark rhythm with an off-white marquee band ("Join our beta" repeated in giant text) before a light footer. The our-story page is a single narrow centered column, story told in alternating oversized statements, photos, and chat-bubble screenshots, ending in a full-bleed indigo (#5B5BD6) mission block.

## Components
Buttons: small pill shape; primary is solid white fill with black text ("Join beta"); secondary appears as dark ghost pills. Tag chips: pill, thin gray border or violet fill, tiny uppercase-ish labels. Cards: charcoal (#141416-ish) rounded 10-16px panels, no visible shadows, hairline separation by tone rather than border; product screenshots embedded edge-to-edge inside them. Eyebrow labels: small violet diamond/plus glyph + violet text preceding section headings. Feature icons on the story page sit in violet rounded squares.

## Signature
- Pure-black canvas with one violet accent word inside otherwise white headlines ("Impactful", "frustrated", "convenience").
- Bento grids of charcoal cards filled with real product UI and mini data-viz (charts, stat tiles).
- Giant repeated marquee band "Join our beta" in off-white breaking the dark page before the footer.
- Tiny violet glyph + label eyebrows introducing every section; overall type scale swings from very small body to enormous display.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the background true near-black and build hierarchy with grays (white heads, ~#9B9B9F body, charcoal cards), not borders or shadows.
- Use violet (#8B7CF7) sparingly: one highlighted word per headline, eyebrow labels, chips, icon tiles.
- Make the primary CTA a small solid-white pill with black text; keep it the only high-contrast button on screen.
- Showcase the product with large embedded screenshots and bento card grids containing live-looking charts and stats.
- Break long dark scrolls with one inverted moment (light marquee band or a full-bleed indigo block).

Don't:
- Don't introduce gradients, glows, or neon — accents here are flat, matte violet/indigo.
- Don't put colored fills behind headlines; color lives inside the text as a single accented word.
- Don't use heavy card borders or drop shadows; surfaces separate by tonal steps only.
- Don't make body copy large or high-contrast; it stays small, gray, and secondary to display type.
- Don't crowd the layout — sections rely on wide black whitespace and a narrow content column (especially on story pages).
