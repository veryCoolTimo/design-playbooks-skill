## Overview
A warm, friendly minimal-light page built almost entirely from white space, dark forest-green rounded type, and one light spring-green CTA color. Soft ambient gradients — peach fading into pale lime — wash the hero and mid-page backgrounds instead of hard section dividers. Product credibility comes from realistic macOS window mockups (traffic-light dots, note text) dropped straight onto the gradient.

## Layout
Floating pill nav bar centered at top: wordmark left, three text links, one small pill Download button. Hero is fully centered: pill announcement chip, huge two-tone headline (dark green keywords, silver-gray connective words), short gray subhead, single green pill CTA, then a before/after pair of overlapping app windows labeled "Your notes + transcript" vs "AI enhanced." Below: grayscale logo strip, then long single-column rhythm of centered section headings with generous vertical whitespace; occasional two-card grid (How it works, pricing). Italic green pull-quote testimonials with avatar punctuate between sections. Tweet-card masonry (3 columns) for social proof. Footer CTA repeats the hero formula: centered headline + green pill button.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, flat light-green fill (#a2e681) with near-black text and Apple glyph; no border, no visible shadow, no gradient on the button itself. Secondary nav Download is a smaller white/outlined pill. Cards: white, large radius (~16-20px), hairline light border, very soft diffuse shadow; pricing cards are plain white with centered title, oversized price, hairline divider, checkmark feature line. Inline keyword highlights: rounded light-green pill backgrounds behind green words like "transcribes"/"enhances" inside headings. Template/tag chips: small outlined or dark-filled pills. App mockups carry macOS traffic-light dots and gray skeleton bars.

## Signature
- Two-tone headline trick: key phrases in dark forest green (#133622), filler words in pale silver gray (#b1b7b5)
- Peach-to-lime ambient gradient washes bleeding behind white app-window mockups
- One color does all CTA work: the light spring-green pill button, repeated hero and footer
- Rounded, slightly chunky geometric sans everywhere, with italic green pull-quotes as section breathers

## Do
- Keep every section centered and single-purpose, separated by very generous whitespace instead of background bands
- Use soft radial peach/lime gradients only as backdrops for hero and feature moments; let mid-page rest on plain white
- Render the product as realistic desktop windows with traffic-light dots, not abstract illustrations
- Highlight one verb per heading with a rounded green pill background
- Repeat the exact same pill CTA (light green fill, dark text, Apple glyph) top and bottom

## Don't
- Don't use saturated or dark green fills for buttons — the CTA green stays light and pastel (#a2e681)
- Don't add borders, gradients, or heavy shadows to buttons; flat pill only
- Don't set headlines in a single color — the gray/green alternation is core to the look
- Don't introduce sharp corners; everything from chips to cards is pill or 16px+ rounded
- Don't crowd sections with multi-column feature grids; max two cards side by side, otherwise stack and center
