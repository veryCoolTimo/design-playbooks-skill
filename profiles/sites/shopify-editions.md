## Overview
An editorial launch page that collages Renaissance oil paintings (floating figures in classical dress, cypress landscapes, starry night skies) with modern props: skateboards, hot-pink shopping bags, laptops, a purple superhero mask. The page moves from a dark painterly hero into a long, calm greige "paper" canvas via a torn-paper edge. Type does the theatrics: enormous white grotesque product names over the art, then serif prose with calligraphic drop caps for the feature chapters.

## Layout
Slim dark top bar: logo + "Winter '26" label left, Editions dropdown and Search center-left, Shopify.com link and a white pill "Start for free" CTA right. Full-bleed painted hero with a thin hairline rectangle framing the centered title. Inside chapters, a fixed left rail holds the "The Renaissance Edition" wordmark (with "ai" set in italic serif inside "Renaissance"), a chapter table of contents with Roman numerals (I-XII) and dotted leaders, plus tiny footer legal. Content sits in a wide right column: script-initial serif section headline, small bold sans kicker label, 2-3 line serif description, small "Read help doc" button, then a large illustrative area — e.g. a fanned deck of rotated browser-window cards with vivid gradient thumbnails, or ghosted floating chat cards. Generous vertical whitespace between chapters; the dark-to-light torn-paper transition is a major rhythmic beat.

## Components
Primary CTA: solid white pill, black text, no border, no shadow. Secondary "Read help doc" button: canvas-colored fill, thin dark hairline border, ~4px radius, tiny text with external-link arrow icon. Cards: white browser-window chrome with ~12px radius, soft diffuse shadows, playfully rotated and overlapped like scattered polaroids; inner gradient artwork panels with smaller radius. Chat/prompt cards render semi-transparent and blurred, fading into the background. Video embed is a plain rectangular thumbnail with a rounded-square play button. Hairline 1px rules and thin frame outlines throughout.

## Signature
- Renaissance paintings remixed with modern commerce props (pink shopping bag, skateboard, laptop, purple mask) as full-bleed scene art
- Torn-paper edge transition from dark starry hero to warm greige paper canvas
- Script swash drop caps opening serif headlines ("Complexity, delegated"), against giant flat grotesque product names ("Sidekick")
- Persistent left rail with Roman-numeral chapter index and dotted leader lines, magazine-style

## Do / Don't
- Do stage one oversized grotesque word over full-bleed art for each chapter hero, then switch to serif prose below
- Do keep the canvas a warm muted greige (#DEDDD0) and let saturated color live only inside artwork and small props (magenta, purple, gradients)
- Do use hairline borders, dotted leaders, and Roman numerals for navigation chrome instead of boxes and pills
- Do rotate and overlap content cards loosely with soft shadows rather than aligning them to a strict grid
- Don't use colored buttons — the only CTA fill on the page is white on dark, and secondary buttons are outlined hairline rectangles
- Don't put body copy in a sans; long-form descriptions are serif, sans is reserved for labels, nav, and hero words
- Don't use flat solid backgrounds in the hero — it is always layered painting collage with atmospheric depth
- Don't round everything uniformly; pill is reserved for the primary CTA while secondary buttons stay near-square
- Don't crowd chapters — each feature gets a full viewport-scale block with large empty greige space around it
