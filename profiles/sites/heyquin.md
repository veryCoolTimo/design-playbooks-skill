## Overview
Warm minimal-light design built on a two-mode palette: cream (#F3F0E4) hero and feature sections alternating with deep forest-green (#0E3D2C) blocks, punctuated by a single electric chartreuse (#DDE93A) used for CTAs and highlighted keywords. Nearly monochromatic green-on-cream typography keeps it calm; photography of real people plus floating white UI-snippet cards make the AI product feel human. Overall feel: premium, organic, quietly confident.

## Layout
Ultra-minimal nav: wordmark left, one dark-green pill CTA right — no menu links visible. Hero is center-aligned: large two-line headline, small subcopy, dual pill buttons (chartreuse primary + outlined secondary), then a rounded photo with an overlaid message-bubble card. Sections alternate cream/dark-green full-bleed bands. Feature grid is a 2x2 of pale mint gradient cards, each with centered heading, copy, small CTA, and product-UI collage bleeding off the card bottom. Final CTA section scatters rounded photo thumbnails with chartreuse label pills around a centered waitlist form on dark green. Footer strip is a scrolling chartreuse marquee ("With Quin, it's personal"). Generous vertical whitespace throughout; content column is narrow and centered.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, flat, no shadow — chartreuse fill with dark-green text (primary), thin dark-green outline on cream (secondary), dark-green fill with cream text (nav). Cards: large radius (~16-20px), pale mint-to-green subtle gradient fills, no visible border, minimal shadow. Floating UI snippets: white, medium radius, soft drop shadow, avatar + text rows. Inputs: white pill/rounded fields with placeholder text, no visible border, sitting on dark green. Accordion-style feature list on dark green uses chartreuse labels with "+" toggles. Thin hairline contour lines decorate backgrounds (light on cream, darker on green).

## Signature
- Three-color discipline: cream, forest green, chartreuse — chartreuse reserved strictly for action and emphasis.
- Alternating full-bleed cream and dark-green bands instead of a single page background.
- Floating white message-bubble/UI cards layered over lifestyle photography.
- Scrolling chartreuse marquee footer with repeated tagline and circular avatar photos.
- Faint topographic/contour line textures in section backgrounds.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the palette to cream + forest green + chartreuse; use chartreuse only for CTAs, highlighted keywords, and label pills.
- Alternate light and dark full-bleed sections to create rhythm without dividers or borders.
- Use pill buttons everywhere and large-radius (16px+) cards with soft gradient fills.
- Overlay small white rounded UI-snippet cards (avatars, messages, ratings) on photos to demonstrate the product.
- Center-align headlines and keep body copy short and narrow.

**Don't**
- Don't introduce blue, red, or any second accent hue — the system depends on one acid highlight.
- Don't use pure white as the page canvas; the warm cream is core to the look.
- Don't add heavy shadows, borders, or gradient buttons — surfaces are flat and clean.
- Don't crowd the nav with links; this site runs on a wordmark plus a single CTA.
- Don't set headlines in a serif or condensed face; the grotesque with modest weight carries the tone.
