## Overview
A minimal-light B2B SaaS system that stays almost monochrome (white canvas, near-black ink, gray #F2F2F2 hero bands) and then punches with huge flat blocks of saturated candy color — green #1DAF4D, yellow #FEE00E, pink #FF62EB, sky blue #6AA9F9 — each holding a floating white product-UI card. One indigo (#5755F4) owns every CTA. Dark charcoal (#202020) sections and a giant footer wordmark anchor the pages.

## Layout
Slim white sticky nav: left logo, centered dropdown links, right "Login" + indigo pill CTA. Heroes are centered on a light-gray dotted/textured band: eyebrow tag, 2-3 line headline, short subcopy, then an inline email-input + pill-button combo (not a bare button). Below the hero: a monochrome logo wall of customer marks in bordered cells. Section rhythm alternates: white text sections with a 3x3 dot-cluster ornament, then a full-bleed saturated color block containing a white screenshot card, then back to white. Mid-page dark (#202020) CTA and integration bands break the light flow. Feature grids run 3-up cards (pricing "Key features"); testimonials use an asymmetric 2-column mosaic of gray and dark cards. Footer is near-black with link columns and an oversized cropped "Default" wordmark spanning the full width.

## Components
Buttons: small pill, indigo #5755F4 fill with white label and a trailing arrow; dark charcoal pill variant ("Read story") on cards; most CTAs are welded to a white email input in one pill-shaped combo. Cards: white or light-gray #F5F5F5 fill, hairline border, 8-14px radius, effectively shadowless — flatness comes from color contrast, not elevation. Feature cards embed a screenshot on a flat color field with a small play badge. Pricing table: plain white card, thin row dividers, indigo check dots. FAQ: hairline-divided accordion rows with arrow glyphs. Testimonial cards carry small circular avatars with name/role in tiny gray type.

## Signature
- Full-bleed flat blocks of green/yellow/pink/orange/blue, each framing a floating white product-UI screenshot
- Repeating 3x3 dot-cluster ornament (indigo/black dots) marking section starts
- Email-input + indigo pill CTA fused into a single capsule, used everywhere a button would be
- Massive bold "Default" wordmark bleeding off the dark footer

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the base strictly white/gray/near-black; let color arrive only as full-bleed section blocks or the indigo CTA
- Put product screenshots on white cards floated over saturated flat color, not in browser-chrome mockups
- Use pill-shaped CTAs with trailing arrows, ideally paired with an email field in one capsule
- Mark section openings with the small dot-cluster motif and an eyebrow label
- End pages with a dark section and an oversized cropped wordmark

Don't:
- Add gradients, drop shadows, or glassmorphism — every surface here is flat with hairline borders
- Use the accent colors (green/yellow/pink/blue) for buttons or text; they are backgrounds only, indigo owns interaction
- Mix in serif or display typefaces — the whole system is one grotesque at varying weights
- Round cards to pill-level or leave them square; keep the 8-14px card / pill button split
- Let heroes go dark or photographic — heroes stay light gray with centered type
