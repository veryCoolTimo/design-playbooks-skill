## Overview
Near-black indigo canvas with a friendly, toy-like accent system: warm amber for every primary CTA, periwinkle and sky blue for secondary actions and illustration shapes. The tone is dark-tech but playful, not brooding — dotted motion-path lines, floating vector shapes, and cursor glyphs animate the "motion design" premise directly in the chrome. Product UI screenshots sit in slightly-lighter dark panels so the app feels native to the page.

## Layout
Fixed top nav: logo left, center dropdown links in small uppercase, right-side "LOGIN" text link plus a white pill "GET STARTED" button. Home hero is centered: uppercase micro-eyebrow, large two-line headline, short subcopy, single amber pill CTA, then a large app screenshot followed by a monochrome logo strip. Sections alternate left/right two-column blocks (heading + copy on one side, UI panel or abstract illustration on the other), each introduced by a colored uppercase eyebrow (CREATE, ANIMATE, EXPORT...). Feature lists render as airy 3-column icon grids with tiny icon, bold label, 2-line caption. Resources page is a dense masonry gallery of template thumbnails on the dark canvas. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; a repeated full-width centered CTA band ("Start building your motion design system") with amber primary + outlined secondary button precedes the multi-column footer everywhere.

## Components
- Buttons: full pill shape, small uppercase letter-spaced labels; primary = solid amber #FDB52D with dark text, secondary = white or outlined/ghost on dark; product page uses a sky-blue pill variant, pricing cards use periwinkle and amber fills per tier.
- Cards: panels in #1B1B23–#1E1E28 (one step lighter than canvas), 10-14px corners, no visible borders or shadows — separation is purely tonal.
- Pricing cards: four equal dark panels, plan name bold, large price, full-width pill CTA, checkmark feature lists in muted gray.
- FAQ: hairline-divided full-width accordion rows with chevrons, no card wrapper.
- Toggle: pill switch (Monthly/Annual) with amber "-20%" note; inline links also amber.
- Testimonial: full-width periwinkle (#8481E8) rounded band with white quote text — the loudest color block on the site.

## Signature
- One accent per job: amber owns conversion (every primary CTA sitewide), periwinkle/blue own illustration and secondary emphasis.
- Dotted bezier "motion path" lines with keyframe handles and cursor icons drifting through hero backgrounds — the product's subject matter drawn into the page.
- Uppercase letter-spaced micro-labels everywhere: eyebrows, nav items, button text.
- Borderless tonal layering: dark-on-darker panels with no strokes or shadows.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas near-black indigo (#131319) and build cards one tonal step lighter instead of using borders or shadows.
- Reserve amber exclusively for primary CTAs and small emphasis (links, discount notes); use pill shapes with uppercase labels.
- Introduce every section with a small uppercase colored eyebrow before the headline.
- Use playful flat-vector shapes (blobs, orbits, motion paths, cursors) in periwinkle/blue/amber as section illustrations.
- Alternate text-left/media-right and text-right/media-left in long feature sections, punctuated by 3-column icon grids.

Don't:
- Add glassmorphism, glow, gradients, or neon effects — surfaces here are flat and matte.
- Use amber for large background areas; the only big color block allowed is the periwinkle testimonial band.
- Put borders or drop shadows on cards, or use pure black backgrounds.
- Use square or small-radius buttons — every button on the site is a full pill.
- Set headlines in extra-bold or display serifs; headings stay medium-weight geometric sans at moderate sizes.
