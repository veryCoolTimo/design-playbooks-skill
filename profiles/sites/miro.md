## Overview
A light-gray "canvas" landing page that literally mimics the Miro whiteboard: a #EFEFEF dot-grid background with floating white cards, selection handles, collaborator cursors, and name tags scattered around the hero headline. Near-monochrome black-on-gray base with one loud accent — electric purple (#9C33FA) highlighting the word "AI"/"Intelligent" in headings — plus pastel sticky-note colors (yellow, periwinkle, pink) confined to product mockups. Alternating light and near-black (#151515) full-bleed sections create strong rhythm.

## Layout
Floating white pill-shaped nav bar (logo left, five text links, black CTA right) detached from the page top, sitting over the dot grid. Hero is centered: small intro line, oversized two-line headline wrapped in a Miro-style selection frame with corner handles and cursor decorations, short subhead, stacked black primary + white secondary buttons, then a huge rounded pastel-gradient demo video card. Sections alternate: centered display headings, then 1 large + 2-column feature card grid, two full-width dark inverted sections, 50/50 text-left/mockup-right white cards, a lavender testimonial card, 2-up "Discover more" cards, and a dense multi-column mega-footer on white.

## Components
Buttons: near-black (#171717) filled rectangles with ~12px radius and white label; secondary is white fill, same radius, subtle border/shadow; on dark sections the CTA inverts to white. Cards: white, 16-24px radius, effectively shadowless — they separate from the gray canvas by value contrast alone. Whiteboard props as UI: rounded name-tag pills (peach, pink, lavender) with cursor arrows, a "Miro AI" white pill tooltip, selection rectangles with dot handles. Waitlist form on the dark section: dark input fields with thin light borders, white button. Sticky-note tiles in mockups use saturated pastel fills (#F5D128 yellow, periwinkle, pink).

## Signature
- The page background IS the product: dot-grid canvas, selection handles, live cursors, and name tags decorating real page content.
- Single electric-purple accent word inside otherwise pure-black headings ("with AI", "Intelligent widgets").
- Detached floating white pill nav over gray, with a black rectangular CTA.
- Hard light/dark section alternation (#EFEFEF vs #151515) with the same centered-headline structure on both.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the base near-monochrome (#EFEFEF canvas, #111 ink, black buttons) and spend color only on the purple accent word and in-mockup sticky pastels.
- Decorate sections with whiteboard metaphors — cursors, name-tag pills, selection frames — around headlines and cards.
- Use large centered display headings (2 lines, tight tracking) to open every section.
- Alternate full-bleed near-black sections for feature and waitlist moments, inverting buttons to white.
- Let white cards float on the gray dot grid with big radii and no visible shadows.

Don't:
- Don't color buttons or links purple — purple is reserved for highlighted heading words; CTAs stay black (or white on dark).
- Don't use pure white as the page background; the gray dot-grid canvas is the identity.
- Don't add heavy drop shadows or borders to cards — separation comes from value contrast.
- Don't left-align hero or section headings; the composition is symmetric and centered.
- Don't let pastel sticky colors leak out of product mockups into page chrome.
