## Overview
A playful big-type landing built on three colors: warm cream, near-black cocoa brown, and banana yellow. Enormous all-caps condensed headlines carry the page; everything else is small and quiet. Hand-drawn doodle mascots and sketchy icon illustrations give it a friendly notebook feel that offsets the shouty typography.

## Layout
Sticky minimal top nav: wordmark left, three text links plus one yellow pill CTA right. Hero is pure centered type — a 3-line screaming headline ("YOUR KNOWLEDGE BUDDY!!!") over one small body line, then a product screenshot in a rounded outlined frame. Sections alternate full-bleed background bands: cream → yellow → dark brown → warm gray → yellow → dark brown, each band a two-column split (big left-aligned headline + short paragraph on the left, doodle illustration card on the right). Repeated stacked text as decoration ("FOR EVERYTHING" x5, fading). Generous vertical padding per band; content held to a wide centered container. Product page swaps in a split hero (headline left, UI mock right) on a yellow band but keeps the identical band rhythm and shared sections (feature trio, FAQ, dark footer).

## Components
Buttons: small pills, yellow fill with thin dark border and dark label for primary; dark brown pill with light label as alternate on cream/yellow bands; tiny icon + label combos ("Start on Telegram", "Add to Chrome"). Cards: cream or off-white fill, thin 1-2px dark outline, large 16-24px radius, containing sketchy line-art doodles with yellow spot fills. Filter/tag chips: outlined pills, active state filled yellow. FAQ rows: plain text accordion with right-aligned chevron/plus, no boxes. Testimonial card sits on a subtle grid-paper texture. Footer: dark brown, four link columns in small caps labels, yellow highlight tags ("We're hiring").

## Signature
- Three-color world: cream #FAF6EC, cocoa #2B2313, banana yellow #FBD65B — nothing else except gray band breaks.
- Colossal all-caps condensed black headlines with quirky letterforms (odd Y), often with "!!!" energy, on every section.
- Full-bleed alternating color bands (yellow/brown/gray) instead of boxed sections.
- Hand-drawn bookmark-mascot doodles and sketchy line icons inside outlined rounded cards.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Set every section headline in ultra-bold condensed caps at very large size; keep body copy small, short, and sentence case.
- Alternate full-bleed background bands (cream, yellow #FBD65B, dark brown #2B2313) to create section rhythm; flip text to cream/yellow on the dark bands.
- Use thin dark outlines on cards and buttons; pills for all buttons, 16-24px radius for cards.
- Illustrate features with sketchy hand-drawn doodles and a recurring mascot rather than photos or 3D renders.
- Keep the nav sparse: logo, 3 links, one yellow pill CTA.

**Don't**
- Don't introduce blues, purples, or gradients — the palette is strictly cream/brown/yellow plus a gray band.
- Don't use drop shadows for depth; separation comes from outlines and band color changes.
- Don't set headlines in mixed case or light weights — display type is always black-weight caps.
- Don't box sections in cards on a shared background; each section owns a full-width color band.
- Don't crowd bands with multiple ideas — one headline, one short paragraph, one illustration per band.
