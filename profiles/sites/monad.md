## Overview
Warm cream paper canvas with near-black serif display type, all-mono supporting text, and soft airbrushed gradient blobs in periwinkle, green, and orange that glow behind line-art diagrams. The effect is a "technical stationery" look: editorial serif headlines meet terminal-style monospace, softened by pastel mesh gradients. Illustration is thin-stroke schematic line art (orbits, pipelines, nodes) with small solid-color dots, never product screenshots.

## Layout
Top nav is sparse uppercase mono links left, dotted-circle logo mark centered, single black pill CTA right. Heroes are centered serif headlines (2 lines max) over a short mono subline and one pill CTA; the product page flips to a left-text / right-diagram split. Sections alternate text-left/illustration-right in a 2-column rhythm, separated by full-width 1px hairline rules. Every page ends with the same centered "Try Monad for free today" CTA block sitting on a blue gradient wash that bleeds into the footer. Generous whitespace throughout; content sits in a wide centered container.

## Components
Primary button: solid near-black pill with white uppercase mono label ("TRY MONAD FREE"); secondary is a hairline-outlined pill on cream ("CONTACT SALES"). Cards (integrations grid) are white/off-white with 1px gray borders, ~8px radius, no shadows: small logo tile top-left, mono description below, arranged in horizontal carousels with circular arrow paginators. Diagram panels are large rounded rectangles with thin borders containing line-art plus a gradient glow. Inputs are hairline pill fields with mono placeholder text ("YOUR EMAIL") next to a black pill submit. Node pills in the hero pipeline diagram echo the outlined-pill language.

## Signature
- Dotted-circle logo mark (ring of dots) used as logo, favicon-scale nav mark, and node inside diagrams
- Serif headline + uppercase monospace everything-else pairing on a cream paper background
- Soft radial gradient "auras" (green, periwinkle, orange) glowing behind thin black line-art physics/orbit diagrams
- Blue gradient wash rising from the footer on every page; hairline rules dividing sections
- Live-clock footer detail ("22:21 AT YOUR LOCATION") in mono

## Do
- Set headlines in a serif with sentence case and a closing period ("Set it and forget it.")
- Use uppercase monospace for nav, buttons, labels, and even body paragraphs
- Keep buttons pill-shaped: solid black for primary, 1px outline for secondary
- Draw features as thin-stroke line diagrams with small solid-color dot accents over blurred gradient blobs
- Separate sections with full-width 1px hairlines and repeat the gradient-footed CTA block on every page

## Don't
- Don't use pure white backgrounds — everything sits on warm cream
- Don't use sharp-edged or hard-gradient buttons; color gradients stay in background blobs, never on UI controls
- Don't add drop shadows — depth comes from gradient glows and hairline borders only
- Don't use a sans-serif for headings or lowercase text for UI labels
- Don't show dashboard screenshots; the product is always abstracted into schematic line art
