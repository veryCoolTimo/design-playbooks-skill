## Overview
Near-black navy canvas lit by an iridescent pink-magenta-violet gradient ribbon that arcs across the hero and returns at the closing CTA, bookending the page. Mid-page flips to a pale lavender-white band (#F5F6FF) for product content, then dives back into dark for the finale. Neon is used as large organic 3D shapes and gradient fills, not as glowing outlines; UI chrome itself stays clean and restrained.

## Layout
Compact left-aligned logo with a minimal stacked text nav plus pill Login button at top right. Hero is left-aligned copy over the gradient ribbon, followed by a centered logo-strip of customer brands on the dark canvas. Long dark storytelling zone (product panel with circuit-line connectors flowing downward into a circular "single point of integration" motif) precedes the light band. Light band uses a centered display heading ("Galileo at a glance."), a staggered fan of five gradient stat cards at varying vertical offsets, then a 2-col text+photo split and a 3x2 grid of icon feature blurbs. Full-width saturated blue promo band and a dark video panel break the rhythm before the dark footer. Generous vertical spacing throughout; content column roughly 12-col with wide margins.

## Components
Buttons: pills, two variants — white fill with indigo label + arrow on dark backgrounds ("Let's Talk →"), solid indigo #3E50FC fill with white label + arrow on light backgrounds ("Contact Sales →"); arrows inline after label. Cards: large-radius (16-24px) gradient-filled stat cards (teal, blue, purple, magenta, indigo) with white numerals; white product cards with soft shadow on the light band; dark glassy panels with faint borders on the navy zones. Small tinted-square icon chips lead each feature blurb. A pill-shaped segmented tab control introduces the products section. Eyebrow labels are tiny uppercase with a small glyph, in accent color.

## Signature
- Iridescent pink/magenta 3D gradient ribbon framing hero and footer CTA on near-black navy
- Fan of five staggered gradient stat cards, each a different hue of the neon palette
- Thin circuit/connector lines linking sections vertically down the dark canvas into a circular hub
- Dark-light-dark page sandwich: navy hero zone, lavender-white product zone, navy close

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve the neon gradient for large decorative shapes and card fills; keep buttons flat single-color
- Pair every CTA label with a trailing arrow inside a pill
- Swap button variant by context: white pill on dark, indigo #3E50FC pill on light
- Use tiny uppercase accent-colored eyebrows above display headings
- Stagger card grids vertically instead of aligning them to one baseline

Don't:
- Don't put the pink/magenta gradient on interactive controls or text links
- Don't use pure black or pure gray backgrounds — the dark canvas is blue-tinted navy #02081A
- Don't add glow, neon strokes, or outer shadows to buttons; edges stay crisp
- Don't run the whole page dark — the mid-page light band is part of the rhythm
- Don't use serif or condensed display type; everything is one clean grotesque family
