## Overview
Editorial, print-inspired fintech. Large serif headlines set like magazine deks ("Track Your Wealth. Trade. Invest.", "Titan in Your Inbox.") over warm cream and near-black panels, with a single muted-gold pill CTA as the only saturated color. Product UI screenshots, real human photography (advisors on video calls, a desert road shot), and hairline rules carry the credibility; ornament is nearly absent.

## Layout
- Nav: minimal three-part bar — one promo link left ("Smart Cash" with a tiny gold dot), centered wordmark "Titan.", Log in + gold pill "Get Started" right.
- Home hero: dark gradient panel, centered serif headline, inline email input + gold CTA, small trust stats row beneath.
- Section rhythm alternates full-width color panels: dark charcoal, white rounded card, pale sage, cream — each a rounded-corner slab with generous vertical padding.
- Recurring two-column module: big serif heading left, explanatory sans paragraph and small CTA right, separated by 1px horizontal rules (pricing rows, newsletter signups both use it).
- Numbered circular step badges (01, 02...) mark feature sections on home.
- Full-bleed lifestyle photo band pre-footer with serif overlay headline and gold CTA; deep cream footer with 4-column sitemap and long compliance fine print.

## Components
- Buttons: pill-shaped, flat, no shadow. Primary = muted gold (#F2C744) with dark ink text, often with a trailing arrow-in-circle glyph; secondary = solid near-black pill with white text (Subscribe); tertiary = plain text link with arrow.
- Cards: white on cream (or cream on white), 16-24px radius, hairline #E5E1D6 borders, no drop shadows; product cards on the strategies page are compact with small tag chips and "Learn More" text links.
- Inputs: borderless with a single bottom hairline rule ("Email" underline fields on newsletters), or white pill input embedded next to the CTA in the hero.
- Data: comparison tables where the Titan row is highlighted as a full gold pill band; bar charts use gray bars with the winning bar in brand gold.
- Accordions ("More Details.") as hairline-divided rows with plus icons.
- Small pill tags/chips ("Popular", strategy names) in gold or light gray.

## Signature
- One accent color: everything is cream/charcoal/white except the gold, which is reserved for CTAs and the "Titan wins" data point in every chart and table.
- Serif headlines that end with a period, mixing display serif with tiny grotesque labels and eyebrow text.
- Rounded full-width color slabs (dark, sage, cream) stacked like magazine spreads instead of boxed sections.
- Hairline horizontal rules organizing pricing rows, FAQs, and newsletter blocks — table-of-contents energy.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve the gold (#F2C744) strictly for primary CTAs and the single highlighted row/bar in comparisons.
- Set headlines in the editorial serif, sentence case, ending with a period; keep supporting copy in small grotesque sans.
- Alternate full-width rounded panels (charcoal, cream, pale sage, white) to create rhythm instead of borders or shadows.
- Use 1px hairline rules and generous whitespace to structure lists, pricing, and FAQs.
- Show real product screenshots and human photography (advisors, lifestyle) as the main imagery.

Don't:
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients on cards/buttons — everything is flat with hairline borders.
- Don't introduce a second saturated color; charts stay gray + one gold highlight.
- Don't use bold heavy sans headlines — display weight lives in the serif, not in weight.
- Don't square off buttons; CTAs are always pills, often with the arrow-in-circle suffix.
- Don't use pure white (#FFF) as the page canvas — the base is warm cream, with white reserved for cards.
