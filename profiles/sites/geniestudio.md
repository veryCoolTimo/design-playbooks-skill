## Overview
A soft, sky-themed minimal-light landing page: icy blue-white canvas, near-black charcoal type, and a friendly blue cloud mascot with confetti-like sparkle doodles scattered through the whitespace. Sections alternate between white, pale blue, pale lavender, and pale peach tints, giving a pastel storybook rhythm while UI chrome stays clean and restrained. All CTAs are dark charcoal pills, so the playful color never competes with the conversion path.

## Layout
Top nav is a slim white bar: wordmark left, two text links (How it works, Pricing), and a charcoal pill "Join waitlist" button right. Hero is fully centered: oversized two-line headline with an inline circular emoji-badge embedded mid-sentence, one short gray subline, a single dark pill CTA, and the waving cloud mascot rising from the fold. Sections stack in generous full-width color bands (white → pale blue → lavender → peach → white), each with a centered heading and roomy vertical padding. Mid-page uses a horizontal image-strip gallery, a scroll-story of giant single words ("Create / Like / Magic") floating on cloud shapes, an arced fan of style cards, a 2x2-ish bento of feature cards, a single centered pricing card, a two-to-three-column testimonial grid, and a left-label/right-accordion FAQ. Footer is a full-bleed illustration of the smiling mascot behind a final centered CTA.

## Components
Buttons: charcoal (#191d28) pill with white medium-weight label; identical treatment in nav, hero, pricing, and footer — no visible secondary button style. Cards: large-radius (roughly 20-24px) rounded rectangles, flat pastel fills (pale blue, mint, lavender) or white with no visible border and little-to-no shadow; the pricing card is a plain white rounded panel on a lavender-tinted band. Feature list items use small blue four-point-star "plus" icons instead of checkmarks. Inputs: the prompt bar is a white pill/rounded field with a small dark submit chip inside its right end. Toggle: small pill switch between Pay monthly / Pay yearly, with a lavender "-25%" chip (#925bda text on #f2e5ff). FAQ rows are white rounded bars with chevron affordances. Testimonials are borderless white cards with small avatar, name, role, and quote.

## Signature
- Blue cloud mascot (#66b9ff) with a hand-drawn face recurs from hero to a full-width smiling footer illustration.
- Pastel section banding: white, icy blue, lavender, and peach tints replace dividers and create the page rhythm.
- Scattered doodle confetti — four-point sparkles, stars, and gem shapes in orange, purple, and blue — floats in the margins of nearly every section.
- Inline circular emoji/icon badges embedded inside display headlines ("Designs [brush] don't have to start from scratch").
- One dark pill CTA style everywhere; color is reserved for illustration, never for buttons.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep every CTA a charcoal #191d28 pill with white text; repeat the same button in nav, hero, pricing, and footer.
- Alternate full-width pastel bands (#ecf4ff, #f2e5ff, #ffeee6) between white sections instead of using rules or borders.
- Sprinkle small sparkle/star doodles in section margins, and use the blue four-point star as the list bullet.
- Center headlines and give sections tall, airy vertical padding; let the mascot and doodles fill the whitespace.
- Use flat pastel fills on large-radius cards with no borders and minimal shadow.

**Don't**
- Don't use the mascot blue or purple as a button fill — bright color stays in illustration and icons only.
- Don't add outlined or ghost buttons; the site has exactly one button treatment.
- Don't box sections with borders, drop shadows, or hard dividers; tint changes do the separation.
- Don't use sharp or small-radius corners; everything interactive is pill or generously rounded.
- Don't crowd the layout — no dense multi-column feature grids beyond the loose bento; sections carry one idea each.
