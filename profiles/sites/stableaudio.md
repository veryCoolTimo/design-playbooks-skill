## Overview
A warm, paper-like minimal-light design: everything sits on a beige-khaki canvas instead of white, with near-black ink and a small set of muted-but-cheerful pastel accents (periwinkle blue, lavender, green, khaki). The recurring visual motif is a semicircle "sunrise over hills" mark rendered in gradients and holographic blurs, giving an otherwise flat, typographic site a distinct album-cover personality. Sections are ruled off with thin horizontal hairlines, like a printed brochure.

## Layout
Sticky-free top bar: wordmark left, pill CTA + outlined "Log in" + hamburger circle right (hamburger persists even on desktop). Home hero is a split card: left panel with headline and CTA, right panel a large interactive audio-player card showing a generated track with prompt tags and transport controls. Below, feature rows alternate small icon glyphs, a label + short claim on the left/center, and a gradient "album art" tile on the right, each row separated by a full-width 1px rule. A full-bleed near-black "Our mission" band breaks the light rhythm mid-page. Pricing and user-guide pages use a two-column editorial grid: section title in the left rail, content cards/tables on the right. Generous whitespace throughout; content max-width is wide with large side margins.

## Components
- Buttons: pill-shaped. Primary = solid periwinkle blue (#9EC5F8) with dark text; secondary = transparent with 1px dark outline ("Log in", "Get started for free", "View"). No shadows.
- Pricing cards: flat panels with colored header bands (khaki / lavender / green), a folded/notched top-right corner detail, thin internal divider lines between spec rows, and a full-width pill button at the bottom. No drop shadows, no border beyond the fill.
- Licensing table: column headers filled with the same tier colors; green check / red cross glyphs in cells; hairline row dividers.
- Guide cards: soft off-white panels (slightly lighter than canvas) with small radius, containing tip text or lists of playable audio rows (play icon, timestamp, progress bar, volume, kebab menu).
- Footer: solid near-black band, white text, thin top rule, three link columns (Company / Resources / Socials).

## Signature
- Beige/khaki paper canvas instead of white — the whole site feels printed, not glowing.
- Semicircle sunrise-and-waveform emblem repeated as gradient album-art tiles in green, red, blue, orange, and holographic blur variants.
- Pastel tier color-coding (khaki = Free, lavender = Professional, green = Enterprise) carried consistently from pricing cards into the licensing table headers.
- Thin full-width hairline rules dividing every major section; pill buttons as the only rounded element against squared cards.

## Do
- Keep the canvas warm beige (#E8E5DA) and reserve pure near-black (#1A1A1A) for text, the mission band, and the footer.
- Use one solid pastel-blue pill as the primary CTA everywhere; make secondary actions outlined pills on the canvas color.
- Separate sections with 1px dark hairlines running edge-to-edge or across the content column.
- Color-code parallel offerings (tiers, categories) with flat pastel header bands and repeat those exact colors wherever the tiers reappear.
- Use gradient semicircle artwork tiles as the illustration system; keep photography out entirely.

## Don't
- Don't use white card backgrounds or drop shadows — panels differ from the canvas by a subtle tint shift only.
- Don't round card corners heavily; rounding belongs to buttons (full pill), cards stay near-square.
- Don't introduce saturated neon or dark-mode sections beyond the single black mission/footer bands.
- Don't set headings in a serif or display face — the entire hierarchy is one grotesque family scaled by size/weight.
- Don't decorate with icons beyond the small line-drawn sun/hill glyph set already in use.
