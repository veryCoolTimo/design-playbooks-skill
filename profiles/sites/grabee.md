## Overview
A playful minimal-light SaaS landing that alternates a white canvas with full-bleed saturated indigo bands and pale lavender sections. Headlines are large, dark navy, and heavily bolded, with inline color-highlighted phrases (blue, teal, pink) doing the branding work. Product UI mockups, four-point sparkle stars, and small pastel icon chips give it a friendly product-tool character without illustration-heavy clutter.

## Layout
Sticky-feeling top nav: logo left, sparse text links (Features, Pricing, Blog) plus a small filled indigo "Join Waitlist" pill-ish button right. Hero is centered: uppercase eyebrow label flanked by small arrow glyphs ("◂ EXPLORE FEEDBACK CYCLE ▸"), a 2-3 line H1 with colored keywords, short subcopy, single CTA, then a tiny red microcopy line ("Only 24 slots left"). Below the hero sits a circular 4-step diagram (Collect → Prioritize → Integrate → Announce) built from floating UI-card vignettes. Section rhythm alternates backgrounds: white → saturated indigo (#5B4DF0) problem section with two dark navy persona cards → lavender feature blocks. Feature sections use a repeating pattern: eyebrow + left-aligned H2 with one colored phrase, paragraph, product screenshot on the right, then a 3-up row of small white benefit cards. A stat row (3 white cards with big numbers), an integrations split section, a two-column FAQ (heading left, accordion right), and a dark navy footer band with sparkle decorations and centered CTA close the page.

## Components
Buttons: solid indigo fill (#5B4DF0), white label, small radius (~4-6px), compact padding; same style at every scale from nav to footer. Cards: white fill, very light border or none, soft minimal shadow, medium radius; benefit cards hold a small pastel-tinted square icon chip (lavender, mint, peach, pink) top-left with title and 2-line description. Persona cards on the indigo band are dark navy with tiny uppercase colored eyebrow tags and embedded mini-screenshots. FAQ rows are white accordion bars with chevron indicators. Screenshots render as framed dark or light app windows with floating badge overlays. Eyebrow labels are tiny uppercase letterspaced text in the section's accent color, bracketed by small triangle/arrow glyphs.

## Signature
- Uppercase eyebrow labels flanked by small arrow glyphs above every heading.
- Bold navy headlines with one phrase per section recolored (blue, teal, pink, red) as the accent system.
- Alternating full-bleed bands: white → vivid indigo → pale lavender → dark navy footer.
- Four-point sparkle/star decorations scattered in colored and dark sections; emoji-like inline icons inside headlines ("Reduce churn ✦ by listening to your users").

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Recolor exactly one key phrase per headline, rotating accent hues (blue, teal, pink) per section.
- Alternate section backgrounds between white, saturated indigo, and pale lavender to create rhythm.
- Keep one CTA style everywhere: small-radius solid indigo button with a scarcity microcopy line in red beneath the primary instances.
- Pair each feature block with a 3-up row of small white cards using pastel icon chips.
- Use the arrow-flanked uppercase eyebrow above every section heading.

**Don't**
- Don't use gradients — every band and button is a flat solid fill.
- Don't use large or pill radii on buttons; corners stay nearly square.
- Don't render photography — all imagery is product-UI mockups and abstract card vignettes (avatars only as tiny inline clusters).
- Don't put dark text on the indigo band; that section flips to white/teal text and dark navy cards.
- Don't add heavy shadows or borders on cards; separation comes from background-color contrast.
