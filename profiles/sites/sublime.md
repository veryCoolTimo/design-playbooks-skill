## Overview
An almost-empty white page where typography does nearly all the work. Enormous headlines (bold tight grotesque on home, classic Times-style serif on the manifesto) sit over a stark white canvas, with body copy shrunk into an unusually narrow centered column. The only color is a single pale-green CTA and soft photographic collage elements — moss-covered stone and cloud sculptures spelling shapes under the hero, and scattered "saved note" cards mid-page.

## Layout
- Nav: near-invisible — small wordmark top-left, ghost "login" plus pale-green "try now" chip top-right. No menu links.
- Hero: giant 2-3 line centered headline directly under the nav, followed by a surreal centered image (stone garden on home, cloud sculpture on manifesto).
- Body runs in an extremely narrow centered column (roughly 20-25% of viewport width), small type, centered alignment, with 3-6 short lines per block.
- Section rhythm alternates: tiny centered paragraph blocks → oversized display statement → collage of note cards fanned loosely around the center column. Vertical whitespace between sections is enormous (multiple viewport heights of near-empty space).
- Manifesto page is a pure typographic scroll: no images after the hero, just alternating small serif paragraphs and large serif declarations.
- Footer: a single rounded light-gray panel containing wordmark, link columns, and a green CTA chip.

## Components
- Primary CTA: pale-green (#CAFFA5) rounded rectangle with dark-green (#2B6125) text, no border, no shadow, lowercase label ("try now").
- Closing CTA: very large light-gray (#EFEFEF) rounded block with gray lowercase text ("try sublime") — deliberately understated, almost disabled-looking.
- Cards: small screenshot/note tiles (quotes, images, tweets, sticky notes in pale yellow/green) with slight rotation, small radius, faint shadows, arranged as a loose collage around the text column; each has a tiny gray caption label.
- Search input mock: white rounded field with magnifier icon and green typed text.
- Testimonial: dark rounded app-window screenshot with a circular avatar photo beside it.
- No visible borders anywhere; separation is done purely with whitespace.

## Signature
- Extreme centered narrow text column (tweet-width) floating in oceans of white space.
- Dual typographic voice: heavy tight grotesque for product claims, bookish Times-style serif for the manifesto — both at poster scale.
- Single pale-green accent (#CAFFA5) as the only saturated color on the page.
- Surreal nature imagery (mossy stones, cloud sculptures) as hero art instead of product screenshots.
- Lowercase, quiet UI copy ("try now", "login", "try sublime").

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas pure white and let vertical whitespace (multiple screen heights) separate sections.
- Constrain body copy to a very narrow centered column with short 2-5 word lines.
- Use one pale-green chip as the sole colored element; keep every other control gray or ghost.
- Scale display type huge and set it tight; switch to a Times-style serif for editorial/manifesto content.
- Scatter content cards as a hand-placed collage with slight rotations and tiny gray captions.

Don't:
- Don't add nav menus, borders, dividers, or background tints — structure comes from spacing alone.
- Don't use gradients, dark sections, or more than one accent hue.
- Don't set body text full-width or left-aligned; everything is centered.
- Don't make CTAs loud — even the final CTA is a muted gray block with gray text.
- Don't replace the surreal photographic hero objects with generic product screenshots or illustrations.
