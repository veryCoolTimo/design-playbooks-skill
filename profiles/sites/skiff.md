## Overview
A warm off-white (cream, not pure white) minimal system where color arrives only through full-bleed product-tinted heroes: pink for Mail, orange-red for Pages, green for Drive, sky blue for Calendar. Everything else is near-black type, hairline-bordered white cards, and real product UI screenshots doing the visual heavy lifting. CTAs are solid black pills, keeping the brand neutral while each product owns a hue.

## Layout
Floating centered black pill nav (logo dot, links, white "Try Skiff free" pill) detached from the page edge. Product heroes are full-width color blocks: two-line headline top-left with an inline emoji-style product icon, supporting blurb and CTA pair top-right, then a large rounded real-app screenshot filling the lower hero. Below: a bold intro sentence with color-highlighted keywords, a 2-column grid of feature cards (title + short copy + embedded mini-UI), a 3-column row of text-only feature blurbs, an inverted near-black "open source" section with code snippets, a mobile section with an iPhone mockup, a "startup credits" banner with partner app icons, and a giant centered "Join the community" closer with colorful circle blobs bleeding in from the corners. Pricing swaps the color hero for a plain cream page: three plan cards (Business card inverted to black) plus a long checkmark comparison table.

## Components
Buttons: solid black pill with white label; secondary is white pill with black label; both compact with modest padding. Links/tertiary CTAs are orange-red text with an arrow ("Download app →", "Claim credits →"). Cards: white or #FAFAF7 fill, 1px light gray border, 8-14px radius, no visible drop shadow; content is heading + small gray body + embedded product screenshot. Hero screenshots and phone mockups get large radii and sit flush inside the tinted block. Pricing table uses hairline rows, small check icons, and a subtly shaded "popular" column; plan badges are tiny uppercase chips. Nav dropdowns indicated by small chevrons.

## Signature
- Product-coded full-bleed hero colors (pink/orange/green/blue) on an otherwise monochrome cream site, with a big light page number (01-04) in the hero corner.
- Inline color-highlighted words inside black headlines ("thoughts", "end-to-end encryption") matching each product's hue.
- Floating black pill navigation capsule centered at the top, detached from the page.
- Real, dense app UI screenshots (inbox, file table, calendar grid) as hero art instead of illustration.
- Emoji-style rounded product icons (envelope, page, folder, calendar tile) dropped inline into headlines and the community footer.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas warm off-white (#F2F1EC) and reserve saturated color for full-bleed hero blocks and highlighted headline words.
- Use solid black pill buttons for every primary CTA, paired with a white pill secondary, regardless of the section's hue.
- Present features as bordered white cards containing genuine product-UI crops, alternating with plain 3-column text blurbs.
- Assign each product/area one hue and repeat it in its hero, headline highlights, and icon.
- Use one dark inverted section per page (open source / Business plan card) for contrast, with monospace code accents where relevant.

Don't:
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients to cards — separation comes from 1px borders and background steps only.
- Don't color the CTA buttons in the product hue; buttons stay black/white even inside a pink or green hero.
- Don't use a stark #FFFFFF page background or pure-black text blocks outside the designated dark section.
- Don't introduce decorative illustration in heroes — the product screenshot is the hero image.
- Don't stretch the nav full-width; it is a compact centered capsule floating over the content.
