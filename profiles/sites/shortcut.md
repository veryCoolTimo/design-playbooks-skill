## Overview
Deep-navy dark-tech landing page where full-color product screenshots are the hero imagery, framed in soft blue-to-pink gradient mats that glow against the near-black canvas. Headlines use a two-tone trick — first word in powder blue, remainder in white — and sections are stitched together by thin vertical connector lines running down the page center. Tiny star/sparkle glyphs and space-themed illustrations (rockets, astronauts) give it a playful cosmic undertone without breaking the engineering-tool seriousness.

## Layout
Fixed top nav on the navy canvas: logo left, center links with dropdown carets, "Sign up" solid-blue button right. Hero is split: left column with amber "New" announcement pill, two-tone H1, gray-white paragraph, dual CTAs (solid blue + white Google button), and a "REPLACES: Jira / Confluence…" pill row; right side holds an isometric illustration. Below: a large light-mode app screenshot carousel bleeding past the viewport edges with a warm pink/orange glow behind it. Then a grayscale logo strip, followed by a long journey narrative (Ideate → Plan → Build → Ship) marked by a segmented pill stepper and vertical connector lines linking centered section headers. Sections alternate centered intros with two-up gradient-framed screenshot cards and 2x2 feature-card grids. Ends with a stats band (3x2 tiles of big blue numbers), an integrations icon cluster, and a final CTA repeating the hero buttons next to an astronaut illustration. Generous vertical whitespace throughout; content column roughly 1100-1200px.

## Components
Primary button: solid #3a96c9 fill, white semibold label, ~8px radius, no border. Secondary: white fill, dark label, same radius (Google sign-up). Tag chips ("Jira", "Confluence"): full pills, translucent dark fill with subtle light border and small mono-tone icons. Announcement badge: dark pill with amber "New" text and circular arrow icon. Screenshot cards: light-mode UI captures sitting on blue→pink gradient mats, top corners rounded ~12-14px, bottom edge cut by the section. Feature cards: slightly-lighter navy panels (#111c2d–#182334), medium radius, title + gray body + small "Learn more" link with chevron. Stats tiles: flat navy panels, large light-blue numerals over small caption text. Testimonial: large italic-free quote in a bordered panel with logo strip above and paginated arrows below.

## Signature
- Two-tone headlines: leading word in powder blue (#7fb4d9), rest pure white, on every section header.
- Blue-to-dusty-pink gradient mats framing light-mode product screenshots against the dark navy canvas.
- Thin vertical connector lines and an Ideate/Plan/Build/Ship pill stepper threading sections into one continuous journey.
- Cosmic garnish: scattered four-point sparkles, rocket/astronaut isometric illustrations, soft warm glows behind hero screenshots.

## Do / Don't
- Do keep the canvas a single deep navy (#0f1827) and let light-mode app screenshots provide most of the color and contrast.
- Do color only the first word of each headline powder blue and leave the rest white.
- Do frame screenshots in soft blue→pink gradient mats with rounded tops and let them bleed off section edges.
- Do use pill-shaped chips for competitor/integration tags ("REPLACES: …") with small icons and translucent fills.
- Do connect stacked sections with slim vertical lines and repeat the four-step pill stepper as a progress motif.
- Don't use pure black backgrounds or neon cyberpunk glows — the palette is muted navy with soft powder-blue and dusty-pink light.
- Don't make the primary CTA a gradient or pill; it is a flat medium-blue rounded rectangle (~8px).
- Don't render product UI in dark mode — the screenshots are deliberately light to pop against the navy.
- Don't add heavy card borders or drop shadows; panels separate by slightly lighter navy fills only.
- Don't use serif or display faces; everything is one clean grotesque family varied by weight.
