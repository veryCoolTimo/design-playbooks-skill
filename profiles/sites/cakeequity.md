## Overview
Bright, candy-colored startup fintech: white canvas punctuated by full-bleed color blocks in lavender-violet gradients, flat sky blue, and an electric chartreuse lime. Chunky black geometric-sans headlines sit against playful floating product-UI cards, hand-drawn arrows, and cursive scribble annotations ("no credit card required"). The mood is confident and fun without losing SaaS clarity — data tables and legal templates rendered as pastel toy-like mockups.

## Layout
Sticky white nav bar: wordmark left, dropdown links center-left, then Login (text), "Talk to Sales" (light pill), and "Get started free" (black pill) right. Heroes alternate between full-width color fields (lavender gradient on home, flat #8AC2FF blue on resources, blob-shaped lime/green splashes on about) with left-aligned headline + CTA and right-side floating app screenshots. Section rhythm: white content band → tinted band (peach, lavender, blue gradient) → white, ending in a signature full-width lime CTA block with tilted UI-card cutouts bleeding in from the corners, then a near-black (#141115) footer with a giant wordmark. Content sits in a centered ~1140px column; feature sections use alternating two-column text/mockup rows.

## Components
Buttons: violet #695CFD filled rects (8-12px radius) for in-page CTAs, deeper #470EFE on pricing plan buttons, black pills in the nav and on lime sections; white text, no visible borders or shadows. Cards: white, 12-16px radius, hairline gray borders (pricing plans, template cards) or soft lavender fills (product feature mockups); very light shadows. Pricing uses segmented Annual/Monthly toggle, green "Save 20%" pills, and emoji plan icons; comparison table uses green check emoji. Testimonials are bordered white cards with star rows and circular avatars. Small lozenge/pill labels ("Features", "Reviews", "Most popular") outline the section starts.

## Signature
- Electric lime (#E3FF5D) pre-footer CTA band with rotated, cut-off product-UI cards poking in from the edges plus a handwritten "no credit card required" scribble and arrow.
- Violet-lavender gradient fields (#B5B2FE → white) hosting floating pastel app screenshots with pie charts and vesting numbers.
- Heavy, tight, slightly rounded black display type ("Equity, the secret to a winning culture") in sentence case.
- Emoji used as functional iconography (plan tiers 🍰🍪🚀, check marks ✅, city cards 🌴🎡🏰).

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate white sections with full-bleed tinted bands (lavender gradient, flat sky blue, peach) rather than boxing color inside cards.
- End every page with the lime CTA block + tilted UI cutouts + scribble annotation, followed by the black footer with oversized wordmark.
- Render product UI as simplified pastel mockups (lavender/blue fills, rounded 12-16px, big numbers) instead of raw screenshots.
- Use emoji and small colored pills (green "Save 20%", violet "Most popular") for lightweight labeling.
- Keep headlines short, sentence-case, extra-bold, near-black even on colored backgrounds.

Don't:
- Don't use gradients on buttons — CTA fills are flat violet or flat black.
- Don't add heavy drop shadows or dark borders; cards stay hairline-bordered or borderless on tinted fields.
- Don't introduce corporate navy/teal palettes — accents stay in the lime/sky-blue/violet/green candy range.
- Don't set headlines in serif or light weights; display type is always heavy geometric sans.
- Don't crowd sections — each band carries one idea with generous whitespace around a single mockup or card grid.
