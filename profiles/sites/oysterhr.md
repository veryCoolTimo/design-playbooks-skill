## Overview
Oyster pairs a warm cream canvas with a full pastel candy palette — butter yellow, coral, mint green, lavender, and pink — anchored by a single serious color: deep forest green used for every primary CTA and the logo. Hand-drawn black-and-white line illustrations (globes, people, sparkle doodles) and highlighter-marker text effects give it a zine-like, human energy. Headlines mix an upright grotesque with italicized accent words for emphasis ("Hire *anywhere*, thrive *everywhere*").

## Layout
Sticky white top nav: wordmark left, dropdown links center, "Speak to an Expert" outline pill + solid dark-green "Get Started" button right. Heroes are either soft pastel gradient panels (peach-to-mint) with centered or left text, or split two-tone color blocks (yellow/sky-blue on product). Section rhythm alternates: cream base sections, then full-bleed pastel color bands (lavender, sunburst yellow-pink rays, mint green) holding white content cards. Grids of 3-4 equal cards for feature sets; alternating text/screenshot rows for product tours. Footer is a giant yellow newsletter block on cream (older pages) or a charcoal footer with an oversized "Oyster" wordmark (newer pages). Generous whitespace between sections; content stays in a comfortable centered column.

## Components
- Buttons: solid deep-green (#17493B) fill with white text, small 2-4px radius, no shadow; secondary is white/transparent with thin dark border, same shape. Arrow "Learn More →" text links in green.
- Cards: flat pastel fills (one color per card: green, orange, yellow, lavender), large 16-24px radius, no border, no shadow; black line-art icon top, bold heading, short body, arrow link.
- Pricing tiers: white cards with thin dark borders, near-square corners, black header bar on each tier, checkmark lists; middle tier elevated with a sticker-style badge.
- FAQ accordions: hairline dividers with circular green plus icons.
- Product screenshots: floating app-UI mockups on pastel painted/scribble backgrounds, tilted slightly, with hand-drawn black arrows and sparkles pointing at them.
- Inputs (newsletter form): white fields, thin border, small radius, on yellow background.
- Testimonials: circular avatar photos with hand-drawn scribble rings and doodle accents.

## Signature
- Deep forest green CTAs as the only "corporate" color inside an otherwise pastel candy palette on cream.
- Black hand-drawn illustration system: line-art globes and diverse people, scribble circles around avatars, sparkle/arrow doodles overlapping photos and UI shots.
- Highlighter effect: key headline words get pink/yellow marker-swipe backgrounds or switch to serif italic mid-sentence.
- Full-bleed flat pastel section bands (including a yellow-pink sunburst ray pattern) that carry white cards on top.

## Do
- Reserve #17493B green for primary buttons and links; let pastels do the mood work.
- Alternate cream sections with flat full-width pastel bands to create rhythm.
- Use black monoline illustrations and doodle accents (arrows, sparkles, scribble rings) to humanize screenshots and photos.
- Keep buttons small-radius and rectangular while cards go big-radius — the contrast is part of the look.
- Italicize or highlight one or two emphasis words per headline, not the whole line.

## Don't
- Don't use drop shadows or gradients on buttons/cards — everything is flat fills and hairline borders.
- Don't introduce saturated primaries (pure red/blue); the palette is strictly desaturated pastels plus deep green and near-black.
- Don't use photographic backgrounds behind text sections; photos live inside rounded cards or circles.
- Don't render icons in color — iconography stays black line art even on colored cards.
- Don't use pure white as the page background; the canvas is warm cream (#F7F5EF).
