## Overview
A storybook-illustrated developer cloud: pastel watercolor scenes of hot-air balloons, clouds, lighthouses, and floating islands carry the brand while the copy stays irreverent and technical. Warm serif display type over a near-white lavender canvas, with deep indigo/violet used for dark heroes, footers, and CTAs. The pages feel hand-crafted and whimsical, deliberately opposite to typical dark-neon infra sites.

## Layout
Centered logo-left nav with links grouped in a rounded pill, Sign In (ghost) + Get Started (filled purple) on the right. Home hero: centered serif headline, short paragraph with inline highlighted keyword, single purple CTA, then a full-width panoramic illustration bleeding into the next section. Section rhythm alternates: light lavender content bands, a full-bleed saturated purple gradient band (framework logos grid), and a near-black indigo footer. Security page inverts the formula with a dark sketchbook-illustration hero, then a white body of alternating text/illustration two-column rows. About uses an illustrated gradient hero over a dense 3-column team grid. Whitespace is generous; content column is narrow (~700-1100px).

## Components
Buttons: solid violet (#7C3AED-ish) fills with white text, 4-6px radius, no visible shadow; secondary buttons are dark charcoal or outlined ghost; small arrow chevrons follow CTA labels. Feature list items use small rounded-square icon chips in pastel mint/blue/yellow/orange with matching tinted icons. Cards: framework tiles on the purple band are translucent white-border rounded rectangles; team cards are square photos with 8-12px radius, doodle-frame overlays, name + role + tiny social icons. Checklists use green check glyphs on ruled rows. Inputs not observed.

## Signature
- Hand-drawn pastel watercolor illustrations (balloons, globes, eyeball monsters, a SOC2 goat) as the primary visual device on every page
- Warm serif display headlines with joke-forward copy ("Without the Cortisol", "security nerds") over a lavender-white canvas
- Indigo-to-violet spectrum doing all the heavy lifting: pale lavender canvas, saturated purple CTAs/bands, near-black indigo footer
- Full-bleed illustrated hero panoramas that dissolve into the following section instead of a hard section break

## Do / Don't
- Do use a warm serif for all headlines and a plain small grotesque for body text
- Do lean on custom pastel illustration for section imagery instead of photos or 3D renders
- Do alternate pale lavender bands with one saturated purple full-bleed band and a near-black indigo footer
- Do keep CTAs as small solid-violet rectangles with slight rounding and chevron arrows, one primary per hero
- Do write headline copy with personality; the visual whimsy depends on the voice matching
- Don't use neon-on-black terminal aesthetics or glassmorphism; darkness appears only as deep indigo with sketch-line illustration
- Don't use stock photography or realistic device mockups; the only photos are team headshots
- Don't add drop shadows or heavy borders; depth comes from color bands and illustration
- Don't use pure white or pure gray backgrounds; the canvas always carries a faint lavender/warm tint
- Don't make buttons pill-shaped; the pill treatment is reserved for the nav link container
