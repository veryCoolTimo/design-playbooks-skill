## Overview
A cool, airy minimal-light system: pale blue-gray canvas with faint isometric grid lines and dot-matrix textures, near-black text, and one loud neon chartreuse reserved for CTAs. Sections alternate between white bordered card grids and near-black full-bleed bands where the neon button glows. Product credibility is carried by embedded UI screenshots (dashboards, spend charts) rendered as soft white cards.

## Layout
Slim centered top nav: wordmark left, four text links center, "Sign in" right, all tiny type on the pale canvas. Hero is fully centered: 2-line headline, one-line subhead, single CTA button, then a "trusted by" monochrome logo wall in 2-3 rows. Body is a strict bordered grid — 2-up feature cells separated by 1px hairlines rather than floating cards, each cell holding a small illustration/3D icon above a bold one-line title and 2-line muted caption. Rhythm: light section → light section → near-black CTA band ("Start Saving Same-Day") → footer. Generous vertical whitespace; decorative chevron/dash dividers between major sections.

## Components
Buttons: primary is neon chartreuse fill with dark text, small radius (~6px), sometimes nested inside a dark navy pill-ish container in the hero; on dark bands it becomes a full-width glowing neon bar. Secondary/login options are white bars with 1px gray borders. Cards: white or off-white, 1px light borders, very soft or no shadows, medium radius; feature grids share hairline borders so cells read as one lattice. Embedded product UI mockups appear as elevated white panels with subtle drop shadow. Testimonials: full-bleed photo card with white overlay text, or bordered quote cells with small avatars. Stat displays use segmented horizontal bars in blue/dark with dollar figures.

## Signature
- Single neon chartreuse (#D9F522) used only for CTAs and glow effects, against an otherwise desaturated blue-gray/black palette.
- Hairline-bordered feature lattice: 2-up grid cells divided by 1px lines, each with a centered soft 3D/blueprint icon.
- Faint engineering textures — dot grids, isometric wireframe lines, ASCII-like encrypted text blocks — as background decoration.
- Near-black rounded full-width band before the footer with a glowing neon full-width demo button.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the canvas a pale, slightly blue gray (#EEF1F6) and let white cards sit on it with 1px borders, not heavy shadows.
- Reserve the neon chartreuse exclusively for "Book a demo"-class CTAs; add a soft glow when it sits on the dark band.
- Use tight, centered headlines at semibold weight with short muted subheads directly beneath.
- Separate feature cells with shared hairline borders to form a lattice, one small icon + title + 2-line caption per cell.
- Break the light flow with one near-black rounded section for the closing CTA.

**Don't**
- Don't introduce a second saturated brand color; blues appear only inside data-viz and small icons.
- Don't use pill buttons or large radii on controls — corners stay tight (4-8px).
- Don't fill the layout edge-to-edge; keep wide margins and let logo walls and grids float centered.
- Don't render headlines in display serifs or ultra-bold weights; the voice is quiet neo-grotesque.
- Don't put colored backgrounds behind body sections; color lives in CTAs, charts, and the single dark band.
