## Overview
A stripped-down editorial poster of a page: warm off-white canvas covered in large serif sentences that repeat the mantra "Make a song..." at rotated angles, most rendered in ghosted warm gray with one line pulled forward in near-black. Almost no chrome — the entire visual identity is typography as artwork. A full-black footer inverts the palette with white serif paragraphs.

## Layout
- Nav: minimal floating pill at top-left (logo + "About" / "Team" links) with a single black pill CTA at top-right; no full-width nav bar.
- Hero: not a conventional hero — a tall collage of oversized serif headlines rotated at varying diagonal angles, layered edge-to-edge and cropped by the viewport. One horizontal line ("Make a song for your friend.") in full ink with a small orange dot bullet acts as the focal point.
- Mid-page: huge whitespace, then a centered lockup — black rounded-square logo mark, serif headline "Make a song with Suno.", small pill CTA beneath.
- Footer: full-bleed black section, single narrow text column with thin light hairline rules separating "About" and "Team" blocks; sans labels, serif body, legal links + social icons at bottom.
- Single-column throughout; no grid of features, no cards, no imagery or screenshots.

## Components
- Buttons: small black pill, tiny white sans label ("Make a song"), no border, no shadow. Same treatment reused in nav and mid-page.
- Nav container: white/off-white pill capsule grouping logo and links, subtle contrast against canvas.
- No cards, no inputs, no icons beyond the logo glyph and footer social marks.
- Dividers: 1px light gray hairlines in the black footer.

## Signature
- Rotated, overlapping serif "Make a song..." sentences in ghosted gray (#A9A79F-ish) with exactly one line in full black — typography is the hero image.
- Tiny orange (#E8501E) dot bullet as the sole color accent on an otherwise monochrome page.
- Serif for everything expressive, micro-sized sans only for UI chrome (nav, buttons, footer labels).
- Hard palette inversion: cream page, pure black footer with white serif prose.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set headlines in a classic transitional serif at very large sizes, sentence case with terminal periods.
- Layer repeated headline variations at rotated angles, ghosting all but one in warm gray to create a single focal line.
- Keep exactly one accent color (orange) and use it in only one or two tiny places.
- Use small black pill buttons with sans labels; keep CTAs modest in size despite the huge type.
- Give the page extreme whitespace between the type collage, the centered logo lockup, and the footer.

Don't:
- Add feature grids, product screenshots, gradients, or illustration — the page carries zero imagery besides the logo mark.
- Use bold or condensed display weights; the serif stays at book/regular weight.
- Introduce colored buttons or link styling — interactive elements are black-on-cream or white-on-black only.
- Use rounded cards, drop shadows, or borders; separation is done with whitespace and hairlines.
- Mix in a second accent hue or tint the canvas cool; the palette is warm cream, near-black, and one orange dot.
