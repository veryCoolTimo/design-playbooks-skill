## Overview
Caldera pairs a warm off-white gray canvas with three loud brand colors — hot orange, electric violet, and pale acid yellow — plus large black blocks. Almost every section is a big rounded card or "sticker" panel sitting on the neutral background. Black isometric machine illustrations (routers, printing presses, game controllers) and halftone/dither dot textures give it a playful, print-shop industrial character. Headings are enormous, black-weight grotesque set in Title Case.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on the canvas color: small logo left, 4 text links, social icon cluster and one orange pill CTA right. Heroes are either a full-bleed halftone gradient band (home) or a two-card split — colored illustration card on the left, off-white text card on the right (rollups, metalayer). Sections alternate between centered giant headlines on the raw canvas and dense bento grids of rounded cards; card colors rotate orange / violet / black / off-white / yellow. Stat rows use four equal orange tiles with big numbers. Footer is a black rounded card with an orange pill CTA next to a row of orange circular social icons. Generous outer gutters, tight consistent gaps (~16-24px) inside grids.

## Components
- Buttons: pill-shaped throughout. Primary = solid orange fill with white or black text ("Get Started", "Book A Call"); secondary = off-white/transparent pill with thin dark border ("Read article", "Explore The Ecosystem"); dark pill on light cards ("Explore Metalayer", "View more").
- Cards: 16-24px radius, flat fills in brand colors, no shadows, no borders on colored cards; blog cards nest a dark media panel inside an off-white card.
- Tags/labels: small pale-yellow pill badges ("Announcement", "Customer", "Metalayer").
- Filter bar: segmented pill row, active segment filled orange with white text, inactive on off-white with icons.
- UI mockups: black rounded "device" panels with white mono-ish data readouts (stats: 17,843 TPS, 99.9%), barcode graphics.
- Numbered step chips: small black rounded squares with outlined numerals (1, 2, 3).

## Signature
- Three-color pop palette (orange #F94D0E, violet #6B57EE, acid yellow #EFF0A3) over a warm gray canvas, with heavy black used as a fourth "color".
- Black isometric line-art machines and characters rendered as flat illustrations inside colored cards.
- Halftone/dither dot textures — hero gradients, section backdrops, card fills.
- Everything is a rounded rectangle: page = stacked bento cards, buttons = pills, no visible shadows anywhere.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Compose pages as flat rounded cards on the #E9E8E2 canvas; rotate card fills through orange, violet, black, off-white, and occasional yellow.
- Set headlines in black-weight grotesque, Title Case, very large, with tight tracking; keep body copy small for strong scale contrast.
- Reserve solid orange pills for primary CTAs; use thin-border off-white pills for secondary actions.
- Use black isometric machine illustrations and halftone dot textures as the decorative layer instead of photos.
- Tag content with small pale-yellow pill badges.

Don't:
- Add drop shadows, gradients on buttons, or glassmorphism — the system is entirely flat fills.
- Use blue links or generic SaaS pastels; stay inside the orange/violet/yellow/black set.
- Use sharp-cornered cards or rectangular buttons — everything is pill or 16px+ rounded.
- Put long paragraphs in heroes; copy stays terse under giant headlines.
- Use photography or realistic 3D renders; illustration style is flat black iso line art only.
