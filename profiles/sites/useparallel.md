## Overview
A big-type job-board landing that pairs an enormous black grotesque headline on a warm cream canvas with chartreuse-yellow CTA slabs and playful sticker doodles (hand-drawn scribble badges, circular avatar cutouts). Full-width black bands alternate with cream sections, giving a high-contrast poster rhythm. The mood is energetic and slightly zine-like without losing the tidy card grids of a SaaS product.

## Layout
Slim top nav on cream: wordmark left, center links, Sign in + boxed "Sign up Free" right. Hero is pure typography — a two-line staggered headline filling the viewport width, decorated with scattered avatar circles and yellow scribble stickers over a faint grid + yellow-to-lilac gradient wash at the section edge. Sections alternate cream/black full-bleed bands: a black value-prop band with three columns, then left-aligned section headings ("Trending jobs", "Top Companies") with "View all" links and 4-up horizontal card rows. A "Find your culture" section is just rows of bordered text chips. Steps section splits into a numbered list (left) and a lavender panel holding a product screenshot (right). Black FAQ band with stacked accordions. Closing CTA repeats the gradient wash and stickers, and the footer ends with a giant full-width lowercase "parallel" wordmark in white on black.

## Components
Buttons: rectangular with small radius, chartreuse fill with black text for primary CTAs ("Explore", "Get started free", "Start for free"); secondary buttons are white with 1px black border; card buttons ("Apply Direct", "Browse Jobs") are white with black border and a hard black offset shadow (no blur). Cards: white, thin black or light-gray border, medium radius, logo + title + meta + full-width button; company cards lead with a photo. Chips: white, 1px black border, small radius, black text. Inputs: plain white rectangle with thin border, paired inline with a yellow button. FAQ items: dark charcoal rounded rows with chevrons on black. Collection tiles: rounded photo cards with a white caption bar and a circled plus icon.

## Signature
- Chartreuse (#E3FF3C) used exclusively as the action color against black and cream — buttons read as neon slabs.
- Hand-drawn sticker scribbles and floating circular avatar cutouts scattered around oversized headlines.
- Hard offset black shadows on white bordered card buttons — a neo-brutalist wink inside otherwise clean cards.
- Alternating full-bleed black and cream bands, capped by a footer-filling giant lowercase wordmark.
- Yellow-to-lilac gradient wash with a faint grid texture at hero and closing-CTA edges.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Reserve the chartreuse fill for CTAs only; keep everything else black, white, cream, or a single pastel (lavender).
- Set headlines huge and tight in a heavy geometric grotesque, staggered across two lines to fill the width.
- Alternate full-width black sections with cream ones to create poster-like rhythm between content blocks.
- Border cards and chips with thin black lines; add hard offset shadows to buttons inside cards for tactility.
- Decorate type-only heroes with small stickers, scribbles, and avatar circles rather than large illustrations.

**Don't**
- Don't use blue links or a conventional SaaS blue anywhere — links and emphasis stay black or underlined.
- Don't round buttons into pills; corners stay near-square (2-6px) even when cards are more rounded.
- Don't add soft blurred drop shadows; depth comes from borders and hard offsets.
- Don't fill the hero with imagery — the headline is the hero; imagery lives in mid-page card rows.
- Don't let the cream canvas go pure white or the black bands go gray; contrast between bands must stay stark.
