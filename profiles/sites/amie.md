## Overview
A near-monochrome light interface: off-white #FAFAFA canvas, pure-white cards, near-black type, with one saturated sky-blue (#12A8FD) reserved for CTAs and small pops of yellow, green, orange, and purple used as semantic markers. Headlines do the heavy lifting — huge, bold, tightly set — while everything else stays quiet and airy. Product screenshots of the actual app are the main imagery; there is no decorative illustration.

## Layout
Slim top nav: small black logo mark, two text links (Features, Pricing) left-of-center; Login text link plus a blue pill "Get started" on the right. Hero is left-aligned (home) or centered (pricing): giant two-line headline, a blue primary CTA next to a white outlined secondary CTA, then a full-width app screenshot in a rounded light card. Sections alternate on the same #FAFAFA/white field with generous vertical whitespace; a grayscale "Trusted by" logo row sits under the hero. Pricing uses two side-by-side plan cards plus a full-width Enterprise card, followed by a long thin-ruled feature-comparison table and an accordion FAQ list.

## Components
- Primary button: solid #12A8FD fill, white medium-weight label, ~10px corner radius, no shadow.
- Secondary button: white fill, 1px light-gray border, black label with a small leading icon, same radius.
- Pricing CTAs: full-width solid black pills ("Start 7 day free trial", "Talk to sales") with white text.
- Cards: pure white on the gray canvas, 1px hairline border, large 16px+ radius, barely-there shadow.
- Toggle: green (#01C847) pill switch for monthly/yearly, paired with a green uppercase badge and a hand-drawn arrow doodle.
- Tables/FAQ: hairline gray dividers, small green checkmarks, chevron accordions — no fills or zebra striping.

## Signature
- Headline words highlighted with a yellow marker block (#FDEEAB behind #D39100 amber text) or a gray block — text-as-hero, highlighter-as-accent.
- Two-tone headlines: black phrase followed by the same size set in light gray ("Which plan is right for you? Use our 7 day free trial.").
- One electric sky-blue reserved almost exclusively for CTA fills; pricing CTAs flip to solid black pills.
- Handwritten script annotations with curly hand-drawn arrows pointing at UI details ("These companies have 10+ Amie users").
- Tiny colored plan glyphs (orange diamond, green building, purple globe) as the only per-card color.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the page 95% grayscale (#FAFAFA canvas, white cards, near-black ink) and let #12A8FD appear only on CTAs and small interactive elements.
- Set headlines very large and bold with tight tracking, and de-emphasize the second clause in light gray instead of shrinking it.
- Use the yellow highlighter treatment on one key phrase per hero, not scattered through body copy.
- Show the real product UI in large rounded white frames as the primary visual.
- Add personality through small touches: handwritten annotations, doodle arrows, tiny colored glyphs.

Don't:
- Don't add gradients, colored section backgrounds, or drop shadows heavier than a hairline — surfaces stay flat white/off-white.
- Don't use blue for links, icons, or decoration; off-CTA color is limited to semantic greens/yellows and tiny plan glyphs.
- Don't mix button languages: marketing CTAs are blue rounded rectangles, pricing/commit actions are black pills — not both in one context.
- Don't introduce stock photography or abstract illustration; imagery is app screenshots only.
- Don't crowd sections — rhythm depends on oversized vertical whitespace between blocks.
