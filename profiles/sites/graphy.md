## Overview
Near-black canvas used as a stage for saturated neon chart imagery: glowing magenta, violet, lime, and orange gradients render actual bar, line, donut, and heatmap graphics as the decoration itself. Text is quiet white grotesque; all color energy lives in the data visuals and soft radial glow blooms. Occasional full-white sections invert the scheme for contrast relief.

## Layout
Slim top nav: small logo left, sparse links (Pricing, Log in) plus a white pill Sign up button right. Hero is center-aligned headline + short subcopy + single pill CTA, framed by scattered floating 3D chart glyphs at the page edges. Sections alternate black and white backgrounds in long full-width bands; content sits in a narrow centered column with big vertical whitespace. Mid-page uses bento-style grids of dark chart cards and a sticky-feel feature list (active item white, inactive items dimmed gray) paired with a neon product panel. Pricing: three equal dark cards over a glowing background, then a huge white rounded FAQ sheet with a single-column accordion.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped; primary is solid white fill with black text; secondary is dark/transparent with a thin light border ("Get started with Free"). Small pill segmented toggles (Monthly/Annually, Data/Visualize/Share) with the active segment lighter. Cards: very dark gray (#141414-ish) panels on black, large 16-20px radius, hairline borders, no visible drop shadows — separation comes from tone and inner glow of the chart art. Product screenshot framed with a neon gradient border. FAQ rows: light gray rounded rows with plus icons and hairline dividers. Logo chips for social proof: small dark pill badges with colored marks.

## Signature
- Charts as ornament: real-looking bar/donut/line graphics in neon gradients scattered around the hero and footer like confetti.
- Soft radial glow blooms (pink/orange/violet) leaking from behind black sections and pricing cards.
- Strict black/white base with color reserved exclusively for data visuals and inline text highlights (key phrases tinted pink, green, purple within otherwise plain copy).
- White pill CTA on black — the only solid-filled button on the page.

## Do
- Keep the base strictly black and white; inject color only through chart graphics, glows, and highlighted words.
- Use pill buttons everywhere, with solid white fill reserved for the single primary action per view.
- Alternate full black and full white section bands for rhythm; put dark cards on black, light accordions on white.
- Render decorative elements as plausible data visualizations (bars, donuts, heatmaps) in magenta/lime/violet gradients.
- Separate dark cards with tone shifts and hairline borders rather than shadows.

## Don't
- Don't color buttons or nav with the neon accents — CTAs stay white/black.
- Don't use hard-edged or small-radius cards; everything rounded 16px+.
- Don't add heavy drop shadows or bevels on the dark surfaces.
- Don't set headlines in serifs or heavy display weights; the grotesque stays medium and calm while imagery carries the drama.
- Don't flood-fill sections with gradient backgrounds; glows stay soft, local, and behind content.
