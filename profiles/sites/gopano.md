## Overview
Warm cream canvas with near-black ink and one screaming neon-green accent, softened by rounded everything. Sections are big cream rounded-corner panels floating on the darker ecru background, with organic blurred green "squiggle" shapes bleeding behind them. Headlines are chunky, tightly set, and always highlight one keyword in the accent color. Friendly and toy-like but restrained: color is scarce, whitespace is generous, and all CTAs are solid black pills.

## Layout
Sticky-feel top nav: logo left, center text links, one black pill "Get demo" right. Hero is centered stacked text — huge two-line headline with a green (or pink) accent word, short subhead, single black pill CTA — followed by a product screenshot framed in a thick neon-green rounded border. Page body is a vertical rhythm of large rounded cream panels (lighter than the canvas), alternating text-left/screenshot-right and vice versa inside them. Grids are simple 3-column card layouts (blog posts, integrations, pricing tiers, team use cases). Footer is minimal: logo, copyright, three legal links, one more black pill CTA. The teams page swaps the green accent for magenta pink, keeping the identical structure.

## Components
- Buttons: solid near-black (#1C1B15) full pill, white bold label, no border or shadow; small white pill secondary chips (e.g. "Browser extension"). Filter tabs are small pills, active one filled bright green with dark text.
- Cards: near-white (#FBF8EE) on cream, large 16-24px radius, no visible border, little to no shadow; blog cards top an illustration zone with a green blurred-blob motif, then title, excerpt, and a green-tinted category pill with read time.
- Pricing cards: white rounded rectangles with bold plan name, oversized price, checklist rows using small green rounded-square icons, full-width black pill CTA.
- Product screenshots: rendered as rounded dark- or green-outlined browser frames; hero shot gets a thick vivid green stroke.
- Inputs: single newsletter field — white pill input with placeholder plus adjoining black pill "Subscribe".

## Signature
- Thick neon-green (#3FE41F) rounded border framing the hero product screenshot.
- One accent-colored word inside every otherwise-black headline ("need your attention", "for free").
- Organic blurred green squiggle/blob shapes drifting behind and between cream panels.
- Green smiley "ticket" mascot marking the final CTA section on every page.
- Nested cream-on-ecru: page canvas is darker than the big rounded section panels sitting on it.

## Do
- Keep the palette to cream, near-black, and one neon accent per page (green default, pink for teams); let white cards create depth instead of shadows.
- Highlight exactly one word per headline in the accent color; keep the rest near-black and extra-bold with tight tracking.
- Use pill buttons in solid black for every CTA, including inside cards and the footer.
- Round generously: 16-24px on panels and cards, full pill on buttons, chips, and tabs.
- Frame product UI in rounded browser mockups; give the hero shot a thick accent-green stroke.

## Don't
- Don't use blue links or a colored CTA — the accent green never fills a primary button, it only colors words, borders, icons, and blobs.
- Don't use pure white page backgrounds or pure black text; everything sits in the warm cream-to-charcoal range.
- Don't add visible card borders, heavy drop shadows, or gradients on UI chrome — depth comes from layered cream tones.
- Don't use thin or condensed display type; headlines are always heavy, rounded, and tightly set.
- Don't mix green and pink on the same page — the accent swap is per-page, one hue at a time.
