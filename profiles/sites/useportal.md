## Overview
A calm, illustration-led freelance SaaS landing page: a painterly twilight landscape (starry violet sky, pink-lit mountains, a cute orange robot on a flowery hill) fades into a flat near-white page. Big editorial serif headlines carry the voice while UI does the proof: a large, rounded product mockup floats inside the hero illustration. One saturated blue does all interactive work against otherwise neutral grays.

## Layout
Sparse top nav (logo left; Product/Blog/Contact right, active item in a translucent pill) sits directly on the hero illustration. Hero is centered: serif two-line headline, sans subparagraph, single pill CTA, then a huge rounded browser/product mockup overlapping the illustration's horizon. Below, the illustration dissolves into a #f7f7f7 canvas and the page becomes a narrow left-aligned single column (roughly the left 60% of the container) with generous vertical padding; sections are separated by thin dashed hairlines. Rhythm alternates serif-headline blocks, two-column icon feature lists, floating collaged screenshot clusters, a numbered 5-step vertical timeline with a dotted connector, a pricing badge block, and a handwritten "founder memo" card. The illustration returns at the very bottom as a footer landscape, bookending the page.

## Components
Buttons: full pill; hero CTA is off-white (#f7f7f7) with dark label sitting on the illustration, mid-page/footer CTAs and the in-mockup "Pay" button are solid blue #297dfa with white label and a soft drop shadow. Cards: white, large radius (16-24px+), very soft diffuse shadows; the hero mockup gets an extra thick rounded bezel. Feature list items: small solid-color circle icons (blue, green, orange, pink) with plain dark labels, no card chrome. Step markers: solid blue circles with white numerals joined by a dotted vertical line. Pricing uses a blue scalloped-seal badge with a serif "1%". Callout: blue left-border rule on plain text. Hand-drawn blue arrow + handwriting annotation ("Latest feature release") as an accent. Footer links are plain underlined dark text.

## Signature
- Painterly anime-adjacent landscape illustration (twilight sky, orange robot) framing the top and bottom of an otherwise flat off-white page
- Editorial high-contrast serif for every headline against a plain grotesque body — magazine tone for a payments tool
- One blue (#297dfa) reused everywhere interactive: CTAs, step numbers, seal badge, annotations, callout rule
- Handmade touches on a clean grid: hand-drawn arrows, handwriting notes, a signed founder memo, dashed section dividers

## Do
- Fade the hero illustration into the flat #f7f7f7 canvas with a soft white gradient rather than a hard edge
- Set all headlines in the editorial serif at near-black, keep body copy in the grotesque at ~#3d3d3d
- Keep one saturated blue for every actionable element; let feature icons carry the small green/orange/pink pops
- Use pill buttons and generously rounded white cards with soft, low-contrast shadows
- Left-align long sections into a narrow column with dashed hairline dividers and big vertical gaps

## Don't
- Don't add gradients, glassmorphism, or dark sections mid-page — the body stays flat near-white
- Don't introduce a second CTA color or outline-style buttons; blue fill and the off-white hero pill are the only two treatments
- Don't set headlines in the sans or bold the serif — the contrast between serif display and quiet sans body is the system
- Don't box the icon feature lists into bordered cards; icons sit bare on the canvas
- Don't crowd the illustration with UI beyond the single floating mockup; artwork stays atmospheric, not decorative clip-art
