## Overview
A two-tone forest-green and lime-green world where everything is a shade of green: dark #273F2B for hero, footer, and text; pale green tints for section bands; electric lime #79EA54 for every CTA. Headlines are set in a chunky, slightly bouncy all-caps condensed display face that dominates each section, while body copy stays in a quiet neutral grotesque. Serif is used sparingly for pricing plan names and prices, adding an editorial touch. The overall feel is friendly, eco-coded B2B with unusually strong personality for legaltech.

## Layout
Sticky dark-green nav bar with pill-shaped lime CTA at right, floating over a full-bleed dark-green hero (home) or a pale-green hero band (product, solutions, pricing). Hero is center-aligned: giant all-caps headline, short subhead, lime primary pill + white secondary pill, star-rating proof row, and circular customer avatars scattered at the hero edges. Below the hero, a monochrome logo marquee on white. Sections alternate white, pale-green (#F2F6EB/#EBF8DF), and dark-green full-width bands, each anchored by a large centered all-caps heading. Feature sections are two-column: text with green bullet dots on one side, product-UI screenshot on the other, sitting on soft cream panels. Blog is a 3-column card grid with a lime hero band. Footer is a tall dark-green multi-column sitemap with a lime "Learn more" pill callout.

## Components
Buttons: fully rounded pills; primary is lime #79EA54 with dark-green text, secondary is white or outlined dark-green pill; on light cards the primary flips to solid dark-green pill with white text. Cards: 12-16px radius, flat, no visible shadow; light cards use cream/pale-green fill (#F3F7EE), the highlighted pricing tier inverts to solid dark green with lime CTA and a lime "The most popular" pill badge on top. Testimonial cards are plain off-white rectangles with quote, avatar, name, and a row of five green dots. Feature bullets are small solid lime-green dots. FAQ is a stacked accordion of full-width off-white bars. Small metadata tags and add-on labels are tiny gray pills. Circular dark-green arrow buttons drive the testimonial carousel.

## Signature
- Everything-green palette: dark forest #273F2B + electric lime #79EA54 on white/pale-green bands, with no third hue anywhere
- Huge chunky all-caps condensed display headlines, including a stacked "OUR CUSTOMERS / TALK ABOUT IT / BETTER THAN US" trio rendered as pill-shaped banner rows
- Hand-drawn wobbly lime line illustrations (trophy, loops with dot nodes) decorating award and pricing sections
- Floating circular customer avatars with tiny pill labels scattered around the dark hero

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep text and UI chrome in the single dark forest green #273F2B — it serves as both ink and surface color
- Reserve lime #79EA54 strictly for CTAs, bullets, and playful accents; flip CTA to dark-green pill when it sits on a light card
- Alternate full-width white, pale-green, and dark-green section bands to create rhythm
- Set every section heading centered, all-caps, in the chunky display face, even for mundane content ("COMPARE PLANS", "OUR FEATURES")
- Use flat cream/pale-green cards with medium radius and no shadows

**Don't**
- Don't introduce blue, purple, or any non-green brand hue — even the star ratings are green dots
- Don't use sharp-cornered or small buttons; every actionable element is a generous pill
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients to cards; depth comes from color-band inversion (light card vs solid dark card)
- Don't set headlines in mixed case or light weights; the display voice is always heavy caps
- Don't use stock photography as decoration outside people/team contexts; product sections rely on real UI screenshots on cream panels
