## Overview
Minimal light SaaS landing built on a warm off-white canvas with near-black text and a grid of large pastel feature tiles (lavender, pink, cyan, peach). Nearly all chrome is stripped away: no borders, almost no shadows, black pill CTAs. The signature device is the two-line headline where the first sentence is solid ink and the second repeats in a muted or tone-on-tone tint.

## Layout
Single-row top nav: logo + small "BETA" chip left, "Sign in" text link and black pill "Join Waitlist" right. Hero is a centered two-line headline (no subparagraph, no hero CTA) followed directly by a large product screenshot in a white rounded card. Feature story told as a bento-style block: one full-width pastel card, then a 2-up row of pastel cards, then another full-width card — each pairing a small product-UI vignette with a short bold headline. Mid-page: "Packed with essentials" heading over a 3-up row of small light-gray cards. Pricing is a split section: heading left, checklist with hairline row dividers right. Final CTA section centers a headline and black pill button over faded screenshot collage. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; content sits in a moderate centered container.

## Components
- Buttons: solid black pill, small white text, compact padding (nav and final CTA). Secondary action is plain text link. Small white pill "Post"/"Open in Figma" buttons appear inside product vignettes.
- Cards: two families — big pastel feature tiles (flat fill, large ~20-24px radius, no border/shadow) and small neutral gray feature cards with subtle rounded product thumbnails.
- Product vignettes: white rounded mini-UI mockups floating on the pastel fills with soft diffuse shadows.
- Pricing list: checkmark + label rows separated by 1px hairlines, no card wrapper.
- Chips/tags: tiny pill badges (BETA chip, tag pills in mockups).

## Signature
- Two-tone echo headlines: bold black sentence, second sentence in gray or a tint of the card's pastel ("Loop in the right people." / "Cross-post to Slack." in deeper pink).
- Pastel bento grid: full-width and half-width flat pastel tiles (lavender, pink, cyan, peach) carrying white product-UI miniatures.
- Black pill CTA as the only saturated-dark element on an otherwise soft off-white page.
- Real product screenshots doing the illustration work — no abstract art or stock imagery.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Pair every feature headline with a muted or tone-on-tone second line instead of body copy.
- Use flat pastel card fills at large radius with zero borders; let the white UI mockups inside carry the depth.
- Keep exactly one button style: black pill with white text; demote secondary actions to text links.
- Alternate full-width and 2-up pastel cards for rhythm rather than uniform grids.
- Render pricing/feature lists as hairline-divided checklist rows, not boxed cards.

Don't:
- Add gradients, outlines, or drop shadows to the pastel tiles — they are flat color fields.
- Introduce a saturated brand color for buttons or links; the palette's color lives only in card backgrounds.
- Write paragraph-length body copy; sections communicate in one bold line plus one echo line.
- Use square or slightly-rounded buttons — CTAs are fully pill-shaped.
- Crowd the hero: no subheadline paragraph or badge stack, just headline then screenshot.
