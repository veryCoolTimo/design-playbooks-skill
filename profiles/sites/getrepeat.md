## Overview
Big-type CPG SaaS with an editorial-poster attitude. Enormous all-caps grotesque headlines fill the viewport width, punctuated inline with small circular avatars and icon chips that break the text flow. The page alternates between a warm greige canvas, electric chartreuse bands, and pure black bands, with lavender and coral as small doodle-like accents. Thin-outline line drawings of CPG packaging (cans, pump bottles) float behind and beneath content.

## Layout
Sticky top nav is a floating pill cluster: logo left, center pill group of links (active link inverted to a black pill), and LOGIN plus a chartreuse "BOOK A DEMO" pill on the right. Heroes are 3-4 line all-caps headlines at near-viewport width with a short paragraph and one pill CTA beside or below. Sections stack as full-width color bands (greige → chartreuse → white/greige → black) with huge rounded corners where bands meet, giving a card-on-card rhythm. Content inside bands splits into a big left-aligned section title and right-column body text or lists; tiny all-caps eyebrow labels ("THE SHELF LIFE", "REPEAT BLOG") sit at band top-left. Footers repeat a chartreuse band with a 4-column link grid and a bottom frieze of outlined product bottles. Giant single words ("RESTOCK", "…OAD REFR…") bleed off-screen as section dividers.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, chartreuse fill with black text and a leading arrow (→) for primary; black pill with white text for secondary/subscribe; white outlined pills for tertiary ("VIEW ALL"). Nav links are borderless pills that invert to solid black when active. Cards: white rounded rectangles (12-16px) with flat fills, no shadows — podcast cards pair a photo thumbnail with an all-caps title; newsletter cards on black use a chartreuse icon tile in the corner. Inputs: pill/rounded text fields with thin dark borders and all-caps placeholder ("ENTER YOUR EMAIL..."), paired with a small black pill submit. Blog list items are typographic rows separated by hairlines with a top-right ↗ arrow. FAQ rows on black use hairline dividers and circled + icons. Chat-bubble and speech-balloon graphics in lavender/coral appear as playful collage elements.

## Signature
- All-caps headlines interrupted mid-sentence by inline circular avatars and pastel icon chips
- Chartreuse (#E0F64C) used both as CTA fill and as full-bleed section-band background
- Full-width color bands with oversized rounded corners stacking greige / chartreuse / black
- Thin monoline illustrations of CPG packaging (bottles, cans) as backgrounds and footer frieze
- Marquee-scale cropped words bleeding off the viewport between sections

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Set headlines in tight all-caps grotesque at viewport-filling sizes, 3-4 lines max
- Drop circular photos or icon chips inline within headline text to break up the type
- Alternate full-width greige, chartreuse, and black bands with 24px+ rounded top corners
- Lead every CTA with an arrow glyph (→ on buttons, ↗ on outbound list links)
- Use hairline dividers and typographic rows for lists instead of boxed cards

**Don't**
- Add drop shadows or gradients — every surface is a flat fill with at most a thin border
- Use the coral or lavender for buttons; they are accent doodles and chips only
- Set headlines in mixed case or light weights — the voice is uppercase and medium/bold
- Introduce a second saturated color family beyond chartreuse + coral + lavender
- Center body copy; paragraphs stay left-aligned in narrow right-hand columns
