## Overview
Terminal-meets-print aesthetic: a near-black forest-green canvas covered in electric chartreuse type, thin 1px keylines, and monospace labels. Headlines are huge, condensed, all-caps, and often the loudest element on the page. Occasional full-width color-block sections (burnt orange stats, sky-blue resources, coral illustration) punch through the dark green monotone.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on the dark canvas: wordmark left, uppercase mono links center, two small rectangular CTAs right (one filled chartreuse, one outlined). Hero is a split layout — giant multi-line all-caps chartreuse headline left, thin-line schematic diagram or product screenshot right. Section rhythm alternates: dark-green base sections, inset rounded color-block panels (orange, blue, black), then back to base. Feature storytelling uses zig-zag two-column rows (screenshot/video thumb on one side, mono heading + short copy on the other). Logo strips sit in slim bordered bands. Footer is a dense multi-column mono link grid on the same dark green.

## Components
Buttons: small rectangles with 2-4px corners; primary filled chartreuse (#E4F97B) with dark green mono uppercase label, secondary 1px chartreuse outline on transparent. Cards: 1px chartreuse or dark keyline borders, square-ish corners, transparent or black fills; large stat tiles use outlined boxes with giant chartreuse numerals inside an orange panel. Video/demo thumbnails are black cards with dotted-vortex artwork, uppercase titles, and small circular play buttons. Resource cards: thin-bordered rectangles with mono titles and tiny tag/CTA row. Final CTA band is a pale chartreuse rounded panel with an outer neon glow, black display type centered.

## Signature
- Electric chartreuse (#E4F97B) on near-black forest green — essentially a two-color system with rare color-block interruptions.
- Everything typographic: all-caps condensed display headlines plus monospace uppercase for nav, labels, buttons, and body-adjacent copy.
- Thin 1px line-work: schematic pipeline diagrams, keyline borders, bordered logo bands — a blueprint/terminal feel.
- Glowing pale-chartreuse CTA panel at page bottom, like a lit-up sign on the dark canvas.

## Do / Don't
- Do keep the canvas one continuous dark forest green and let chartreuse carry nearly all emphasis.
- Do set headlines huge, condensed, all-caps, and left-aligned in heroes; center them only in standalone section intros.
- Do use monospace uppercase for nav links, buttons, eyebrows, stats captions, and footer links.
- Do draw diagrams and borders as thin 1px chartreuse strokes; use outlined boxes for stats and cards.
- Do reserve saturated color blocks (burnt orange, sky blue, coral) for one inset panel at a time, with rounded corners.
- Don't use white backgrounds or light sections — even the closing CTA panel is chartreuse, not white.
- Don't round buttons into pills or add drop shadows; corners stay near-square and surfaces flat (the only glow is the final CTA band).
- Don't use lowercase or sentence-case headings; display type is uppercase throughout.
- Don't introduce gradients or photography — imagery is line schematics, flat illustration, and dark product screenshots.
- Don't let chartreuse become a fill for large areas mid-page; it works as type, borders, and small button fills.
