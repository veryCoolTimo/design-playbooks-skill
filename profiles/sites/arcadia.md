## Overview
A big-type climate platform built on a strict three-color rhythm: warm cream, deep forest green, and neon signal green. Headlines are enormous ultra-condensed all-caps slabs with a single word italicized for emphasis ("ENERGY", "SOLUTIONS"). Isometric pixel-cube 3D renders and dark terminal/code screenshots give the energy-data story a tactile, developer-facing texture.

## Layout
Cream top nav with dark-green wordmark and links; two buttons at right (outlined "Sign in", filled "Get Started"). Hero is asymmetric: giant condensed headline plus short paragraph and two buttons on the left, isometric green cube render bleeding off the top-right corner. Below: cream press-logo strip, then a full-bleed aerial photo with a white condensed headline overlaid. Sections alternate backgrounds in blocks — cream, aqua-mint (#B7EEED), white, deep green — each a text-column-plus-product-screenshot split. Partner logos sit in a thin-ruled table grid. A dark testimonial band uses a halftone dot-matrix pattern in neon green. Page closes with a centered "CONTACT US" mega-headline on forest green above a matching dark footer.

## Components
Buttons: rounded rectangles (~10-12px radius), no shadows. Primary = dark forest-green fill with neon-green bold label; secondary = 1px dark-green outline on cream with dark label; on dark sections, "Learn more" is a neon-green outline with neon label. Cards: dark terminal windows with code/JSON and dashboard UI screenshots, medium radius, floating over flat section color without heavy shadows. Logo cells divided by hairline rules rather than boxed cards. Circular neon-green avatar/logo chips punctuate section corners.

## Signature
- Skyscraper condensed all-caps headlines with one italicized word per heading for emphasis
- Cream / forest-green / neon-green (#0EFE89) triad, applied in full-bleed section blocks
- Isometric pixel-cube 3D renders and dark code-terminal screenshots as the main imagery
- Neon halftone dot-matrix texture on dark green testimonial band

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set every headline in the ultra-condensed black weight, all caps, with tight leading and one italic emphasis word
- Alternate full-width section backgrounds (cream → mint → white → forest green) instead of using card containers
- Pair dark-green button fills with neon-green labels, and invert to neon outlines on dark sections
- Show the product as dark terminal/code and dashboard screenshots to signal API depth
- Use hairline-ruled grids for logo walls and keep imagery either 3D cube renders or aerial photography

Don't:
- Introduce blues, purples, or gradients — the palette is strictly greens plus cream and one aqua tint
- Use light-weight or wide display type; the identity depends on extreme condensation and heft
- Add drop shadows, glassmorphism, or pill buttons — surfaces are flat and corners stay at medium radius
- Put body copy in the display face; paragraphs stay small in the rounded grotesque
- Center hero content; heroes and feature sections are left-anchored with art bleeding off one edge
