## Overview
Warm cream-on-black minimalism: an ecru off-white canvas with near-black ink, punctuated by full-bleed black sections that invert to cream text. Color arrives only in controlled bursts — pastel sticky-note stat tiles, tiny colored diamond icons, and grainy purple-orange gradient artwork. Product screenshots and hand-drawn doodle annotations ("see the insight!") add a human, editorial touch over an otherwise disciplined SaaS layout.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on cream: left logo, center dropdown links, right paired pills ("Open app" outline, "Get a demo" solid black). Heroes are either centered (home) or split two-column (text left, photo/video right) with an inline email + CTA capture row and a Gartner/G2 rating line beneath. Section rhythm alternates cream → black → cream, each band generously padded; mid-page content sits in alternating text/screenshot two-column rows. Social proof recurs constantly: logo marquees, carousel testimonials with headshots, and a mosaic grid of pastel stat cards (20x, 9x, 10h). Every page closes with the same black "Unlock your AI sales superpowers" CTA band and a four-column footer on black.

## Components
Buttons: solid near-black fill, cream/white text, small 4-6px radius, no shadow; secondary is a thin-border outline pill of the same shape. Email-capture inputs are flat white fields with hairline borders, the black CTA button butted directly to the right. Cards: white or pastel fills on cream, 8-14px radius, hairline borders, very soft or no shadow; feature cards lead with a small colored diamond glyph. Pricing table uses zebra-striped hairline rows with black check dots; the popular plan gets a black tab and border. Blog cards: image top, white body, outlined "Read more" button. FAQ rows are hairline-divided accordions.

## Signature
- Ecru #F1F0E9 canvas alternating with pure-black inverted sections — the black CTA band is on every page
- Pastel sticky-note stat grid (yellow/pink/lavender/mint) with oversized "20x / 9x / 10h" numerals and customer logos
- Hand-drawn script doodles and arrows annotating sections, plus grainy purple-orange gradient art blobs
- Small colored diamond bullets (blue/red/yellow/green) marking feature card headers

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas warm off-white (#F1F0E9), never pure white — pure white is reserved for card fills
- Use solid black small-radius buttons for every primary CTA and pair them inline with an email input
- Insert full-width black sections for testimonials and closing CTAs, with cream text
- Confine color to pastel card fills, tiny diamond icons, and gradient artwork; text stays black/cream
- Repeat social proof rhythmically: logo rows, star-rating lines, headshot testimonial carousels

Don't:
- Don't use a saturated brand color for buttons or links — the CTA is always near-black
- Don't add heavy drop shadows or thick borders; separation comes from fill contrast and hairlines
- Don't use pill or 16px+ radii on buttons; corners stay tight and squared-off
- Don't set headings in heavy black weights or wide tracking — display type is medium and tight
- Don't fill long stretches without a proof element or a black band; the alternation is the pacing
