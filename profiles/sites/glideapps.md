## Overview
A Swiss-leaning, product-first language that alternates hard between pure white and near-black full-bleed sections, with one loud cyan reserved almost exclusively for CTAs. Real app screenshots inside rounded black device frames carry most of the visual weight; decoration is minimal. Typography is a tight neo-grotesque set large, doing the branding work that illustration would elsewhere.

## Layout
Slim top nav on white: wordmark left, center dropdown links, Log In + cyan pill Sign Up right. Heroes are either left-aligned two-column (headline + subcopy left, floating product screenshot right, as on Apps/Business) or centered display type (Pricing, dark Home sections). Section rhythm is block-based: full-width rounded panels in light gray (#EFEFEF), deep teal, navy/black, or lavender alternate with plain white; the home page flips to long pure-black stretches mid-page. Generous vertical padding between blocks, 3-column feature grids with small line icons, logo strips in muted gray, centered oversized closing CTA headline before the multi-column footer.

## Components
Buttons: solid cyan pills with black or dark text ("Start for free", "Start a free trial"), paired with white/outlined pill secondaries ("Contact Sales", "Get started"); no gradients, no shadows on buttons. Cards: large-radius panels with flat fills (light gray, teal, lavender, near-black) and no borders or drop shadows — color blocks, not elevated cards. Pricing table: white plan cards over a flat gray canvas, highlighted "Popular" column tinted pale cyan with a cyan header tab; comparison rows zebra-striped in faint gray. Small pill tags/badges (cyan "Explore 300+ templates", eyebrow labels with tiny icons). Screenshots sit in matte-black rounded tablet/phone bezels. FAQ rows are plain text with chevron toggles, no boxes.

## Signature
- Strict white/black section alternation on one page — light marketing blocks colliding with full-bleed near-black product sections.
- A single electric cyan used only for CTAs, badges, and highlights against otherwise neutral grayscale.
- Product screenshots framed in chunky rounded black device bezels as the primary imagery.
- Flat color-block cards (gray, deep teal, lavender, navy) with big radii and zero shadows or borders.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve the cyan (#12CCE6) for primary CTAs and small badges; keep everything else grayscale or a flat block color.
- Use full-width rounded panels to segment content instead of borders, dividers, or drop shadows.
- Set headlines large in a tight neo-grotesque with sentence case ("Your data never looked so good.").
- Show real UI screenshots in rounded black device frames rather than abstract illustration.
- Alternate white and near-black sections for pacing on long pages; keep dark sections truly dark (#0A0B0D range).

Don't:
- Add gradients, glows, or glassmorphism — every surface here is a flat fill.
- Use blue link-styled buttons or square corners; CTAs are always pills.
- Introduce more than one saturated accent per section (teal, lavender, and cyan never compete in the same block).
- Put borders or heavy shadows on cards; separation comes from background-color contrast alone.
- Crowd sections — each block gets one idea, one visual, and generous vertical padding.
