## Overview
An arcade-loud, gaming-native marketing system on a deep indigo page (#0a0d3a) that is constantly washed by an animated Blurple-to-violet-to-magenta gradient mesh. Massive 800-weight all-caps headlines, saturated color bands, 3D character art, and everywhere-soft corners make software feel like a toy rather than a product. Blurple #5865f2 is the sole brand anchor; electric green and magenta orbit it as the only other loud voices.

## Layout
Dark indigo top nav (logo, links, Login, download CTA) collapsing to hamburger under 768px; matching dark footer topped by a giant wordmark. Content sits in a centered ~1200px column on the full-bleed canvas. Page rhythm alternates: gradient-mesh hero with all-caps headline and CTA pair, then two-column text+media splits and stacked full-width color cards (magenta gradient, raised indigo, pure-black showcase), a full-bleed Blurple marquee/CTA band, then footer. Big vertical indigo gaps between sections collide with saturated bands for pacing; a very dense breakpoint ladder keeps oversized type balanced.

## Components
Buttons are solid fills at 12-16px radius: Blurple with white label as the workhorse, electric green with black label reserved for the one top-intent CTA, plus white and translucent-indigo ghost variants. Cards use no borders and almost no shadow — separation comes from color fields (magenta gradient panels, #1e2353 raised indigo, black bands) at 40px radius with 32-40px padding. Only one shadow exists: a very wide violet-tinted glow under floating media. Signature pieces include a Blurple big-number stat card, magenta step cards and badges, an indigo Nitro pricing table, indigo game-rank rows, and indigo FAQ accordions. No text inputs appear on marketing pages; the hero media block gets swooping 88px bottom-only corners.

## Signature
- Animated Blurple→magenta gradient mesh over a deep indigo canvas — the background is never flat or neutral
- Heavy all-caps ABC Ginto Nord display (up to 82px/800) dropping straight to 400-weight body with no mid-weight
- Exactly three loud colors: Blurple owns actions and bands, green marks the single highest-intent CTA, magenta fills playful feature panels
- Toy-like radii escalating from 12px controls to 40px panels to 120px signature shapes, with 3D props overflowing card edges

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the base #0a0d3a indigo and let the moving Blurple-magenta gradient do the atmosphere work
- Use Blurple for every routine action; save #35ed7e green (black text) for one "get started"-grade CTA per page
- Set headlines in 700-800 all-caps display type, then drop hard to light 16px body
- Round aggressively: 12-16px controls, 40px+ on media and feature panels
- Build depth with color bands and overlapping 3D art instead of shadows

**Don't**
- Don't swap the canvas to neutral gray or plain black — the indigo+gradient pairing is the identity
- Don't spread green around as a general accent color
- Don't use mid-weight (500-600) headlines; the voice is 700-800 or nothing
- Don't square off cards or media frames
- Don't add a fourth saturated accent beyond Blurple, green, and magenta
