## Overview
Serene, nature-infused calm laid over a warm eggshell canvas. Painterly CGI landscapes (misty lakes, clouds, auroras) and an engraved architectural floor-plan illustration carry the visual weight, while UI chrome stays minimal: hairline rules, tiny uppercase labels, dark forest-green pills. Footer sections flip to a deep teal night scene with aurora glow, embedding a dark-mode product screenshot floating over water.

## Layout
Sticky-free slim top nav: uppercase letter-spaced links left, centered wordmark logo, "WATCH DEMO" ghost pill + "GET STARTED" solid pill right. Home hero is a full-bleed landscape photo-illustration with centered headline overlaid; product page hero is text-only, centered, on cream. Sections alternate cream blocks separated by hairline borders; a numbered feature list sits left with media collage right. Big-stat rows (200M+, 5B+, 99.998%) use oversized numerals with hairline row dividers. Pricing is a dense feature-comparison table with hairline grid lines and a tinted-green highlighted "Grow" column. Blog is a strict 3-column card grid. Every page ends with the same dark "Start your journey" aurora section over a lake, then a huge wordmark footer.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, uppercase small-caps labels with wide tracking; primary is solid dark forest green (#2C4A36) with cream text, secondary is outlined/ghost pill on cream, and a pale sage pill appears on dark sections. Cards: blog cards are full-bleed abstract imagery with white text overlaid, small radius, arrow icon bottom-right, "min read" meta label. Logo bars use small outlined pill chips or hairline-divided cells. Tables rely on 1px hairlines, tiny green check glyphs, and a soft green column tint for the featured plan — no shadows anywhere. FAQ rows are hairline-divided accordions with chevrons. Product screenshots sit in thin-bordered frames on grassy background imagery.

## Signature
- Painterly, atmospheric nature CGI (mist, clouds, aurora, water) as hero and card imagery instead of product-first heroes
- Recurring dark-teal "night lake with aurora" closing section on every page, with a floating dark-mode app screenshot
- Warm eggshell canvas + dark forest-green ink/CTAs, with a single lime-highlighter swipe on a hero keyword
- Hairline-rule everything: stat rows, pricing grid, FAQs — flat, borderless-shadowless engineering-paper feel, including an engraved floor-plan line illustration

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Use the warm eggshell (#F2F0E7) canvas with dark green ink and reserve saturated color for imagery, not UI chrome
- Set all button and nav labels in small uppercase with wide letter-spacing inside pill shapes
- Separate sections, table rows, and cards with 1px hairlines instead of shadows or heavy backgrounds
- Close pages with the dark aurora/lake section containing a centered invitation headline and the dark product UI
- Use large bare numerals (no cards, no icons) for stats, aligned to a hairline grid

**Don't**
- Don't use drop shadows, gradients on buttons, or elevated card surfaces — the system is entirely flat
- Don't use pure white backgrounds or pure black text; everything is warm-tinted cream and deep green
- Don't put bright blue/purple SaaS accent colors anywhere — the accent range stays lime-sage-teal
- Don't crop the nature imagery into small decorative thumbnails on heroes; it runs full-bleed with text overlaid
- Don't mix radii loosely: buttons are always pills, media frames stay near-square with small rounding
