## Overview
An indie-film platform that pairs hand-drawn cinematic illustration with a stark, near-monochrome product UI. Full-width pastel illustrations (rocket launch over pink clouds, a lone figure on a lunar plain) bookend the page, while the middle runs on flat light-gray and white bands, tiny uppercase labels, and a single electric-violet CTA color. The mood is "film festival zine meets SaaS": expressive art, restrained interface.

## Layout
Slim white sticky nav: wordmark left, two uppercase links, search field, SIGN UP text link, and a solid violet SUBMIT A FILM button right. Hero is a full-bleed illustration with a centered condensed-uppercase headline, one subline, and a white search bar — no hero CTA button. Below, a long single column of alternating feature rows (text block one side, floating product-screenshot collage the other), swapping sides each section on flat #F1F1F1 or #FFFFFF bands. Pricing is a centered comparison (dark #333 card with violet border vs. a plain gray "Old Path" card). The closing section dissolves into a second full-width illustration; footer is flat light gray with a purple triangular logo badge and mono-style link columns.

## Components
Buttons: sharp-cornered (or barely rounded) solid #6B01F4 rectangles with small wide-tracked uppercase white labels; secondary actions are plain uppercase text links, sometimes underlined (APPLY FOR INDUSTRY ACCESS). Cards: white product-mockup cards with soft diffuse drop shadows, layered and slightly rotated into loose collages with hand-drawn scribble accents. Chat-bubble cards in purple (#8B44FF) and yellow (#FFC400) with rounded corners and black text on yellow / white on purple. Inputs: flat white rectangular search fields with gray placeholder and a magnifier icon, no visible border weight. Pricing card: dark charcoal fill, violet outline, violet price tag, white checklist.

## Signature
- Every section headline is two lines with exactly one word tinted violet (#8B44FF): "The homepage", "All the details", "Get feedback".
- Bookend illustrations: detailed pastel sci-fi/space artwork at top and bottom framing an otherwise flat gray UI.
- Tiny letterspaced uppercase mono-flavored microcopy everywhere — nav, buttons, footer, captions.
- One violet, used hard: the only saturated interface color against gray/white/charcoal, with yellow as a rare foil.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve #6B01F4 fills for the primary CTA and highlight one keyword per heading in #8B44FF.
- Alternate flat #F1F1F1 and #FFFFFF full-width bands with generous vertical padding between feature rows.
- Compose feature visuals as layered, slightly tilted screenshot cards with soft shadows plus hand-drawn scribbles/arrows.
- Keep buttons and inputs sharp-edged rectangles with small uppercase tracked labels.
- Use illustration only at the page's top and bottom edges, at full bleed.

Don't:
- Don't round buttons into pills or add gradients — fills are flat and corners near-square.
- Don't introduce a second saturated UI color beyond violet (yellow appears only as a small accent, e.g. one chat bubble and a score dial).
- Don't put photography in the hero — the brand's imagery is illustrated; photos live only inside product-mockup cards.
- Don't set headings in mixed color or lightweight fonts; they're heavy grotesques in near-black with one violet word.
- Don't box body copy in bordered containers; text sits directly on the flat band with tight left-aligned measure.
