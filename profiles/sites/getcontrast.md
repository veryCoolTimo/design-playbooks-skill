## Overview
Minimal-light SaaS with a playful, sticker-book personality. A stark white canvas and near-black type carry the structure, while a candy palette of pastel tint cards (mint, butter yellow, lilac, blush, ice blue) and a coral-red CTA inject energy. A light neo-brutalist layer — thin black outlines, hard offset shadows, highlighter-marker pills behind headline words — keeps it from feeling sterile.

## Layout
Centered logo-left nav with text links, a "Book a demo" ghost link, and a coral pill-ish CTA at top right. Hero is a huge centered two-line headline with a marker-highlighted keyword, short subcopy, then a full-width dark product screenshot in a rounded frame. Sections alternate: white → full-bleed pastel band (blush, ice blue, cream) → white, giving clear rhythm. Content sits in loose bento/masonry grids of small tinted cards (feature chips on home, 2-3 column blog cards, 3-up pricing cards). Handwritten script annotations with hand-drawn arrows point at CTAs and screenshots. Footer is a full-width black rounded-top slab with coral logo.

## Components
- Primary button: coral #FA5A5A fill, white label, small 4-6px radius, thin black outline on the nav/footer versions.
- Secondary button: white fill, thin black border, same small radius ("Book a demo", "Book a call").
- Cards: pastel or white fill, 1px black border, large 16-20px radius, hard black offset shadow (down-right) on pricing cards, testimonial chat card, and CTA panels; blog/feature tiles drop the shadow and use flat tint + small icon.
- Headline highlights: rounded rectangle "marker" swatches (pink, yellow, lavender) behind single words.
- Tables (pricing): borderless rows with alternating light-gray stripes and plain checkmarks; FAQ as thin-divider accordion rows.
- Toggle: monthly/yearly pill switch with coral knob.

## Signature
- Bento grids of pastel tint cards with tiny outline icons, scattered like stickers on white.
- Marker-highlight pills behind key headline words in pastel pink/yellow/lavender.
- Handwritten script annotations + hand-drawn dashed arrows guiding the eye to CTAs.
- Thin black outlines with hard offset shadows on cards and buttons — soft brutalism at low volume.
- Dark near-black product screenshots framed in rounded slabs contrasting the white page.

## Do
- Keep the page white and let color live only inside cards, highlight pills, and full-bleed pastel section bands.
- Use one coral-red for every primary CTA; pair it with a white outlined secondary button.
- Give cards 1px black borders and, on emphasis cards (pricing, CTA panels), a hard black offset shadow.
- Rotate the pastel set (mint/yellow/lilac/blush/cyan) across sibling cards so no two neighbors match.
- Add sparse handwritten annotations and arrows near key CTAs — one or two per page, not more.

## Don't
- Don't use gradients, glassmorphism, or soft blurred shadows — shadows here are flat, black, and offset.
- Don't make buttons pill-shaped or heavily rounded; button radius stays small while cards stay large.
- Don't introduce a second saturated brand color; saturation is reserved for the coral CTA alone.
- Don't tint body copy — text is near-black on white or white on the black footer, nothing in between.
- Don't fill sections edge-to-edge with dense content; the grid relies on generous white gutters between small cards.
