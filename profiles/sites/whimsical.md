## Overview
A purple-saturated dual-mode language: heroes are deep plum-to-violet gradients with neon magenta light effects and particle textures, while content sections sit on pale lavender or white. Product screenshots are the main imagery, framed in glowing purple bezels or browser chrome. Everything is soft and rounded — pill buttons, rounded display type, rounded cards — giving a playful but polished tool-brand feel.

## Layout
Floating pill-shaped white nav bar (logo left, dropdown links center, "Contact sales / Log in / Sign up" right) hovering over the hero. Heroes are centered: eyebrow badge, huge rounded-sans headline, one-line subhead, single pill CTA. Long scrolling pages alternate background modes per section (dark purple → pale lavender → white → near-black), each section a self-contained band. Feature sections use a two-column split: short bold heading + bullet list on the left, large framed product screenshot on the right. Generous vertical whitespace between bands; content constrained to a wide centered column. Oversized display type occasionally spans nearly full width ("Flow through every diagram", "Get Whimsical") as a section divider. Footer is dark-ink with four link columns and a thin purple-gradient rule above it.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped; primary is deep plum (#2E0D45) fill with white label and often a circular arrow chip (white or purple-gradient circle) docked at the right end; secondary is white pill on dark backgrounds; the AI page uses a violet-to-blue gradient pill. Cards: 12-16px radius, white or dark-plum fills, minimal borders, soft shadows only on the floating nav and pricing cards; feature cards lead with a purple/magenta gradient thumbnail, colored icon + label, and an arrow text link. Pricing tiers are translucent dark cards on the purple hero, with the highlighted Pro tier inverted to white. FAQ rows are white pill-cornered accordion bars with chevrons. Inputs barely appear; small checkmark bullets in circles list plan features. Category tags ("Engineering", "Design", "Marketing") render as colored flag-shaped labels scattered around CTAs.

## Signature
- Monochromatic purple world: nearly every surface, gradient, glow, and illustration stays inside a violet-magenta-blue band.
- Dark neon hero bands with particle/crystal 3D textures and radial magenta glows, alternating with pale lavender content bands.
- Pill CTA with an attached circular arrow chip — the recurring button silhouette across all pages.
- Chunky rounded custom display face with quirky letterforms, used huge and centered; gradient-colored words inside otherwise solid headlines ("only pay for the editors you need").

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate full-width bands between deep plum/near-black and pale lavender (#F2ECF8) rather than using one background throughout.
- Make every button a pill; give primary CTAs the deep plum fill plus a circular arrow chip on the right.
- Showcase the product with real UI screenshots inside rounded, purple-glow frames or browser chrome.
- Use violet-to-magenta or violet-to-blue gradients for section panels, card thumbnails, and highlighted headline words.
- Set headlines in a heavy rounded sans, centered, at very large sizes with tight tracking.

Don't:
- Don't introduce colors outside the purple/magenta/blue family except tiny functional accents (flag tags, diagram nodes).
- Don't use sharp corners anywhere — no square buttons, cards, or badges.
- Don't use stock photography or human imagery; the only imagery is product UI and abstract purple 3D particles.
- Don't put hard borders or heavy drop shadows on cards; separation comes from background-band contrast and subtle elevation.
- Don't left-align hero content; heroes and section headers are consistently centered.
