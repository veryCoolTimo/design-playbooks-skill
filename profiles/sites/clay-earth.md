## Overview
Editorial dark-luxury with a soft neon undercurrent. Near-black canvas, elegant serif type at every level (even body copy), warm off-white ink, and dim jewel-toned 3D shapes and purple glows floating behind content on the home page. The pricing page flips to a warm cream canvas with the identical type system, so the brand reads as "candlelit magazine" more than techno-neon.

## Layout
Slim top nav: wordmark left, wide-tracked uppercase links center (active link underlined), "SIGN IN" text + pill "GET STARTED →" right. Home hero is centered serif headline over a glowing product screenshot, followed by very long scroll sections separated by huge black voids — copy blocks are narrow (~40ch), left-aligned, introduced by tiny letterspaced uppercase kickers ("OUR STORY", "WORK WITH US"). Careers uses a scattered collage of grayscale photos; stories uses a 3-column card grid with dates as small-caps eyebrows. Pricing uses a 4-column card row on cream with checkmark feature lists below each card. Whitespace is extreme; sections breathe by hundreds of pixels.

## Components
Buttons: primary CTA is a light pill (off-white fill, dark serif/small-caps label, trailing arrow →) on dark pages; on the cream pricing page it inverts to a near-black rectangle. Plan buttons are full-width outlined rectangles with 4-6px radius, letterspaced uppercase labels, arrow glyphs. Cards: pricing cards are flat white with faint diffuse shadow and ~10px radius; story cards are borderless — just a photo with text stacked below, no container at all. Toggle (monthly/annually) is a soft pill segmented control. Small discount badges in muted orange. Photos are frequently desaturated to grayscale.

## Signature
- Serif everywhere — headlines AND body — on near-black, giving a printed-book feel rare in SaaS
- Tiny wide-tracked uppercase eyebrows/labels punctuating every section and nav item
- Dim floating 3D geometric shapes and purple/violet glow fields behind dark sections
- Grayscale photography collages and handwritten signature scribbles in white ink (careers)
- Dark site with a single cream-inverted page (pricing) sharing the exact same type system

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set headings and body in the same refined serif; reserve sans only for the wordmark if at all
- Use wide-tracked uppercase micro-labels (11-12px) as section kickers and nav links
- Keep neon restrained: dim purple glows and small jewel-toned shapes on black, never saturated flood color
- Leave enormous vertical gaps between sections; keep text columns narrow and left-aligned mid-page
- Desaturate photos to grayscale on dark pages so the palette stays black/cream/violet

Don't:
- Don't use bright saturated neon strokes or glassmorphism cards — glows here are soft and background-only
- Don't put borders or containers around story/blog cards; image + text stack is the pattern
- Don't use a colored primary button — CTAs are off-white pills on dark, near-black on cream
- Don't use geometric sans headlines or bold weights; the type is light, elegant, high-contrast serif
- Don't apply one radius everywhere — pills, 4-6px buttons, and ~10px cards coexist deliberately
