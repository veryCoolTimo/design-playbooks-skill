## Overview
Clean white SaaS chrome that stays disciplined while injecting color through content: teal brand CTAs, mustard-yellow and coral stat blocks, mint avatar halos, and playful blob-character illustrations. Long marketing pages alternate white sections with full-bleed near-black or dark-teal inverted bands, so the palette reads colorful without the base UI ever leaving neutral. Product screenshots (both light and dark UI) do much of the visual storytelling.

## Layout
Sticky white top nav: logo left, six dropdown items, search field with keyboard shortcut hint, language picker, Log in ghost button, teal Sign up pill-ish button. Heroes are mostly centered headline + subcopy + single teal CTA; home hero is split with left-aligned headline and clustered blob characters right. Section rhythm is long and modular: centered H2 intro, then a card grid (2-3 col), then a full-width dark feature band with product screenshot, repeating. The developer/integrations page inverts entirely to a dark-teal canvas with circuit-line motifs. Every page ends with the same black pre-footer band ("We believe motion should be accessible") with app-store badges, then a dense multi-column dark footer. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; content column stays fairly narrow on text-heavy pages (case studies read like articles).

## Components
Buttons: solid teal fill, white label, ~8px radius, no shadow; secondary is thin-outlined ghost on white, white-outlined ghost on dark; small tertiary text links in teal. Cards: white with 1px light-gray border OR flat saturated fills (yellow, coral, teal, blue) for stat/quote cards, 12-16px radius, essentially shadowless. Community members shown as circular photos on mint (#D9F6EF) discs; hero evangelists get tall arch-top cards in saturated pink/yellow/purple/green. FAQ accordions: bordered rounded rows with chevrons on white or light-gray canvas. Pricing: three bordered plan cards, middle plan highlighted with teal top border and teal CTA, enterprise CTA in black; long checkmark comparison table below. Upload dropzone (tools page): dashed 1px border, centered icon, teal Browse button. Code blocks in dark rounded panels with tab rows.

## Signature
- Teal (#00C1A2) as the single interactive color everywhere, with yellow/coral/blue reserved for flat stat and showcase cards holding oversized numerals ("45%", "10x", "600%")
- Alternating white and near-black full-bleed bands, including the constant black "We believe motion should be accessible" pre-footer on every page
- Circular photos on pastel-mint discs and arch-topped colorful hero cards for community/people content
- Playful gradient blob characters and small line-art spot illustrations sprinkled through otherwise restrained layouts
- Dark-teal developer subsection with circuit-board line art, a distinct sub-theme within the same system

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the base UI strictly white/neutral and route all playfulness into content cards, illustrations, and stat blocks
- Use one teal for every primary CTA and link; render secondary actions as thin ghost outlines
- Alternate white sections with full-bleed black or dark-teal bands to pace long pages
- Show real product screenshots inside dark rounded panels as section centerpieces
- Put big bold numerals on flat yellow/coral/teal cards for proof points

Don't:
- Use gradients on buttons or backgrounds — fills are flat; gradients appear only inside mascot illustrations
- Add drop shadows to cards; separation comes from 1px borders and background shifts
- Mix multiple CTA colors on one page (black enterprise CTA on pricing is the lone sanctioned exception)
- Let accent colors tint typography — headings and body stay near-black on light, white on dark
- Round buttons to full pills or crank card radii past ~16px; the system is rounded but not bubbly
