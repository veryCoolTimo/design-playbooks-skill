## Overview
A warm minimal-light SaaS look: near-white warm-gray canvas, near-black ink, and a single loud yellow used almost exclusively for CTAs. A chunky rounded serif carries every headline while a plain grotesque handles everything else. Hand-drawn arrows and script annotations ("We eat our own dog food") plus a lightning-bolt mascot add a friendly, scrappy tone on top of an otherwise restrained layout.

## Layout
Centered top nav with icon-prefixed dropdown items and a yellow "Get started" pill at the right. Heroes are centered, headline-first, with short subcopy and a yellow CTA; the home hero scatters tilted screenshot/testimonial cards around the heading. Sections alternate as single-column centered blocks with generous vertical whitespace; product and use-case pages run long vertical stacks of full-width bordered panels (one integration or step per panel) with annotated product screenshots. Pricing uses a classic 3-card row with the middle Pro card tinted lavender. Every page closes with an oversized full-width yellow CTA block, then a small FAQ accordion (pricing/home) and a multi-column footer with icon-prefixed links.

## Components
Buttons: yellow (#F6CB5C) fill, dark ink label, arrow suffix, thin dark border, small radius on nav/inline sizes; the page-end CTA is a giant yellow slab with a thick ~3px black border, large radius, and soft drop shadow. Secondary buttons are white/light with thin gray borders ("Contact us", "Connect"). Cards: white on the gray canvas, 1px light borders, 12-16px radius, minimal or no shadow; the highlighted pricing tier gets a lavender fill and violet border. Feature checklist rows use filled dark check dots; Pro-only items carry tiny bordered badge icons. FAQ rows are full-width white bars with plus toggles. Small tags/toggles (Yearly/Monthly, category chips) are pills. Product screenshots sit inside bordered panels with hand-drawn arrow callouts labeling UI parts.

## Signature
- Loud single-accent yellow reserved for CTAs, echoed by the lightning-bolt logo mark; the oversized black-bordered "Get started →" slab ends every page.
- Fat rounded serif headlines against a quiet warm-gray canvas, sometimes underlined or paired with inline emoji-style icons.
- Hand-drawn marker arrows and handwriting annotations pointing at prices, logos, and screenshot details.
- Lavender (#E4DDFB) as the sanctioned secondary tint: Pro pricing card, lightning icon tiles, and highlight states in product shots.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep yellow strictly for primary CTAs and the logo; use lavender tints for emphasis surfaces like the featured pricing tier.
- Set every headline in the heavy rounded serif and drop immediately to a plain grotesque for body and UI labels.
- Add at least one handwritten annotation with a curved arrow per page to keep the human, playful voice.
- End long pages with the giant black-bordered yellow CTA followed by the "Start for free. No card required." microcopy.
- Frame product screenshots in white bordered panels on the gray canvas and label them with callout arrows instead of captions.

**Don't**
- Don't introduce gradients, dark sections, or photography backgrounds — the whole site stays flat light gray/white.
- Don't use yellow for links, icons, or decorative fills; secondary actions stay white with thin gray borders.
- Don't apply heavy shadows to cards — depth comes from 1px borders; only the mega-CTA gets a visible shadow.
- Don't set body copy or nav in the serif; it appears in headlines and the wordmark only.
- Don't crowd sections — each idea gets its own full-width panel with wide vertical breathing room.
