## Overview
Loud color-block maximalism: full-bleed sections alternate between electric royal blue, hot orange, cream, and white, separated by wavy scalloped edges. Giant condensed all-caps headlines dominate each section, with playful 3D renders (keycaps, toy rocket, retro computer, shipping box) floating over the type. Confetti-like geometric doodles and halftone dot fields fill negative space. It reads as a design-agency toybox — energetic, retro-pop, unafraid of clashing brights.

## Layout
Minimal top bar: small wordmark left, hamburger menu right — no visible link row even on desktop. Heroes are typographic: a single massive word (MAKE A SPLASH / ABOUT / PACKAGES) at near-viewport width with a 3D object physically overlapping the letters. Sections are full-width color slabs in alternating hues, joined by wavy/scalloped dividers rather than straight cuts. Content inside sections sits on a two-column rhythm (image or big display word on one side, paragraph + list on the other). Feature and spec lists use thin horizontal rules in two-column label/description tables. Repeated stacked outline text ("WORK THE SYSTEM" x4) is used as a full section background. Footer is a blue slab with three columns: brand blurb, navigation, contact/legal.

## Components
Buttons: solid-fill pills, small (compact padding), no border or shadow; orange fill on light sections ("Let's Collaborate"), pink-red fill on blue sections ("Start the Conversation"), teal used for secondary ("Help my Brand"); white or dark label. Cards: package-selector cards are near-square with small radius, 1px blue border on white, selected state flips to solid orange fill with white text — no shadows anywhere. Lists/tables: thin blue hairline rules, blue label column left, small description right, occasional check-circle icons. Inputs not shown. Social icons are simple outlined glyphs in the footer.

## Signature
- Wavy scalloped edges between every color-block section instead of straight seams
- Viewport-width condensed all-caps headlines with 3D toy-like renders overlapping the letterforms
- Electric blue + hot orange + cream tri-color scheme with pink/teal/yellow confetti accents
- Repeated/stacked display text as texture (outline + filled variants of the same phrase) and halftone dot patterns bleeding in from corners

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate full-bleed section backgrounds (blue / cream / white / orange) and join them with wavy dividers
- Set headlines huge, condensed, all caps, and let a playful 3D object or sticker overlap the type
- Use blue as the working text color on light backgrounds (headings, body, hairlines all blue-family)
- Keep buttons as small solid pills in orange/pink/teal with zero border and zero shadow
- Scatter small geometric squiggles, zigzags, and halftone dot fields into empty corners

Don't:
- Use drop shadows, gradients, or glassmorphism — every surface here is flat solid color
- Default to neutral gray text; grays barely exist outside 3D render props
- Round cards heavily — containers stay near-square while only buttons go pill
- Add a traditional multi-link navbar; the site hides nav behind a hamburger at all sizes
- Let sections meet with straight horizontal edges when a wave/scallop transition fits
