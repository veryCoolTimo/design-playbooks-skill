## Overview
A soft lavender-gray canvas dressed up as an infinite whiteboard: real product screenshots, sticky-note callouts, and multiplayer cursor tags are scattered across the page as if the landing page itself were a Kosmik board. Hand-drawn, stippled ink doodles (rocket, Saturn, moon, cone, office chair) float in the margins as the sole illustration language. One warm orange CTA is the only saturated brand color; everything else is content imagery.

## Layout
Sticky-feel top bar: bold sans wordmark left, five plain text links plus a small orange "Download" button right. Hero is centered: light serif headline ("Your Desk, Reimagined" with an italic flourish), a small CTA, a hand-written-style annotation with an arrow, then a large floating app screenshot flanked by ink doodles. Sections alternate between conventional centered heading + short paragraph blocks and canvas-style collages where screenshots, PDFs, and image cards overlap at slight offsets with soft radial color glows (blue, pink, green) behind them. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; a thin dashed rule separates the four-column footer.

## Components
Buttons: small orange (#F2662D) rectangles with slightly rounded corners and white label — used sparingly (nav, hero, one mid-page, one pre-footer). Cards: white-framed app screenshots and image tiles with medium radius and soft diffuse drop shadows, often overlapped or rotated a few degrees. Sticky-note callouts: small white rounded rectangles with a plain sans caption and a bold mini-heading, sometimes with a highlighter-swipe label (lavender pill behind text). Multiplayer cursor chips: tiny colored pills with user names (green, blue, purple, yellow, red) attached to arrow cursors. A dashed-border selection rectangle appears as a canvas metaphor. No visible form inputs.

## Signature
- Landing page staged as a live canvas: overlapping screenshots, drop shadows, named cursors, and a dashed selection box.
- Stippled/etched black-ink space doodles (rocket, planets) as the only illustration set.
- Light serif display type against a cool lavender-gray background with a single orange CTA.
- Soft pastel radial glows (blue/pink/green halos) behind screenshot clusters instead of section background changes.

## Do
- Keep the background a single cool off-white (#F1F0F6) across the whole page and let content imagery carry the color.
- Reserve the orange fill strictly for CTAs; keep nav links and headings plain ink.
- Stage feature demos as overlapping, softly shadowed screenshot collages with named cursor chips and sticky-note captions.
- Use light-weight serif for headlines and a quiet grotesque for body; add occasional italic words for emphasis.
- Sprinkle small monochrome stipple-ink doodles in negative space, never behind text.

## Don't
- Don't add colored section bands or dark-mode blocks; the page never breaks its pale canvas.
- Don't use gradient or pill-shaped mega-CTAs; buttons stay small, flat, and squarish.
- Don't render illustrations in color or flat-vector style; the doodle language is black stipple linework only.
- Don't align screenshots into a rigid grid; slight rotation and overlap are part of the canvas metaphor.
- Don't introduce heavy borders or hard shadows; cards float on soft, diffuse blur.
