## Overview
A playful, sticker-book take on a dev-tool landing page: crisp white and light-gray sections, near-black type and CTAs, and hand-drawn cartoon shapes (red rectangles, blue squares, green bars, orange triangles) with googly eyes and thick wobbly black outlines scattered as mascots. The palette stays restrained — color lives almost entirely in the illustrated characters, while UI chrome is monochrome. Tone is whimsical but the structure underneath is a conventional, tidy SaaS layout.

## Layout
Sticky-feeling top nav: outlined logo lockup left, four center links (Problem / Solution / Pricing / FAQ), ghost "Sign in" plus solid black "Get started" right. Hero is centered: small "Backed by" pill badge, huge two-line serif headline, a parenthetical gray sub-line, single black CTA; mascot shape clusters anchor the top-left and right corners. Sections alternate white and light gray (#F4F4F4) full-bleed bands. Rhythm: 3-column numbered problem cards, a two-column solution block (accordion list left, product screenshot right), 3+2 pricing card grid with a monthly/yearly toggle pill, FAQ with mascot cluster left and accordion right, then a near-black newsletter band, and a green (#54B16B) footer dominated by a giant outlined "Sparkles" wordmark. Generous whitespace throughout; content sits in a comfortable centered container.

## Components
Buttons: solid near-black (#171717) fill with white label, ~10px radius, no shadow; secondary buttons are pale gray (#F4F4F4) with dark text and a hairline border. Cards: white, large ~16px radius, hairline light border, essentially shadowless; each carries one cartoon shape mascot in a corner. Accordions: plain rows with hairline dividers and chevron carets. Pills: segmented monthly/yearly toggle and the hero badge are full pills. Newsletter input: dark rounded field with placeholder inside a black band, paired gray Subscribe button. Featured pricing tier is marked only by swapping its button to solid black.

## Signature
- Hand-drawn geometric shape mascots (red, blue, green, orange) with googly eyes and thick imperfect black outlines, used as corner decorations on cards and in hero/FAQ clusters.
- Ultra-heavy rounded serif display type against an otherwise plain grotesque body — the headline itself is the decoration.
- Monochrome UI (black buttons, white/gray bands) so the cartoon accents carry all the color.
- Giant outlined wordmark filling the green footer like a sticker.

## Do / Don't
- Do keep all CTAs near-black on light backgrounds; reserve red/blue/green/orange strictly for the illustrated shapes.
- Do give every card one small mascot shape in a corner rather than icons or photos.
- Do alternate #FFFFFF and #F4F4F4 section bands to pace the page.
- Do set headlines in a heavy rounded serif and everything else in a plain grotesque; use parentheses and lowercase asides for the playful voice.
- Do use hairline borders instead of drop shadows on cards and secondary buttons.
- Don't use gradients, glassmorphism, or glow effects — surfaces here are flat and matte.
- Don't color buttons or links with the accent hues; they never appear as UI fills.
- Don't make buttons full pills — pills are only for badges and toggles; buttons stay at ~10px radius.
- Don't render the mascots as clean vector shapes; the wobbly hand-drawn outline and eyes are the point.
- Don't crowd sections — each band holds one idea with wide margins.
