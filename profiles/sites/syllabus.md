## Overview
A warm-paper minimal-light system: cream canvas, near-black indigo ink, and a pale butter-yellow CTA, punctuated by full-bleed bands in deep teal-green and dark indigo. All product imagery is schematic line-art — wireframe webpages, bar charts, and donut charts drawn in thin ink strokes with yellow, maroon, and salmon fills — connected by dotted flowchart lines. The mood is editorial and diagrammatic rather than glossy SaaS.

## Layout
Slim top nav: wordmark left, two text links plus a small dark-indigo "Join Waitlist" button right. Home hero is asymmetric two-column (left-aligned H1 + paragraph + yellow CTA, wireframe illustration cluster right); about hero is centered with illustrations flanking below. Body is a long zig-zag rhythm of alternating two-column feature sections (heading + short copy + bullet list on one side, line-art diagram on the other) with generous vertical whitespace. Full-width colored interludes break the cream: a deep-green statement band and a dark-indigo section with 3-column feature grid; both use clipped/angled corners. Footer: link columns on cream, then a dark-indigo strip with an "Early access" cream panel and a repeated yellow CTA. A dotted-grid texture strip separates body from footer.

## Components
Buttons: primary CTA is a butter-yellow (#FBE59A) rectangle with a thin dark outline, one clipped corner, a hard offset dark shadow (no blur), and an uppercase monospace label ("JOIN WAITLIST"); nav variant is small, dark indigo fill with light text, square corners. Cards/panels: no rounded corners anywhere — outlined boxes with 1px ink strokes; the dark section uses an outlined quote panel with a clipped corner. Bullet lists use small yellow square/dot markers. No soft shadows, no gradients, no glassmorphism; depth comes only from hard offset shadows and layered outlined shapes. No inputs visible (waitlist links out).

## Signature
- Butter-yellow notched-corner CTA with hard offset shadow and uppercase monospace label — repeated identically in hero, mid-page, and footer.
- Wireframe line-art illustrations of webpages and charts (ink outlines, yellow/maroon/salmon fills) linked by dotted connector lines with small yellow nodes, like an annotated diagram.
- Cream #FFFCF7 canvas alternating with full-bleed deep-green (#19605D) and near-black-indigo (#0D012A) bands that have clipped/angled corners.
- Yellow highlighter treatment over key phrases in headline copy on the green band.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the canvas warm cream (#FFFCF7) with near-black indigo (#0D012A) text; reserve pure white for illustration card fills only.
- Style every primary CTA the same way: yellow fill, thin ink outline, clipped corner, hard un-blurred offset shadow, uppercase monospace label.
- Illustrate features as schematic wireframes and charts joined by dotted connector lines, not screenshots or photos.
- Alternate left/right two-column feature sections down the page, each with a short heading, brief copy, and a yellow-markered bullet list.
- Use the deep-green and dark-indigo full-bleed bands sparingly (one or two per page) as rhythm breaks, with clipped corners.

**Don't**
- Don't round corners on buttons or cards — the language is sharp rectangles plus angled notches.
- Don't use soft blurred drop shadows, gradients, or glossy 3D imagery; depth is flat, hard-edged offset only.
- Don't introduce a saturated blue/purple SaaS primary; color accents stay in the teal-green / maroon / salmon / yellow family.
- Don't use real product screenshots or stock photography; everything visual stays in the line-art wireframe idiom.
- Don't center every section — the layout depends on asymmetric zig-zag columns, with centered headings only for major section intros.
