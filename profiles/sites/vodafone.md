## Overview
A campaign-poster web presence: full-bleed editorial photos (tight-cropped portraits, city scenes) topped by enormous 126-144px uppercase headlines in the brand's own sans at weight 800, alternating with quiet white content bands. One accent only — Vodafone red #e60000 — against near-black ink and grayscale. The tension between shouting 800-weight uppercase displays and gentle 300-weight sub-headlines defines the typographic voice.

## Layout
Dark ink (#25282b) top nav with white links, logo left. Hero band fills the viewport with photography plus a top-left headline overlay; a white content band always follows, then a dark footer — the page cycles dark/light/dark with no gray mid-bands. Wide near-edge container capped ~1400px with 32px gutters; story-card grids run 2-up on desktop, 1-up on mobile. 4px spacing base; tight copy clusters inside cards, generous air between them.

## Components
Three button variants, all 60px pills at 18px/400 labels: red fill + white text (primary), white with red border and red text, white with ink border and ink text; plus borderless white circular icon buttons for media controls. Cards are flat white with 6px corners and 16:9 thumbnails — zero drop shadows anywhere; depth comes from flipping between ink and white bands, or a 1px hairline border. Inputs: white, 1px ink border, 6px radius. Chips: light gray #f2f2f2 pills (32px radius) with bold 14px labels. A red square "speechmark" logo orb (quote-mark glyph, 6px corners) serves as the sole decorative anchor.

## Signature
- Colossal uppercase weight-800 headlines at ~144px with -1px tracking floating over editorial photography
- Exactly one accent: #e60000 red, spent only on CTA pills and the speechmark orb
- 60px pill geometry on every button — square CTAs never appear
- Shadow-free flat surfaces; elevation via dark-band / light-band polarity flips

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set hero copy uppercase, weight 800, with -1px letter-spacing at 100px+ sizes
- Keep red exclusively for conversion pills and the logo orb; everything else is ink + grayscale
- Alternate ink hero, white content, ink footer for page rhythm
- Drop to weight 300 (40-48px) for calmer section headlines after the hero shout
- Overlay headlines on real photography — portraits, streets, infrastructure

Don't:
- Add a second accent color or gradients — the palette is red, near-black, and neutrals
- Use sentence case or loose tracking on hero displays
- Square off CTA corners or shrink pills below the 60px radius
- Put soft shadows under cards; use surface contrast or a 1px hairline instead
- Swap in illustration — photography is the only decorative system
