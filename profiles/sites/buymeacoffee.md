## Overview
Warm, friendly minimalism on a white (sometimes cream #FAF5EB) canvas with near-black ink and one loud signature yellow. The tone is deliberately un-corporate: handwritten script logo and page titles, floating testimonial cards, coffee-cup mascot, emoji hearts. Everything else stays quiet — flat surfaces, soft gray fills, almost no shadows — so the yellow CTA and playful UI mock-ups carry the personality.

## Layout
Centered single-column layout throughout. Nav: sparse left links (FAQ, Wall of ❤️, Resources), script-logo centered, search pill plus Log in and yellow pill Sign up on the right. Home hero is a centered stacked headline with one yellow pill CTA and small tilted creator-testimonial cards floating at both edges. Sections alternate rhythm: tiny uppercase letter-spaced eyebrow label, big two-line heading (second line often gray), short paragraph, then a product-UI collage instead of photography. About and FAQ are narrow essay-width columns (~600-700px) with huge whitespace; About even uses a drop cap and a handwritten sign-off. Wall of Love groups social proof into horizontal card carousels per platform (TikTok, Twitter, YouTube) on a cream background.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped; primary is solid yellow #FFDD00 with dark text and no shadow; secondary is white pill with thin dark outline (FAQ chips); in-mock-up CTAs appear as coral-red or light-blue pills. Cards: white, 12-16px radius, hairline border or very soft diffuse shadow, often slightly rotated for a hand-placed feel. FAQ accordions are light-gray (#F2F2F2) rounded blocks with bold question text and a chevron — no borders. Search input is a gray pill with a magnifier icon. Checklist bullets are thin circled checkmarks. Footer is a single quiet row of small links plus social icons.

## Signature
- Signature yellow #FFDD00 used almost exclusively for the primary CTA and small logo accents — never as a section background wash on white pages
- Cursive handwritten script for the logo and editorial page titles ("Our Story", "Wall of ❤️"), paired with a sturdy rounded sans everywhere else
- Slightly rotated, scattered testimonial/product cards that make the page feel hand-assembled rather than grid-locked
- Product story told through embedded fake-UI collages (support widget, membership tiers, shop card) instead of screenshots or photos

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep one yellow pill CTA per viewport and let it be the only saturated element on the white canvas
- Use two-line centered headings with the second line in mid-gray to create hierarchy without size changes
- Reserve the script face for page titles and the logo; set all UI and body copy in the rounded sans
- Swap the canvas to cream (#FAF5EB) for social-proof/community pages to warm them up
- Show features as playful in-page UI mock-up collages with small floating stat chips

Don't:
- Add heavy drop shadows or gradients — surfaces here are flat with hairline borders at most
- Use yellow for links or body accents; text links stay dark and bold instead
- Square off buttons — every button and input on the site is a full pill
- Set long headings in the script face or use it below title level; it is a display-only voice
- Crowd sections; each section gets one idea, one visual, and generous vertical padding
