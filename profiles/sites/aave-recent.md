## Overview
A soft, product-led minimal-light language built on a pale lavender canvas with near-black ink and one periwinkle-purple brand hue doing all the accent work. Long homepage alternates full-width light sections with charcoal/plum dark "shell" sections, each rounded like a giant card. Headlines mix a clean grotesque with italic serif words for a single flourish per section; everything else is restrained and airy.

## Layout
Fixed top nav on white: left logo, centered text links (Products/Solutions/Developers/Resources/About), one black pill CTA "Use Aave" at right. Hero is fully centered: small icon+eyebrow ("Aave App"), giant two-tone headline, one-line subhead, dual pill buttons, then a large device mockup (three phones) bleeding below the fold. This eyebrow → headline → sub → button-pair → product-mockup rhythm repeats for every major section (Aave Pro, Aave Kit, Trusted by Default). Sections sit inside huge rounded-corner containers (~40px) with visible gutters to the viewport edge, alternating light lavender and near-black backgrounds. Mid-page uses a left-aligned heading + right-aligned "Learn More" pill, followed by a 3-column card row (markets, partner stats) with a horizontal carousel and chevron paging on the partner row. Generous vertical whitespace throughout; subtle noise/texture on lavender backgrounds.

## Components
Buttons: always pills. Primary = solid periwinkle (#8F80F3) with white label; secondary = translucent lavender-tinted pill with purple label; on dark sections primary flips to solid white with dark label and secondary to translucent dark gray; nav CTA is a solid black pill. No borders or heavy shadows on buttons. Cards: large-radius (16-24px) with 1px faint borders; on dark sections they are slightly lighter translucent panels with purple category eyebrows, white titles, muted body, and rows of small pill-shaped asset tags with token icons; on light sections partner cards are white-lavender with app-icon, big stat numeral, and muted caption. Product mockups (phone screens, pro.aave.com browser frame) are rendered as real UI with data tables, search inputs, and sidebar nav. Soft diffuse shadows only under mockups and 3D cube illustrations.

## Signature
- Two-tone headlines: upright grotesque plus italic serif accent words tinted periwinkle ("Savings for *Everyone*", "*Trusted* by Default").
- Giant rounded section shells (~40px) alternating pale lavender and near-black plum, with gutters exposing the page edge.
- Everything pill-shaped: CTAs, asset tags, filter chips, the nav button.
- Single-hue discipline: one periwinkle purple carries eyebrows, italics, buttons, icons, and 3D brand cubes; token icons are the only multicolor elements.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Repeat the section formula: icon eyebrow, centered two-tone headline, one-line subhead, two pill buttons, oversized product mockup.
- Pair every solid periwinkle primary pill with a quieter translucent secondary pill; invert to white-on-dark inside dark sections.
- Wrap major sections in very large rounded containers and alternate lavender-light with charcoal-plum dark.
- Reserve the italic serif for one or two accent words per headline, tinted purple.
- Show real product UI (phone screens, dashboard tables) as the hero visual instead of abstract art.

Don't:
- Introduce a second accent hue; the palette is one purple plus neutrals (token icons excepted).
- Use square or small-radius buttons anywhere - every interactive element is a pill.
- Add hard borders, heavy drop shadows, or gradient buttons; depth comes from soft diffuse shadows under mockups only.
- Left-align hero content; heroes are strictly centered (left alignment is reserved for mid-section heading + card rows).
- Fill whitespace with decoration; sections breathe with large empty margins and at most a faint noise texture.
