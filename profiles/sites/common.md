## Overview
A stark white editorial canvas punctuated by soft airbrushed gradient glows in primary-school colors: blue, pink, yellow, red. Black is used decisively — solid black CTA buttons, near-black headlines — while color arrives only as diffuse radial halos behind product cards and badges, hard confetti dots, and crayon-style illustration. The result is playful and crafty (kid-drawing portraits, dot-matrix graphics) but held together by rigid typographic discipline and monospace system labels.

## Layout
Top nav is minimal: small logo left, four uppercase monospace links, one black rectangular "Explore Common" button pinned right. Heroes are either centered (home: headline, subline, single black CTA over drifting gradient blobs) or left-aligned editorial (about, ecosystem) with an eyebrow label above the headline. Sections alternate: centered oversized headline, then left-text/right-visual feature rows where floating UI cards sit on gradient glows or blue dot-matrix fields. Community/partner grids are 3-4 columns of rule-divided list items, not boxed cards. Every page ends with the same signature footer: a full-bleed flat blue band filled edge-to-edge with a giant white bubble "COMMON" wordmark, monospace links below. Whitespace is extremely generous — sections breathe with tall empty gaps.

## Components
Buttons: solid black rectangles with barely-rounded corners, white uppercase monospace text, no shadow, no gradient. Cards: mostly "anti-cards" — logo avatar plus name over a thin horizontal rule, description text, "READ MORE" monospace link over another rule; no border, no fill, no shadow. Floating product-UI mockups are small white rounded cards (medium radius) with soft drop shadows, set against radial gradient halos. Testimonials pair a small avatar/label column with large quote text, divided by hairlines. Inputs in mockups are simple bordered fields with small radius. Metadata everywhere (member counts, roles) is tiny uppercase monospace.

## Signature
- Diffuse radial gradient "glow spots" in blue/pink/yellow/red floating on pure white, standing in for photography
- Giant flat-blue footer with an edge-to-edge white bubble COMMON wordmark on every page
- Two-voice typography: soft geometric grotesque display vs. strict uppercase monospace for all UI chrome, labels, and buttons
- Handmade art direction: crayon portraits, confetti circles, and dot-matrix pixel graphics instead of stock imagery

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas pure white and let color exist only as soft radial glows, flat accent dots, and the blue footer band
- Set every button, nav link, eyebrow, and micro-label in uppercase monospace with wide tracking
- Use solid black rectangles for all CTAs — one primary action per hero, no secondary button styles
- Separate list/card content with thin horizontal rules instead of boxes, borders, or shadows
- Reserve heavy scale contrast: oversized display headlines against tiny monospace metadata

Don't:
- Add borders, background fills, or shadows to content cards — only floating UI mockups get a white card with soft shadow
- Use color for buttons or headlines; accents stay decorative (glows, dots, badges), never functional
- Introduce dark sections or gray section backgrounds — the only non-white band is the blue footer
- Round corners beyond a few pixels on buttons, or use pill shapes
- Replace the playful hand-drawn/dot-matrix visuals with photography or slick 3D renders
