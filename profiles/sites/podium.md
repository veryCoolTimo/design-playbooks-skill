## Overview
A stark black-and-white sports production portfolio that flips polarity as you scroll: pure white hero, pitch-black work and contact sections. Almost all type is heavy geometric all-caps; imagery does the color work through gritty film-toned video stills. A recurring 3D "fossil rock" object carrying the running-figure logo acts as the brand totem.

## Layout
Fixed top bar: blob-mark logo + wordmark left, three sparse all-caps links (WORK / ABOUT / CONTACT) right — persists over both white and black sections. Hero is nearly empty white space with a kinetic blob-masked video collage center, a two-line bold statement pinned bottom-left, and "SCROLL DOWN" bottom-right. Work index is a loose, scattered masonry on black: mixed-size stills placed asymmetrically with big caption + tiny right-aligned year/client meta. About section uses white canvas with dashed contour/pattern linework behind a large centered statement, plus two offset flat gray panels (SERVICES, CLIENTS) as lists. Footer/contact is a full-viewport black stage: giant 3D rock centered, tiny tagline pair at the far left/right edges, huge centered CTA, dotted-line divider above a one-row footer.

## Components
Effectively buttonless: the main CTA is oversized white underlined display text ("WORK WITH US") on black — no fill, no border, no radius. Nav links are plain small bold caps. Project "cards" are bare rectangular images with zero border, shadow, or radius; caption sits outside the image, meta stacked in tiny caps at the right edge. Info panels are flat light-gray rectangles (#e8e6e3-ish) with a small caps label and a bold caps list — no borders or shadows. Footer links are small underlined caps.

## Signature
- Polarity flip: white minimal hero and about vs. full-black work and contact sections, same nav floating over both.
- Organic blob mask revealing a moving video collage inside an otherwise empty white hero.
- Recurring photoreal 3D rock with the fossilized runner logo carved into it, floating on black.
- Scattered, overlapping asymmetric image grid with tiny right-aligned year/client meta in condensed caps.
- Everything set in one extra-bold geometric all-caps voice, from 12px labels to viewport-scale headlines.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Set every headline and label in heavy geometric all-caps; vary only size, never typeface or case.
- Alternate full white and full black viewport-height sections; let the scroll flip the polarity.
- Place images loose and asymmetric with overlap; keep captions outside the frame with tiny stacked meta.
- Use underlined display-size text as the CTA instead of a filled button.
- Reserve all color for photographic/video content; keep UI chrome strictly black and white.

**Don't**
- Add filled or rounded buttons, pills, borders, or drop shadows — nothing on this site has them.
- Introduce brand accent colors into the UI; the palette is monochrome plus photo tones.
- Snap the work grid into a uniform symmetric card layout with equal gutters.
- Use lowercase or light-weight type for headings or navigation.
- Round image corners or frame images in cards — media sits raw and edge-sharp on the canvas.
