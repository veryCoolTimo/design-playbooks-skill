## Overview
A near-black, type-first site where the wordmark itself is the hero: "supertape" is set edge-to-edge across the full viewport in a thin geometric sans, and repeats at giant scale in the footer. Body sections alternate huge light-weight sans headings with uppercase monospace utility text, punctuated by saturated full-bleed color blocks (yellow, orange, sky blue) framing device mockups. Violet is the single action color; small candy-colored circles (sticker badges, platform icons) provide playfulness against the dark ground.

## Layout
Sparse uppercase-mono top nav (logo left, TEMPLATES / PLATFORMS / PRICING spread across full width, GET STARTED + LOGIN stacked right; active page marked with a violet dot). Home hero is the viewport-wide wordmark followed directly by a heading + gray paragraph — no hero image, no hero button. Sections are full-width horizontal bands separated by thin 1px rules; a recurring two-column pattern puts a big heading left and content (violet CTA link, FAQ accordion, paragraph) right. Product shots live inside full-bleed colored rectangles or dark bands with floating circular platform icons. Every page ends with the same "Get started / Build a website in minutes →" rule-lined band and a giant footer wordmark.

## Components
No filled buttons anywhere: CTAs are large violet text links with a thin arrow (→), and utility CTAs are small uppercase mono links in bordered rectangles ("SEE OUR TEMPLATES"). Platform cards on a slightly lighter dark panel with ~6px corners, each holding a large colored circle icon and a mono checklist (PROFILE / MUSIC / MERCH…) with sync or × glyphs per row, separated by hairlines. FAQ rows are 1px-rule accordions with mono question text and a chevron. Feature lists use small green dot bullets with hairline row dividers. Badges are rotated circular stickers (orange, green "25% LESS", blue). Boxed uppercase-mono tags ("WHAT YOU GET") use thin square outlines. Template colors are shown as raw hex chips (#FF3D00, #353232, #7B44FC).

## Signature
- Full-bleed lowercase wordmark as both hero and footer, set in a thin geometric sans at viewport width.
- Dual type system: large light geometric sans for headings/prose vs. uppercase monospace for nav, labels, FAQs, and legal.
- 1px horizontal rules structuring the whole page into bands; violet text-link CTAs with arrows instead of buttons.
- Saturated flat color blocks (yellow, orange, sky blue) and circular candy-colored icons/stickers popping off the near-black canvas.

## Do / Don't
- Do set headline type very large and light-weight; let scale, not boldness, carry hierarchy.
- Do use uppercase monospace for all secondary text: nav items, section tags, checklists, FAQ questions, footer.
- Do render CTAs as violet text links with a thin arrow, underlined or rule-bounded, on the dark canvas.
- Do separate sections with thin 1px gray rules and keep the two-column heading-left / content-right rhythm.
- Do frame screenshots and device mockups inside full-bleed flat color rectangles (yellow, orange, light blue).
- Don't use filled or pill-shaped buttons; the site has zero solid button fills.
- Don't introduce gradients, shadows, or glassmorphism; surfaces are flat panels one step lighter than the black canvas.
- Don't use violet as a background or large surface; it appears only as text/link color and small dots.
- Don't round corners past ~8px on rectangles; only icons and sticker badges are circular.
- Don't add hero imagery above the fold on the home page; the oversized wordmark plus plain text is the hero.
