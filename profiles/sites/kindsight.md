## Overview
An editorial, warm-earth-tone SaaS look: cream paper canvas, elegant serif display type with italic emphasis words, and full-bleed color blocking in dark olive, deep maroon, chartreuse, and salmon pink. It reads more like a boutique magazine or a heritage brand than a CRM vendor — humanity-forward photography of real people, hand-drawn squiggle line illustrations, and terracotta CTAs keep it soft and personal.

## Layout
Sticky-feel top nav: quirky sans wordmark left, five center links, outlined Login plus filled terracotta "Get a Demo" right. Heroes alternate per page: dark olive or maroon full-bleed blocks with chartreuse/orange serif headlines (home, solutions, company) versus quiet cream heroes with a huge centered serif line (pricing "Find the best plan for you.", resources "Resources Hub"). Body sections alternate image-left/text-right zig-zag rows with generous whitespace, then 3-column card grids. Section rhythm is punctuated by full-width color slabs — pink testimonial blocks, olive CTA bands, lime quote panels — and thin hand-drawn looping lines that trail across section edges. Footer is a full-bleed dark slab (olive or maroon, varies by page) with a chartreuse or orange wordmark and a pill email-signup input.

## Components
Buttons: primary CTA is a terracotta/salmon fill (#DF8462) with white label and small 4-6px radius; secondary is a 1px outlined rect in the same hue; on dark heroes the CTA flips to a chartreuse pill; large tertiary CTAs ("Book a demo today!", "Load More") are black-outlined serif-label pills on cream. Cards: white, 12-16px radius, no visible border, extremely subtle or no shadow, roomy padding; pricing cards use hairline rules to separate title/price/feature list, orange sub-labels, and checkmark feature rows. Testimonial slabs are flat salmon-pink or lime rounded rectangles with large serif quotes and a hand-drawn scribble. Resource cards pair squared photo tops with orange category eyebrows and arrow "Read More" links. Inputs: pill-shaped email field with embedded arrow submit button in footers.

## Signature
- Four-way earth palette (olive, maroon, chartreuse, salmon) used as full-bleed page-section blocking, not just accents
- Elegant serif headlines with a single word set in italic for emphasis ("incredible", "effortless", "mission")
- Hand-drawn single-line squiggle illustrations and underline circles woven between sections
- Flat pink/lime testimonial slabs with oversized serif pull quotes
- Terracotta CTA buttons that stay warm against both cream and dark backgrounds

## Do
- Set headlines in a high-contrast serif and italicize one emphasis word per headline
- Block entire sections in olive, maroon, or lime with cream type; return to cream canvas between them
- Use terracotta fills for primary CTAs on light sections and chartreuse on dark olive sections
- Add hand-drawn looping line accents and scribbles at section boundaries
- Keep cards white and borderless on cream, separated by hairline rules inside pricing cards

## Don't
- Don't use blue, purple, or any cool tech-SaaS hue — the palette is strictly warm and earthy
- Don't put drop shadows or heavy borders on cards; flat color and whitespace do the separation
- Don't set headlines in bold sans — display type is always serif, light-to-regular weight
- Don't use pure white (#FFF) as the page canvas; the base is warm cream with white reserved for cards
- Don't render illustrations in filled or 3D styles; only single-weight hand-drawn line art fits
