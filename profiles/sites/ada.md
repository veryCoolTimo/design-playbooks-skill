## Overview
Warm off-white canvas punctuated by full-bleed color-block sections: deep forest green, pale yellow, periwinkle blue, and a dark plum. Headlines are lowercase sentence-case grotesque with one word italicized for emphasis ("accelerate", "effortless", "whenever"). Blurred motion-photography strips serve as color fields behind quotes and stat tiles, giving the editorial pastel palette a kinetic, painterly texture. Product UI appears as small, crisp light-mode screenshot cards floating on these fields.

## Layout
Floating pill-shaped white nav bar detached from the page top, with logo left, dropdown links center-left, and a "Book a demo" pill CTA right. Heroes are asymmetric: left-aligned lowercase headline on a solid color field (yellow home, forest green platform/pricing, powder blue industries), sometimes split with a blurred-photo panel or an inline lead-gen form card (pricing). Section rhythm alternates canvas ↔ saturated full-bleed bands; logo marquees on thin strips separate major sections. Content grids: 4-up stat tiles, 2x2 customer-quote color blocks (each block a different accent color with photo strip, testimonial card, and pill CTA), vertical timeline with orange node dots on the dark plum section, and a hairline-divided 4-column case-study grid on white cards.

## Components
Buttons: text pill with a distinct black circular arrow button fused to its right end — the pill fill varies by context (periwinkle blue, pink, black, forest green) while the arrow circle stays dark. Cards: sharp or barely-rounded rectangles, no shadows, flat color fills or white on hairline-divided grids; stat tiles are solid accent-color squares with huge numerals top-left. Inputs: plain white rectangles with thin gray borders and small uppercase-ish labels (pricing form, footer newsletter with inline arrow-circle submit). Checklist rows use thin circled checkmarks. Quotes sit as white rounded chips over blurred photo backgrounds with a circular avatar.

## Signature
- Lowercase headlines with exactly one italicized emphasis word per heading.
- Pill CTA + attached black circle-arrow as a two-part unit, recolored per section.
- Blurred long-exposure photography used as abstract color texture behind quotes, stats, and screenshots.
- Giant pastel-pink lowercase "ada" wordmark filling the dark-green footer.
- Multi-color 2x2 testimonial blocks: each cell a different flat accent (ochre, green, yellow, pink) pairing a brand logo, stat, and quote card.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep headlines lowercase and italicize a single key word; center short section intros, left-align hero copy.
- Rotate section backgrounds through the fixed set: off-white, forest green #0B3B2C, pale yellow, powder blue, dark plum — never gradients.
- Pair every pill button with the fused black circular arrow; vary pill fill (blue, pink, green, black) to sit on its background.
- Use flat, shadowless cards with hairline dividers; let solid color fills do the separating.
- Insert thin logo-marquee strips between hero and first content section.

Don't:
- Don't round card corners past ~4px or add drop shadows — only buttons and quote chips are pill/rounded.
- Don't use stock photos literally; photography appears either motion-blurred as texture or as small candid editorial crops.
- Don't set headlines in bold caps or heavy weights — display type stays regular/medium and lowercase.
- Don't use one fixed CTA color; the black arrow circle is the constant, the pill fill is not.
- Don't put product screenshots edge-to-edge; they float as small framed cards over color or blurred-photo fields.
