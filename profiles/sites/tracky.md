## Overview
Playful cartoon-illustration landing page on a warm off-white paper canvas. A mascot dog (line-drawn, hand-inked feel) appears throughout — inside the headline, in product mockups, in the footer speech bubble. Heavy black ink typography, one vivid blue-violet CTA color, and scattered doodle stickers (thumbs-up, smileys, squiggle arrows, paper planes) give it a sketchbook energy while UI mockup cards stay crisp and product-like.

## Layout
Minimal nav: logo mark top-left, single "Early Access" pill top-right — no link list. Hero is centered, text-dominant: a huge black headline with the mascot illustration embedded mid-sentence and colored words ("be addicted to." in purple/blue) plus a pill CTA with a live timer chip beside it. Sections alternate: light canvas → full-width dark charcoal (#262420) rounded panels for product/"Coming soon" blocks → back to light. Mid-page shows tilted overlapping UI-card mockups connected by hand-drawn squiggle arrows to a 3-column feature row. Two-column pricing cards. Footer closes with an oversized repeating marquee "It's easy to Tracky" (gray with the brand word in yellow) and a slim pill-shaped footer bar.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped, solid #4a2cfe fill, white text, no border, flat (no shadow); disabled/coming-soon variant in pale lavender (#b9a8f7-ish) with white text. Cards: white UI-mockup cards with medium radius and soft diffuse shadows, often rotated a few degrees; feature tiles on light gray (#ecebe7) with small square icon chips in brand colors (blue, red, purple). Dark sections are giant rounded rectangles with white text and checkmark lists. Small "label" chips (STANDARD / TRACKING / TO-DO'S) as tiny uppercase bordered tags. Inputs in mockups: thin-bordered, small radius, quiet gray labels.

## Signature
- Hand-drawn mascot dog and doodle stickers (thumbs-up, smiley, squiggle arrows) scattered over an off-white paper background
- Single electric blue-violet (#4a2cfe) reserved for pills/CTAs against near-black ink and warm canvas
- Alternating rhythm of warm-light canvas and huge dark-charcoal rounded panels
- Oversized "It's easy to Tracky" text marquee before the footer, brand word highlighted in yellow

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas warm off-white (#f5f3ef), never pure white, so ink drawings read like paper
- Use one saturated blue-violet for every primary action, always in a pill shape
- Embed illustration inline with headline text and connect sections with hand-drawn squiggle arrows
- Alternate light sections with full-bleed dark #262420 rounded panels for feature groups
- Use small colored square icon chips (red, blue, purple) and tiny uppercase tag chips for labeling

Don't:
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients to buttons — CTAs are flat solid fills
- Don't introduce photography or 3D renders; imagery is line-art mascot + flat UI mockups only
- Don't use multiple CTA colors — red, yellow, and green exist only as sticker/icon accents
- Don't square off the buttons; pill radius is consistent everywhere from CTA to footer bar
- Don't fill the nav with links — the header stays logo + one pill and nothing else
