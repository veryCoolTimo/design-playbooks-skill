## Overview
A stark white, text-first page brought to life by loose black-ink doodle illustrations (hands, faces, squiggles, arrows) scattered in the margins like notebook marginalia. Hot magenta scribbles and highlighted words punch through an otherwise near-monochrome black-on-white system. The overall feel is Notion-adjacent: quiet UI chrome, dense readable copy, and a single saturated blue reserved for the create-form CTA.

## Layout
Slim single-row nav: wordmark left ("tally*" in a serif logotype), text links right, one small solid-blue "Create form" button. Home hero is centered: large bold headline, short subcopy, blue CTA, then a live embedded form demo in a bordered card; doodles float on both sides of the hero. Sections alternate between centered headline blocks and 2-3 column feature grids of small icon + title + paragraph cells; large product screenshots sit in light-bordered frames. Single-quote testimonials in italic serif punctuate the rhythm between sections. Pricing leads with a huge left-aligned headline ending in magenta words, followed by a 3-column checkmark feature list. Generous whitespace throughout; content column stays narrow (~900-1200px).

## Components
Buttons: small-radius (~5px) solid fills — blue (#0f70d9) for the primary "Create form" action in nav and hero, solid black for mid-page/pricing CTAs ("Sign up and upgrade", "Create form"); white text, no shadows, no gradients. Cards: white with hairline light-gray borders, 8-12px radius, flat (no drop shadows); one hero feature card is outlined in magenta with a hand-drawn wave illustration. Inputs in the embedded form demo are minimal: light gray hairline borders, small radius, quiet placeholder text. Links are either underlined black or colored magenta/purple. Checkmark lists use small monoline check icons. Footer is plain multi-column text links on white.

## Signature
- Hand-drawn black-ink doodles (faces, hands, stars, arrows) floating in page margins around otherwise flat, minimal content
- Hot magenta (#f71de9) used as a highlighter: scribbles over illustrations, colored words inside black headlines, magenta card outline
- Serif italic wordmark "tally*" and serif italic pull-quote testimonials contrasting a sans-serif everything-else
- Near-monochrome black/white base with exactly one functional color (blue) reserved for the primary CTA

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas pure white and let hairline gray borders, not shadows, define cards and frames
- Reserve the blue fill strictly for the primary "create" CTA; use solid black buttons for secondary conversion moments
- Color individual words in headlines magenta for emphasis instead of using badges or gradients
- Scatter small hand-drawn ink illustrations at section edges and let them overlap whitespace, not content
- Break up feature grids with centered italic serif testimonials attributed with small avatars

Don't:
- Add drop shadows, gradients, or colored section backgrounds — every section sits on flat white
- Use large pill buttons; buttons stay compact with ~5px corners
- Let illustration color creep beyond black ink plus magenta scribble accents
- Set headings in a serif — serif is only for the logotype and quotes
- Crowd the layout; sections rely on tall whitespace gaps and a narrow content column
