## Overview
Krepling pairs a quiet, warm-gray canvas (#F1F1F1) with loud, saturated gradient panels — violet-to-magenta, mint-to-aqua, lilac — that carry flat product illustrations and floating UI vignettes. Near-black panels and a black footer/CTA band punctuate the pastel flow, and a vivid purple (#B255FB) is reserved for primary buttons. The overall feel is a modern no-code SaaS: soft, illustrative, and gradient-forward but disciplined in type and spacing.

## Layout
Slim sticky top nav on the light canvas: logo left, center links (Features, Pricing, Company, Resources), Log in + white pill "Get started" right. Page heroes are editorial: a huge left-aligned lowercase-feeling headline ("Pricing", "Blog") with a small two-line descriptor beside it, often over a 3D isometric tile illustration. Section rhythm alternates full-bleed gradient bands (each opening a labeled chapter — Workflows, Storefront, Dashboard — with a small pill badge) with white feature-card grids, then a black "essentials" strip, and always closes with a dark gradient CTA band showing a floating dashboard screenshot. Blog uses a 2-column card grid with a full-width featured card on top; pricing uses a 4-up plan row where the popular plan is inverted to black.

## Components
Buttons: medium-rounded rectangles; primary = solid #B255FB with white label, secondary = white fill with thin gray border, dark sections flip to white-on-black or purple-on-dark. Cards: white, 16px+ radius, borderless with barely-there shadow on the gray canvas; category tags are tiny outlined pill chips. Pricing cards: white with light border, popular plan solid black with the purple CTA; feature counts shown as small black circular number badges (inverted to white "Unlimited" pills on Enterprise). Gradient media panels have large radius and contain flat vector illustrations or floating mini-UI cards. FAQ rows are thin-bordered accordions.

## Signature
- Warm gray #F1F1F1 canvas everywhere — never pure white pages; white is reserved for cards.
- Full-bleed pastel gradient bands (violet/magenta, mint/aqua, lilac) hosting flat illustrations and floating product-UI vignettes.
- Chapter pattern: small pill badge + section name (Workflows / Storefront / Dashboard) opening each themed block, repeated across home and features.
- Black inversion as emphasis: popular pricing plan, "essentials" strip, and the closing CTA band all go near-black with the purple button popping.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the page background at #F1F1F1 and let white cards and gradient panels supply contrast.
- Reserve #B255FB strictly for primary CTAs; secondary actions stay white with a hairline border.
- Use large-radius gradient panels with flat illustrations or floating mini-UI cards to explain features.
- Set oversized, tight, regular-weight grotesque headlines left-aligned, with a small descriptor column beside the page title.
- End every page with the dark gradient "Build your e-commerce storefront today" band and dashboard screenshot.

Don't:
- Don't use pure white as the page canvas or heavy drop shadows on cards — elevation here is near-flat.
- Don't introduce photography beyond founder/team shots; product storytelling is illustration and UI vignettes.
- Don't render multiple purple CTAs in one section — one primary button per block, everything else neutral.
- Don't use bold/black display weights or condensed type; headlines stay regular-to-medium and airy.
- Don't color body copy — it stays black for headings and mid-gray (#6B6B6B) for supporting text, with color confined to panels.
