## Overview
A quiet, paper-gray spreadsheet aesthetic: near-white warm gray canvas, near-black grotesque type at modest weights, and restrained color that arrives only as pixel-mosaic strips of "spreadsheet cells" in teal, coral, indigo, purple, and yellow. Everything reads like a well-formatted workbook — dashed cell-range outlines float in the margins, section markers are tiny numbered colored squares, and product UI screenshots do the visual heavy lifting. Every page ends in a solid black footer with a giant monospace "Equals =" wordmark.

## Layout
- Nav: slim top bar on the canvas color; monospace "= Equals" logo left, 4-5 plain text links center-left, "Sign in" text plus one small lavender "Get started" button right.
- Hero: left-aligned headline with short subcopy and a single small CTA; decorated by pixel-mosaic cell strips or dashed spreadsheet-range outlines instead of imagery. Guides page uses a split hero (copy + email input left, book render right).
- Sections alternate full-width bands of canvas gray, slightly darker gray, muted blue, or pale yellow; numbered feature sections (small colored square chips with 1/2/3) each pair a claim headline with a large bordered product screenshot.
- Generous whitespace, long single-column scrolling pages, 2-3 column grids only for feature checklists, team photos, and template cards.
- Footer: black, multi-column sitemap in small monospace text, then the oversized cropped "Equals =" wordmark bleeding off the bottom.

## Components
- Buttons: small, subtly rounded (4-6px) rectangles; primary is pale lavender (#D9C3F5) fill with dark text and a thin darker border; secondary is white/outline. Teal (#2A7E74) filled buttons appear on the guides funnel; older pages use white and mint pill buttons with 1px black borders.
- Cards: white fill, 1px light-gray (or black on older pages) border, small radius, no or minimal shadow; template cards pair a solid pastel color block with an icon on the left.
- Testimonials: plain rows with small avatar, name/title, company logotype; key phrases in quotes get a flat yellow (#F5F571) marker highlight.
- Inputs: simple white fields with thin borders and small radius (email capture on guides).
- FAQs: thin-rule divided accordion rows with plus icons.
- Decorative: dashed-outline empty cell ranges in margins; multicolor pixel-cell mosaic strips as section transitions.

## Signature
- Pixel-mosaic strips of colored "spreadsheet cells" (teal/coral/indigo/purple/yellow) used as hero decoration and section dividers.
- Giant cropped monospace "Equals =" wordmark filling the black footer on every page.
- Yellow marker-highlight on testimonial phrases and expert bios.
- Dashed spreadsheet cell-range outlines floating in page margins as background texture.
- Pale lavender CTA button — an unusual, low-saturation primary against a gray canvas.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas a warm near-white gray and let real product screenshots (bordered, on white) carry the visuals.
- Use spreadsheet metaphors as decoration: dashed cell ranges, colored cell mosaics, numbered square section chips.
- Keep CTAs small and quiet — one pale lavender button, thin border, dark text; never a giant saturated block.
- Set headlines in a regular-weight grotesque at large sizes; create hierarchy through size and gray-toned secondary lines, not boldness.
- End pages with the black footer and oversized monospace wordmark; use monospace for logo and footer sitemap.

Don't:
- Don't use gradients, glassmorphism, or soft drop shadows — surfaces are flat with 1px borders.
- Don't introduce large saturated hero backgrounds on core pages (the yellow/blue blocks belong to the older era; current pages stay gray).
- Don't use heavy/black display weights or condensed type — the voice is calm and regular-weight.
- Don't round anything beyond ~6px on current pages; no floating pill cards.
- Don't scatter accent colors into text or icons broadly — color lives almost exclusively in the cell mosaics, highlights, and template card tiles.
