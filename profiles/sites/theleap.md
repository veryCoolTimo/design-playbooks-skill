## Overview
Warm off-white paper canvas with dark chocolate-brown serif type, punctuated by full-bleed blocks of saturated candy color (tomato red, kelly green, sky blue, lilac, chartreuse) and rainbow-gradient squiggle ribbons. The mood is editorial-meets-sticker-sheet: big magazine serif headlines, marker-style lilac highlights on key words, emoji sprinkled into copy, and photo-heavy creator imagery. Everything interactive is a pill; everything container-like is a big soft-cornered card, often shaped like a manila folder with a tab.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on the cream canvas: logo left, uppercase sans links (BLOG, RESOURCES, ABOUT), right-aligned "SIGN IN" text link plus a pill "SIGN UP FREE" CTA. Home hero is centered: small chartreuse tag chip ("Powered by AI"), large serif headline with one word colored blue, short sans subcopy, single pill CTA, then a wide product-UI screenshot card. Section rhythm alternates the cream canvas with full-bleed color slabs (green, blue, lilac, yellow, red) and 50/50 splits — colored image panel left, text right with a tag chip, serif subhead, and small sans body. About page uses off-center serif hero with a right-hand highlighted table-of-contents list, then giant centered serif statements inside huge rounded white cards floating on gradient backgrounds. Blog and resources are simple 3-column card grids with generous gutters and centered serif titles under square images; pill category filter chips row the top of the blog.

## Components
Buttons: pill shape, chartreuse (#E9F55E) fill with dark brown text, thin dark outline, uppercase or small-caps labels; no shadows. Tag/eyebrow chips: small pills or rectangles in chartreuse or lilac with brown text. Cards: very large radius (~20-24px) white or colored panels; a recurring "folder" motif adds a tab notch to the top-left corner (testimonial cards, mission statements, creator profile cards). Blog/resource cards are borderless — square image with medium radius, centered serif title below, no card chrome. Inputs: pill outline field ("YOUR EMAIL") with an attached chartreuse pill "SIGN UP" button. Category filters: outlined pills, active state filled lilac. Highlighted words in headlines get a lilac/pink marker-swipe background.

## Signature
- Editorial serif headlines on warm cream with hand-marker lilac highlights on key words (freedom, fulfillment, making money)
- Chartreuse pill CTAs with dark brown text — the yellow-green reads as the brand action color everywhere
- Rainbow-gradient squiggle/ribbon graphics looping across section edges and the footer
- Folder-with-tab card shape holding quotes, missions, and creator profiles on saturated full-bleed color blocks

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set all headlines in the serif at generous sizes, even card titles in grids; reserve the sans for body, nav, and labels
- Use the chartreuse pill exclusively for CTAs and keep its dark brown label and slim outline
- Alternate cream sections with full-bleed slabs of one saturated accent color, placing white rounded cards on top
- Highlight one or two emotive words per headline with a lilac marker swipe or a blue color change
- Use square, candid creator photography and playful prop-styled flat-lay imagery in cards

Don't:
- Don't use dark mode or pure black — the ink is warm dark brown (#40241C) on warm off-white
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients to buttons/cards; depth comes from color blocking, not elevation
- Don't set headlines in bold sans — the identity depends on the regular-weight serif contrast
- Don't square off corners: buttons are always pills and cards always heavily rounded
- Don't limit the palette to one accent; the system mixes red, green, blue, lilac, and chartreuse across adjacent sections
