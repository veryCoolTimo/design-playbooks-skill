## Overview
A cheerful underwater-themed brand system: a lime-green fish logo, wavy "water line" section dividers, and floating fish/bubble/coral doodles scattered across full-bleed pastel section bands. The base is a warm off-white canvas punctuated by saturated color blocks (mint, pink, lavender, coral, midnight navy), all held together by thin near-black outlines on nearly every element. Feels like a friendly illustrated storybook wrapped around a serious support product.

## Layout
Floating pill-shaped nav bar (white, thin dark border) with logo left, dropdown links center, "Sign in" ghost + lime "Book Demo" pill right. Heroes are centered or split text-plus-widget on a colored band (green-to-blue gradient on home, mint on pricing, lavender on product), closed off by a scalloped wave edge into a horizontally scrolling logo marquee. Sections alternate full-bleed background colors — off-white, coral pink, gradient mint-to-lavender — creating strong rhythm; each opens with a small pill "eyebrow" chip plus a large heading. Content sits in bordered white panels; case studies use a 2-col card grid; the why page goes fully illustrative with an iceberg scene on midnight navy. Footer repeats the mint band with an oversized fish wordmark.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, lime-green fill (#BFF02F), 1px black border, black text with small icon prefix; secondary is white pill with the same border; dark cards invert to white-outline pills. Cards: white or pastel fill, 1px near-black border, 10-14px radius, occasionally a hard offset drop shadow (sticky-note quote cards tilt a few degrees). Pricing add-on cards use dashed inner dividers and pastel gradient fills. Eyebrow labels are tiny outlined pill chips with an emoji-ish icon. FAQ rows are bordered pill-corner accordions. Inputs: simple white rounded fields with a lime Subscribe pill. Product-UI mockups appear as bordered browser/widget frames with lime headers.

## Signature
- Wavy scalloped dividers between hero and body, reinforcing the underwater metaphor; fish, bubbles, starfish, and coral doodles sprinkled in section corners.
- Thin 1px near-black outline on almost everything — nav, buttons, cards, chips — over pastel fills.
- Lime-green (#BFF02F) reserved for CTAs, the fish logo, and highlight bars, so it always reads as "action."
- Full-bleed section color blocking: off-white, mint, coral pink, lavender gradient, and one dramatic midnight-navy iceberg section.
- Giant footer wordmark: lime fish icon plus "Brainfish" set at display-poster scale.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Outline interactive and container elements with a thin dark border, even on colored backgrounds.
- Alternate full-bleed pastel section backgrounds and separate the hero with a wavy scalloped edge.
- Use lime-green pills with black text for every primary CTA; pair with a white-outlined secondary pill.
- Start each section with a small icon-plus-label pill chip above the heading.
- Scatter small line-art sea doodles (fish, bubbles, coral) sparingly at section edges.

**Don't**
- Don't use heavy black or extra-bold display weights — headings stay medium/semibold and friendly.
- Don't apply soft blurred shadows; depth comes from borders, tilts, and hard offset shadows.
- Don't spread lime green as a background wash — it's an accent for CTAs and highlights only.
- Don't use sharp corners or fully square cards; everything is rounded, buttons always pill.
- Don't render product mockups as raw screenshots — frame them in bordered, rounded widget/browser chrome with lime headers.
