## Overview
A candy-toned, illustration-heavy take on enterprise security software: warm cream canvas alternating with saturated periwinkle-purple blocks, punctuated by mint, peach, and pink pastel cards. Elegant serif headlines (often with an italicized word) sit against small grotesque body text, and hand-drawn isometric line illustrations with black outlines carry the personality. The pricing page goes full purple; the resources/podcast page flips to a near-black canvas with gradient episode cards.

## Layout
Centered pill-shaped nav bar floating on the page background (logo left, links in a rounded capsule, Sign up pill right). Home hero is a full-bleed purple band with a giant two-line serif headline staggered left/right around an illustration, small paragraph and two small pill CTAs below, then a two-row logo wall. Sections alternate cream and purple/lavender backgrounds; content sits in generous whitespace with a centered small icon + serif heading intro pattern. Testimonials and stat cards form a masonry-like collage of mixed pastel tiles. Big rounded-corner banner sections (lavender CTA panel, pink startup panel) close pages before a dense multi-column footer on cream (or dark on resources).

## Components
Buttons: small pill shapes, low visual weight — filled purple or dark plum, white-outlined ghosts on purple, white fills on dark; paired primary+secondary side by side. Cards: 10-16px radius, flat fills in pastel green/peach/pink/lavender or purple, no shadows, occasional 1px dark outline (Community Edition card) and layered/stacked-card edges on stat tiles. Quote cards open with a small square icon-mark, serif quote text in a deep tone-on-tone color, tiny attribution below. Feature tiles on pricing use darker translucent purple panels with framed illustrations. Marquee/ticker rows of pill-shaped topic chips with tiny pastel icon dots.

## Signature
- Serif display headlines with a deliberate italic word ("The answer is *yes*") over cream or saturated purple
- Outlined, toy-like isometric illustrations (contraptions, plants, rockets) in the pastel palette with black linework
- Stat tiles rendered as stacked purple cards with layered edges, mixed into pastel testimonial collages
- Pill nav capsule and small pill buttons everywhere; faint grid/dot texture on colored hero bands

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate warm cream (#FAF5EE) and brand purple (#8D75E6) section bands rather than plain white
- Set headlines in a light editorial serif and italicize one emphasized word; keep body text small and grotesque
- Color-code quote/stat cards per-testimonial (mint, peach, pink, lavender) with tone-on-tone darker text
- Keep CTAs small and pill-shaped, always primary + ghost secondary as a pair
- Use flat pastel card fills with occasional thin dark outlines instead of drop shadows

Don't:
- Use photography — every visual is a line-drawn illustration or a framed product screenshot
- Use heavy bold sans headlines; display weight stays light/regular serif
- Add drop shadows or gradients to cards on light pages (gradients appear only on the dark resources page)
- Make buttons large or full-width; oversized CTAs break the toy-scale feel
- Render cool gray backgrounds; neutrals are warm cream, darks are plum/aubergine, not black-gray
