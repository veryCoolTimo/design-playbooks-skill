## Overview
Minimal-light developer landing page built on stark white with a faint blueprint grid texture, black type, and one loud orange-red (#ef3701) reserved for CTAs. Isometric Rubik's-cube illustrations in saturated candy colors (purple, pink, yellow, green, blue) with hard flat shadows supply almost all the color; everything else is white, cream, and hairline gray. A monospace face for labels, nav, and buttons gives it a terminal/engineering voice against a big tight grotesque display face.

## Layout
Top nav: boxed square logo mark + wordmark left, center mono nav links (Docs, Use Cases, Pricing, Blog with external-link glyphs), filled orange Sign Up + outlined Log In right. Hero is centered and extremely sparse: floating isometric cube, one giant one-line headline, single orange CTA with keyboard-shortcut hint (⌘↵), a small mono caption below. Sections alternate white / #fafafa / cream #f3f0eb backgrounds. Logo bar of investors sits in bordered cells forming a strip. Features is a 4x2 grid of bordered white cards, each with a cube popping out over the top-right corner. Full-bleed red-orange footer band with a giant wordmark cropped at the page bottom. Generous vertical whitespace throughout; centered section titles ("Features", "Use Cases", "Transparent pricing").

## Components
Buttons: rectangular, zero radius; primary = flat #ef3701 fill with white mono label; secondary = white fill with 1px black border and black mono label; no shadows or gradients. Cards: white, 1px light-gray border, square corners, no shadow; feature cards use an icon chip (small gray square), mono card title, and red square-bullet lists. Pricing: recommended plan is a solid purple (#a56ff9) card with white text and a translucent pill "Recommended" badge; other tiers are flat cream cards; plan names in mono (accent red on light cards); disabled features rendered in faded text. Pricing sliders: thin track with square stepper value boxes. Testimonial cards: plain white bordered tweets with avatar, handle, and X glyph on cream background.

## Signature
- One aggressive orange-red (#ef3701) used only for CTAs, bullets, and the footer band — all other color comes from isometric cube illustrations.
- Monospace UI voice: nav, buttons, card titles, captions, and plan names all in mono; big marketing copy in a tight grotesque.
- Hard-cornered everything — flat fills, 1px borders, flat polygonal shadows under illustrations, zero border-radius and zero blur.
- Faint blueprint grid lines and paper-grain texture on the white canvas; giant cropped wordmark filling the footer.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Reserve #ef3701 for primary CTAs and tiny accents (square list bullets, links); let white/cream carry the page.
- Set labels, nav items, buttons, and card headings in a monospace; keep display headlines in a tight grotesque at very large sizes.
- Use isometric, flat-shaded cube/block illustrations with hard dark drop shadows as the sole decorative element.
- Keep buttons and cards perfectly square with 1px hairline borders instead of shadows.
- Alternate white, #fafafa, and warm cream #f3f0eb section backgrounds; end with a saturated red-orange footer and an oversized cropped wordmark.

**Don't**
- Don't add border-radius, gradients, glassmorphism, or soft blurred shadows — nothing here is rounded or blurred.
- Don't introduce a second brand color for buttons; purple/pink/yellow belong to illustrations and the one highlighted pricing card only.
- Don't use photography or realistic 3D renders; illustration stays flat, geometric, isometric.
- Don't set body copy in the mono — mono is for labels and headings inside components, body stays in the plain sans.
- Don't crowd the hero; it holds exactly one illustration, one headline, one button, one caption.
