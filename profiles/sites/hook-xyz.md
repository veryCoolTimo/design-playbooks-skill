## Overview
A warm, toy-like Web3 landing built on a single sunset palette: peachy-cream canvas, lighter cream cards, and orange-to-chocolate gradient headlines. A cinematic 3D illustrated hero (a cozy adventurer's study rendered like a Pixar set) anchors the page, and small 3D still-life props (coin stacks, message bottle, spinning top) carry each section instead of screenshots. The mood is playful treasure-hunt storytelling applied to an NFT options protocol.

## Layout
Minimal nav: centered wordmark inside the hero image with a small "New" announcement pill beneath it; no visible link row. Hero is a full-bleed rounded-corner 3D scene with centered white headline, a single black pill CTA, and a one-line tagline pinned at the bottom. Below, the page is a vertical stack of three full-width rounded cards, each identically composed: centered 3D prop, huge two-line gradient headline ending in a period, small muted subcopy. Then a "Features" heading over a 3x2 grid of small icon tiles, and a solid-orange rounded footer card. Generous vertical padding inside cards; consistent gutter keeps every card inset from the viewport edges.

## Components
Buttons: solid near-black pill with white ~12px label ("Get Started"); footer uses tiny outlined white pill links (Docs, Blog). Announcement banner: black pill with a red "New" tag and external-link glyph. Cards: two tiers — large cream (#FBF1DC) story cards and small feature tiles, both borderless, shadowless, flat fills distinguished from the canvas only by a lighter value; radius roughly 24px+. Feature tiles hold a single orange (#E8821E) rounded-stroke line icon above 2-3 lines of small tan caption text. No inputs seen.

## Signature
- Orange-to-brown gradient display headlines, always sentence case and always ending with a period.
- Rendered 3D props on flat cream cards — one object per section, floating with no ground shadow.
- One hue family for the entire page: everything from canvas to footer sits on a cream-to-orange ramp; the only outsider is the black CTA pill.
- Full-bleed illustrated 3D hero with rounded corners, treated as a card like everything else.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the whole page inside the warm ramp: cream canvas, lighter-cream cards, orange accents, brown depths.
- Use gradient text (orange → dark brown) on heavy rounded-geometric headlines, sized far larger than body copy.
- Reserve black exclusively for the primary CTA pill so it pops against the warm field.
- Center everything inside story cards: prop, headline, subcopy stacked on one axis.
- Round every container generously (20px+) — cards, hero, footer all share the pill-adjacent softness.

Don't:
- Introduce cool colors (blues, purples) or pure white backgrounds — nothing on the page leaves the warm palette.
- Use borders or drop shadows to separate cards; contrast comes from fill value alone.
- Use photography or UI screenshots — imagery is stylized 3D renders and line icons only.
- Set headlines in a neutral grotesque or in all-caps; the voice is rounded, friendly, sentence case with terminal periods.
- Crowd sections: each card carries exactly one idea with abundant padding around it.
