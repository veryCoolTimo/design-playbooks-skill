## Overview
Minimal-light fintech design built on a white/warm-gray canvas with near-black text, then punctuated by a small set of loud brand colors: emerald-green CTAs, a saturated orange-red banner, cobalt blue, and dark forest-green editorial tiles. Illustration carries the personality — payment cards rendered as flat collages of abstract geometric shapes, and faint line-art doodles etched into dark surfaces. The overall read is engineer-friendly restraint with confident color bursts.

## Layout
Sticky white top nav: small mark + wordmark left, 6 text links center-left, "Log In" plus a pill "Explore Sandbox →" button right. Heroes alternate: home/platform use a left-aligned headline on a light gray (#F4F4F2) band with floating illustrated cards on the right; solutions/services use a centered headline with a small pill category tag above it and a single green CTA below; company uses a full-bleed dark forest-green hero. Section rhythm alternates white and light-gray (#F4F4F2) full-width bands. Content sits in a ~1080px column; feature grids run 3-4 columns of equal gray tiles. Every page ends the same way: full-width orange "Get Started" banner with doodle line-art, then a "Latest Updates" two-card strip, then a black footer with a 5-column sitemap and outlined doodles bleeding in at the edges.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped; primary is solid emerald green (#0E7C4F) with white label and trailing arrow ("Contact Sales →"); secondary is white/transparent with a thin gray border ("Explore Sandbox →"); on the orange banner the primary flips to solid black. Cards: flat light-gray (#F4F4F2) tiles with no border and no shadow, small radius, title top-left + small body copy; larger alternating text/image feature panels are white with a hairline border and medium radius. Blog/guide cards are flat images (dark forest-green with white line-art, or orange/blue collage) above plain text — no container chrome. Testimonial block pairs a solid cobalt-blue logo panel with a white quote panel. Newsletter input: plain white field, thin border, orange pill Subscribe button. Filter chips on blog: small pills, active state solid black.

## Signature
- Payment cards illustrated as flat collages of orange/blue/pink/green abstract shapes on black or white card bodies — the brand's hero motif everywhere.
- The recurring full-width orange-red "Get Started" banner with tone-on-tone doodle outlines closing every page.
- Faint white line-art doodles (arrows, squiggles, card shapes) etched into the black footer and dark green surfaces.
- Green pill CTAs with trailing arrows against an otherwise monochrome white/gray/black page.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the base strictly white + #F4F4F2 gray bands + near-black text; let color arrive only through CTAs, banners, and illustrations.
- Use flat, borderless, shadowless gray tiles for feature grids — dense 3-4 column matrices of short title + 2-3 line body.
- End every page with the same sequence: orange CTA banner → latest-updates cards → black doodle footer.
- Pair pill buttons with trailing arrows; green for primary, outlined white for secondary, black when sitting on orange.
- Render product/domain imagery as flat geometric-shape collages, not photos or 3D.

Don't:
- Don't add drop shadows, gradients, or glassmorphism — surfaces here are flat fills and hairlines only.
- Don't use the orange or cobalt for buttons on white pages; primary CTAs are green (orange is reserved for the banner and illustration accents).
- Don't round grid tiles heavily — big radii belong only to the large banners/panels; tiles stay near-square.
- Don't set headlines in a serif or ultra-bold display face; the voice is a medium-weight geometric grotesque with tight tracking.
- Don't fill dark sections with photography; dark green/black areas use white line-art doodles (the one photo strip on the company page is grayscale).
