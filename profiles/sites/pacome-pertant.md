## Overview
A near-black, single-focus entry gate that reads more like a cinema title card than a website. One glowing 3D smiley orb, two lines of lowercase copy, and a single white pill CTA float in a vast dark void. Color exists only inside the rendered 3D object (neon green/mint gradients); the UI itself is strictly monochrome. Note: metadata tags this ML, but the evidence is unambiguously a dark theme.

## Layout
No visible navigation, header, or footer chrome — the entire viewport is one centered vertical stack: 3D orb, tagline, CTA, all on the vertical axis. Whitespace is extreme; content occupies maybe 15% of the viewport height, centered with a slight upward bias. A single low-contrast text link ("enter without sound") sits alone at the very bottom edge as a quiet secondary escape hatch. No grid, no sections — rhythm comes from generous even gaps between the three stacked elements.

## Components
- Primary CTA: fully rounded white pill (#F7F7F7) with black lowercase label and a small solid dot suffix acting as a live/audio indicator; no border, no visible shadow.
- Secondary action: bare lowercase text link in dim gray (~#8A8A8A), no underline, no container.
- The 3D orb functions as the logo/avatar — a photoreal-lit sphere with a smiley face, lit in green/mint against the black.
- No cards, inputs, or other chrome visible on this gate screen.

## Signature
- Sound-first entry ritual: "enter with sound" as the hero CTA, "enter without sound" demoted to a faint footer link.
- A single playful 3D character (glowing smiley sphere) is the only source of color on an otherwise pure black/white screen.
- All copy is lowercase, short, and conversational ("motion & sound designer based in paris").
- One white pill button as the sole strong UI element — maximum contrast against near-black.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the canvas near-black (#0B0B0B) and let 3D/media content carry all the color.
- Use a single white pill CTA with black lowercase text as the one dominant interactive element per screen.
- Set everything lowercase in a clean neo-grotesque; keep copy to one or two short lines.
- Center content on the vertical axis with huge empty margins; push secondary actions to the screen edge in dim gray.
- Use a small dot or glyph inside the pill to signal state (audio on) instead of icons with labels.

**Don't**
- Don't add navigation bars, menus, or footers to the entry screen — the gate has zero chrome.
- Don't introduce accent colors into buttons, links, or text; green/mint belongs only to the rendered 3D object.
- Don't use borders, outlines, or drop shadows on the CTA — flat white fill on black does the work.
- Don't capitalize headings or use display-size type; the voice is small, lowercase, understated.
- Don't compete with the 3D centerpiece — one focal object per viewport.
