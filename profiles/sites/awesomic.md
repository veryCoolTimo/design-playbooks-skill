## Overview
A dark cosmic-playful take on dark-tech: deep indigo-navy canvas (#212236) sprinkled with tiny stars, soft rainbow aurora washes bleeding in from the edges, and cartoon astronaut illustrations with photographic faces. Warm peach-to-salmon gradient CTAs and mint-green stat numbers pop against the dark field. The tone is friendly and spacey rather than corporate-severe.

## Layout
Sticky top nav: wordmark left, text links center-right, "Log In" plus a dashed-outline "Book a Demo →" button. Hero is fully centered: two-line uppercase H1 (with one phrase tinted in a pastel rainbow gradient), one-line subhead, single gradient CTA, "Backed by Y Combinator" proof line, then floating astronaut illustrations. Sections alternate: stat counters + press logos, left-aligned oversized statement copy, embedded product video card (light-mint framed), checklist vs. pain-point split, fanned/tilted designer trading cards, a masonry portfolio collage of colorful work samples, testimonial slider, 3-column pricing, accordion FAQ, and a bordered CTA banner before the footer. Generous vertical breathing room throughout; content column stays narrow and centered.

## Components
- Primary button: horizontal peach-to-salmon gradient fill (#FFD9A4 → #F89898), dark navy bold label, ~8-10px radius, with a signature offset white outline rectangle behind it (drop-shadow-as-border effect).
- Secondary/nav button: transparent with dashed light border and arrow.
- Cards: slightly lighter navy surface (#2A2B42), 12-16px radius; designer cards are tilted with holographic mint-to-pink gradient borders; pricing "Best Value" card gets a cream (#FBE1A5) header cap and border.
- Pricing CTAs: outlined pill-ish buttons on side plans, gradient fill on the featured plan.
- FAQ: full-width dark rows with thin dividers and plus icons.
- Checkmarks in mint/white; stat numbers in mint green (#4CD6C0) or cream.

## Signature
- Space narrative everywhere: starfield speckles, astronauts with real photo faces, rocket and space-dog illustrations, "awesome on a cosmic level" footer tagline.
- Peach-to-salmon gradient CTA with an offset outline "echo" frame — repeated identically at hero, mid-page, pricing, and footer banner.
- Holographic iridescent gradient borders on tilted designer cards and the footer CTA panel.
- Soft rainbow aurora glows melting into the dark canvas corners; one hero headline phrase tinted with the same pastel rainbow.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep one CTA style (warm gradient + offset outline) and repeat it verbatim at every conversion point.
- Reserve mint green for numbers/stats and checkmarks only; keep large surfaces navy.
- Tint a single key phrase in headings with the pastel rainbow gradient; leave the rest pure white.
- Use tilted, gradient-bordered cards for personality moments (people, testimonials), straight cards for pricing/FAQ.
- Let colorful portfolio imagery carry the saturation; frame it on the dark canvas with wide gutters.

Don't:
- Don't use cold blue/purple neon accents — the CTA palette here is deliberately warm (peach/salmon/cream).
- Don't put long body copy in low-contrast gray on the aurora-washed areas; secondary text stays #9C9EAE on flat navy.
- Don't square off buttons or go full pill — corners stay at modest 8-14px.
- Don't render illustrations flat-corporate; the astronaut characters mix cartoon bodies with photo faces on purpose.
- Don't crowd sections — every block gets a large dark buffer with only star speckles between.
