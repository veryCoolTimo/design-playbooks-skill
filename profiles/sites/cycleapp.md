## Overview
Minimal light SaaS marketing site built on pure white with a single vivid royal blue (#2A44E8) doing all the CTA and brand work. Playfulness comes from small toy-like garnishes — a yellow circle, green diamond, pink toggle, dashed-line paths, emoji "reacji" badges — sprinkled over an otherwise restrained, whitespace-heavy grid. Heroes sit on a faint periwinkle wash that fades to white; heavy near-black grotesque headlines often carry one phrase colored in the brand blue.

## Layout
Sticky top nav: logo left, center link row (Product, Changelog, Manifesto, Pricing, Resources), Log in + pill blue "Get started" right. Heroes are centered stacks: kicker pill, huge 2-line headline (one phrase in blue), short gray subhead, one or two pill CTAs; home adds a large 3D pastel torus render. Sections alternate white and soft pastel washes (lavender, mint-to-pink gradients) with generous vertical padding. Feature content uses 2-4 column bento-style card grids; pricing is three cards with the middle "Pro" card outlined in blue and badged "Most picked", followed by a long thin-ruled comparison table. Changelog uses a left date rail with right content column. Every page closes with the same full-width royal-blue "Try Cycle today" rounded banner decorated with dashed paths and the shape trio, then a light-gray rounded footer.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped; primary is solid #2A44E8 with white text, secondary is white with thin gray border (or white-outline on the blue banner); small in nav, larger in heroes. Cards: white, large 16-24px radius, hairline gray borders, essentially shadowless — flat and crisp; feature tiles embed product-UI screenshots or emoji-badge clusters. Pricing table rows separated by 1px hairlines; checkmarks in blue/dark; small rounded "New"/"Beta" chips in blue and red tints. Testimonial cards pair quote text with a colored pill name-badge (green, blue, pink, yellow) plus tiny avatar. Blog cards: rounded thumbnail, avatar + meta line, bold title, gray excerpt.

## Signature
- One saturated royal blue (#2A44E8) as the only strong color on white; used for CTAs, headline highlights, and the recurring full-width closing banner.
- Toy-shape decoration kit: yellow circle, green diamond, pink toggle/pill, white dashed paths with rounded corners — reused across banners and heroes.
- Extra-bold tight grotesque headlines with a single blue-colored phrase ("all in one place", "weirdly fast").
- Emoji/reacji badges and playful mascot-like details inside otherwise flat, hairline-bordered cards.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the page white and reserve #2A44E8 for primary buttons, key words in headlines, and the closing banner.
- Use pill buttons everywhere; pair one solid blue with one outlined secondary.
- End every page with the rounded royal-blue CTA banner carrying dashed paths and the circle/diamond/toggle shapes.
- Frame cards with hairline borders and 16-24px radius; let real product screenshots be the imagery.
- Give heroes a faint periwinkle-to-white gradient wash and center-align the whole stack.

**Don't**
- Don't add drop shadows or heavy elevation — the system is flat with 1px borders.
- Don't introduce a second strong hue for actions; yellow/green/pink stay decorative only.
- Don't use serif or thin-weight display type; headlines are always heavy, tight grotesque.
- Don't fill sections edge-to-edge; keep wide margins and tall vertical rhythm between blocks.
- Don't square off the buttons — everything interactive is pill-shaped.
