## Overview
Voice-AI marketing site styled like a restrained print magazine. Everything sits on a warm off-white page with near-black warm-gray text; the only color comes from five soft pastel gradient orbs (mint, peach, lavender, sky, rose) floating as pure decoration. No saturated brand hue exists anywhere — the main CTA is simply a dark ink pill. Serif display type at an unusually light 300 weight is the defining voice.

## Layout
Fixed 64px light nav: wordmark left, horizontal product menu center, Sign In + ink-pill CTA right; collapses to hamburger under 768px. Hero is a centered light-serif headline (64px desktop) over the off-white canvas with a gradient bloom behind it, subhead, and two pill CTAs. Content caps around 1200px on a 12-column grid; sections breathe at a 96px vertical cadence while cards within a band sit tight (16-24px gaps). Feature grids run 3-up, splitting to 2-up and 1-up down the breakpoints. Footer is a 5-column link block on the same canvas.

## Components
Primary button: 40px-tall pill filled #292524 with white 15px/500 Inter label, darkening to #0c0a09 on press. Secondary: transparent pill with a 1px strong-hairline border and ink text; tertiary is a plain ink text link. Cards are pure white on the off-white floor, 16px radius, 1px hairline (#e7e5e4) borders, gaining only a faint single-tier shadow (0 4px 16px, 4% black) on hover; orb showcase cards soften to 24px radius on a #fafafa base. Pricing tiers share the white-card shell; the featured tier flips to a #0c0a09 dark surface with white text. Inputs: white, 8px radius, 44px tall, strong hairline border that thickens to 2px ink on focus. Badges are small uppercase pills (12px/600, wide tracking) on a #f0efed plate; voice rows use 32px circular icon plates with hairline dividers.

## Signature
- Serif display locked at weight 300 with tight negative tracking — editorial, never bold.
- Pastel gradient orbs (5 fixed stops) as the sole color moments, always decorative, never functional.
- CTA is a colorless near-black pill; the palette has zero saturated action hues.
- Warm-gray neutral ramp (stone tones, not cool grays) from #f5f5f5 canvas to #0c0a09 ink.

## Do
- Keep every display headline at serif weight 300 with negative letter-spacing.
- Fill primary CTAs only with the #292524 ink pill; keep secondaries as outlined transparent pills.
- Track Inter body slightly loose (+0.15-0.18px) at weight 400/500.
- Confine the pastel gradients to background blooms and orb-card decoration.
- Hold the 96px section rhythm and white-card-on-off-white layering with hairline borders.

## Don't
- Don't add a saturated or colored CTA — the ink pill is the only action fill.
- Don't bold display type; it breaks the editorial register into generic SaaS marketing.
- Don't use the gradient stops as button fills, text colors, or card surfaces.
- Don't square off CTAs or badges — pill geometry is non-negotiable.
- Don't thin body text to 300 to echo the display; body stays 400/500.
