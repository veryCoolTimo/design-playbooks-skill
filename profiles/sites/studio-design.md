## Overview
Stark black-on-white big-type language where short, punchy headlines ("Dream it. Make it. Love it.", "Make it alive") carry each section and product screenshots do the rest. The page alternates between pure white and near-black (#0E0E0E) full-bleed bands, with one loud signature element: a hot-pink-to-purple gradient pill CTA that is the only saturated brand color on otherwise monochrome chrome. Impressionist paintings (Monet) are used as demo content inside editor mockups, giving the product UI an art-gallery feel.

## Layout
Sticky top nav: small wordmark left ("/ STUDIO"), sparse text links right, gradient pill CTA at far right. Heroes are centered: tiny pink eyebrow label in caps, huge bold headline, one-line subcopy, then a large product screenshot or collage of template thumbnails. Section rhythm is strict: eyebrow → H2 → short paragraph → 2-3 column card grid, repeated down the page with big vertical whitespace. Long pages end with a giant ghosted watermark headline ("Unleash Creativity") in ultra-light gray behind the final "Make your site in STUDIO" CTA block. Features page adds a thin left-rail anchor menu; templates page is a dense 5-column masonry-ish thumbnail grid on white.

## Components
- Buttons: primary CTA is a pill with a left-to-right pink→magenta/purple gradient fill (#F13A9B → #B44BF2 range), white bold label, sometimes a small arrow glyph; secondary buttons are white pills with a 1px black/gray border ("View details", "Get Plan") or solid black pills.
- Cards: light-gray (#F4F4F5) or white rounded rectangles (~10-12px) with no shadow and no border on white, holding a small pink caption, bold title, gray body text, and a screenshot; on black sections cards invert to dark gray panels.
- Pricing: white bordered cards, the featured plan outlined in pink with a pink "Most Popular" tag and the gradient CTA; comparison table uses circle/cross glyphs and a pink-tinted highlighted column.
- FAQ: full-width white bars with a plus icon, hairline separators.
- Inputs: minimal — a pill search field with icon on the templates store; toggle segmented control (Annual/Monthly) as a small pill.

## Signature
- Single gradient pink→purple pill CTA repeated verbatim (nav, hero, footer) as the only brand color against black-and-white everything.
- Alternating full-bleed white and near-black sections with identical centered eyebrow/H2 structure.
- Giant ghost watermark typography ("Unleash Creativity") bleeding off-screen before the footer on every marketing page.
- Fine-art demo content (Monet paintings, "Claude Monet" headlines) inside product screenshots instead of lorem-ipsum SaaS mockups.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep chrome monochrome: black text on white, white text on #0E0E0E; reserve the pink→purple gradient exclusively for primary CTAs and tiny eyebrow labels/tags.
- Write headlines as 2-5 word declaratives in extra-bold and let a real product screenshot follow immediately.
- Alternate white and near-black full-bleed bands to pace long pages; invert card colors with the band.
- Use borderless light-gray rounded cards in 2-3 column grids with a pink micro-label above each card title.
- End pages with an oversized ghosted watermark headline plus a centered H2 + gradient pill CTA.

**Don't**
- Don't add drop shadows, glassmorphism, or decorative illustration — depth comes only from screenshots and the white/black band contrast.
- Don't use the pink as a text-link or background color for whole sections; links and nav stay black/gray.
- Don't square off buttons — every button is a full pill, even small "View more" tertiary ones.
- Don't introduce a second accent hue in chrome; blues/greens/yellows appear only inside template thumbnails and screenshots.
- Don't crowd sections — each keeps one idea, one headline, one visual, with wide vertical padding between.
