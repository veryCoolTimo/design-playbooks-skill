## Overview
A single saturated marigold-yellow canvas carries the whole page, with near-black text, cream pill buttons, and white rounded cards floating on top. The voice is typewriter-monospace for the hero and body copy, switched to a chunky bold grotesque for section headings and the numbered "4 Images in 1 Email with 0 Algorithm" strip. Artist artwork supplies all the color variety; the chrome itself stays strictly yellow/black/cream.

## Layout
Slim top bar: cream pill nav links (FAQs, Pricing, Release Notes) on the left, hand-drawn wordmark centered, Login (cream) and Sign Up (black) pills on the right. Hero is a centered monospace headline over a large rounded screenshot collage of artist images framed in black film-strip style. Sections stack in a calm center-aligned rhythm: numbered value-prop strip, monospace paragraph, a 4-column "How it works" card row, a 2x4 "Discover Artists" grid of rounded artwork tiles with circular avatar badges, then a cream rounded CTA panel and a minimal footer of cream pills. Generous vertical whitespace; content column stays narrow and centered.

## Components
Buttons: pills throughout — cream (#EFE7DA) with black text for secondary (nav, "See more", footer links), solid black with light text for primary CTAs (Sign Up, "CLAIM YOUR BOOTH!" which is a wide black pill inside the cream panel). No visible borders or shadows. Cards: white, large radius (~16-20px), flat, no border or shadow, containing a small uppercase monospace step label, bold grotesque heading, gray placeholder illustrations, and tiny monospace body text. Artist tiles: rounded-corner artwork images with a black-ringed circular avatar overlapping the bottom edge and an underlined monospace name link below. The final CTA sits in a cream rounded panel with a subtle dark border.

## Signature
- One flat, unbroken marigold-yellow background from header to footer — no alternating section colors.
- Monospace typewriter voice for hero headline and all body copy, paired with a bold grotesque only for headings.
- Cream and black pill buttons as the entire interactive vocabulary.
- Numbered black circles ("4 / 1 / 0") inline with heading text as a graphic value-prop device.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the entire page on the single yellow canvas and let user artwork provide all other color.
- Set hero headline and body paragraphs in monospace; reserve the bold grotesque for short section headings.
- Use pill-shaped buttons only: cream for secondary, solid black for the primary CTA.
- Float flat white or cream cards with 16px+ radius directly on the yellow, without shadows.
- Center-align headings, copy, and CTAs; keep body text small with wide line-height.

Don't:
- Add drop shadows, gradients, or borders to cards and buttons — everything here is flat.
- Introduce a blue/purple SaaS accent; the palette is strictly yellow, black, cream, plus artwork.
- Use rectangular or small-radius buttons; nothing interactive is squared off.
- Set long body copy in the grotesque — the monospace carries all reading text.
- Alternate section background colors or add dividing rules; rhythm comes from whitespace alone.
