## Overview
Near-black, hairline-divided dark-tech design where a single chartreuse-yellow does all the work: page-label eyebrows, CTA pills, checkmarks, and glowing accent lines. Headlines are large, light-weight grotesque set in white with dimmed gray spans for emphasis contrast. Everything else stays monochrome, letting colorful blog thumbnails and one indigo enterprise card read as deliberate bursts.

## Layout
Slim top nav: logo + text links left, "Request Demo" text link, white pill "Try for Free," and gray pill "Sign in" right. Each page opens with a small yellow breadcrumb/eyebrow label ("About," "Blog," "Pricing") above a large left-aligned headline. Sections are separated by 1px gray hairlines spanning content width; investor/customer logos sit in hairline-bordered grid cells. Home hero is centered with a vertical yellow beam bisecting a dark product screenshot. Alternating text-left/visual-right blocks mid-page; pricing uses a 3-column plan row plus full-width indigo Enterprise banner and a long hairline feature-comparison table. Every page closes with the same "The VPN replacement your workforce will love." block: yellow pill + ghost "Request a Demo >" link, then a dense multi-column footer with OS badges.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped throughout — chartreuse fill with black text for primary ("Try Twingate for Free >"), white fill with black text in nav, dark gray/outlined pill secondary ("Get Started >", "We're hiring…"); tertiary actions are plain text links with ">" chevrons. Cards: blog thumbnails are flat, vividly colored (violet/blue/orange grid-pattern illustrations) with small ~8px radius and no shadow; pricing plans are borderless columns separated by hairlines; Enterprise card is an indigo-violet gradient panel with medium radius. Checkmark lists use small gray/yellow ticks. FAQ rows are plain text with ">" toggles and hairline separation. No visible shadows anywhere — depth comes from tone shifts (#0B0B0C vs slightly lighter panels) and hairlines.

## Signature
- Chartreuse yellow (#E7F55D) as the only interface color on near-black: eyebrows, pill CTAs, glow lines, dotted-globe location markers
- Small yellow page-label eyebrow top-left of every page, echoing the current nav section
- Two-tone headlines: white phrases mixed with dimmed gray phrases in the same sentence
- 1px hairline dividers and hairline-celled logo grids instead of boxed cards or shadows

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve the yellow strictly for primary CTAs, eyebrows, and tiny accents; keep whole sections monochrome between them
- Use pill buttons with black text on yellow/white fills, and ">" chevrons on text links
- Structure sections with full-width 1px hairlines and hairline-bordered logo cells
- Mix white and ~50% gray spans inside one large light-weight headline for emphasis
- Let blog/illustration tiles carry saturated flat color (violet, blue, orange) as the only imagery color

Don't:
- Add drop shadows, glassmorphism, or gradient buttons — surfaces are flat; the sole gradient is the indigo Enterprise panel
- Use blue or purple for CTAs or links; interactive color is yellow only
- Round cards heavily — thumbnails stay at small radius while only buttons go full pill
- Brighten the canvas; backgrounds stay near-black with at most a subtle lighter panel or faint grid texture
- Center body copy; headlines and paragraphs are left-aligned except the home hero and closing logo strips
