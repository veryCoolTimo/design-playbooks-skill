## Overview
A deep forest-green world where nearly everything is green: dark #033F2D canvas, lime #C8F06A blocks, and cream #EDF2E3 surfaces stacked in bold, poster-like bands. Large soft-serif headlines sit inside lime highlight slabs with notched, interlocking rounded corners, giving an editorial, print-collage feel. UI screenshots are stylized flat mockups in near-black panels, not raw product shots.

## Layout
Cream pill-cornered nav bar floating on the dark green canvas: chunky slab-serif "Mode" wordmark left, four text links, then outlined "Sign in" plus dark-green filled "Try for free". Hero is an asymmetric collage: three staggered lime blocks carrying one headline across three lines, a photo tile bottom-left, a small chart card top-right, body copy and two buttons bottom-right. Sections alternate full-bleed color bands — dark green, mid green #296F2C, bright lime #78C61A — each holding one big rounded card or a centered serif heading. Two-column feature cards (cream + lime) with stylized UI mockups. Generous vertical padding; content max-width with wide margins.

## Components
Buttons: rectangular with small 2-6px radius; primary is cream #EDF2E3 fill with dark green text, secondary is 1px cream outline ghost on dark green; on the cream navbar the filled CTA inverts to dark green with cream text. Cards: large 16-24px radius, flat fills (cream, lime, dark green), zero shadows, zero borders; several cards use notched corner cutouts where blocks interlock. UI mockups: near-black #242422 panels with tabbed folder corners, lime and periwinkle accent bars. Quote card: giant lime rounded rectangle with an oversized blocky lime quote-mark glyph and inset photo tile.

## Signature
- Monochrome-green palette: four greens (near-black forest, mid, bright lime band, pale lime) plus cream — almost no other hue on the page.
- Headline text set inside staggered lime highlight blocks with rounded, notched, interlocking corners.
- Soft rounded serif display type against a chunky slab-serif logotype and plain grotesque body.
- Giant cut-out brand shapes as graphics: the huge lime "M" block and oversized quote marks.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep everything in the green family; use cream #EDF2E3 as the only "neutral" and #242422 only inside mockup panels.
- Put display headlines in a soft serif on lime blocks or directly on dark green — never small, never gray.
- Alternate full-bleed section bands (dark green → mid green → bright lime) instead of separating sections with borders.
- Use flat color and large card radii for depth; interlock blocks with notched corners.
- Keep buttons near-rectangular and quiet: cream fill or thin cream outline, dark green text/label.

Don't:
- Don't introduce blue/purple/red as structural colors — periwinkle and pink appear only inside illustration mockups.
- Don't use drop shadows, gradients, or borders on cards; every surface is a flat fill.
- Don't set headlines in the body sans or in white — display is always serif, dark-green-on-lime or cream-on-green.
- Don't round buttons to pills; pill shapes are reserved for nothing here — buttons stay crisp and boxy.
- Don't show literal product screenshots; recreate UI as simplified flat mockups in dark panels with lime accents.
