## Overview
Near-black canvas with pure white display type and vivid saturated color used as controlled bursts: gradient-filled headline text, glowing gradient hero backdrops, and neon gradient feature tiles. Product UI screenshots are the dominant imagery, framed in rounded dark cards. The tone is confident dark-tech with a designer's polish — big statements, tight tracking, lots of breathing room between sections.

## Layout
Slim top nav on black: logo left, center dropdown links (Features, Resources, Support, Updates, Enterprise, Pricing), Login + pill Sign up right. Heroes are centered: one short bold headline, one gray subline, single CTA row; hero backgrounds alternate between a wall-of-sites mosaic (home), a glowing color-field gradient (features: orange; marketplace: blue), and plain black with a lead-capture card (enterprise). Long pages alternate full-black sections with color-gradient feature card grids; sections open with a large centered heading — often gradient-filled in a different hue per section (cyan, magenta, purple, lime). Bento-style grids of 2-3 columns hold feature tiles; pricing uses a 5-column card row with the recommended plan lit in glowing blue, followed by an exhaustive dark comparison table. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; content column is wide but centered.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped; primary CTAs solid saturated blue (#0055FF) with white text, secondaries solid white on black or dark gray (#1a1a1a-ish) full-width blocks inside cards; some section CTAs adopt the section accent (yellow, pink, blue). Cards: large-radius (16px+) dark gray (#111-#1a1a1a) panels with no visible border or a hairline; feature tiles filled edge-to-edge with vivid multi-stop gradients (magenta-orange, cyan-blue, purple) containing mini product-UI mockups. Marketplace template cards: rounded thumbnails with tiny title/author/price meta below, price in blue. Inputs (enterprise contact form, FAQ accordions): dark filled fields, subtle 1px borders, medium radius. Category filter chips: small pill tags with emoji-like icons; active state blue. Checkmark feature lists in pricing cards; highlighted plan glows with outer blue bloom.

## Signature
- Gradient-filled section headlines that change hue per section (cyan, magenta, purple, lime) on pure black.
- Bento grids of large-radius tiles filled with neon multi-stop gradients wrapping real product-UI screenshots.
- Glowing color-field hero backdrops (orange, blue) that fade to black before the first content section.
- Pill buttons everywhere, with #0055FF blue as the transactional CTA color and the "recommended" pricing card lit like a light source.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the canvas essentially pure black and let color arrive only through gradients: headline text fills, hero glows, and feature-tile backgrounds.
- Set headlines in a tight-tracked bold grotesque, white, centered, short (2-5 words), with body copy in mid-gray (#8a8a99 range).
- Use pill-shaped buttons; reserve solid #0055FF for the main conversion CTA and white pills for secondary actions.
- Frame product screenshots inside large-radius dark or gradient cards rather than floating them bare.
- Vary the accent hue per section so a long page reads as a sequence of distinct color moments.

**Don't**
- Don't use light or gray page backgrounds — every page, including pricing tables and forms, stays on black.
- Don't add drop shadows on dark cards; separation comes from surface-lightness steps and glow, not shadow.
- Don't use small or square-cornered buttons, or outlined ghost buttons as primary CTAs.
- Don't apply gradients to body text or UI chrome — gradients are for headlines, heroes, and tile backgrounds only.
- Don't crowd sections; each section gets one heading, one short paragraph, one CTA, then its grid.
