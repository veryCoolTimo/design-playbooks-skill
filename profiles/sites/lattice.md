## Overview
Clean white-canvas SaaS marketing design anchored by a deep evergreen teal, warmed with mint tints and soft multicolor gradients. Product-UI screenshots in floating cards carry most of the visual weight; typography is a single friendly geometric grotesque used at large scale. Occasional pastel illustration and playful editorial art (blog covers, emoji, sticker badges) keep the corporate palette human.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on white: left logo (multicolor asterisk mark), center dropdown links, right "Sign in" + solid dark-teal "Request a demo" pill-ish button. Heroes vary by page: home uses a full-bleed dark-teal gradient hero with white display type and a bento grid of product cards; interior pages use centered headlines on white or pale mint with a small pill eyebrow label above ("ABOUT LATTICE", "USE CASES"). Section rhythm: hero → grayscale logo strip ("5,000+ organizations trust Lattice") → alternating feature sections, some sitting on soft teal-to-lavender gradient bands with numbered 01/02/03 side markers. 3-4 column card grids for stats, leadership, articles, and customer stories; left filter sidebar on listing pages. Every page ends with the same CTA band ("Invest in your people. Accelerate your business results.") plus a dense multi-column footer with teal all-caps column headers.

## Components
Buttons: solid dark evergreen (#17594F) fill, white label, ~6-10px radius; secondary is white/outline with teal text; ghost text links are teal with a "›" chevron. Cards: white, 16px+ radius, hairline light border, very soft shadow, generous padding; product-screenshot cards float on gradient or mint panels. Pill badges: mint-outlined all-caps micro labels used as section eyebrows and category tags ("HIRING", "COMING SOON", "LIFE SCIENCES"). Pricing cards: white, centered icon + title, teal price, full dark-teal "Get started" button. Inputs: simple white fields with light gray border, small radius (search, newsletter email). Accordions with plus/minus toggles for FAQs; checkbox filter lists in sidebars. Stats rendered as huge teal numerals on pale mint tiles.

## Signature
- Deep evergreen teal (#17594F) as the single CTA color everywhere, paired with pale mint tints rather than pure gray for section backgrounds.
- Soft blurred teal→green→lavender gradient bands behind feature sections and the home hero.
- Floating product-UI screenshot cards with large radii as the primary imagery; real team photography as secondary.
- Mint-outlined all-caps pill eyebrows above every section headline.
- Playful editorial layer: multicolor abstract blog art, emoji, circular sticker award badges.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve the dark evergreen fill strictly for CTAs; use teal text links with chevrons for secondary actions.
- Put product screenshots inside large-radius white cards floating on mint or gradient panels.
- Open sections with a small outlined pill eyebrow label, then a large centered grotesque headline.
- Use huge thin-teal numerals on pale mint tiles for stats (5,000+ / 5.55M / 18,000+ pattern).
- Repeat the same dark-teal CTA band before the footer on every page.

Don't:
- Don't introduce blue or red as UI colors; non-teal color lives only in illustrations, logos, and blog art.
- Don't use hard drop shadows or dark borders — cards read as hairline + faint shadow at most.
- Don't set headlines in a serif or condensed face; everything is one geometric grotesque.
- Don't fill whole sections with saturated color; gradients stay pastel and blurred, dark teal appears only in hero/CTA/award bands.
- Don't round buttons to full pills — pills are for tags and badges, buttons stay at modest radius.
