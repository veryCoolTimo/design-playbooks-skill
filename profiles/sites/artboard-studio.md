## Overview
A crisp white-canvas SaaS site energized by one loud chartreuse-lime CTA color and a rotating set of pastel hero tints (peach, lavender, mint-lime) that recolor each page's top band. Near-black grotesque headlines set very large and tight carry most of the personality; hand-drawn sticker-collage illustrations and real product screenshots supply the color. Dark charcoal full-bleed bands bookend pages before the footer.

## Layout
Slim sticky top nav: wordmark left, center links (Solutions, For Teams, Resources, Pricing), right side a plain "Log in ↗" text link plus the lime pill "Start Free." Heroes are either centered (home, pricing) or split two-column with headline left and a chaotic collage illustration right; each interior page tints the entire hero band a different pastel. Sections alternate text-left/media-right in a two-column rhythm with huge vertical whitespace; eyebrow labels are prefixed with an em dash ("—For Teams", "—Creative Automation"). Logo strips of grayscale partner marks appear under heroes. Template/resource pages use dense masonry thumbnail grids. A dark (#2B2B2B or deep green) full-width CTA band with a product screenshot precedes a light-gray footer.

## Components
Primary buttons: pill-shaped, solid lime (#C8F546) fill with black text; secondary buttons are solid black pills with white text or white/outlined pills with thin gray borders. Small ghost buttons ("Explore Features") are white pills with 1px gray borders. Segmented toggles (Monthly/Annually, template categories) are pill chips where the active state flips to black fill/white text. Pricing cards: light-gray (#F4F4F2) rounded rectangles, borderless and shadowless; the featured plan inverts to black with the lime CTA inside. FAQ rows are light-gray rounded bars with plus icons. Screenshot frames get thin dark borders and 10-14px radii; feature tiles on pastel pages use matching pastel fills with small line icons.

## Signature
- One saturated chartreuse-lime reserved almost exclusively for primary CTAs against otherwise neutral or pastel surfaces
- Em-dash-prefixed eyebrow/kicker labels ("—Design and Animate in One Place") used consistently across every section
- Per-page pastel hero recoloring (peach for teams, lavender for product, lime-green for solutions) over an otherwise white site
- Busy hand-drawn sticker-collage illustrations of UI fragments paired with strict, tight grotesque typography
- Inverted black featured card / black CTA band pattern to create contrast peaks on light pages

## Do / Don't
- Do keep the lime fill for the single primary action only; make secondary actions black or outlined pills
- Do set headlines very large in a tight neo-grotesque with near-black ink on white, letting whitespace do the layout work
- Do tint full hero bands with soft pastels (peach, lavender, mint) rather than adding gradients or imagery backgrounds
- Do prefix section eyebrows with an em dash and keep them small and plain
- Do close pages with a dark charcoal CTA band containing a real product screenshot
- Don't use drop shadows or borders on cards; differentiate surfaces with flat gray/pastel fills instead
- Don't introduce blue or corporate gradient palettes; the accent range is lime, pastel peach, and lavender
- Don't round buttons to anything less than a full pill or square off the toggle chips
- Don't set headlines in serifs, condensed faces, or loose tracking; the type voice is tight grotesque throughout
- Don't recolor partner logos or illustrations to the brand lime; logos stay grayscale, illustrations stay multicolor collage
