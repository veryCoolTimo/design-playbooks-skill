## Overview
Warm, optimistic eco brand that alternates photography of real tree-planting communities with flat, naive nature illustration. Deep forest green does the heavy lifting; white and a putty-beige hero band keep it airy, while candy accents (mustard, coral, sky blue, pink) appear in illustrated foliage, stat cards, and a donut chart. Organic wavy dividers make the page feel hand-drawn rather than corporate.

## Layout
Slim top bar: wordmark left, pill search input center-left, small pill utility buttons right (tree counter, News) — search is part of the chrome, not just the hero. Hero is a split band: putty-green text panel left (H1, subhead, pill CTA, live tree counter) and a full-bleed photo right with a circular "Discover Ecosia" overlay badge. Sections alternate white / deep-green full-bleed bands, joined by wavy organic edges topped with illustrated bushes and trees. Rhythm: yellow uppercase eyebrow, centered bold heading, then content — a 3-card country grid, a two-column editorial row (text + satellite photo), a dark stats band with giant counter, world map, and 4-up stat row, a financial-transparency donut, a 2x2 icon/value grid, and a green CTA band before a dark footer.

## Components
Buttons: pill, solid deep green (#215C39) with white label for primary; small white pill outlines ("Learn more", "Discover all projects", "See our financial reports") for secondary; hero CTA carries a leading Firefox icon. Search input: full pill, white fill, thin gray border, placeholder + magnifier icon. Country cards: solid flat fills (yellow, coral, green), photo on top, dark text, small white pill button, medium rounded corners, no shadows. Stats rendered as oversized bold numerals with tiny caption lines. Donut chart in brand candy colors with a white rounded value card floating on top. Footer: near-black green, tiny white link columns, app-store badges, B-Corp seal.

## Signature
- Wavy, organic section dividers crowned with flat multicolor illustrated shrubs and trees (green, mustard, coral, pink).
- Live tree counter (157,196,608) with a small tree glyph, repeated in hero, stats band, and closing CTA.
- Deep forest green used as full-bleed section background, not just as an accent.
- Yellow uppercase eyebrow labels over centered bold headings on both light and dark bands.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate white and deep-green full-bleed bands, always transitioning through a wavy illustrated edge.
- Use pill buttons everywhere: solid green on light, white outline/fill on dark or colored surfaces.
- Show impact as giant numerals (counters, "20 million", "35+") with tiny lowercase captions.
- Pair documentary photos of real people/land with flat naive illustration in the same section flow.
- Keep accent colors (mustard, coral, blue, pink) for illustration, card fills, and data viz only.

Don't:
- Use straight horizontal rules between sections — every band change here is an organic curve.
- Add shadows or gradients to cards; fills are flat and saturated with crisp edges.
- Introduce a second typeface — headings and body are the same grotesque at different weights.
- Use accent colors for CTAs; primary actions are always deep green or white pills.
- Let the layout go dense — sections hold one idea each with generous vertical padding.
