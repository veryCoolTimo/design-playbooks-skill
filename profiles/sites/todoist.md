## Overview
Warm, calm, hand-drawn productivity aesthetic: an overwhelmingly white canvas punctuated by soft watercolor-style illustrations in muted sage, mustard yellow, and blush pink, with a single saturated brand red reserved for CTAs and the logo. Product UI screenshots are rendered as tidy floating cards with pale peach/cream backdrops. The tone is airy and human rather than corporate — decorative squiggles, flags, and plant motifs soften a very disciplined typographic grid.

## Layout
Slim single-row nav: logo left, text links right, one red pill-ish CTA at the far right. Heroes are center-aligned: large bold headline (2 lines), short gray subhead, single red CTA, then a full-width or wide illustration/product shot. Body sections alternate text-left/image-right and image-left/text-right in a two-column rhythm, each block introduced by a small red or green eyebrow label above the heading. Big centered pull-quotes (press or user testimonials) act as section dividers. Pre-footer CTA band sits on a cream-to-blush gradient (#FFF5EB tones). Generous vertical whitespace throughout; content column is fairly narrow (~1100px).

## Components
Buttons: solid red (#DC4C3E) fill, white text, ~8px radius, no border or shadow; secondary buttons are white with light gray border. Cards: white, 1px light gray border or very soft shadow, 8-12px radius (feature grid tiles, integration cards, pricing columns). Product screenshots framed on pale peach/cream rounded panels with subtle drop shadows. Pricing uses a segmented Yearly/Monthly toggle (small pill), a sparse comparison table with hairline row dividers and green check icons. Search input on resources page: light gray fill, pill-rounded, icon left. Links are plain blue (#2E69AE) or red text with small arrow glyphs.

## Signature
- One red: the brand red appears only in the logo, CTAs, eyebrow labels, and in-product screenshots — everything else stays neutral or pastel.
- Hand-painted illustration system: textured gouache/watercolor scenes (isometric garden hero, crowds of tiny characters, plants in pots) in sage/mustard/blush.
- Eyebrow-label + bold headline + gray body left-aligned blocks alternating with floating product-UI cards on cream backdrops.
- Centered oversized press/user quotes in bold black type used as section breathers.

## Do
- Reserve #DC4C3E strictly for primary CTAs, the logo, and small eyebrow labels; keep large surfaces white or cream.
- Use muted, desaturated illustration colors (sage green, mustard, blush) with visible painterly texture, never flat vector-corporate style.
- Frame real product screenshots on soft peach/cream rounded panels rather than raw drop-in images.
- Alternate text/image sides section by section and precede each heading with a tiny colored eyebrow label.
- Keep pricing and comparison tables airy: hairline dividers, green outline check icons, lots of padding.

## Don't
- Don't use gradients, dark sections, or heavy shadows — the darkest element on any page is the body text and footer text.
- Don't introduce a second saturated accent competing with the red; yellow/green/pink exist only inside illustrations and small icons.
- Don't make buttons pill-shaped or sharp-cornered; the whole system sits at a modest 8-12px radius.
- Don't center-align body copy in feature sections — centering is reserved for heroes, quotes, and section intros.
- Don't crowd sections; each idea gets one heading, 2-3 short lines of gray copy, and generous vertical whitespace.
