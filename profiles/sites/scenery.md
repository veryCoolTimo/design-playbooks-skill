## Overview
Near-black dark-tech surface with restrained gray body text, punctuated by a single loud brand yellow used for the primary CTA and one enormous full-width yellow pre-footer band. Big, bold neo-grotesque headlines carry the pages; color arrives through jewel-toned feature panels (blue, green, violet) framing dark product screenshots. The overall feel is a pro video-editing tool: cinematic, high-contrast, screenshot-heavy.

## Layout
Fixed dark top nav: yellow flag logo left, text links center-left, ghost "Log in" plus solid yellow "Sign up" pill-adjacent buttons right. Heroes are either centered (home: headline over a dimmed full-bleed editor screenshot with floating collaborator cursors/name tags) or left-aligned giant type (blog, pricing, company). Section rhythm alternates: eyebrow/tab chips + heading, then a large rounded feature panel filled with a saturated gradient (blue, purple, green) containing a product screenshot. Long pages end identically: a huge rounded-corner yellow CTA block ("Try Scenery for your next video project") with an inline email + button combo, then a 4-column black footer. Pricing uses a 3-column card row plus a long comparison table with thin hairline rows.

## Components
- Buttons: small-radius (~4-6px) rectangles; primary = solid yellow #f5c518 with black text; secondary = white fill with black text; tertiary = ghost with 1px gray border on black ("Log in", "Join us →", "Meet the team →").
- Email capture: white input paired with yellow submit button; inside the yellow band the pair sits in a darker-yellow/olive rounded container.
- Cards: near-black (#141414-ish) panels with 1px subtle borders; pricing Pro card gets a yellow border highlight; "Explore all of Scenery" cards use colored borders (green, purple, green) with icon + title + body.
- Chips/badges: violet #6d28d9 pill labels ("Announcements", "Our mission"); yellow-outlined toggle chips ("What we do", "Monthly") with inactive siblings dimmed.
- Tables: hairline-divided rows, check/x glyphs, no zebra striping.
- Collaborative cursors: small colored name tags (purple "Designer", green "Marketer", blue "Editor") floating over screenshots as a product motif.

## Signature
- One yellow: brand yellow appears only on the logo, primary CTAs, and the giant rounded pre-footer band — everything else stays monochrome.
- Saturated gradient panels (electric blue, deep violet, green) as picture frames for dark UI screenshots.
- Floating collaborator cursor tags over screenshots, selling the multiplayer angle visually.
- Oversized bold grotesque headlines ("Making video a team sport") on pure near-black with generous vertical whitespace.

## Do
- Keep the background a uniform near-black across every page; let product screenshots and gradient panels supply the color.
- Reserve yellow strictly for the primary CTA and the single full-width pre-footer CTA band with large (24px+) corner radius.
- Frame product screenshots inside rounded, saturated gradient panels (blue/violet/green), one hue per feature section.
- Use violet pill badges for eyebrows/labels and ghost-bordered buttons for secondary actions.
- Dim inactive states hard (logos, toggles, tab chips render at low opacity gray).

## Don't
- Don't introduce a second bright accent competing with yellow at the CTA level — purple/blue/green stay as panel backgrounds and badges only.
- Don't use large radii on buttons or cards; corners stay crisp (~4-6px) except the hero screenshot frames and the yellow CTA block.
- Don't put yellow text on dark or dark text anywhere but on yellow surfaces; body copy is muted gray on black.
- Don't add drop shadows or glassmorphism; depth comes from 1px borders, gradients, and soft glow behind panels.
- Don't lighten the canvas mid-page — the only light-background moment is the yellow CTA band.
