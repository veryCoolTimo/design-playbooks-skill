## Overview
Near-black purple canvas with playful, saturated pops: a mint-green CTA, a hot-pink bubble logo, and violet glow halos behind floating product UI. The dark-tech base is softened by cartoon-adjacent details (marquee-style logo lettering, hand-drawn arrows, sticker-like callout chips), landing between serious devtool and toy. Product screenshots do most of the storytelling; copy stays short and punchy.

## Layout
Sticky slim top nav: pink wordmark left, four text links beside it, "Log in" ghost pill and a filled signup pill right. Hero is fully centered: huge two-line headline with a color-highlighted word, a floating extension-popup mockup lit by a purple glow, one mint CTA, star-rating microcopy, then a grayscale enterprise logo strip. Sections alternate: centered heading blocks, a 2x2 feature-card grid, an integrations grid of small logo tiles, a giant stat counter (1,449,497), a horizontal testimonial-card row with big grayscale company logos, and a short centered closing CTA. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; content column stays narrow and centered.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, solid mint #73e5b8 fill with dark text for the primary CTA; nav pills are dark with subtle borders. Feature cards: slightly lighter dark-purple fills (#221829), medium rounded corners, no visible borders, each containing a light-mode UI mockup or terminal snippet that contrasts hard against the dark card. Chips/badges: small outlined pill tags with icons (feature list row). Integration tiles: dark rounded rectangles with logo + label in a tight grid. Testimonials: dark cards with circular avatars, quote text, and large grayscale brand logos. Callout stickers on mockups: bright cyan/pink rounded labels with hand-drawn arrows.

## Signature
- Mint-green pill CTA (#73e5b8) as the only saturated button on a near-black purple page
- Purple glow/halo lighting behind floating light-mode product mockups
- Playful bubble-letter pink logo and sticker-style annotations (arrows, "Fix this plz!" labels) undercutting the dark-tech seriousness
- Color-highlighted single words inside otherwise white headlines (purple "love", lavender "loved")

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas a purple-tinted near-black (#130f1a), not pure #000
- Reserve mint green exclusively for primary CTAs; repeat the identical pill at top and bottom of the page
- Embed light-mode product UI inside dark cards for high-contrast "screenshot as hero" storytelling
- Highlight one emotional word per headline in violet/lavender
- Use grayscale-only treatment for customer/enterprise logos

Don't:
- Add borders or heavy shadows to cards — separation comes from a one-step lighter fill
- Use the pink brand color for buttons; it stays in the logo and small accents
- Set body copy in pure white — it is muted lavender-gray (#ada4be) against white headings
- Square off buttons; every button and tag is a full pill
- Let sections sprawl full-width — content is centered in a narrow column with big vertical gaps
