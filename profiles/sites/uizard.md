## Overview
Uizard is a dark-neon AI design SaaS with a split personality: marketing pages (home, AI UI design) sit on near-black canvases washed with violet-to-purple aurora gradients, while utility pages (solutions, templates, blog) flip to clean white with the same components. The constant is a vivid blue CTA, purple/violet glow imagery, and playful product screenshots doing the visual heavy lifting. Type is a friendly geometric grotesque used big and bold, often with gray-to-white emphasis shifts inside a single headline.

## Layout
Slim sticky top nav: logo left, center links (Product, AI Design, Templates, Solutions, Blog), right-aligned Log in plus a solid blue "Sign up for free" pill-ish button; active nav item highlighted in purple/blue. Home hero is centered: large two-tone headline, one-line subhead, then an inline prompt input with a purple "Generate" button, followed by a full-width product screenshot floating over the purple gradient. Below the hero: grayscale logo strip ("Trusted by..."), then alternating rhythm of 2-column feature rows (text one side, screenshot the other) and bento-style card grids (mixed 1x and 2x cards). Feature pages use zebra text/image rows with generous vertical whitespace; templates page is a catalog of 3-up thumbnail rows each with a "Show all" button; blog is a 3-column card grid with pill category filters. Most pages close with FAQ accordions and a full-width black CTA banner with phone-mockup collage, then a deep multi-column footer.

## Components
Buttons: solid fills, no borders, no shadows; primary CTA is saturated blue (#2E6FF2) with white text, secondary "Read more" buttons same blue at smaller size; on light pages a solid black button variant appears; the AI "Generate" button is purple with a sparkle icon. Radius reads 8-12px, tags and category chips are full pills in loud colors (orange, blue, purple, violet). Cards: large-radius (16-20px) surfaces — white cards on black sections and vice versa, or dark cards with embedded UI screenshots; soft or no shadow, contrast comes from fill inversion rather than elevation. Inputs: the hero prompt field is a large rounded bar (white on dark hero) with the action button docked inside on the right. Testimonials render as tweet-style cards with avatar, name, verified badge, and emoji-rich text in a masonry grid. FAQ rows are thin-divider accordions with chevrons. Blog meta uses small black pill badges ("4 min read").

## Signature
- Near-black pages lit by violet/purple aurora gradients behind heroes and inside blog/feature thumbnails.
- A prompt bar as the hero centerpiece — type an idea, hit the purple Generate button — selling the AI workflow on sight.
- Inverted-contrast bento cards: white cards on black sections, black banners on white pages, minimal shadows.
- Loud pill tags in orange/blue/purple/violet against otherwise monochrome layouts; wizard-themed voice ("Blog of Spells").

## Do
- Keep one saturated blue for every primary CTA across both dark and light pages; let purple mark AI-specific actions (Generate).
- Use huge product screenshots and phone-mockup collages as the main imagery — the tool's own UI is the artwork.
- Alternate near-black gradient sections with clean white sections; invert card colors against each section's background.
- Put a functional-looking prompt input directly in the hero with the button docked inside it.
- Use pill-shaped, vividly colored category tags to break up monochrome grids.

## Don't
- Don't add borders or heavy drop shadows to buttons and cards — separation comes from fill contrast and radius.
- Don't use neon green, cyan, or red; the palette stays in the blue-violet band plus one orange accent for tags.
- Don't set headlines in a serif or condensed face; everything is one rounded geometric grotesque at varying weights.
- Don't run the purple aurora gradient on utility pages (templates, solutions) — those stay flat white with gray thumbnails.
- Don't center-align long body copy; only heroes and section intros are centered, feature rows are left-aligned two-column.
