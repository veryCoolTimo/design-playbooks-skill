## Overview
An editorial neobank aesthetic that runs two synchronized modes: near-black (#0F0F0F) canvases for the home page and magazine, and warm cream/ecru (#EFEDE6) with blush-pink hero tints (#F7E7E2) for product pages. Serif display headlines drop into italics on the key word ("financial *architects*", "more *rewarding*"), set against tiny grotesque body text. A single signal red (#E8442E) appears as the logo, list bullets, and badge chips — never as large fills. The result reads more like a finance magazine than a SaaS template.

## Layout
Centered serif wordmark in the top nav, with pill nav chips left (Business / Personal / Magazine, active one red) and pill CTAs right (Launch App, Book a Demo). Heroes are centered: large serif headline, one-line subcopy, small pill buttons, then a full-bleed nature photograph (mountains, forest, waves) with a floating product-UI screenshot overlaid. Sections alternate cream, blush, pale blue (#DDE9F6 testimonial bands), and black blocks (FAQ, insights, footer) in long full-width strips separated by hairline rules and tiny em-dash markers. Feature grids are 3-4 columns of sparse text cards with hairline vertical dividers and lots of whitespace; the magazine uses a strict card grid with thin column rules on black. Footer is black with a giant cropped serif "Coinshift" bleeding off the bottom edge.

## Components
Buttons: small pill capsules — black fill/white text on light sections, white or outlined pills on dark; secondary pills in light gray. Arrow glyphs (→) inside many CTAs. Cards: flat, borderless or hairline-bordered rectangles with barely-there 4-8px radius, no drop shadows; pricing cards are white panels on cream with tiny red index numerals (01, 02) and red dot bullets. Inputs: minimal — the newsletter field is a thin outlined pill with a circular black arrow submit button. FAQ rows are full-width accordions with +/arrow toggles and hairline separators on black. Product screenshots sit in framed light-UI windows floating over photography. Small uppercase/red chip tags label categories on magazine cards.

## Signature
- Serif headlines that switch to italic on the emotional keyword, paired with very small grotesque body text
- Dual-canvas rhythm: warm cream + blush product pages vs near-black home/magazine, stitched with hairline rules and em-dash section markers
- Full-bleed landscape photography (mountains, waves, forest) as hero backdrops with floating app screenshots
- Red used only as punctuation: logo, dot bullets, active nav chip, tiny numerals — never as button fill
- Giant cropped serif wordmark running off the bottom of the black footer

## Do
- Keep buttons as small, quiet pill capsules (black-on-light, white-on-dark) with arrow glyphs; let type do the persuading
- Use the serif display face for every H1/H2 and italicize exactly one phrase per headline
- Separate sections with 1px hairlines, small em-dash glyphs, and hard background swaps (cream → blush → blue → black)
- Ground abstract crypto features in natural photography and clean floating UI screenshots
- Reserve #E8442E for micro-accents: bullets, chips, index numbers, the wordmark

## Don't
- Don't use red as a large surface or primary button fill — the site never does
- Don't add drop shadows, glassmorphism, or gradients; every panel here is flat with hairline borders
- Don't round cards past ~8px or square off the buttons — pills are for actions, near-square is for containers
- Don't set headlines in bold sans; the identity depends on the light-weight serif with italic emphasis
- Don't crowd sections — feature grids run 3-4 sparse columns with tiny text and heavy whitespace
