## Overview
A dual-mode system: the home page runs near-black charcoal (#1F1C24) with white headlines and single accent-colored keywords, while product, pricing, developer, and customers pages run warm cream (#F7F4EF) punctuated by huge deep-indigo (#2B1B4E) bands with dramatically rounded corners. Purple #7856FF is the constant — every primary CTA, chart bar, and UI mock leans on it. Real dashboard screenshots and colorful data-viz do most of the visual storytelling; decorative flourishes are concentric geometric line motifs and occasional detailed flat illustration.

## Layout
Sticky slim top nav: wordmark left, center dropdown links, Log In (ghost pill) + Get Started (purple pill) right. Heroes are centered: big headline, one-line subhead, one or two pill CTAs, then a large framed product screenshot floating below. Section rhythm alternates background color blocks — cream, deep indigo, purple, back to cream — each dark band getting oversized rounded corners top and bottom so pages read as stacked rounded slabs. Content sits in a narrow centered column for text and 2-3 column card grids for features/case studies; generous vertical padding throughout. Small all-caps kicker labels (EXPLORE, MEASURE, DATA GOVERNANCE) introduce sections. Footer repeats on every page: cream, 3-4 link columns, with a soft pink-lavender gradient horizon just above it.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped; primary is solid #7856FF with white label, secondary is ghost with thin dark outline (or white outline on dark); teal/coral/yellow pill variants appear on pricing tiers. Cards: white on cream (or dark on indigo), 12-16px radius, hairline borders, essentially shadowless — flat and crisp; testimonial cards are plain white rectangles in a horizontal marquee. Product screenshots framed as white browser-like cards with subtle shadow. Pricing table: airy comparison grid with circled purple checkmarks and thin row dividers; plan cards carry a colored top border (teal/coral/yellow). Stat callouts: giant purple numerals over small dark captions. Icon-plus-caption trios use small purple line icons.

## Signature
- Constant #7856FF purple carried through CTAs, chart bars, icons, and in-product screenshots — the UI itself is the brand asset.
- Stacked color-band composition: cream and deep-indigo sections with exaggerated 32-48px rounded corners, like rounded slabs.
- Single accent-colored word inside otherwise monochrome headlines ("Powerful", "Simple", "Connect", "answered.") in coral, teal, or yellow.
- Concentric squares/circles line motifs and rich data-viz screenshots instead of stock photography.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Make every primary CTA a solid #7856FF pill with white text; keep secondary CTAs as thin-outline ghost pills.
- Alternate cream (#F7F4EF) and deep-indigo (#2B1B4E) full-width sections, rounding the dark bands' corners generously.
- Color exactly one keyword per headline with coral #FF7557, teal #80E1D9, or yellow #F8BC3B.
- Lead sections with tiny letterspaced all-caps kickers, then a large tight-set headline and short body paragraph.
- Show real dashboard/chart mockups in white rounded frames as the main imagery, with purple as the dominant chart color.

**Don't**
- Don't add heavy drop shadows or gradients to cards — surfaces here are flat with hairline borders.
- Don't use sharp-cornered or small-radius buttons; everything interactive is a full pill.
- Don't rainbow the headlines — accents are rationed to one word or one element per section.
- Don't use photography of people; the vocabulary is product UI, flat illustration, and geometric line motifs.
- Don't run long dark stretches on subpages — deep indigo appears as bounded rounded bands inside cream pages, not as the whole page (only the home page goes full dark).
