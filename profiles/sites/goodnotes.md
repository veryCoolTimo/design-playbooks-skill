## Overview
Warm, playful productivity brand built on lifestyle photography and iPad device mockups rather than abstract graphics. White canvas alternates with full-bleed color-block sections in deep navy (#1F233F), chartreuse (#CBD980), lavender (#B6B1CC), and powder blue (#BBCCE2). Hand-drawn squiggle lines in cyan and yellow loop across section boundaries, echoing the handwriting product. Everything with corners — buttons, photos, tablets, cards — is heavily rounded.

## Layout
Sticky white nav bar: left logo, right text links plus one cyan pill "Download" CTA. Home hero is a large rounded-corner photograph (person writing on a tablet) with white headline text overlaid directly on the image and app-store badges below. Features/pricing heroes are flat color-block panels with heading left, tilted device mockup right. Section rhythm = full-width color bands stacked edge to edge (white, navy, lime, white), each self-contained with its own contrast scheme. Inside bands: two-column zigzag rows alternating text and floating device/photo imagery; three-up card grids for feature clusters. Blog is a dense uniform card grid (3-4 columns) of image + category tag + title + date. Near-black (#140D0B) multi-column footer with newsletter signup.

## Components
- Buttons: pill-shaped, solid fill, no border, no shadow. Fill adapts to section: cyan (#49BFDE) on white, dark navy on light bands, dark teal-green (#1F5B56) on chartreuse, deep plum on lavender. Icon (Apple/Android/Windows glyph) + label is common.
- Cards: white, large radius (~16-24px), soft diffuse shadow; pricing cards are plain white on warm-gray canvas with checkmark feature lists and thin divider rules.
- Small pill badges/labels ("Available on iOS only", "Highlight", "Shape Tool") — outlined or tinted, pill radius, icon + text.
- FAQ: full-width accordion rows with hairline dividers and chevrons.
- Imagery: photos and iPad mockups always in rounded rectangles, often rotated a few degrees.

## Signature
- Hand-drawn looping squiggle strokes (cyan/yellow/dark green) that bleed across sections and behind device mockups.
- Color-block section bands in navy / chartreuse / lavender / powder blue, each with a section-matched button color instead of one global CTA color.
- Headline text overlaid with a handwritten-underline annotation ("Notes that ~correct your math homework~") mimicking in-app ink.
- Tilted iPad mockups showing real handwritten note content as the dominant imagery.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Alternate white sections with full-bleed saturated color bands and flip text color per band (white on navy, dark green on chartreuse).
- Round everything: pill buttons, 16-24px radii on photos, cards, and device frames.
- Recolor the pill CTA to harmonize with each band (teal-green on lime, plum on lavender) rather than forcing one brand color everywhere.
- Use casual lifestyle photography of people actually writing/studying, plus tablet screenshots with genuine handwritten content.
- Add loose hand-drawn squiggles or underlines as connective decoration between sections.

**Don't**
- Don't use gradients — every band and button is a flat solid fill.
- Don't put borders or heavy shadows on buttons; pills are flat solid shapes.
- Don't use sharp corners anywhere; even small badges and tags are fully rounded.
- Don't render device mockups perfectly straight — slight rotation and float is part of the look.
- Don't reach for pure black text; ink is a dark navy (#1F233F), with true near-black reserved for the footer.
