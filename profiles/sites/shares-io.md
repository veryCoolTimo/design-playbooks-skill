## Overview
A playful, sticker-driven fintech look: warm beige and white canvases, near-black plum text, and a violet-blue CTA, punctuated by candy-colored chat-bubble stickers (yellow, orange, green, pink) with hard black offset shadows. Photography of real friends and phone-in-hand product shots keep it human; the finance content (tickers, portfolio charts) is dressed up as social conversation. Reads as consumer-social first, brokerage second.

## Layout
Sparse top nav: small logo left, three plain text links right, no nav CTA. Hero is a split layout on a full-bleed beige band — heavy two-line headline plus short copy and one pill CTA on the left, a rounded photo card with floating speech-bubble stickers on the right. Sections alternate beige band → white → contained rounded panels (features carousel, press trio, download banner) → deep-purple footer. Generous whitespace; content sits in a centered container with large rounded cards acting as section frames. Features section uses a numbered 01/05 stepper with circular dots.

## Components
Buttons: solid #5B4FF2 pill with white text, no border, no shadow; same pill reused in footer on dark purple. Ghost/pill "Get early access" input-style button on the footer is a white pill. Cards: large radius (roughly 20-24px), flat fills (beige #E1DACF, off-white #F1F0EC/#F1EEF4), no borders, no elevation. Speech-bubble stickers: pill/rounded bubbles in saturated fills with a solid black offset drop shadow and small circular avatar chips overlapping the corner. Press cards end with underlined "Read More" links plus external-arrow glyph. App Store / Google Play badges in black.

## Signature
- Chat-bubble stickers with hard black offset shadows and avatar chips, scattered over photos and product shots
- One violet-blue (#5B4FF2) pill CTA per section against beige/white — the only interactive color
- Ticker sticker in orange ("AAPL NFLX GOOG" with arrows) treating stock data as street-poster graphics
- Beige-not-white base canvas with a near-black plum ink and deep-purple footer

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the warm beige #E1DACF as the hero/section base and reserve pure white for mid-page breathing room
- Use pill buttons in #5B4FF2 only; let stickers, not buttons, carry the loud colors
- Give every accent sticker a hard, non-blurred black offset shadow and an overlapping circular avatar
- Set headlines in a heavy geometric grotesque with tight tracking and sentence case ("Money moves with mates.")
- Wrap feature and press content in large-radius flat cards with no borders or drop shadows

**Don't**
- Don't add gradients, glassmorphism, or soft ambient shadows — every fill here is flat
- Don't use blue-link styling; links are underlined ink-colored text with an arrow glyph
- Don't render charts in serious-brokerage style; the portfolio UI stays rounded, friendly, green-line simple
- Don't multiply CTAs — each section carries exactly one pill button
- Don't cool down the palette; grays are warm (beige/greige), never neutral or blue-tinted
