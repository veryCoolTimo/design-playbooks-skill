## Overview
Crisp minimal-light SaaS design built on pure white canvas, near-black ink, and one saturated blue. Enormous ultra-bold headlines carry each page, while soft off-gray panel cards and product-UI illustrations do the storytelling. Deep navy (#0A102E) blocks punctuate the flow — testimonial bands, CTA duos, and a full-height footer — giving strong light/dark contrast rhythm without ever leaving the two-color-plus-blue system.

## Layout
- Nav: white bar, left logo + text links, right utility links (Support, Login) and a dark navy pill "Free trial" button with a circular arrow chip inside.
- Heroes: centered, massive black display headline (often 2 lines, ~80-120px), small kicker line above or subline below, minimal supporting copy. Home hero drops straight into a dark-framed product video/screenshot.
- Section rhythm: alternating light-gray rounded panels (feature story cards with heading left, illustration right) → white breathing space → dark navy blocks. Features page is a dense uniform grid of small white cards (3-4 columns) each with an icon, title, blurb, and "View this feature" blue link; "New"/"Coming up" yellow and gray badges on card corners.
- Footer: deep navy, starts with a "Want to try a test project first?" CTA row, then "Book a virtual meeting" input-style pill + 4-column link columns; generous height.
- Whitespace is generous; content sits in a wide centered container with fat vertical padding between sections.

## Components
- Buttons: fully pill-shaped. Primary nav CTA = dark navy fill, white label, white circular arrow icon at right. Secondary = white/light-gray pill with dark text + circular arrow. Blue (#2563EB) pill buttons appear on dark navy sections ("View all our features").
- Cards: white or very light gray (#F5F6F8), large radius (~16-24px), extremely soft diffuse shadows or none; pricing cards white with the featured tier flooded bright blue (#2563EB) with white text.
- Pricing checklist rows: small green circular check icons + short labels; hairline divider under the price.
- Toggle/segmented control on pricing: two pills, active = solid dark navy, inactive = white with light border.
- Inputs (footer meeting booker): white pill field with placeholder text and circular arrow submit chip.
- Badges: small rounded tags in yellow ("New") and muted gray ("Coming up").

## Signature
- Gigantic ultra-bold, tightly tracked black headlines on white — the loudest element on every page.
- Pill-everything: buttons, tags, toggles all fully rounded, most CTAs carrying a circular arrow chip inside the pill.
- Deep navy (#0A102E) full-bleed bands (testimonial with bleeding portrait photo, CTA pair, tall footer) alternating against white.
- Flat blue-line product illustrations and real floor-plan/unit-card UI screenshots as the primary imagery; almost no photography outside testimonials.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Let one massive black headline dominate each section; keep supporting copy short and small.
- Use pill buttons with an embedded circular arrow icon for every CTA, navy-filled for primary on light, blue-filled on dark navy.
- Alternate white sections, light-gray rounded panels, and deep navy blocks to create rhythm.
- Reserve #2563EB for interactive emphasis: featured pricing tier, links ("View this feature"), buttons on dark sections.
- Show the product via embedded UI screenshots and flat blue line illustrations inside rounded cards.

**Don't**
- Don't use square or lightly-rounded buttons — everything interactive is a full pill.
- Don't introduce additional hues beyond blue, navy, and small green/yellow functional accents.
- Don't apply heavy borders or hard shadows; cards rely on background-tone shifts and very soft blur.
- Don't use decorative stock photography in content sections — photos belong only to testimonial portraits.
- Don't loosen headline tracking or lighten headline weight; the identity depends on black, tight, oversized type.
