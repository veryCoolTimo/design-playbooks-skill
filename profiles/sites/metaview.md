## Overview
Warm cream canvas punctuated by near-black panels and vivid gradient-mesh art in electric blue-to-mint. The gradient work is the identity: 3D ribbon and wave renders that flow behind heroes, fill card headers, and coil across the bright cobalt footer band. Type is a single friendly geometric grotesque used at every scale, and mint-green pill CTAs pop against both cream and black.

## Layout
Sticky-feeling top nav on cream: left logo, centered text links (Customers, Security & Privacy, Resources, Pricing, Careers), right "Sign in" plus mint pill CTA. Heroes are centered stacked headlines on cream (home, pricing, security) or a rounded near-black card with gradient ribbon art and inset photo (careers). Sections alternate cream, near-black rounded panels, and soft blush (#F5E9DE) blocks, ending in a recurring pattern: dark "Ready to try Metaview?" CTA banner with inline email field, then a full-bleed cobalt-blue footer band with green-blue ribbon graphics behind a cream rounded footer card. Content sits in a generous centered container; card grids run 2-3 columns with wide gutters.

## Components
Buttons: solid mint (#7DF0A8) pills with black text, no border or shadow; secondary actions are plain text links. Email capture is a pill input paired with a nested mint pill button (dark pill on dark banners, cream pill in hero). Cards: white or blush fills, 16-24px radius, very soft diffuse drop shadows, no visible borders; feature cards open with a small squircle icon tile holding a blue-green gradient glyph. Testimonial and CTA panels are near-black rounded rectangles with white text and blue/mint highlighted phrases. Pricing cards are white with checklist rows, blue "All features" links, and mint pill CTAs; "Chosen by" logo strips sit beneath in attached trays. FAQ rows are thin-divider accordions with +/- toggles on a blush panel.

## Signature
- Blue-to-mint 3D gradient ribbons and mesh waves as recurring hero, card, and footer art
- Cream (#F7F2E9) base alternating with rounded near-black panels — high-contrast rhythm instead of white
- Mint-green pill CTA repeated identically on every page, including inside dark banners
- Full-bleed cobalt #1F3DFF footer band with ribbon graphics wrapping a cream footer card
- Squircle icon tiles with gradient glyphs marking every feature card

## Do
- Reuse the exact blue→mint gradient family (cobalt #1F3DFF through #4FE3C1/#7DF0A8) for all decorative art
- Alternate cream, blush, and rounded near-black sections to pace long pages
- Keep every primary CTA a mint pill with black text, even on dark backgrounds
- End pages with the dark email-capture banner followed by the cobalt ribbon footer
- Use one geometric grotesque for both display and body, differentiating only by weight and size

## Don't
- Don't use pure white page backgrounds — the canvas is warm cream throughout
- Don't add borders or hard shadows to cards; keep fills clean with soft diffuse shadows
- Don't introduce warm accent colors (red/orange/yellow) — the palette stays blue-green-mint plus neutrals
- Don't square off buttons; every button and input is a full pill
- Don't render dark sections as full-bleed strips (except the footer) — dark panels are inset rounded cards
