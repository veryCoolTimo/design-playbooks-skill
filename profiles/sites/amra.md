## Overview
A restrained, gallery-like minimalism built on white space, a single quiet grotesque typeface, and photography doing the emotional work. Color arrives through one recurring motif — a large soft-gradient orb (lavender-to-teal) — and pale periwinkle surfaces, while everything else stays monochrome. The Insights page flips the whole canvas to near-black (#161616), proving the system works inverted. Note: despite the EL metadata tag, no serif appears anywhere; this is strictly sans-serif minimalism.

## Layout
Sticky-free, ultra-thin top nav: lowercase wordmark left, four tiny uppercase links centered, one pill "SIGN IN" button right. Heroes are text-first: a centered 2-3 line headline on white, followed by a full-bleed image or the signature gradient orb. Sections alternate: centered statement headline → asymmetric two-column block (image left, small labeled text columns right) → full-width data visual (wireframe globe, world map, avatar rows). Generous vertical padding (often a full viewport of white between sections). Company page uses a 2-col label/value grid ("Values", "Leadership") with tiny numbered markers. Every page ends identically: "See what technology can do for music" headline above the gradient orb cresting the footer like a horizon.

## Components
Buttons: small pill shape; primary CTA is a pale lavender fill (#C6BFF3) with dark text ("SIGN IN"); secondary actions are tiny pill outlines or dot-prefixed uppercase micro-labels ("● EXPLORE THE PLATFORM"). Cards: near-square corners (2-6px), flat, no shadows; insight cards are dark tiles with a tiny dot + uppercase category label, headline, then a large circular image crop; news cards on light pages use flat periwinkle fills. Filter chips on Insights are small white/outline pills. Job listings are full-width hairline-divided rows with arrow glyphs. Feature cards on Platform sit on light-gray (#F0F0EE) panels with embedded charts. No visible inputs; no gradients on controls — gradients are reserved for the orb.

## Signature
- The gradient orb: a huge soft lavender-teal sphere used as hero art and as a rising "horizon" above every footer.
- Circular image crops inside rectangular dark cards (Insights grid) — circle-in-square is the recurring geometric motif.
- Tiny uppercase micro-labels with a leading dot or index number ("01", "● PRESS") annotating otherwise unlabeled white space.
- One typeface, two colors of text (black + mid-gray), everything else expressed through photography and the orb.

## Do / Don't
- Do keep headlines short, centered, sentence-case, and surrounded by extravagant white space.
- Do reserve the lavender-teal gradient exclusively for the orb motif; keep UI chrome flat.
- Do use dot- or number-prefixed uppercase micro-labels to structure sections instead of heavy subheads.
- Do crop imagery into circles when placing it inside dark rectangular cards.
- Do let full sections invert to near-black (#161616) while keeping type and pill shapes identical.
- Don't introduce serifs or decorative display faces — the system is a single neutral grotesque.
- Don't add shadows, borders, or heavy rounding to cards; corners stay near-square and surfaces flat.
- Don't use saturated brand color for buttons; CTAs stay pale lavender pills with dark text.
- Don't crowd sections — never stack two dense blocks without a full band of white (or black) between them.
- Don't put the gradient anywhere except the orb; text and backgrounds remain solid.
