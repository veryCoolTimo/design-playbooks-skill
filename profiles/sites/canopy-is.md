## Overview
Near-black dark-tech canvas softened by warm, human touches: a violet pill CTA, olive-gold highlighter marks swiped across keyword phrases in headings, and dreamy pastel illustrations for content thumbnails. Product screenshots (light-mode app UI) sit inside tinted rounded panels, so the light UI glows against the dark page. The overall feel is calm and coaching-like rather than aggressive SaaS.

## Layout
Slim top nav: logo left, text links center-left, "Sign in" + violet pill CTA right; active nav item gets a subtle dark-gray pill background. Heroes are fully centered: large bold headline (with 1-2 highlighted phrases), one-line subhead, single CTA, then a large media block. Section rhythm alternates two-column rows (product screenshot in a colored rounded frame on one side, short heading + paragraph on the other, sides swapping each row), punctuated by centered pull-quote testimonials with a small circular avatar underneath. A grayscale "Trusted by 30,000+ managers..." logo strip recurs on most pages. Every page ends with a centered violet CTA, a 4-column footer, and a playful "Now on iOS" app icon → arrow → App Store badge coda.

## Components
Primary button: solid violet (#8b5cf6) pill, white bold label, no shadow. Secondary: transparent pill with 1px gray border (pricing "Start for free"), or dark ghost button. Cards: #1a1a1c fill with faint 1px border, 12-16px radius; the featured pricing tier gets a violet border. Badges: small yellow pill tags ("New", "Upcoming live event") with dark text. Toggle switch fills violet when on. Pricing comparison table uses thin hairline row dividers and plain checkmarks. Webinar/resource cards pair a pastel illustration thumbnail (rounded) with title + truncated teaser on the dark card. Testimonials are quote-first: large white quote line, small gray attribution, tiny round avatar.

## Signature
- Olive-gold "highlighter marker" background swiped behind key words in nearly every section heading — the single most recognizable motif.
- Light-mode product screenshots framed inside muted-tint rounded panels (plum, ink-green, khaki) floating on the black canvas.
- Soft pastel gradient illustrations (ladders, plants, mountains, candles) as content art, contrasting deliberately with the dark UI.
- Plant emojis as pricing-tier icons (seedling, potted plant, tree) and a friendly hand-drawn-feeling "Now on iOS" footer coda.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Highlight 1-3 key words per heading with the dark olive-gold marker treatment; keep the rest plain white.
- Use one violet pill CTA per section, always with the same "Start for free" weight and shape.
- Frame light screenshots or media in rounded, subtly tinted panels rather than dropping them bare on black.
- Break up feature rows with centered single-line testimonials and circular avatars.
- Keep logo strips and secondary content at low opacity gray so violet and gold stay the only loud colors.

Don't:
- Don't use pure #000 or bright white section backgrounds — everything lives between #101010 and #1c1c1c.
- Don't add glows, neon gradients, or heavy shadows; surfaces are flat with hairline borders.
- Don't square off the buttons — they are pills sitewide, never 4-6px rectangles.
- Don't use violet for body links and badges everywhere; badges are yellow, inline links violet, highlights olive — the roles don't swap.
- Don't crowd sections: each row carries one screenshot, one short heading, one short paragraph, generous vertical air.
