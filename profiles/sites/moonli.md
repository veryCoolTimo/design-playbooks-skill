## Overview
A three-color system: white page, near-black ink, and one loud lime green. Everything lives inside big rounded panels floating on white, illustrated throughout with thin-line black doodles (plugs, coins, laptops, plants) tipped with tiny lime accents. The tone is playful indie-studio, not corporate crypto — small body text, big friendly headlines, highlighter-marker emphasis on single words.

## Layout
Slim centered top nav: black dot logo + wordmark left, small text links center, pill "Stake Now" CTA right. Hero is not full-bleed — it's a giant rounded lime card inset from the page edges with a centered black headline and line illustrations flanking it; a black circular scroll button overlaps its bottom edge. Sections alternate between contained rounded panels (gray, lime, black) and open white sections with plain headlines. A horizontal card carousel bleeds off the right edge for Networks. Achievement cards sit in a staggered/masonry two-column cluster rather than a strict grid. Numbered (01/02) columns with thin top rules for the Reliability facts. Footer is a full-width black bar repeating the nav.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped; the "Stake Now" CTA is a white pill with a hairline gray border on light bg and inverts on the black footer; the scroll affordance is a solid black circle with white chevron. Cards: large radius (~16-24px), flat fills in white, light gray #F0F0EF, lime, or black — no shadows except a whisper-soft one on the white network cards; no borders. Inline emphasis uses lime rounded-rectangle highlight behind single words ("support", "what?"). Social links are small solid black circles with white glyphs. Network cards pair a bold name with a small circular token badge.

## Signature
- One acid-lime green (#B4F53C) doing all the accent work against strict black-and-white
- Hero as an inset rounded lime card instead of a full-bleed section
- Hand-drawn-feel thin-line black illustrations with lime droplets/details scattered through every section
- Highlighter-style lime pill behind single emphasized words in headlines

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the palette to exactly white, near-black, and lime; use gray #F0F0EF only as a quiet panel fill
- Put major sections inside large-radius rounded containers inset from the viewport, alternating lime/gray/black fills
- Use thin monoline black illustrations with small lime accents instead of photos or 3D renders
- Emphasize one word per headline with a lime rounded highlight
- Keep buttons as small, quiet pills — the color blocks carry the energy, not the CTAs

Don't:
- Add gradients, glassmorphism, or glow effects — every fill here is flat
- Use blue/purple crypto clichés or token-chart imagery
- Give cards heavy shadows or visible borders; separation comes from fill contrast
- Set body text large — this design pairs oversized headlines with deliberately small paragraph text
- Stretch the hero full-bleed; the inset rounded card is the identity
