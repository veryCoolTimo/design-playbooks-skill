## Overview
Terminal-schematic brutalism in two invertible themes: near-black canvas with acid chartreuse accents (home) and a full acid-green canvas with black blocks (alt). Everything is uppercase monospace, gridded, and annotated like an engineering diagram. Zero softness — no rounded corners, no gradients, no photography; the only imagery is pixel-cluster glyphs and circuit-like logo marks.

## Layout
Sticky-thin top nav: wordmark left, sparse uppercase links, small solid chartreuse CTA block at the far right. Hero is oversized staircase-aligned uppercase headline ("PRIVACY-FOCUSED / TO CONNECT / THE WORLD") over a dotted-grid background with scattered pixel-block ornaments and tiny corner metadata labels. Sections alternate: big stat quads (315K / 330K / 130K / 65) in hairline-ruled cells, black feature panels, a full-width chartreuse marquee strip ("TRANSGATE · TRANSGATE"), a 3+2 feature grid with crosshair/plus markers at cell corners, a monospace benchmark data table, logo rows for partners/backers, and a giant footer glyph. Rhythm is dense and modular — thin 1px rules divide everything; whitespace comes from the dotted grid field, not padding generosity.

## Components
Buttons: small, sharp-cornered solid chartreuse rectangles with black uppercase mono text; secondary actions are bare bracketed/underlined mono labels. Cards: borderless black panels or hairline-outlined cells with tiny index numbers (01, 02...) and corner tick/plus marks; no shadows anywhere. Tables: true data-table styling — thin rules, mono digits, chartreuse header row. Ornament vocabulary: pixel-cluster squares, crosshairs, small filled squares as bullets, corner coordinate labels.

## Signature
- Acid chartreuse (#c6f21e) + black duotone that fully inverts between themes (green-on-black vs black-on-green)
- Everything set in one uppercase techno monospace, including body copy and giant display headlines
- Blueprint/HUD annotation layer: dotted grids, crosshairs, index numbers, tiny corner metadata labels on every section
- Pixel-block cluster motifs standing in for illustration; oversized "P" glyph as footer artwork

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the palette strictly duotone: black canvas, chartreuse for CTAs, stats highlights, and the marquee strip
- Set all text — headlines, nav, body — in one monospace family, uppercase, with staircase/offset headline alignment
- Divide sections with 1px hairlines and dotted-grid backgrounds; add corner ticks, plus marks, and 01/02 index labels to cells
- Use hard-edged rectangles for every element; a small solid chartreuse block is the CTA pattern
- Present numbers as raw data: huge mono stats and bordered benchmark tables

Don't:
- Add border-radius, drop shadows, glassmorphism, or gradients — the language is flat and sharp
- Introduce a second accent hue (no blues/purples); white is reserved for rare highlight chips
- Use photography, 3D renders, or organic illustration; imagery stays pixel-cluster/schematic
- Set body copy in a humanist sans or mixed case — that breaks the terminal voice
- Loosen the density with big airy padding; structure comes from tight modular rules, not whitespace
