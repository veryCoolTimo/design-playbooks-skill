## Overview
Warm minimal-light landing page: a speckled off-white paper canvas, near-black charcoal type, and one indigo-purple CTA color, punctuated by saturated coral-to-purple gradient product cards. Almost everything visual is a product screenshot; the page chrome itself stays quiet and typographic. The result reads as calm stationery wrapping a colorful multiplayer app.

## Layout
Single sticky-thin top nav: wordmark left, a row of circular animal-avatar badges plus one purple "Get Ready" button right — no visible nav links. Hero is centered stacked type (tiny purple all-caps eyebrow, two-line bold headline, short paragraph) sitting directly above a large layered app screenshot that bleeds behind the fold. Section rhythm alternates: centered H2 + short subhead, then either a full-width horizontal strip of gradient meeting cards or a loose collage of overlapping tilted screenshot cards with small hand-drawn accent strokes. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; content column stays narrow (~700px) for text while imagery goes wide or edge-to-edge. Finale: dark full-width calendar screenshot with a purple glow, then a centered closing pitch and gradient email-capture pill. Minimal two-line footer.

## Components
- Buttons: solid indigo-purple (#7364F8) rounded-rectangle (~10px) with white bold label; the footer email capture is a full pill with a coral-to-purple horizontal gradient, inline placeholder text left and a darker "Get Ready" segment right.
- Cards: two families — (1) meeting-window cards with vivid multicolor gradient headers (coral/teal/orange/green) over white bodies, large radius, soft shadows; (2) feature cards that are entirely gradient-filled (orange-to-purple, blue-to-purple) with white titles and embedded UI fragments, slightly rotated and overlapping.
- Text highlight: lavender (#CBC5F5) selection-style block behind headline words with a cursor flag ("Febby") mimicking multiplayer text selection.
- Dark UI panels: near-black (#1E1D1B) menus and toolbars inside screenshots, high radius, used as contrast against the pale canvas.
- Small chips/tags: pale outlined pills listing features ("Desktop App", "Keyboard Shortcuts") mid-page.

## Signature
- Off-white canvas with visible fine noise/speckle grain — paper texture instead of flat white.
- Multiplayer-cursor motif: highlighted headline text with a named cursor flag, avatar stacks everywhere.
- Saturated coral-orange-to-purple gradients confined to cards and the email pill, never the page background.
- Collage sections: rotated, overlapping screenshot cards with tiny hand-drawn dash accents.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the page background a single warm off-white (#F6F4F0) with subtle grain; let color live inside product imagery.
- Center every section header; use bold tight two-to-three-word-per-line headlines with sentence case.
- Use one flat indigo-purple for the standard CTA and reserve the coral-purple gradient for the single hero-moment email pill.
- Show features as real app windows — gradient-headed meeting cards, dark menus — instead of icon grids.
- Add playful multiplayer cues: avatar clusters, selection highlights with name flags, slight card rotation.

Don't:
- Don't add a conventional multi-link navbar or mega-menu; the nav is wordmark + avatars + one button.
- Don't use pure white backgrounds or hard borders around sections; separation comes from whitespace.
- Don't spread gradients onto backgrounds or text; page chrome stays charcoal-on-cream.
- Don't use serifs or thin display weights; all type is one bold-leaning grotesque.
- Don't align imagery to a rigid grid in feature sections — the collage overlap and tilt are intentional.
