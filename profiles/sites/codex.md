## Overview
High-contrast black-and-acid design: a near-black canvas alternating with cool light-gray bands, punctuated by a single electric chartreuse used for CTAs, a glowing 3D "CODEX" cube motif, and stat highlights. Headlines are large, uppercase, regular-weight grotesque set flush and tight, giving an industrial data-terminal feel. Glossy dark 3D renders (chrome petals, translucent neon cubes) supply all imagery; there is no photography except team shots on About.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on a white/light pill strip at right: logo left, five uppercase text links plus a chartreuse "GET STARTED" pill-corner button. Heroes are text-first: giant uppercase multi-line headline (centered on home/customers, left-aligned on blog/product) over a dark 3D background, small paragraph beneath, one or two compact CTA buttons. Sections alternate strictly dark → light-gray → dark, each full-bleed; customer-logo rows sit in dashed/cornered bracket frames. Mid-page rhythm mixes 3-column feature-card grids, a live chart/terminal demo panel, big-number stat rows (4.5M / 57 / 1B) with thin rules above labels, and alternating image-left/right case-study pairs. Footer is a dark rounded slab with an email signup and 3-column link list.

## Components
Buttons: small-radius rectangles, black fill with chartreuse icon glyph + uppercase label on light grounds, chartreuse fill with black text for the primary CTA; secondary buttons are white/outlined dark. Cards: large-radius (16-24px) flat fills in olive-gray, chartreuse, and white for the home feature trio; dark glossy testimonial cards with embedded 3D artwork and small avatar-quote footers. Stat callouts use a thin top rule + huge numeral + small uppercase label. Inputs: light rounded fields paired with a dark or chartreuse SIGN UP button. Comparison table on product uses magenta/purple check squares against neutral gray dashes. Code demo panel: black terminal with syntax-highlighted query/response and chartreuse-bordered response pane.

## Signature
- One acid chartreuse (#DFFF2E) against pure black and cool gray — no other saturated color except purple checks in the comparison table
- Glowing 3D chartreuse "CODEX" cube and chrome-black petal renders recurring across every page
- Regular-weight uppercase grotesque headlines at very large sizes, tightly leaded, often 3-4 stacked lines
- Dashed bracket-corner frames around partner logos; thin-rule stat rows with oversized numerals

## Do
- Reserve chartreuse for CTAs, stat bands, and glow accents; keep everything else black, white, or gray
- Set all headlines uppercase in a regular-weight grotesque with tight leading; scale up rather than bolding
- Alternate full-bleed dark and light-gray sections down the page for rhythm
- Pair small CTA buttons with a tiny chartreuse glyph/icon before the uppercase label
- Use big-numeral stats with thin rules and small uppercase labels to carry proof points

## Don't
- Don't use bold or condensed display weights — the type voice is regular weight at large size
- Don't introduce blues, reds, or gradients as UI colors; the palette is monochrome plus one acid green
- Don't round buttons into pills; corners stay near-square while cards go generously rounded
- Don't use stock photography — imagery is glossy 3D renders of cubes and chrome forms
- Don't set headlines in sentence case or loose tracking; uppercase and tight is the identity
