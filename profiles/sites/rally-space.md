## Overview
Warm cream canvas, near-black ink, and a shouty hand-printed condensed display face give Rally a zine-meets-broadcast feel. Color is reserved almost entirely for embedded product mockups and thumbnails — saturated purple, magenta, and blue gradients that pop hard against the neutral page. Chrome (buttons, feature cards, footer) is solid black, so the page reads as black-on-cream with jewel-toned media windows.

## Layout
Sticky-slim top nav: logo left (home page centers the logo above the hero), two text links, single black "Join waitlist" button right. Hero is centered stacked type with a one-field email + button inline form, followed by a video still. Sections alternate: centered display headline blocks, a two-column text-plus-product-screenshot split, a 2-column grid of black feature cards, and a repeated email-capture CTA before the footer. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; content column is narrow relative to the 1920px canvas. Footer is full-bleed black with monospace 4-column link lists; blog page wraps its content in a huge rounded light card that sits on top of the black footer.

## Components
Buttons: solid black fill, white label, small radius (~6px), no border or shadow; secondary CTA on gradient banner is white-on-translucent-black. Inputs: white pill-ish rounded field with placeholder, paired inline with a black submit button. Feature cards: solid near-black rounded rectangles (~12-16px), white bold title + gray body, no borders, masonry-ish 2-column grid. Blog cards: white/light cards with very large radius (~24px), image on top with its own rounded corners, monospace author + date meta row with small circular avatar. Small labels ("For teams", "Rally for Product Updates") are outlined pill chips. Checkmark bullet lists use filled black circular check icons.

## Signature
- Ultra-condensed, black-weight display type with rough hand-inked edges used at very large sizes for every headline — the loudest element on an otherwise quiet page
- Warm cream (#F2EDE6) background with pure black chrome; all vivid color lives inside product screenshots and gradient thumbnails
- Monospace typography for all metadata: dates, authors, footer links, section eyebrows ("Product Storyboards", "Features")
- Hand-drawn tilted "rally" wordmark logo repeated as a sticker motif inside illustrations

## Do
- Keep the page surface cream and the interactive chrome (buttons, feature cards, footer) solid black — two-tone first, color second
- Confine saturated purples/pinks/blues to media: screenshots, video stills, gradient cards
- Set headlines in the condensed black display face at aggressive sizes, sentence case, tight leading
- Use monospace for eyebrows, dates, bylines, and footer link columns
- Pair every email-capture form as inline field + black button, and repeat the CTA at page bottom

## Don't
- Don't tint buttons or links with brand color — the CTA is always black on cream (or white on dark)
- Don't add borders or drop shadows to cards; separation comes from fill contrast and radius alone
- Don't use the display face for body copy or UI labels; it is headline-only
- Don't cool the canvas to pure white or gray — the warm paper tone is core to the look
- Don't crowd sections; each headline block gets a large whitespace buffer above and below
