## Overview
A blueprint-flavored big-type page built on two electric blues: full-bleed ultramarine hero panels (#072af8) sitting on a flat gray "drafting table" canvas (#d3d3d3) with a faint dashed grid. Nearly all text — headlines and body alike — is set in saturated blue (#1f00fe); the only warm note is the orange CTA (#ff622d). Headlines are gigantic condensed all-caps grotesque paired with playful monoline sketch illustrations (walking figure, piggy banks, bucket of balls).

## Layout
Minimal nav: script "Patch" wordmark left, orange BOOK A CALL pill-less button plus hamburger right, all floating on the blue hero. Hero is a centered two-line uppercase headline at extreme size, with a white square illustration card below and small caption labels on rules to either side. The page alternates full-width blue slabs and gray-canvas sections; each section is indexed with numbered corner chips (01-07, diagonal-slash squares) like sheet annotations. Content sits in white "spec sheet" cards with internal ruled dividers (label row / number cell / title cell / body). A marquee ticker strip of white uppercase-text tiles separates sections; a horizontal logo strip and a 3-column comparison pricing grid appear mid-page. Footer flips to near-black (#212121) with a giant inline newsletter form.

## Components
Buttons: solid orange rectangles (#ff622d), white bold uppercase labels, ~2-4px radius, no shadow, some with arrow glyph. Cards: white fills, thin blue 1px borders on outlined variants, hairline internal dividers, tiny radius, no shadows. FAQ rows: white bars with 1px blue border, numbered tab chip top-left, blue question text, arrow-down icon cell on the right. Pricing table: bordered plan cards (highlighted plan gets light gray fill), blue circular check tokens vs gray x tokens. Inputs (footer): underline-only fields, gray placeholder on dark, arrow submit. Team photos rendered as blue halftone/duotone images inside white cards with a small blue circular "+" badge.

## Signature
- Everything reads blueprint: dashed grid over gray canvas, numbered section chips, ruled spec-sheet cards, "PROCESS - STEPS" style micro-labels.
- Monochrome blue typography — even paragraphs are #1f00fe, not black; white is reserved for type on blue.
- Hand-drawn black-and-white doodle illustrations plus flat blue/orange/white ball-pit dots as the only imagery besides duotone photos.
- Single orange accent used strictly for CTAs (BOOK A CALL, INQUIRE, GET YOURS), instantly findable against the blue/gray field.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set headlines in huge condensed uppercase, 2 lines max, white on blue or blue on gray.
- Keep all body copy blue (#1f00fe); treat black as forbidden outside the footer.
- Frame content as annotated spec cards: micro-label row, big index number (01/02/03), hairline dividers, corner icon cell.
- Reserve orange exclusively for action buttons and keep them small, rectangular, uppercase.
- Use sketchy monoline illustrations or blue-halftone photos instead of full-color imagery.

Don't:
- Don't add drop shadows or large corner radii — surfaces stay flat with 1px borders and near-square corners.
- Don't introduce extra hues; the system is two blues + orange + gray/white/near-black only.
- Don't use mixed-case or light-weight headlines — display type is always heavy and all-caps.
- Don't fill the canvas edge-to-edge with white; keep the gray grid visible between floating cards.
- Don't use orange for links, icons, or decorations — that dilutes its CTA-only role.
