## Overview
Near-black dev-tool aesthetic built around glowing gradient vignettes: deep navy-to-black canvas with soft blue, violet, and one warm orange accent bleeding out of illustrations. White display headlines on pure dark, muted gray body text, and code-block screenshots doing much of the storytelling. The mood is quiet and cinematic — dark UI mockups floating in shadow rather than flat marketing panels.

## Layout
- Slim top nav: logo left, four text links center, single light "Get API key" pill right; no borders or fill behind the nav.
- Home hero is centered: two-line headline, short subhead, paired CTAs (blue primary + light secondary), then a tabbed strip and a large floating composite of dark app/code screenshots.
- Section rhythm: large centered H2 → bento-style card grid (2-up mixed widths), then a numbered 4-step zig-zag "get started" list alternating text and illustration columns.
- Pricing is a classic 3-column card row with the middle card highlighted by a blue gradient wash and "Most Popular" pill; huge price numerals, checkmark feature lists.
- Generous vertical whitespace between sections (200px+ feel); content column ~1200px, everything center-aligned at the section level.

## Components
- Primary button: saturated royal-blue fill (#3b5bdb-ish) with subtle outer glow, white label, small radius (~6px). Secondary: near-white/light-gray fill with dark label, same size.
- Cards: very dark panels (#12131a-ish) with faint 1px lighter borders, medium radius, interior gradient glows (blue/violet/orange) and dotted-texture corners on pricing cards; screenshots and code blocks embedded edge-to-edge inside.
- Code blocks: dark navy panels with syntax coloring (orange/blue/green tokens), used as first-class visual content.
- Testimonial cards: gradient-tinted (magenta, teal-navy, purple) with small circular avatars.
- Feature lists: thin checkmarks, small "i" info dots; icon tiles are simple line glyphs in rounded squares.

## Signature
- Glowing orange "open box" and key illustrations — a single warm accent burning through an otherwise cold blue/navy palette.
- Bento grids of dark UI/code screenshots that blend into the black canvas, separated only by faint borders and inner glows.
- Blue gradient wash + dotted texture marking the featured pricing tier.
- Alternating light/blue CTA pairs repeated at hero, mid-page, and footer.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas near-black and let gradient glows (blue, violet, one orange focal point) emanate from inside cards, not the page background.
- Use real dark-mode code/UI screenshots as hero and card content; give code panels syntax color.
- Pair the blue primary button with a light neutral secondary in every CTA cluster.
- Highlight one pricing tier with a blue gradient fill and a "Most Popular" pill while keeping siblings flat dark.
- Center headlines and keep body copy muted gray, short, and small relative to the large white H2s.

Don't:
- Don't introduce pure-white sections or light-mode panels — the whole site stays dark end to end.
- Don't use large border radii or pill-shaped cards; corners stay modest (buttons ~6px, cards ~10-12px).
- Don't add heavy drop shadows or visible card outlines — separation comes from faint 1px borders and glow.
- Don't spread the warm orange broadly; it appears only as a concentrated glow inside select illustrations.
- Don't clutter the nav or add banners — it stays to four links and one CTA.
