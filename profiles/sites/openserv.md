## Overview
Near-black violet-tinted dark theme where everything glows faintly purple. Headlines are white with one or two key words flipped to a violet gradient ("Empowering *Autonomy.*", "Browse *Autonomous AI* Agents"). Depth comes from soft radial purple glows behind heroes, horizontal light-streak dividers between sections, and faint circuit-board line motifs in page margins. Content sits in slightly-lighter charcoal-purple cards with hairline borders.

## Layout
Sticky-feeling top nav: logo left, three center links (About, FAQs, Contact), Twitter/Discord icon buttons right; FAQ page collapses to a hamburger. Heroes are centered: large headline, one-line muted subhead, then either a CTA pair (pill primary + ghost secondary joined by a thin connector line) or a product mockup in a light-framed browser window. Home alternates full-width centered sections with 2-column card rows and side-by-side text/diagram splits; small uppercase letter-spaced eyebrow labels (FIND, CUSTOMIZE, INTEGRATE, EARN) with flanking dash-dot rules introduce each section. Generous vertical whitespace; a glowing horizon line separates hero from body on inner pages. Footer: logo + address left, Product/Company link columns right.

## Components
- Buttons: primary is a purple (#6D28D9) pill with white label ("Join Waitlist"); secondary is a ghost pill with thin purple border. Contact form Submit is a full-width medium-radius dark charcoal bar (not purple).
- Cards: #12–#18 charcoal-purple fills, 1px low-contrast borders, 12-16px radius; feature cards contain mini agent-network diagrams with circular avatars connected by thin lines; the highlighted card gets a violet border glow.
- FAQ accordions: dark rows with hairline borders, right-aligned +/− toggles; open item gets a slightly lighter fill and lavender answer text.
- Inputs: dark near-black fills, subtle 1px borders, ~8px radius, gray placeholder text.
- Icons: small squircle tiles with thin line-art glyphs; stat tiles and dashboard-style widgets (donut chart, wallet chip) for the monetization section.
- Team cards: black-and-white portrait photos, name + role, LinkedIn glyph.

## Signature
- Two-tone headlines: white base with select words in violet-to-lavender gradient.
- Glowing purple horizon-line dividers and radial hero glows on a near-black violet canvas.
- Uppercase micro-eyebrows flanked by dash-and-dot rules above every section title.
- Circuit-trace line ornaments and floating orange/gray dot particles in outer margins.
- Agent-network diagrams: circular avatars linked by thin connector lines inside cards.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas near-black with a violet cast; lighten card fills only one step above it.
- Gradient-tint one or two headline words in violet; leave the rest pure white.
- Use pill CTAs in solid purple paired with a ghost-outline secondary.
- Introduce sections with tiny uppercase tracked eyebrow labels between dash-dot rules.
- Convert photography to black-and-white (team grid) to stay inside the monochrome-plus-purple scheme.

Don't:
- Don't use pure #000 flat backgrounds — the site always layers soft purple glows and gradients.
- Don't add heavy drop shadows; separation is done with hairline borders and glow, not shadow.
- Don't introduce saturated secondary hues beyond the small orange particle accents.
- Don't left-align hero copy — heroes and section titles are consistently centered.
- Don't use thick borders or high-contrast card outlines; borders stay near-invisible 1px.
