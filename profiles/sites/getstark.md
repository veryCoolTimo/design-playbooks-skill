## Overview
Warm cream canvas with deep navy ink, punctuated by an electric indigo-to-magenta gradient hero and a candy set of pastel-saturated panel colors (teal, coral, lavender, marigold). Playful but disciplined: hand-drawn squiggle arrows, highlighter marks behind headline words, and monospace uppercase eyebrows give it a sticker-sheet energy while product-UI screenshots keep it credible. Everything sits on big rounded color blocks rather than borders or shadows.

## Layout
Dark gradient nav bar (indigo) with uppercase links and a pill sign-up button; hero is left-text over a vivid #3b1ed3-to-#893fa5 gradient with a cutout team photo and floating UI cards on the right. Below the fold the page flips to cream (#f6f6ea) and runs alternating two-column feature rows: text on one side, a product screenshot floating on a large rounded color panel (teal, yellow, mint) on the other. Center-aligned section headers with yellow highlighter behind key phrases set the rhythm; a logo strip, community avatar cluster, full-width indigo quote block, three-step signup row, and a masonry grid of colored feature cards close it out. Footer is solid deep navy. Whitespace is generous; sections breathe with 150px+ gaps.

## Components
Primary CTA: solid marigold yellow (#fedb62) with navy text, small radius (~6-8px); secondary hero button is outlined white on the gradient. Mid-page CTAs are compact indigo (#3820d3) buttons with white text, same small radius. Feature panels and cards are big flat color rectangles with 24-32px radius, no borders, no drop shadows — white UI-screenshot cards float on top with a soft ambient shadow. Eyebrow labels are monospace, uppercase, letterspaced, colored indigo (or yellow on the gradient). Quote block is a full-width indigo rounded card with white display text. Feature-grid cards carry monospace uppercase captions beneath hand-drawn-styled UI vignettes.

## Signature
- Indigo-to-magenta gradient hero on an otherwise cream page — the only dark, saturated full-bleed moment besides the navy footer
- Yellow highlighter swipes behind headline words ("to minutes", "for the magic") in navy display type
- Monospace uppercase eyebrows and card captions as a recurring labeling system
- Big flat pastel-saturated rounded panels (teal, coral, lavender, marigold) hosting white product-UI screenshots
- Hand-drawn squiggle arrows and blob/dot-texture doodles between sections

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the body of the page on warm cream #f6f6ea with navy #12284c text; reserve the indigo gradient for hero and the solid indigo #3820d3 for quote blocks and secondary CTAs
- Use yellow #fedb62 for the primary CTA and as a text highlighter behind one or two headline words per section
- Float white, lightly shadowed UI screenshots on large flat color panels (16-32px radius) instead of framing screenshots with borders
- Label sections with small monospace uppercase eyebrows in indigo
- Alternate text/image sides between feature rows and center-align major section headers

**Don't**
- Don't use pure white as the page background — the cream tint is core to the look
- Don't add borders, outlines, or heavy drop shadows to cards; color contrast does the separation
- Don't make buttons pill-shaped or oversized — CTAs stay compact with small radius
- Don't introduce cool grays; neutrals here are warm cream and deep navy only
- Don't use more than one accent panel color per section; the rainbow only appears together in the closing feature grid
