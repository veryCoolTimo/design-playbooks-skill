## Overview
A clean white SaaS canvas built around real human faces: framed product screenshots of the video editor with an AI presenter inside, avatar photo grids, and photographic case-study thumbnails do most of the visual work. One saturated cobalt blue carries every CTA, link, and eyebrow label, while deep navy-to-blue gradient panels punctuate the page for Enterprise and closing CTAs. The overall feel is restrained, dense-but-airy enterprise SaaS with warmth supplied entirely by photography.

## Layout
Sticky white top nav: small logo left, center dropdown links (Features, Use cases, Pricing, Resources, Company), right "Log in" text link plus a solid blue pill-ish "Create account" button. Heroes are centered: tiny all-caps blue eyebrow ("#1 AI VIDEO CREATION PLATFORM"), one-line headline, short subhead, blue CTA, then a large framed editor screenshot or video with soft drop shadow. Below the hero sits a grayscale logo strip ("Trusted by over 50,000 companies"). Sections alternate white and very pale blue (#EEF2FB-ish) full-width bands; mid-page a full-bleed navy gradient Enterprise band, and every page closes with a blue gradient CTA band containing an angled product screenshot and an avatar. Content sits in a contained max-width column; 3-column card grids (ROI stats, testimonials, case studies) are the default rhythm. The resources page is a filterable 3-up card catalog with a left filter sidebar; the company page runs a 4-column black-and-white team headshot grid.

## Components
Buttons: solid cobalt blue fill, white text, small radius (~4-6px), no visible shadow; secondary is white fill with thin gray border and dark text; on dark gradient bands the primary flips to white fill with navy text. Cards: white or pale-blue fill, 1px light gray/blue border, small radius, essentially shadowless and flat; case-study cards are borderless image-topped tiles with title, snippet, and a blue "Read case study →" text link. Pricing tiers are bordered white cards with a full-width blue button; the comparison table uses green check dots and pink/red crosses. Tabs are segmented pill rows with a light-blue active state and blue outline. FAQ rows are full-width bordered accordions with chevrons. Video thumbnails always carry a circular blue play button.

## Signature
- Framed, softly shadowed product-editor screenshots with a lifelike AI presenter inside — the product UI itself is the hero image on nearly every page.
- Grids of real human faces: 2x3/3x3 avatar tiles with play buttons, and a large B&W team headshot wall captioned "Hi, I'm..."
- ROI stat cards pairing a client logo with a giant blue "+50% / +90% / +30%" figure in the accent blue.
- Recurring deep navy-to-blue gradient bands (Enterprise, closing CTA) with white-filled buttons breaking the white page rhythm.
- Tiny all-caps blue eyebrow labels ("USE CASES", "AI AVATARS", "ROI") above every section headline.

## Do
- Lead every page with a framed product screenshot or video featuring a human face; let photography carry the visual interest.
- Use exactly one blue for all CTAs, links, and eyebrows; keep everything else white, pale blue, or gray.
- Alternate white and pale-blue section bands, and reserve the navy gradient for one Enterprise band plus the closing CTA.
- Keep cards flat: 1px borders, small radii, no or barely-visible shadows.
- Anchor claims with concrete numbers and client logos (stat cards, "50,000+ customers", G2 badges).

## Don't
- Don't use illustration, 3D, or abstract art as hero imagery — this system is strictly product-screenshot and photo driven.
- Don't introduce large border radii or pill buttons; corners stay tight (~4-6px) across buttons and cards.
- Don't set headlines in heavy black weights or tight tracking; display type stays medium/semibold and calm.
- Don't add a second brand hue for CTAs — greens/teals appear only as decorative swashes inside dark gradient bands, never as buttons.
- Don't crowd sections: each keeps one eyebrow, one headline, one card grid or media block, generous vertical padding.
