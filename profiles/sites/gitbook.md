## Overview
Dark-tech marketing site built on near-black canvases washed with soft aurora gradient blooms in teal, salmon-pink, and lavender. Marketing pages (home, product, solutions, features, integrations) run dark; content pages (blog, pricing, company) flip to a pale mint off-white (#E9EFEC) with the same aurora glow bleeding in from the top edge. A single soft-yellow pill CTA and monospace uppercase micro-labels give it a precise, terminal-adjacent engineering feel.

## Layout
Slim dark nav bar: logo left, center-right link cluster, Login plus a filled pill CTA at far right. Heroes are either left-aligned headline + floating product-UI screenshots (home) or centered headline with mono eyebrow tag above (features, solutions, integrations). Sections alternate full-bleed dark bands with inset light-gradient panels; logo strips of gray partner marks sit directly under heroes. Content sits in a contained ~1200px column with generous vertical rhythm; 3-column feature grids and 3-4 column card grids recur. Every page ends with the same signature two-tone CTA band ("Create, search and manage your knowledge at scale") pairing a dark text panel with a light product screenshot.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped throughout — primary is soft yellow (#F0E585) fill with dark text; secondary is dark/outlined pill with uppercase monospace label plus a small circled-arrow icon (e.g. "START FOR FREE", "SIGN UP WITH GITHUB"). Cards: 8-14px radius, flat, hairline borders (#2A2C31 on dark, #E2E6E4 on light), no drop shadows; integration tiles are dark gray with logo + name; feature cards often carry grainy aurora-gradient fills (yellow, mint, pink pastels). Pricing table uses circular check dots in muted teal; the highlighted plan gets a teal outline. Thin line-drawn icons in small rounded frames head feature blurbs.

## Signature
- Aurora gradient blooms (teal / salmon / lavender) with subtle grain, glowing out of near-black backgrounds and bleeding onto light pages
- Uppercase monospace micro-labels and pill CTAs with circled-arrow icons
- Two-color headlines: white first line, muted teal-blue or accent second line ("Technical docs / made easy")
- Dark-to-light page alternation — dark product marketing, pale mint content pages — unified by the same gradient language
- Black-and-white duotone team photography on the company page, punctuated by one orange-red card

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the near-black canvas and let color come only from soft grainy gradient blooms, never flat brand blocks
- Reserve the yellow pill exclusively for the primary CTA; render secondary actions as mono-label outlined pills with arrow icons
- Split headlines across two lines with the second line in a muted accent color
- Frame product UI in large, realistic screenshots floating over the gradients
- Close every page with the recurring dark-panel + light-screenshot CTA band

**Don't**
- Don't use hard drop shadows or heavy card elevation — everything stays flat with hairline borders
- Don't use saturated pure-hue accents; all color is desaturated pastel (dusty teal, salmon, lavender, soft yellow)
- Don't set micro-labels or eyebrow tags in the display sans — they are uppercase monospace with letter-spacing
- Don't square off buttons; interactive elements are pills while only cards keep modest corner radii
- Don't run full-color photography — people imagery is black-and-white duotone
