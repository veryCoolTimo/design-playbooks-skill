## Overview
Crisp minimal-light fintech language built on stark white/near-black (#181818) alternation, with a single electric violet (#7352ff) as the brand and CTA color. Big centered grotesque headlines carry the page; warm accent pops (yellow #f6c725, coral #fe573d, mint #6ee2b4) appear as kickers, hand-drawn-style bracket arrows, and photo color bars. Product credibility comes from full dashboard/mobile mockups embedded at large scale rather than illustration.

## Layout
Slim white nav: lowercase purple wordmark left, plain text links right, outlined pill "Sign In" at the far right. Hero is fully centered: two-line display headline, short subhead, then a combined email-capture bar (rounded input + attached purple pill button). Below, a full-bleed strip of creator photos with colored overlays, then a large floating dashboard screenshot. Sections alternate hard between white and #181818 backgrounds in long full-width bands; each opens with a small pill badge or a colored eyebrow line (yellow, coral, or purple) above an oversized centered heading. Feature sections use asymmetric two-column splits (copy + checklist left, big product mockup right). Generous vertical whitespace on white sections; dark sections pack 2x2 grids of phone/SMS mockup cards.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped; primary is solid #7352ff with white medium-weight label ("Get Started"), secondary is white pill with 1px purple outline and purple text ("Sign In"). Email capture fuses a pill input (light gray 1px border, gray placeholder) with the purple button on its right end. Section badges are small cream/white pills with an icon + bold label. Product mockups are large rounded-corner (16-24px) windows with soft shadows; the "New Split" modal has a violet header bar over a white body containing small bordered platform cards (subtle 1px gray border, ~8px radius, no shadow). Checklists use small coral or purple check glyphs. Platform list items render as white pill chips with brand icons.

## Signature
- One saturated violet (#7352ff) doing all CTA/brand work against strict white and #181818 bands
- Colored eyebrow kickers and playful yellow bracket-arrow doodles flanking centered display headlines
- Real product UI (dashboards, SMS threads, payout modals) shown huge as the primary imagery
- Dark sections with texture (monospace cipher-text wall in the security band) instead of flat black

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate full-width pure-white and #181818 sections to create rhythm; never use mid-grays as section fills
- Reserve #7352ff exclusively for CTAs, the wordmark, and link/kicker moments; keep body copy near-black
- Open every section with a small badge pill or colored eyebrow line, then an oversized centered grotesque headline
- Use pill geometry for every interactive element (buttons, chips, input bars, badges)
- Let accent colors (yellow/coral/mint) appear only as kickers, doodle strokes, checkmarks, and photo overlays

Don't:
- Don't add gradients or tinted pastel section backgrounds — surfaces are flat white, flat #181818, or flat violet
- Don't use serif or condensed display type; everything is one wide grotesque family at different sizes
- Don't give buttons square or slightly-rounded corners — anything clickable is a full pill
- Don't illustrate features abstractly; show literal product screenshots/mockups at large scale with soft shadows
- Don't scatter multiple accent colors inside one section; each band commits to a single accent
