## Overview
Near-black dark-tech marketing site where indigo-violet is the only real color: it fills CTAs, tints eyebrow labels, and radiates as a glow behind dimmed product screenshots. Headlines are huge, tightly tracked, sentence-case, and end with periods ("Prevent downtime."). Everything else is grayscale restraint — muted gray body text, hairline-bordered dark cards, and occasional off-white sections for contrast. Docs live in a light theme with the same type and purple link color.

## Layout
Slim dark top nav: logo left, center links with dropdown carets, "Sign in" text link plus a small purple "Sign up" button right. Heroes are centered, 2-3 line display headings over a short subhead and one or two CTAs; home and enterprise heroes float dark, purple-glowing product UI behind or below the heading. Section rhythm: small all-caps purple eyebrow label → large two-line heading → short paragraph → screenshot or 2-3 card row. Mostly dark sections punctuated by one or two light-gray (#F4F4F6) sections mid-page (comparison cards, community articles). Social proof appears as a monochrome press/customer logo strip and a masonry grid of tweet-style testimonial cards. Every page ends with a big centered "Downtime ends today" CTA block (email input + purple button) above a dense multi-column footer.

## Components
Buttons: solid indigo (#6C63F0) fill, white label, modest 4-8px radius, small height; secondary actions are plain text links or "Explore X →" purple links. Email-capture rows pair a dark, hairline-bordered input with an attached purple button. Cards: very dark (#141318-ish) panels one step above canvas, 1px low-contrast borders, 8-14px radius, no visible shadows on dark — small icon top, bold white title, gray body, purple "Learn more" link. Testimonials are compact avatar + handle + text cards in masonry columns. Pricing uses a dark comparison table with purple checkmarks and section subheads; docs use a light three-column layout (sidebar nav, article, "On this page" rail) with purple links.

## Signature
- One accent color: indigo-violet used for CTAs, eyebrow labels, links, checkmarks, and the soft glow behind dark product screenshots — nothing else is saturated.
- Sentence-case display headlines that end with a period, split over two short lines ("Log everything. Troubleshoot anything.").
- All-caps letterspaced purple micro-eyebrows above every section heading.
- Human touches on the dark canvas: scattered polaroid team photos (company page) and a handwritten script accent ("impact makers.", team signature).

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas near-black (#0C0B0E) and reserve #6C63F0 exclusively for CTAs, links, eyebrows, and screenshot glows.
- Set headlines in a tight-tracked grotesque, sentence case with terminal periods, max ~3 words per line.
- Render product screenshots dark-themed and dimmed, with a violet ambient glow bleeding into the background.
- Break long dark pages with one light #F4F4F6 section for comparisons or community content.
- End pages with the email-input + purple-button CTA band before a deep multi-column footer.

Don't:
- Don't introduce a second saturated hue — status green appears only as tiny dots/checks, never as a surface.
- Don't use large radii or pill buttons; corners stay 4-14px throughout.
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients to cards on dark — separation comes from 1px borders and a slightly lighter fill.
- Don't set headlines in title case, all caps, or with playful display faces; the script font is a rare inline accent only.
- Don't brighten body copy to pure white; it stays muted gray (#A1A1AA) with white reserved for headings.
