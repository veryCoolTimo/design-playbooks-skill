## Overview
Light lavender-washed SaaS design built around one hue: purple, from pale #EBEAF6 canvas to vivid violet gradients to near-black indigo panels. The home hero is a giant glowing purple orb with an audio-waveform edge that bleeds into the page; interior pages calm down to soft frosted cards on the tinted canvas. Despite the DN metadata tag, only the CTA bands are dark — the site is predominantly light with gradient energy.

## Layout
Sticky slim top nav: wordmark left, sparse text links right, one dark indigo pill CTA. Home hero is centered stacked type over a full-bleed radial purple gradient with floating user-photo bubbles; About and Blog heroes are instead a rounded frosted-white card sitting on the lavender canvas. Sections alternate centered headline blocks with 2–3 column card grids and zig-zag feature rows (big display text left, icon-led feature list right). Every page closes with the same dark-indigo rounded CTA panel ("Ready to supercharge your research?") flanked by a faint waveform strip, then a centered minimal footer. Generous vertical whitespace; content column stays narrow (~700–900px) on interior pages.

## Components
Buttons: full pill shape, two variants — solid dark indigo with white label (primary "Try for free"), and white/outlined pill with dark label ("Schedule a demo", "Join our team"); paired side by side in CTA bands. Cards: large-radius (16–24px) white or frosted translucent panels with hairline light borders and very soft diffuse shadows; feature cards hold an icon chip (small rounded square in pale violet) plus title and muted body. Blog list items are horizontal rows: rounded screenshot thumbnail left, title + avatar byline right, separated by hairline dividers. Timeline entries use circular pale-violet icon badges on a vertical rule. Numbered mission steps use thin-outlined circles ("01/02/03") in violet.

## Signature
- One-hue monochromatic purple system: lavender canvas, violet gradients, indigo-black CTA panels — no competing colors.
- Audio-waveform motif bleeding off gradient and panel edges, referencing voice interviews.
- Gradient-highlighted words inside headlines ("supercharge" in pink-to-violet) as the accent device.
- Recurring dark rounded CTA panel with white pill + dark pill button pair on every page.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the entire palette inside the purple family; use the pink-violet gradient only on single emphasized words in headlines.
- Use pill buttons exclusively, pairing a filled dark-indigo primary with a white/ghost secondary.
- Set section eyebrows as small violet labels ("About us", "Our story") above bold indigo headlines, all center-aligned.
- Float content in large-radius frosted white cards over the lavender canvas rather than flat full-width sections.
- End pages with the dark indigo CTA panel plus the waveform strip.

Don't:
- Don't use a pure white or pure gray page background — the canvas is always lavender-tinted.
- Don't introduce hard shadows, visible card borders heavier than a hairline, or sharp corners.
- Don't run the vivid purple gradient across whole interior pages; it belongs to the home hero and dark CTA bands only.
- Don't use link-blue or multi-color accents; secondary text stays muted slate-violet.
- Don't left-align hero or section headers — the rhythm is centered blocks with symmetric card grids.
