## Overview
Near-black cinematic canvas where iridescent light-trail renders (cyan, magenta, violet, lime streaks) act as the only color, contrasted against a light, condensed editorial serif set very large. Body copy drops to a tiny neutral sans in muted gray, creating an extreme scale contrast between poetic serif statements and dense fine print. The whitepaper page inverts to a pale gray document mode with the same serif headlines, reading like a printed spec sheet.

## Layout
Slim centered top nav: wordmark centered on marketing pages, plus a floating pill nav bar (Features/Community/About/Whitepaper + Login/Register) below the hero. Hero is a centered serif headline over a full-bleed light-trail render, followed by a monochrome logo strip of integrations. Sections alternate: horizontal card rows with numbered items (04/05/01/02), full-width oversized serif claims used as dividers ("Censorship-free", "Algorithm-free"), full-bleed 3D/gradient imagery with sparse text floated on top, then a product-screenshot feature block. Whitepaper uses a long-form two-column doc layout: content left, sticky text TOC right, generous margins. Whitespace is vertical and vast; content column stays narrow and centered.

## Components
Buttons: small rectangles with 2-6px corners; primary CTA is an off-white fill (#EFEFEC) with black label, secondary is dark charcoal (#232323) with white label ("Join now"); nav Login/Register sit in a rounded pill bar. Cards: charcoal (#161616) panels with slight radius, no visible border or shadow, containing a small 3D object render, a number tag, serif card title, and tiny sans description. Product screenshots framed as dark-UI panels with subtle rounding. Team portraits: black-and-white photos in small-radius rectangles with italic serif names. Inputs (whitepaper payment mock): dark fields, thin light borders, small radius.

## Signature
- Massive light-trail / fiber-optic gradient renders on pure black as the sole source of color
- Editorial condensed serif at poster scale, including full-viewport one-line serif statements used as section dividers
- Extreme type-scale contrast: giant serif display vs. very small gray grotesque body
- Numbered dark feature cards with floating 3D glass-object renders
- Light-mode whitepaper that keeps the serif voice but switches to a paper-gray document layout

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas true near-black and let iridescent renders/photography carry all color
- Set headlines in a light condensed serif, centered, multi-line, at very large sizes with tight tracking
- Use off-white filled buttons with black text for the primary CTA; charcoal fills for secondary
- Convert dry content (docs, whitepaper) to a light gray paper mode with a sticky right-hand TOC
- Use ghosted/low-opacity serif mega-text as scroll-through section dividers

Don't:
- Introduce saturated flat brand colors for UI chrome — color lives only in imagery
- Use bold geometric sans for headlines; the serif is the identity
- Add borders, glows, or heavy shadows to cards — panels are flat charcoal on black
- Round buttons into full pills (except the floating nav bar) or exceed ~6px radius on cards
- Let body text run large; fine-print scale sans against oversized serif is the intended tension
