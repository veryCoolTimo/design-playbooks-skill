## Overview
A warm sage-tinted off-white canvas carries dark navy serif headlines and a confetti system of overlapping translucent color blobs — yellow, green, blue, magenta, orange — that act as multiply-blended Venn shapes behind product screenshots, logos, and section dividers. The effect is playful and editorial rather than corporate, softening a compliance/ESG product with candy color while text stays strictly two-tone (navy on beige). Product UI screenshots float inside rounded superellipse frames layered over the blob shapes.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on the canvas color: bold sans wordmark left, center dropdown links, then a yellow pill "Log in" and a navy pill "Get started" right. Heroes are centered: large serif headline, short sans subcopy, inline email-capture pill or button pair, then a full-width blob-collage band or framed product screenshot. Section rhythm alternates: logo strip on white, centered serif section headings, 3-column card grids, alternating text/image split rows (about page), full-bleed yellow feature banners (customers, resources), and a recurring pre-footer: serif "Ready to get compliant? Ditto." beside a navy pill CTA, above a giant horizontal row of overlapping color circles. Footer is a dense multi-column sitemap on the same canvas. Generous vertical whitespace throughout; content max-width is moderate with wide margins.

## Components
- Buttons: pills exclusively. Primary = solid dark navy with white label; secondary/action = solid yellow with navy label ("Log in", "Learn more", "Load More", "Try Ditto"); one green pill for "Try now". No borders or shadows; flat fills.
- Cards: flat fill slightly darker/greener than canvas (#E3E5D6-ish), 16-24px radius, no border, no shadow; small eyebrow label with bullet dot, serif card title, small sans body. Customer cards top an image tile of blended color blobs with the client logo in a white pill.
- Inputs: white pill email field with placeholder, paired with a navy pill submit inside a rounded capsule bar.
- Tabs/filters: white pill group with the active tab filled yellow (resources, solutions). Status chips: small green "ACTIVE" / orange "COMING SOON" pills on cards.
- Testimonials: large serif pull-quote cards on the muted card fill with small avatar + name row.

## Signature
- Overlapping translucent color blobs (multiply-blend Venn circles in yellow/green/blue/magenta/orange) used as hero bands, card imagery, section dividers, and logo backdrops.
- Serif display headlines in dark navy on a sage-beige canvas — a serif-on-warm-neutral pairing unusual for SaaS.
- All interactive elements are pills: navy primary, yellow secondary, white inputs.
- Full-width flat yellow banners for featured content (case study hero, featured blog post, mid-page CTA capsule).

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep text strictly navy-on-beige; let color live only in blob graphics, yellow surfaces, and status chips.
- Compose imagery from 2-4 overlapping translucent circles/rounded shapes in the accent palette, often framing a logo or screenshot in a white pill or superellipse.
- Use serif for every headline and card title, sans only for body, labels, and nav.
- Reserve the yellow fill for secondary CTAs, active tab states, and featured banners; navy fill for the single primary CTA.
- Keep cards flat: tinted fill, big radius, no borders, no shadows.
Don't:
- Add drop shadows, gradients on buttons, or outlined ghost buttons — everything here is flat solid fills.
- Use pure white as the page background; white appears only for input fields, logo pills, and small chips.
- Introduce small-radius rectangles; anything below ~16px radius (other than tiny chips) breaks the rounded language.
- Set headlines in sans or bold geometric type; the serif display is the brand voice.
- Use the accent colors for text or links; they are graphic-shape colors only.
