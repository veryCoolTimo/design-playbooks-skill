## Overview
Near-black void canvas where photoreal 3D chain-link renders — chrome rings with molten orange spikes and pink-red glow — carry nearly all the color and drama. Type is restrained: white grotesque headings at modest weight with single phrases flipped to orange, gray body copy kept small and quiet. The effect is premium hardware-meets-crypto: dark, glossy, engineered rather than playful.

## Layout
Sticky top nav: small logo left, dropdown links center-left, twin pill CTAs top-right ("Buy WCFLT" filled white, "Launch App" ghost). Hero is a left-aligned two-tone headline (gray lead-in, white payoff) with short subcopy and a small pill CTA, balanced by a large 3D render bleeding off the right edge. Sections alternate: centered heading over a full-width 3D chain with thin callout leader-lines to three labeled columns; a 3-card feature row; text-left/render-right split; 2x2 text-only feature grid; oversized closing statement with circular social icon buttons. Generous vertical breathing room between sections; content sits in a fairly narrow ~1100px column.

## Components
Buttons: pills throughout — white fill with dark text for the primary nav CTA, near-black fill or hairline-bordered ghosts for secondary ("Learn More", "Bridge Now"); all small with compact padding. Cards: dark charcoal (#141414) panels a step above the canvas, hairline border, small corner radius, no shadow; white card title over small gray paragraph. Inputs: footer email field is a minimal dark underlined/boxed row with a tiny circular arrow submit button. Social links are hairline-bordered circles with white glyphs.

## Signature
- Photoreal chrome chain-link 3D renders with fiery orange/red spike glow as the sole imagery, floating on pure black
- Selective orange (#ee8434) applied to one key phrase per heading; blue reserved for the "dapp" brand fragment
- Two-tone hero headline: muted gray opening line resolving into bright white statement
- Thin leader-lines connecting the 3D chain to its three explanatory columns

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the canvas essentially black and let one high-gloss 3D asset per section provide all color heat
- Color exactly one phrase per heading in orange; leave the rest white
- Use small pill buttons — white fill only for the single primary action, hairline ghosts elsewhere
- Set body copy small and light gray; let headline size, not weight, create hierarchy
- Separate sections with large empty black gaps instead of background-color bands

**Don't**
- Use gradient backgrounds, colored section bands, or glassmorphism — panels are flat charcoal with hairline borders
- Bold or blacken the headings; the site stays at regular/medium weight even at display sizes
- Add photography or illustration styles that compete with the 3D chain motif
- Apply orange to buttons, links, or borders — it lives only in heading phrases and the renders
- Round cards heavily; card radius stays small while buttons alone go full pill
