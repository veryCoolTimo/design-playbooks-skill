## Overview
A vivid underwater-illustration world: coral reefs, fish, and portholes rendered in saturated violet, magenta, pink, and gold gradients. The page alternates between a warm cream canvas (#fffbf7) and deep-purple night sections (#2c034c / #320558), so the scroll feels like diving down and surfacing. Type is a friendly geometric sans, with the hero headline filled with an indigo-to-magenta gradient.

## Layout
Sparse top nav: wordmark left, three text links plus one violet pill CTA ("Reef App") right, floating on cream. Hero is split: dense coral illustration forming a letter shape on the left, stacked one-word gradient headlines ("Reliable / Extensible / Efficient / Fast.") on the right, followed by a slim dark exchange-logo strip. Sections alternate cream/dark in long full-bleed bands; dark bands open and close with illustrated wave/reef edges rather than hard lines. Feature content sits in 2x2 grids of icon tile + heading + short paragraph. A two-column signup/build split is divided by a thin pink vertical rule. Oversized ghosted repeat text ("Introducing Reef c...") is used as background texture. Generous vertical whitespace throughout; content column is centered and fairly narrow.

## Components
- Buttons: solid #681aff pills, white label, no border or visible shadow; used identically in nav, mid-page ("Quick Start", "Start Reef"), and footer.
- Input: pill-shaped email field, cream fill, thin 1px violet outline, violet placeholder, with a circular violet ">" submit button nested inside the right end.
- Cards: white large-radius cards whose top ~80% is a full-bleed underwater illustration, with a small white pill label bar ("REEF-20 tokens and NFTs") overlapping the bottom and short caption text below.
- Icon tiles: large-radius rounded squares (violet or pink gradient fills) containing tiny abstract UI/coral illustrations; paired with a pink or violet feature heading and 2-3 lines of body text.
- Footer: deep purple, script-style gradient "Reef" logotype, four link columns, pill newsletter input, small pill buttons.

## Signature
- Alternating cream and deep-purple full-bleed bands with illustrated coral/wave transitions between them.
- Rich underwater illustration system: gradient corals, fish, portholes, treasure chests in violet/magenta/pink/gold.
- Gradient-filled bold hero words stacked one per line, ending with a colored period.
- Everything interactive is a pill in the single violet #681aff.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Alternate warm cream (#fffbf7) and deep purple (#2c034c) sections; keep text #2c034c on cream and white on purple.
- Use one violet (#681aff) for every CTA and keep buttons/inputs fully pill-shaped.
- Transition into dark sections with illustrated organic edges (reef silhouettes, waves), not straight seams.
- Lead features with illustrated rounded icon tiles and give headings occasional pink (#fd82ab) or violet color.
- Use oversized ghosted headline text as low-contrast background texture in dark bands.

Don't:
- Use pure white or gray backgrounds — the light canvas is warm cream everywhere.
- Add borders or drop shadows to buttons; the flat violet fill carries them.
- Introduce photography — all imagery is flat-gradient vector illustration.
- Use sharp or small-radius corners on cards, tiles, or inputs.
- Render the hero headline in a single flat color; it reads as an indigo-to-magenta gradient.
