## Overview
Poster-like big-type landing on a stark white canvas. A gigantic black grotesque headline dominates the viewport, with solid orange, cobalt-blue, and black rectangles collaged between the words like sticky notes. Everything else — nav, body copy, footer — is tiny by contrast, producing extreme scale tension. Color is used only as flat fills; no gradients, no photography.

## Layout
Minimal top bar: tiny wordmark left, "Log in" text link and small orange CTA pill right. Hero is the headline itself ("Turn your tasks into calendar blocks") set across four lines at viewport-filling size, underlined by thin horizontal rules, with labeled color blocks overlapping the text at offset positions. Below: a full-width light-gray product-shot band, then centered sections with huge lowercase headings ("built for efficiency", "what they say", "pricing", "FAQ"). Features are a masonry-like grid of solid color tiles of varying heights; testimonials are three small white cards styled like mini app windows; pricing is two centered cards with a monthly/yearly toggle; FAQ is an accordion with heading on the left, questions on the right. Generous vertical whitespace between sections, content constrained to a moderate centered column.

## Components
- Buttons: small, tight rounded rectangles (~4px) with flat fills — orange primary ("Try for free", "Get Started"), blue secondary ("Become a supporter"), white bold label, no shadow or border.
- Feature tiles: flat solid rectangles (orange / blue / black), white bold label pinned top-left, square corners, no border or shadow.
- Testimonial cards: white, slight radius, subtle drop shadow, faux window chrome (colored dot + icons row) at top, avatar + name + quote inside.
- Pricing cards: white with light border/shadow, small radius, colored checkmark list items (orange/blue), color-matched CTA at bottom.
- FAQ: hairline-divided accordion rows with chevron toggles.
- Toggle: black pill switch for billing period.

## Signature
- Viewport-scale black headline with colored rectangles collaged over/between the words.
- Three-color system only: orange, cobalt blue, black on pure white — flat, no gradients.
- Lowercase mega-headings for every section ("built for efficiency", "pricing").
- Extreme scale contrast: giant display type vs. very small utilitarian body/nav text.

## Do
- Set headlines at outrageous size in black extra-bold grotesque with tight tracking, spanning multiple lines.
- Use flat solid rectangles in the orange/blue/black trio as decorative and functional blocks, slightly overlapping type.
- Keep section headings lowercase and huge; keep body text tiny and plain.
- Use thin hairline rules to underline hero lines and divide FAQ rows.
- Reserve orange strictly for primary CTAs and key tiles; blue for secondary actions.

## Don't
- Don't add gradients, photos, or illustration — color fields and one product screenshot carry the page.
- Don't round corners heavily; nothing here exceeds a few pixels of radius.
- Don't shrink the hero type to "safe" sizes or center-align it politely — the collage asymmetry is the identity.
- Don't introduce extra hues beyond orange/blue/black plus the gray screenshot band.
- Don't decorate buttons with shadows, outlines, or icons — they stay small, flat, and blunt.
