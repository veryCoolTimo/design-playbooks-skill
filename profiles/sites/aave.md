## Overview
Dark near-black navy canvas with an aurora-like purple-to-teal gradient hero, restrained neon accents, and small white pill CTAs. The visual language is protocol-serious: dense stat numerals (a giant TVL counter), muted gray body text, and playful ghost-mascot illustrations that soften the finance tone. Product sub-pages flip to a light off-white theme with a single saturated brand green (GHO), keeping the same pill buttons and rounded-card grammar.

## Layout
Slim top nav: logo left, centered text links, one pill CTA ("Launch App" / "Open App") at right. Home hero is a full-width gradient field with a small uppercase kicker and a modest two-line headline — the real hero moment is the huge dollar-value stat directly below it. Sections alternate: 4-column icon-card market grid, centered headline + mascot illustration cluster, 3-step numbered horizontal process, wide dark panels for treasury data with charts, and a 2-up card row near the footer. Product page uses a narrower centered column: headline left / one-liner right, big illustrated banner, 2x2 stat-card grid, alternating image+text feature cards, then a two-column FAQ accordion. Generous vertical whitespace between sections on both.

## Components
Buttons: small pill-shaped, solid white fill with dark text on dark pages; solid black pill on the light page; low visual weight, no glow or shadow. Cards: slightly-lighter-than-canvas dark panels (#1C1F2B-ish) with ~10-14px radius and no visible border or shadow; on the light page, flat pale-gray (#F2F3F0) cards with the same radius. Stat cards pair a large numeral with a tiny muted label and a single-color line/gauge chart. FAQ rows are full-width rounded bars with a plus icon. Chain/market cards lead with a circular token logo. Hexagonal outline badges display auditor logos. Links use small text with arrow suffixes ("Learn More ->") in the accent color.

## Signature
- Purple-to-teal aurora gradient hero over a near-black navy body — the Aave brand gradient (#B6509E -> #2EBAC6).
- A single enormous live-stat numeral ($20.8B TVL) as the de facto hero content.
- Cute ghost mascots in beanies dropped into an otherwise sober financial layout.
- Sub-brand theme flip: dark neon for the protocol home, off-white + one vivid green for GHO, unified by pill buttons and rounded flat cards.

## Do
- Use flat, borderless, shadowless cards that are one step lighter than the canvas; let spacing do the separation.
- Make key metrics huge and let headlines stay small; numbers carry the drama, not typography tricks.
- Keep CTAs as small monochrome pills (white on dark, black on light) — accents live in gradients, charts, and icons, not buttons.
- Reserve the purple/teal gradient for the hero band; keep mid-page sections flat dark navy.
- Use one saturated accent per sub-page (green for GHO) across charts, links, and illustrations.

## Don't
- Don't add glows, neon borders, or glassmorphism — the dark theme here is matte and flat.
- Don't fill CTAs with the brand gradient or accent colors; primary buttons are strictly black/white pills.
- Don't use pure black or pure white canvases on the dark pages — the base is a desaturated navy near #14161F.
- Don't oversize headlines; H1s are modest and often paired with an uppercase micro-kicker.
- Don't mix multiple accent hues within one section — each block commits to a single chart/link color.
