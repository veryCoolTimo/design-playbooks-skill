## Overview
A warm-dark editorial identity: deep chocolate-maroon canvas, cream text, and burnt-orange highlights instead of the usual black-and-neon dark-tech look. Headlines interleave a grotesque sans with serif-italic accent words tinted orange ("We make the complex *simple*."). Retro-modern abstract shapes — stacked chevron/wave glyphs in orange, brown, and olive — act as full-bleed graphic sections. The overall feel is a 70s-inflected data brand: analytical content, crafts-y warmth.

## Layout
Sticky-less top bar with small lowercase logo left, sparse center nav, and a single pill "Contact us" button right. Heroes are centered, oversized mixed serif/sans headlines with one small pill CTA below; the home hero floats a framed product screenshot (Power BI dashboard mock, recolored to the brand palette) mid-page. Section rhythm alternates: dark maroon blocks → full-width graphic panels of repeated chevron shapes → a light cream inverse section ("We are humans collaborating with humans") → tilted card fan → FAQ accordion → blog teasers → giant centered closing CTA statement. About page uses a split panel: peach left column with icon+rule feature list, dark right column with a large orange chevron motif; team shown as a strict photo grid on alternating brown tiles. Generous vertical whitespace; 3-column card grids for services.

## Components
- Buttons: small pill, solid orange (#F0561D) fill with dark brown text; nav "Contact us" same pill treatment. No visible outline/ghost variants except text links.
- Links/inline emphasis: orange serif-italic underlined words inside cream headlines and paragraphs.
- Service cards: flat panels on slightly lighter brown, thin 1px orange keyline illustrations, title + one-line copy + small arrow; square corners or barely rounded.
- Testimonial cards: heavily rounded (~24px), fanned at alternating tilts, each a different palette color (cream, olive, dark teal, orange) with logo top, quote, author; one card is a pure "Let's connect" CTA card with serif display text.
- FAQ: full-width rounded-rectangle accordion rows on darker fill with leading dot marker.
- Blog teasers: bordered rows (1px brown outline) with recolored screenshot thumbnails.
- Tool logos: single monochrome cream icon strip.

## Signature
- Chocolate-maroon + burnt-orange + olive + peach palette — warm retro, no blues or neons.
- Serif-italic orange accent words spliced into sans headlines, on nearly every heading.
- Repeated stacked-chevron/wave glyph used as hero art, section dividers, and footer mark (also the logo's apostrophe-like mark).
- Fanned, tilted multi-color testimonial cards ending in a "Let's connect" card.
- Product screenshots (Power BI dashboards) recolored into the brand palette so even data UI reads on-brand.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas a deep warm brown (#42231A) and reserve solid orange strictly for pill CTAs and accent words.
- Mix serif-italic highlight words (orange) into otherwise sans headlines; underline inline links in orange.
- Build graphic sections from repeated flat chevron/wave shapes in orange, dark brown, and olive.
- Insert one light peach/cream inverse section mid-page for contrast and the human-toned message.
- Recolor any embedded dashboard/product imagery to the maroon/orange palette.

Don't:
- Don't use pure black, white, or cool grays — every neutral is warm (cream, peach, brown).
- Don't add gradients, glows, or glassmorphism; all fills are flat and matte.
- Don't give buttons rectangles or medium radius — CTAs are small pills only.
- Don't use photography outside the team grid; illustration is abstract geometry and thin keyline diagrams.
- Don't set headlines in a single typeface — the sans/serif-italic splice is the core device.
