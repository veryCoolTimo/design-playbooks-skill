## Overview
A minimal-light SaaS landing built almost entirely from white space, near-black ink, and full-fidelity product screenshots. The only color comes from a thin golden-yellow "horizon" line with hand-drawn birds, trees, and flying task-cards that repeats as a section divider, plus small functional hues (tag colors, a blue tooltip) inside the app UI itself. Buttons and the closing band invert to the same dark navy-black as the text, so the whole page reads as two tones plus a wink of illustration.

## Layout
Single-column, center-axis page: slim top bar with logo left and one pill CTA right, no visible nav links. Hero is text-only — large bold headline ("Tasks & Docs. Together."), two-line subhead, one dark pill CTA — sitting under an illustrated telephone-wire vignette. Below, a strict repeating rhythm: yellow horizon-line illustration divider → left-aligned bold section heading + short paragraph → large tabbed product screenshot (tab pairs like Fast/Flexible, Intuitive/Approachable, Search/Integrations). Page closes with a full-width dark navy rounded band holding a centered white CTA headline and inverted button, then a sparse multi-column footer on white.

## Components
Buttons: solid dark navy-black (#111727) pills with small white text; the same pill inverts to white-on-navy inside the dark footer band. No visible secondary/outline button style on the page. Screenshots are presented inside browser-like frames with a light gray tab bar (active tab white, inactive gray) and medium rounded corners, hairline borders, essentially shadowless. In-app cards within the screenshots: white, 8px-ish radius, 1px light gray borders, tiny avatars and colored tag chips (yellow, blue, purple, green). Tooltips inside screenshots are small blue (#3e68d1) rounded pills with white text. Footer CTA band: dark navy, large radius, generous padding.

## Signature
- Recurring thin yellow horizon line with monoline hand-drawn birds, trees, and envelope/task-card doodles used as the section divider system.
- Two-tone discipline: near-black navy for text and every CTA on pure white; no brand color on buttons at all.
- Tabbed, browser-framed product screenshots as the sole imagery — each feature section is a live-looking app view, not an abstract graphic.
- Dark navy full-width rounded band as the single high-contrast closing CTA moment.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas pure white and let dense product screenshots carry all visual interest.
- Use dark navy (#111727) pill buttons for every CTA, including the header.
- Separate sections with the thin yellow illustrated horizon line rather than background-color bands.
- Pair each feature section with a tabbed two-state screenshot (e.g., Fast / Flexible) framed like a browser window.
- Reserve saturated color for tiny functional elements inside the UI shots (tags, tooltips, icons).

Don't:
- Don't add a colored brand button — the primary action color here is the ink color itself.
- Don't use heavy drop shadows or gradients; frames rely on hairline borders and flat white fills.
- Don't fill the hero with imagery — it stays text-only with the illustration kept to a thin strip above it.
- Don't introduce tinted section backgrounds; the only non-white surface is the single dark navy closing band.
- Don't crowd the top bar — logo plus one pill CTA is the entire navigation.
