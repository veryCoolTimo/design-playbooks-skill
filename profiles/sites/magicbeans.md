## Overview
A mostly monochrome, whitespace-heavy SaaS page that gets its playfulness from one recurring motif: jelly beans. Outlined multicolor bean doodles frame the hero, and glossy 3D candy-colored beans pile up at the footer. Everything else — type, buttons, product screenshots — is restrained black-on-white, so the color reads as garnish, not structure. Indigo appears as the sole functional accent (Pro plan, FAQ icons, pricing star).

## Layout
Minimal top nav: small logo left, two text links right (Pricing, Twitter), no nav CTA. Hero is centered: large headline with two inline colored icons embedded in the text, short subhead, single black pill CTA, then a large Notion-style product screenshot flanked by outlined bean doodles. Sections alternate: small colored eyebrow label (orange/red), bold left-aligned two-line heading, paragraph, then big product screenshots; feature bullets sit below in 3-column rows of icon + title + one-liner. Pricing page: centered "Pricing" H1 with indigo star, two side-by-side plan cards, split FAQ (heading left, accordion right), then a full-width warm off-white (#F7F5F0) CTA band ending in the 3D jelly-bean pile. Generous vertical whitespace throughout.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, compact; primary is solid black with white text and a small leading Notion icon; pricing Pro card uses a solid indigo (#6366F1) pill; Free plan uses a light gray pill with dark text. Cards: pricing cards on warm off-white with faint gray line illustrations (sprout, unicorn horn) and a squiggle divider, soft 12-16px corners, hairline borders, no heavy shadows. Feature tiles in "toolkit" section are light gray rounded panels containing mini UI mockups. Checklist items use small circular check icons — gray for Free, indigo for Pro. FAQ accordion rows use indigo +/− toggles and bold question text. Screenshots are rendered as realistic Notion/app windows with traffic-light dots and subtle drop shadows.

## Signature
- Jelly beans as the entire brand system: outlined multicolor doodles up top, glossy 3D candy beans piled at the footer CTA
- Black pill CTA with Notion glyph ("Log in to get started") repeated verbatim across pages
- Colored inline icons embedded directly inside the black hero headline
- Tiny colored eyebrow labels (Invoices, Finances, Privacy) introducing otherwise monochrome sections
- Hand-drawn line illustrations and squiggle dividers inside pricing cards

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the page black-on-white and concentrate all saturated color in the bean motifs and small icons
- Use pill buttons; solid black for the universal CTA, indigo only for the paid-plan CTA
- Show the product as full Notion-style window screenshots with macOS traffic lights
- Introduce each section with a small colored uppercase-ish eyebrow label before the bold heading
- End long pages with a warm off-white band and the 3D jelly-bean pile under a centered CTA

**Don't**
- Don't use colored backgrounds or gradients for whole sections — color lives only in beans, icons, and eyebrows
- Don't add a nav CTA button or heavy nav bar; the header stays two text links
- Don't use serif or display typefaces; everything is one neo-grotesque family at varying weights
- Don't apply heavy shadows or dark borders to cards — hairlines and faint line illustrations only
- Don't spread indigo beyond functional accents (Pro plan, FAQ toggles, badges); it never fills large areas
