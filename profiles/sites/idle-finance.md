## Overview
Deep midnight-navy canvas washed with huge soft electric-blue and violet radial gradients that bloom brighter toward page edges and section transitions. All type is white-on-dark; color arrives through glowing gradients, cyan/orange spot-lit 3D icons, and rendered 3D robot/machine illustrations. The mood is polished sci-fi fintech: dark, luminous, and orderly rather than aggressive neon.

## Layout
Sticky slim top nav: logo left, four centered text links, single white "Enter App" pill-corner button right. Hero is left-aligned text (large three-line headline, short subcopy, primary + ghost text CTA) with a 3D illustration on the right. Sections alternate rhythm: eyebrow-label + two-column intro, 3-up product cards, centered big-stat rows (3-4 oversized numbers with tiny labels), a 3x2 feature grid with icons, a two-card asymmetric security block, a 3x2 partner-logo grid, and a centered stats + dual-button governance band. Generous vertical padding (150px+) between sections; content sits in a fixed centered column while gradients fill the full bleed.

## Components
Buttons: primary is solid white fill with dark navy text, small 4-6px radius, compact padding; secondary is a plain text link with arrow chevron, or a dark navy outlined/solid variant ("Visit Forum"). Cards: dark slate-blue panels (#172030-ish) slightly lighter than canvas, 10-14px radius, hairline borders, no visible drop shadows; product cards stack a 3D icon, title, copy, APY/token metric row, and paired Launch/Integrate white buttons. Audit list rows are small nested cards with icon, title, and chevron. Footer includes an inline email input with attached white subscribe button.

## Signature
- Full-bleed navy canvas with massive blurred blue-violet gradient blooms that intensify between sections
- White-filled CTA buttons everywhere — the brightest elements on the page, no colored button fills
- Rendered 3D illustrations and spot-colored 3D iconography (cyan, orange, purple) as the only imagery
- Big-number stat rows ($95.9M, +10K, 3.7K) as recurring section anchors with tiny muted captions

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the base canvas a single very dark navy (#071233) and paint color with soft oversized radial gradients, not solid colored sections
- Use solid white buttons with dark navy text as the sole primary CTA treatment, repeated on cards and nav
- Elevate cards with a slightly lighter slate fill and hairline border instead of shadows
- Punctuate the page with centered oversized stat numbers over tiny caption labels
- Give icons and illustrations a 3D, spot-lit look with one saturated hue each (cyan, orange, purple)

**Don't**
- Don't introduce colored or gradient-filled buttons — color lives in backgrounds and icons only
- Don't use flat line-art icons or stock photography; imagery here is exclusively rendered 3D
- Don't brighten the whole background; gradients bloom locally while text zones stay near-black navy for contrast
- Don't use serif or decorative display faces — everything is one clean grotesque at varying weights
- Don't crowd sections; the design depends on very tall vertical whitespace between bands
