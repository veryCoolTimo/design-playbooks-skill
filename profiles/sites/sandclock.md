## Overview
Dark-tech DeFi aesthetic built on near-black canvases that shift hue per page (green-black on home/token, deep forest green on save, dark navy on invest, warm brown-black on donate), always relieved by large pastel color-block sections (cream, mint, pale cyan, pink). Photography and 3D renders (crystals, portals) are masked into symbolic shapes — arches, a heart, chevrons. Type is a light grotesque sans set very large, with one or two words per headline flipped to italic. Bright signal green (#2CE983) is the recurring highlight color for links, labels, and key phrases.

## Layout
Fixed top nav: small logo left, center text links, right side a cream pill "Get started" CTA. Heroes are full-bleed dark sections with an oversized centered or left-set headline overlapping a shape-masked image, plus a small "scroll" arrow. Body is a rhythm of alternating full-width color blocks: dark section → pastel section → dark → cream footer. Prose paragraphs are center-aligned and narrow, with inline emoji/avatar chips embedded mid-sentence. Step-by-step explainers run as a vertical numbered timeline (pink numbered dots, dotted connectors) down the page center. Generous vertical whitespace; content rarely exceeds a narrow central column except stat rows and card clusters.

## Components
- Buttons: pill-shaped, solid cream/peach fill with dark text and a trailing arrow ("Get started →", "Start Now →"); a bright-green pill variant appears in flows ("Claim"). Dark pill toggle pairs (High Exposure / Low Exposure) for switches.
- Chips: white/cream pill "value tokens" carrying an icon + amount ($1000, Yield $15.5) — a signature explainer element; inline text highlights use green or pink pill-shaped backgrounds behind words.
- Cards: savings-goal cards are arch-topped (semicircle over rectangle) with a photo header, glowing green sparkline, and large dollar figure on white; incentive cards on the pink section are plain white, small radius, flat, no shadow.
- Inputs: footer newsletter email field is a bare underlined/ghost input on cream with an arrow submit.
- Data accents: glowing green line charts, thin rule dividers under large stats (5%—50%, Regulated & Compliant).

## Signature
- Images masked into meaningful shapes: arch/portal, split heart (red/pink halves), chevron arrows — one motif per page.
- Per-page color theming on an identical skeleton: green-black, forest green, navy, brown-black darks each paired with their own pastel (mint, cyan, pink, cream).
- Huge light-weight grotesque headlines with selective italic words ("Deliver Returns", "of Wealth Creation") and green-highlighted phrases.
- Pill "value chips" with icons and dollar amounts used as narrative devices in vertical step timelines.
- Recurring closer: dark "Grow your Wealth responsibly" section with a glowing iridescent portal render, then a cream footer with dotted-underline newsletter input.

## Do
- Alternate near-black sections with saturated pastel blocks (mint, pale cyan, pink, cream) at full bleed; never leave the whole page dark.
- Mask photos and 3D renders inside arch or symbolic shapes instead of rectangular image blocks.
- Use pill geometry everywhere interactive: cream CTA pills with arrows, value chips, highlight pills behind key words.
- Set headlines very large and light, italicizing one word for emphasis; use #2CE983 for links, section labels, and highlighted phrases on dark.
- Explain product mechanics as centered vertical step timelines with numbered dots and value chips.

## Don't
- Don't use a single fixed brand background — the dark hue must shift with the page theme (green, navy, brown-black).
- Don't add drop shadows, gradients on cards, or heavy borders; sections are flat color blocks and cards are flat white.
- Don't use bold/heavy display weights or all-caps headlines; the type voice is light, sentence-case, airy.
- Don't make the primary CTA green or dark — the main button is a cream/peach pill with dark text; green is reserved for highlights and secondary actions.
- Don't box body copy into left-aligned wide columns; long prose is narrow and center-aligned with inline icon chips.
