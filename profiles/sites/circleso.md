## Overview
A crisp white SaaS site punctuated by saturated indigo-blue bands and scattered confetti-like geometric shapes (dots, arcs, quarter-circle wedges) in pink, yellow, cyan, and green. Product-forward: large, realistic app screenshots do most of the storytelling, framed by tidy black-on-white typography. Playfulness lives entirely in the decorative shapes and occasional italic or color-highlighted words inside otherwise sober headlines.

## Layout
Slim white nav bar: wordmark left, sparse links plus Login and a small blue pill-ish "Get started" button right. Home hero is a full-bleed indigo (#3E45EB) band with centered white headline (keywords italicized), a single white CTA button, and a big floating app screenshot that overlaps the band's bottom edge; avatar chips and confetti dots orbit it. Section rhythm alternates: white sections with centered headlines and two-column feature rows (accordion-style text list left, screenshot or photo right), one full-width dark/indigo "Gather with your people" video band mid-page, then a closing indigo CTA band decorated with oversized pastel arcs and dots. Pricing: three-card tier row (popular tier flagged with a blue "Most popular" ribbon, Enterprise with black), followed by a very long plain comparison table with thin hairline dividers, then the same indigo CTA band and a minimal FAQ.

## Components
Buttons: solid #516BEF fill with white text on light backgrounds; inverted solid white with blue text on indigo bands; ~8px corner radius, no visible shadow, generous horizontal padding. Secondary action is a plain underlined text link ("Book a tour"). Pricing cards: white, 1px light-gray border, ~12-16px radius, no shadow, checkmark feature lists; popular card gets a blue top ribbon. Testimonial cards: flat white with thin borders. Comparison table: borderless except hairline row rules, small blue check glyphs. App screenshots rendered as rounded, softly shadowed browser/phone frames. Use-case grid: dark photo tiles with white label text.

## Signature
- Vivid indigo (#3E45EB–#516BEF) full-bleed bands opening and closing every page, with white knockout buttons.
- Confetti geometry: small circles, thick arcs, and wedges in pink #FF788F, yellow #FFEC4A, cyan #81FCFC, and green #01C968 sprinkled around heroes and CTA bands.
- Headlines in near-black bold grotesque with one phrase set in italic or in blue for emphasis ("all-in-one", "you can be proud of").
- Real product UI screenshots as the dominant imagery; no illustration system.

## Do
- Reserve the indigo blue for two jobs only: full-bleed hero/CTA bands and primary buttons; keep all mid-page sections white.
- Scatter 3-6 small geometric accent shapes (dots, arcs, wedges) at band edges; keep them decorative and non-overlapping with text.
- Let large, realistic product screenshots overlap section boundaries to stitch the hero into the page.
- Emphasize one phrase per headline via italics or the brand blue; keep the rest near-black.
- Keep tables and feature lists nearly chromeless: hairline dividers, small blue checkmarks, lots of whitespace.

## Don't
- Don't use gradients, glassmorphism, or heavy drop shadows — fills are flat and borders are hairline.
- Don't color mid-page section backgrounds with the accent pinks/yellows; accents only appear as small shapes, never as surfaces.
- Don't use pill-shaped or sharp-cornered buttons; stay at the ~8px rounded-rectangle.
- Don't introduce illustrations or mascots; imagery is product UI and real people photos only.
- Don't put blue text on the indigo bands — invert to white buttons/white text there.
