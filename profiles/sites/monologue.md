## Overview
A near-black, film-grain dark canvas carrying an oversized, elegant serif wordmark and headlines, punctuated by a single cyan waveform glow from the hardware-styled recorder widget. Body copy runs in tiny monospace, giving a "precision instrument" feel against the romantic serif. Everything is low-contrast and hushed except the cream CTA pills and the aqua audio visualization. The page ends on a deep teal band with a halftone-dot texture fade.

## Layout
No visible persistent nav bar; the hero opens with a top-left serif headline, a top-right micro-blurb, and a giant centered "Monologue" wordmark behind a skeuomorphic recorder device lit by a soft top spotlight. Sections alternate centered serif headings ("Monologue speaks your language", "Pricing") with left-aligned editorial headings paired with right-column monospace subcopy. A horizontal language-ticker strip breaks up the flow mid-page. Feature grid is a 3+3 bento of dark cards; testimonials appear as single centered serif pull quotes with tiny attribution, plus a 3-column card row near the footer. Generous vertical whitespace; content column is wide but restrained.

## Components
Buttons: small cream/off-white pill CTAs ("Download for Mac", "Get started") with dark text; repeated identically in hero, mid-page, and pricing — no large hero button. Cards: charcoal (#1A1A18-ish) rounded rectangles with subtle grain/noise texture, faint 1px lighter borders, soft inner glow rather than drop shadows; persona carousel cards dim at the edges with prev/next arrow chips. Pricing: two dark cards, Pro card slightly lighter with strikethrough old price and a circular stamp badge; checklists with small check glyphs. A monthly/annual toggle pill sits above pricing. The recorder device is a recurring skeuomorphic component: speaker-grille dots plus glowing cyan waveform screen.

## Signature
- Giant light-weight serif wordmark/headlines on near-black, with italic letters mixed in (the "M" reads italic) — editorial, not techy.
- Skeuomorphic dictation device with dot-grille and glowing cyan waveform as the sole vivid color moment.
- Tiny monospace body/label text everywhere, creating a serif-vs-mono tension.
- Film grain and halftone-dot textures on cards and the teal footer transition; spotlight-from-above lighting in the hero.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas near-black with visible grain/noise texture; light content with soft top-down glows, not flat fills.
- Reserve saturated color for one element (the cyan waveform); let everything else stay monochrome warm-gray.
- Set headlines in a light elegant serif at very large sizes and drop all supporting copy to small monospace.
- Use cream pill buttons with dark text as the only CTA style, repeated verbatim down the page.
- Use centered serif pull-quote testimonials with minimal attribution between sections.

Don't:
- Add a conventional nav bar, bright gradients, or multiple accent colors — the palette is essentially two-tone plus one glow.
- Use bold geometric sans headings or heavy font weights; the serif stays light and airy.
- Apply hard drop shadows or high-contrast card borders; separation comes from subtle tonal steps and grain.
- Make CTAs large or colorful; buttons stay small, quiet, and cream.
- Flood sections with imagery; illustration is limited to the skeuomorphic device and texture, not photos.
