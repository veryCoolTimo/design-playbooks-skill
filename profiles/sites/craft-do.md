## Overview
Warm, paper-like minimalism with a scrapbook heart. A cream canvas carries editorial serif headlines and small sans body text, while entire hero bands flood with a single pastel — sky blue on pricing/download, butter yellow on product. Photographic device shots, torn-paper collage scraps, and soft cartoon clouds add craft-table texture without breaking the calm. All action funnels through solid black pill buttons.

## Layout
Floating pill-shaped nav bar centered at top (white capsule on the colored hero), with logo left, links center, black "Try Craft Free" pill right. Heroes are short and centered: serif headline, one-line subhead, single black or white pill CTA. Sections alternate flooded pastel bands with cream ones; product pages use bento-style 2-column mosaics mixing UI screenshots and lifestyle photos of devices in hands. Italic serif pull-quote testimonials with tiny avatars punctuate the rhythm between feature blocks. Pricing uses side-by-side plan cards (light Free card vs. featured card with the chunky CRAFT wordmark), then a slim comparison table in one large rounded white card. Footer is a hard flip to near-black (#0A0A08) with small columnar link lists.

## Components
Buttons: solid black pills with white text at every size, from nav CTA to tiny per-row "Download"/"App Store" chips; occasional white/translucent pill on colored grounds. No visible borders, flat fill, no gradient. Cards: white (#FFFFFF/#FDF9F6) with large 16-24px radius, whisper-soft shadows, generous internal padding; feature tiles in the bento grids are edge-to-edge imagery with overlaid labels. Comparison table lives inside one big rounded card with hairline row dividers and checkmarks. FAQ is a plain accordion with hairline separators, no boxes. Segmented Yearly/Monthly toggle is a small white pill pair. Award badges rendered as skeuomorphic 3D app-icon cubes.

## Signature
- Solid black pill buttons everywhere — the only "brand color" for actions is black, letting pastels own the atmosphere.
- Full-bleed pastel hero bands (sky blue, butter yellow) with soft clouds, torn-paper scraps, and washi-tape collage elements.
- Editorial serif display type against a warm cream canvas, plus italic serif customer quotes as section dividers.
- Chunky rounded CRAFT wordmark used as a graphic element inside pricing cards, not just in the nav.

## Do / Don't
- Do keep every CTA a flat black pill with white text; differentiate hierarchy by size, not color.
- Do flood whole hero/section backgrounds with one pastel (sky #A3D7EF or butter #FDE688) rather than using pastels as small highlights.
- Do set headlines in a classic editorial serif and drop to a small neutral sans for body and UI chrome.
- Do add tactile texture — clouds, torn paper, tape, photographed hands holding devices — inside otherwise clean layouts.
- Do end pages with a near-black footer for hard contrast against the cream body.
- Don't introduce a saturated brand blue/purple for buttons or links; color here is ambient, never functional.
- Don't use sharp corners or heavy borders — everything is pill or 16px+ rounded with shadowless or whisper-soft elevation.
- Don't set headlines in bold geometric sans; the serif display voice is the identity.
- Don't crowd sections: heroes carry one headline, one line, one button.
- Don't use stark pure-white page backgrounds; the canvas is warm cream (#FDF9F6) with true white reserved for cards.
