## Overview
Clean white-canvas minimalism punctuated by hard swings into near-black sections and saturated spring-green gradient blocks. Photography of real merchants and product-UI screenshots carry most of the visual weight; chrome around them stays quiet — thin hairline dividers, small uppercase eyebrow labels, black pill CTAs. The enterprise page flips the scheme to a dark olive-black canvas with lime-green data cards while keeping the same typographic system.

## Layout
Sticky slim top nav: logo left, dropdown links center-left, "Log in" + black pill CTA right. Heroes are asymmetric: large left-aligned headline (often 2-3 lines) with short subhead and single CTA, paired with a photo collage or product screenshot on the right; hero backgrounds are either white with a soft mint gradient wash or a full green-to-teal gradient. Section rhythm alternates white → black → white → green-gradient bands, each opened by a tiny uppercase eyebrow label above the section heading. Grids are 3-4 columns with hairline top borders on list items (resources index is a dense hairline-ruled article grid). A scrolling marquee ticker ("START YOUR FREE TRIAL →") in monospace caps on a green/purple gradient acts as a mid-page divider. Footer is always black, multi-column, with a large solo logo mark.

## Components
Buttons: full pill shape; solid black fill with white text as the primary everywhere except dark sections, where they invert to white or pale lime (#c9f27e "Get in touch" on enterprise); no shadows, no borders. Secondary actions are plain text links, often underlined or with small arrows. Cards: pricing plan cards are white with 1px light-gray borders, ~14px radius, a colored top strip badge (green "$1 for your first month"), and a full-width black pill CTA at the bottom; content cards are borderless — a rounded image tile above plain text with an underlined link. Enterprise stat cards use lime-green gradient fills with large numerals. Inputs: pill email field with the black pill submit button nested flush at its right end. Icons sit in small rounded-square green-tinted chips. FAQ rows on black use circled "+" toggles with hairline separators.

## Signature
- Black pill CTA as the single, unmissable action shape on every page, inverting to lime/white on dark bands
- Spring-green → teal gradient bands bookending pages (hero and pre-footer), plus a monospace all-caps marquee ticker
- Hard light/dark section alternation: white body, pure-black support/FAQ blocks, black mega-footer
- Eyebrow pattern: tiny uppercase gray label → large tight grotesque heading → short muted subhead, left-aligned

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Use one solid black pill button per section; make every secondary action a text link with an arrow or underline
- Alternate full-bleed white, black, and green-gradient sections rather than tinting cards within one background
- Open each section with a small uppercase eyebrow label above a large left-aligned heading
- Separate dense list/grid content with 1px hairlines instead of card boxes (resources grid pattern)
- Let merchant photography and product-UI screenshots supply the color; keep surrounding chrome monochrome

Don't:
- Use the brand green as a button fill on white backgrounds — green is for gradients, badges, icon chips, and dark-section CTAs only
- Add drop shadows or borders to buttons; everything is flat fills and hairlines
- Center-align hero copy on marketing pages (only pricing/pre-footer bands center)
- Introduce blues or warm brand colors; the palette is black/white/green with only incidental photo color
- Round corners past ~16px on cards while buttons stay pill — keep the two-tier radius system
