## Overview
Near-black tech aesthetic with two loud brand notes: acid lime-yellow and soft periwinkle violet. The home page is fully dark with intricate white line-art "circuit/tiger" illustrations; secondary pages alternate white and black full-bleed bands, so the system reads as black-and-white editorial punctuated by a neon-yellow CTA block before the footer. Everything sits on a calm, centered, medium-width column with lots of vertical air.

## Layout
Slim sticky nav on black: logo left, five text links, then Contact Us, an outlined pill Login, and a filled yellow pill "Try for free". Heroes are centered (home, customer stories) or left-aligned with a decorative right-side graphic (pricing's bar-chart streaks). Sections alternate in strict full-width bands — black, white, black — with curved/swooshing divider shapes between some bands. Content follows a 2-3 column card grid or a text-left / visual-right split. Every page ends with the same signature: a full-bleed lime-yellow CTA band (black logo mark, centered heading, two black pill buttons) followed by a black multi-column footer.

## Components
- Buttons: always pills. Primary = solid fill with contrasting text (yellow on black nav; black with white text inside light/yellow sections). Secondary = thin 1px outlined pill in the local ink color. No shadows.
- Cards: light gray (#F4F4F5) rounded rectangles on white with logo, short paragraph, and a "Read the story" link led by a small black circular arrow badge; flat, borderless, no shadow.
- Feature blurbs: thin white line icons over black, small heading, muted gray body text.
- Data/UI panels: white rounded cards with subtle shadow (pricing diagram), dark rounded code blocks with syntax colors, dropdown selects and a slider in the cost estimator, checkmark feature lists.
- Testimonials: oversized yellow quotation-mark glyph, white quote text on black, tiny attribution line; logo rail on the left.

## Signature
- Acid lime-yellow (#F0F95C-ish) used sparingly but loudly: nav CTA, quote marks, social icons, and one full-bleed pre-footer CTA band on every page.
- White single-weight line-art illustrations on black — circuit-like traces forming a tiger head, elephant, loops — as hero and section art.
- Strict black/white band alternation with curved swoosh transitions between sections.
- Pill buttons everywhere, paired with a rounded geometric sans that gives headlines a friendly-technical voice.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the yellow reserved for CTAs and the single pre-footer band; let black/white carry 90% of the page.
- Use thin white line illustrations and icons on black instead of photos or gradients for hero art.
- End every page with the lime-yellow CTA band: centered mark, heading, two black pill buttons.
- Alternate full-bleed black and white sections; give each band generous vertical padding.
- Use flat light-gray cards with circular arrow-badge links for story/logo grids.

Don't:
- Don't use square or slightly-rounded buttons — every button on this site is a full pill.
- Don't add drop shadows or borders to cards on white; they stay flat gray blocks.
- Don't introduce more than the two accents (yellow, periwinkle/violet); the violet is only for data highlights and quote bands.
- Don't left-crowd layouts — headings are centered or in a roomy split, never edge-to-edge dense.
- Don't use heavy display weights or condensed type; headlines stay medium-weight, rounded, and airy.
