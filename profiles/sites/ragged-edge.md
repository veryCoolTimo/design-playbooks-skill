## Overview
Stark monochrome editorial minimalism: pure white canvas, near-black ink, hairline rules, and generous dead space. The only color comes from warm, cinematic case-study photography; everything else is black-on-white. Personality lives in the type pairing — blunt grotesque labels against a refined serif for content — plus solid-black pill buttons.

## Layout
Content sits on a wide left-aligned grid with a hard left margin and huge column gaps: short grotesque labels ("Find us", "Call us", "Email us") in the first column, serif values in the second, gray utility actions ("Copy") in a third. Full-width 1px hairline rules divide horizontal bands. Footer runs as stacked bands: contact rows, a button row split hard left ("Join") vs hard right (social pills), a two-column sitemap of plain serif links, then a tiny copyright line. Whitespace is extreme — bands are separated by 100px+ of empty canvas. Imagery is placed as a large right-aligned rectangular block with no radius or border.

## Components
- Buttons: fully pill-shaped, solid near-black (#141414) fill, white serif label, no border, no shadow, compact padding (e.g. "Join", "Instagram", "LinkedIn").
- Utility links: small gray (#9a9a9a) sans "Copy" actions beside data values; sitemap and legal links are plain serif text, underlined only for legal actions ("Show Details", "Reject All").
- Cookie banner: dark charcoal (#1e1e1e) rectangular panel, white text, light-gray pill "Accept All" button, circular outlined close icon.
- Cards/images: sharp-cornered, flush rectangles; no borders, no shadows, no radius.
- Dividers: 1px light-gray hairlines spanning the full content width.

## Signature
- Grotesque-sans labels paired with a bookish serif for all values, links, and buttons — the pairing is the brand.
- Solid black pill buttons as the only "component chrome" on an otherwise flat white page.
- Hairline-ruled horizontal bands with extreme vertical whitespace between them.
- Warm amber/orange photography as the sole color against strict black-and-white UI, plus the B Corp mark as a trust anchor.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep everything black-on-white; let photography carry all color and warmth.
- Use pill buttons with solid #141414 fill and white text for every action, including social links.
- Structure sections as label (grotesque) / value (serif) / utility action (small gray sans) columns.
- Separate content bands with full-width 1px hairlines and very generous vertical padding.
- Left-align aggressively; push paired elements to opposite edges of the row (Join left, socials right).

**Don't**
- Don't add card borders, drop shadows, or corner radius to images and containers — only buttons are rounded, and they are fully pill.
- Don't introduce brand accent colors into UI chrome; no colored links or colored buttons.
- Don't center text or use symmetric layouts; the grid is hard-left with ragged-right serif text.
- Don't use heavy font weights for body content — serif runs regular; only short sans labels get weight.
- Don't crowd sections; if a band feels empty, that is the intended rhythm.
