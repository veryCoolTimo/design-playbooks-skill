## Overview
One deep aubergine purple carries the entire brand: it fills every primary CTA, the featured pricing tier, and a tall closing footer band. Everything else stays quiet — white canvas, warm cream and pale lavender section washes, and near-black body text. Heroes get soft blurred pastel gradients (peach, lavender, dusty green) with product screenshots floating on top. Saturated blue inline links are the only color that isn't purple or neutral.

## Layout
Light top nav on white: wordmark left, links center, lavender + aubergine pill buttons right; collapses to hamburger under 768px. Content sits in a ~1240px container while pastel-mesh gradients bleed past its edges. Sections run 64-96px of vertical padding on marketing pages, 48px on transactional ones. Pricing goes 4-up to 2-up to 1-up; a 3-column stats row shows huge 50px purple numerals. Every marketing page ends in a full-bleed aubergine CTA band roughly 480-600px tall.

## Components
- Buttons: always pills (90px radius) with heavy padding (14px 28px). Primary is solid aubergine on white text; press state lightens to #611f69. Secondary is a pale lavender fill with dark text; outline variants use 2px borders in aubergine or white depending on surface.
- Cards: 16px radius, 32px padding, 1px #e6e6e6 hairline border on white; the featured pricing card inverts to solid aubergine with white text; feature cards use cream fills.
- Inputs: flat white, 4px radius, 10x12px padding, hairline border — deliberately plainer than the buttons.
- Depth comes from the gradient glow, not shadows; mockups float shadowless over the mesh. Light 0-5-20 shadows appear only on hero-floating buttons and toasts.
- Small all-caps eyebrow pills (12px, +0.96px tracking) on cream backgrounds label pricing tiers.

## Signature
- Chromatic monotheism: one purple everywhere important, no third accent — only blue (#1264a3) for inline links.
- Over-padded pill buttons; no rounded-rectangle or square button exists anywhere.
- Pastel-mesh gradient heroes with UI screenshots floating above (never inside) the wash.
- Display headlines with aggressive negative tracking for editorial density; giant purple statistics numerals on plain white.

## Do
- Keep aubergine scarce and loud: one filled purple button per viewport, plus the featured tier and footer band.
- Make every button a 90px-radius pill with at least 14px 28px padding.
- Apply negative letter-spacing to all display sizes (32-64px) at 600-700 weight.
- Back every hero with a blurred peach/lavender/green gradient and float the product mockup on top of it.
- Reserve #1264a3 strictly for inline text links; underline on hover only.

## Don't
- Don't introduce any accent beyond aubergine + link blue.
- Don't set headlines at default (0) tracking — they read loose in this system.
- Don't wrap product screenshots in card chrome or give them drop shadows; the gradient supplies the lift.
- Don't use aubergine as a body text color — it is a surface/CTA color only.
- Don't shrink pill padding or swap pills for square buttons, even in compact UI.
