## Overview
Minimal-light SaaS landing built on pure white canvas with soft watercolor-like pastel gradient washes (mint, sage, periwinkle, lavender) that bleed in from page corners. A single saturated teal (#47BEBA) carries every call to action against deep-indigo (#160747) headline text. The overall feel is airy, friendly, and product-forward, with in-copy highlighter marks adding an editorial "writing tool" flavor.

## Layout
Slim top nav: wordmark + "New Hiring" badge left, sparse link set center, Login + teal pill CTA right. Hero is fully centered: large two-line headline with word-level color highlights, dual sign-up pills (dark indigo + teal), "no credit card" microcopy, then a stack of three small pill-shaped benefit rows leading into a large product screenshot that overlaps the gradient background. Sections alternate: white social-proof block (giant stat number, mono logo row, 10-cell testimonial grid), a full-width pastel gradient band holding a 2x3 white card grid of use cases, a zig-zag "How it works" sequence where floating app-panel mockups alternate left/right connected by a dotted path, then a centered CTA reprise and a light multi-column footer. Whitespace is generous; content column is narrow and centered.

## Components
Buttons: full pill radius, solid teal #47BEBA fill with dark text/white text, secondary variant solid deep indigo #160747 with white text; small padding, no visible border or shadow. Cards: white fill, ~10px radius, very soft diffuse shadow, icon in a rounded pastel square + title + short copy + teal arrow-link ("Try X ->"). Hero benefit rows: white pill capsules with tiny eyebrow label, bold line, and left icon chip. Inputs (in mockups): thin gray 1px borders, small radius, teal full-width submit button. Testimonials: borderless — avatar, name, role, plain paragraph with yellow and cyan highlighter spans on key phrases.

## Signature
- Pastel watercolor gradient blobs (mint/sage/periwinkle) washing in from corners over an otherwise stark white page
- Highlighter marks inside real copy: yellow-olive highlighted words in headlines ("good", "marketing agencies|") with a text-cursor character, and yellow/cyan marker spans inside testimonials
- One teal, everywhere: nav CTA, sign-up pill, card links, and mockup buttons all share #47BEBA, paired with a deep-indigo secondary
- Zig-zag "How it works" of floating UI panels linked by a dotted connector path

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep body sections on pure white and confine color to gradient bands and corner washes
- Use pill-shaped buttons in flat teal #47BEBA; reserve deep indigo #160747 for the secondary action and headline text
- Highlight 1-2 words per headline with an olive-yellow tint and carry highlighter spans into testimonial copy
- Center the hero and stack: headline -> dual CTAs -> microcopy -> benefit pills -> product screenshot
- Keep cards white with small soft shadows and a teal arrow text-link as the card CTA

Don't:
- Don't use hard saturated backgrounds — all non-white areas are soft pastel gradients, never flat brand color blocks
- Don't add borders or outlines to buttons and cards; separation comes from fill contrast and faint shadows
- Don't introduce a second CTA color; teal is the only action color on the page
- Don't use pure black text — headings are deep indigo #140845, body is near-black indigo #17132F
- Don't crowd sections; every block gets a large vertical gutter and a narrow centered content column
