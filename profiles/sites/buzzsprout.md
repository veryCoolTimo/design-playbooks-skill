## Overview
Big-type, friendly-but-disciplined SaaS design: huge bold dark-green headlines on white, generous whitespace, and product screenshots doing most of the visual talking. Long scrolling pages alternate white sections with full-bleed color blocks — saturated golden yellow, deep indigo, and near-black — so the page reads as a rhythmic stack of distinct chapters. Color-rich podcast cover art and small flat-illustration icons add warmth without clutter.

## Layout
Sticky-feeling top nav: green sprout logo left, center text links (Features, Pricing, How to Start a Podcast, Blog), right-aligned Log In plus a dark-green filled CTA pill-corner button. Heroes are centered: one huge headline, one short subline, one CTA, then a large framed product screenshot. Section rhythm is strict full-width bands — white, yellow, indigo, black, white — each with a centered heading and either a screenshot collage or a 2-column text-beside-image split that alternates sides. Pricing is a classic 4-column card row on white with a row of small icon cards below and a left-aligned FAQ. Blog is a dense multi-column card grid of illustrated thumbnails. Footer sits on a pale sage-gray band with 3 link columns.

## Components
Buttons: solid dark forest green (#1B3C31) with white label, small 4-6px radius, no visible border or heavy shadow; secondary actions are plain underlined text links. Pricing cards: white, hairline light-gray border, small radius, subtle or no shadow, internally divided by thin horizontal rules; red text used inside cards to flag Free-plan limitations. Feature icon cards: small white tiles with soft shadow and a single colored flat icon. Screenshots shown in light browser-chrome frames with rounded corners and soft drop shadows. Star-rating testimonial cards on dark bands. No visible form inputs on marketing pages.

## Signature
- Oversized dark-forest-green headlines ("Simple pricing", "KEEP PODCASTING") on pure white with lots of air.
- Full-bleed golden-yellow and deep-indigo/black sections slicing the long scroll into bold color chapters.
- Real product UI everywhere: analytics dashboards, mobile app trios, embedded players as the primary imagery.
- One consistent CTA treatment — small-radius dark-green "Get Started Free" button — repeated at nav, hero, and every section break.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Set headlines very large and bold in the dark green ink; keep sublines short and plain.
- Alternate white sections with saturated full-width yellow, indigo, or black bands to pace long pages.
- Use exactly one button style (dark-green fill, small radius, white text) for every primary CTA and repeat it often.
- Lean on framed product screenshots and colorful podcast cover-art grids instead of stock photography.
- Keep pricing cards white with hairline borders and thin internal dividers; use red text sparingly to mark plan limits.

**Don't**
- Don't use gradients on buttons or text — fills are flat and solid throughout.
- Don't round buttons into pills or give cards large 16px+ radii; corners stay tight.
- Don't put body copy directly on the yellow/indigo bands without strong contrast (they carry headings, icons, and screenshots).
- Don't introduce a second CTA color; even on dark and yellow sections the button stays dark green or inverts to white/outline, never a new hue.
- Don't crowd sections — each band holds one idea with a centered heading and generous vertical padding.
