## Overview
A playful "testing lab" world built on pale mint canvases with saturated green, marigold yellow, and coral section blocks. Isometric illustrations of roads, robots, and gadgets carry the brand personality, while mono-styled headings and bracketed stat figures (e.g. `[217%]`) give it a developer-terminal wink. The overall effect is friendly dev-infra: bright, flat color fields with near-black green text and crisp white cards.

## Layout
Sticky white/light nav with logo left, dropdown links center, and a pill CTA pair right (outlined "Request a demo" + solid green "Try it free"). Heroes are split: left-aligned headline with highlighted keywords (yellow/coral marker behind words) and dual pill CTAs, right-side isometric illustration; hero backgrounds alternate mint, green, and yellow per page. Sections stack as full-bleed color bands — white, mint, saturated green, yellow — creating a striped rhythm down the page. Content sits in 3-column card grids (toolchain tiles, framework cards, blog posts). Every page ends the same way: a full-width yellow gradient CTA banner ("Deliver quality software continuously") above a near-black dark-green footer with a giant outlined robot glyph bleeding off the left edge.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped; primary is solid bright green (#3EDC81) with dark text, secondary is thin dark outline on light, and pricing/blog CTAs use solid near-black pills with white text. Cards: white fills, 8-14px corners, hairline or no border, essentially shadowless — flat color separates them from tinted backgrounds. Toolchain tiles pair a small green pictogram icon with title and 2-line description. Blog cards lead with a full-bleed illustrated thumbnail in a brand color (green/yellow/coral/dark), then a small pill "BLOG" tag, title, and "Published:" date. Pricing tiers are three bordered white cards with icon, large price, a dropdown select, and a full-width black pill button; the Enterprise tier is a saturated green rounded panel with a white checklist card inside. Stats render in monospace with decorative green bracket glyphs. Accordion rows with chevrons handle pricing details.

## Signature
- Bracketed monospace stat callouts like `[217%]` and `(<6 mos)` with green punctuation glyphs
- Marker-style highlight behind key headline words (yellow and coral rectangles behind "testing" / "development")
- Full-bleed section bands cycling mint → green → yellow → coral → dark green, with the yellow CTA banner + dark footer combo on every page
- Isometric illustrated scenes and a recurring outlined robot mascot ghosted into footers and banners

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Alternate full-width tinted bands (mint, bright green, yellow) instead of separating sections with borders or shadows
- Use pill buttons everywhere; reserve solid #3EDC81 for the top-level CTA and near-black pills for in-card actions
- Set headlines and stats in a mono-flavored face, and dress numbers with bracket/paren glyphs in green
- Give every illustration a flat isometric style using only the brand palette (green, yellow, coral, dark green)
- End pages with the yellow CTA banner followed by the dark green footer with the oversized robot outline

**Don't**
- Add drop shadows or heavy borders to cards — the site relies on flat white-on-tint contrast
- Use pure black text or backgrounds; the dark tone is always a green-cast near-black (#0D231B range)
- Introduce blue or purple; the palette stays strictly in the green/yellow/coral family
- Use photography as the primary visual — photos appear only as small blog thumbnails; illustration leads
- Sharpen button corners; nothing interactive here is rectangular
