## Overview
A minimal-light SaaS design that stays airy and restrained until it detonates color: near-white mint canvas, quiet gray-bordered cards, then full-bleed vermilion-orange band sections and playful memphis-style abstract art blocks. Hand-drawn product illustrations and sticker-like rounded-square logo badges give an AI/data platform a friendly, editorial-toy personality. Dark teal (near-black) anchors the nav pill, CTAs, and footer.

## Layout
Sticky top nav is a floating dark rounded bar variant: white strip with left logo, center text links, and a dark pill "Book a demo" CTA at right. Heroes split text-left against playful vertical/rotated graphic compositions (home) or tilted illustrated cards (features page), followed by a grayscale client-logo marquee (Auchan, Casino, Monoprix, METRO). Section rhythm alternates background planes: white → cream/pastel panel → full-bleed orange "Why Memory?" band → white stats → light-blue testimonial band → dark teal CTA + footer. Content sits in a centered container with wide side margins; stat rows use 4-up columns with oversized numerals; card grids run 3-4 across with horizontal carousels on home.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, dark teal/near-black fill with white label and small arrow/icon glyph ("Book a demo", "Request a demo"); secondary filters are white pill chips with thin gray borders. Cards: white fill, 1px light gray border, small-medium corner radius, no visible shadow, containing a line-art illustration, semibold title, and short muted description. On the orange band, cards become translucent lighter-orange tiles with dark ink text and tiny line icons. Careers page uses a plain table-style job list with row dividers, filter chip tabs, and avatar thumbnails. Photos are tilted polaroid-style with sticker badges overlapping corners. Testimonials are white cards on a pale blue band with carousel arrows in dark circles.

## Signature
- Full-bleed vermilion-orange (#F14E1C) section bands that interrupt an otherwise near-white page.
- Sticker-like rounded-square logo badges scattered in tilted clusters at section edges and over photos.
- Hand-drawn black line-art product illustrations with selective flat color fills inside plain bordered cards.
- Dark teal footer/CTA block with pill-and-icon typographic lockup ("Ready to make shopper-based decisions" with inline pink pill, diamond, arrow capsules).
- Memphis-style abstract color-block art tiles for "Our values" (yellow/pink/blue/orange compositions).

## Do
- Alternate section backgrounds deliberately: white/mint, cream, saturated orange band, pale blue, dark teal — one bold plane per screenful.
- Keep cards quiet: white, hairline gray border, no shadow; let the illustration inside carry the color.
- Use pill shapes for every interactive element (CTAs, chips, tab filters) and dark near-black fills for primary actions.
- Add playfulness through tilted stickers, rotated photos, and inline icon capsules inside headlines, not through gradients or 3D.
- Show oversized stat numerals (4%, 25%, 0, 50%) with tiny muted captions in 4-up rows.

## Don't
- Don't use orange as the button color — it is a background-band and accent color; CTAs stay dark teal/black.
- Don't add drop shadows, glassmorphism, or gradient meshes; surfaces are flat with hairline borders.
- Don't render product imagery as screenshots alone — pair or replace with hand-drawn line illustrations.
- Don't use large corner radii on cards; only buttons/chips are fully pill-shaped.
- Don't crowd the canvas: sections keep wide margins and single-idea focus even when a color band goes full-bleed.
