## Overview
Editorial fashion-magazine energy applied to a social-commerce SaaS. Cream and near-black full-bleed bands alternate down the page, punctuated by lavender, burnt orange, chartreuse yellow, and sage blocks. Oversized high-contrast serif headlines run edge-to-edge like magazine mastheads, while people photography (mostly black-and-white portraits and lifestyle shots) and dark product-UI screenshots carry the proof. The overall read is "Vogue meets DTC growth tool."

## Layout
Fixed light top nav: wordmark left, centered dropdown links, Login plus a black pill "Request a Demo" CTA right. Heroes are typographic — huge centered serif headline (sometimes with a colored word, e.g. lavender "REVENUE"), small eyebrow badge (numbered dot + uppercase microlabel), then a segmented pill toggle or CTA. Section rhythm is a strict stack of full-bleed color bands: cream → black → lavender → mint/green → yellow, each self-contained. Recurring devices: edge-to-edge marquee headlines that bleed off both sides ("the OLD WORLD…", "ORGANIC GROWTH perks…"), 2-column feature rows (text left, UI screenshot in a rounded colored panel right), 3-up card grids for case studies and team, and a circular photo-collage strip on pricing. Generous vertical padding; content column is wide but never cramped.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, solid near-black fill with white uppercase-ish label and a small leading dot/bullet ("• REQUEST A DEMO", "• CONTACT SALES NOW"); secondary is an outlined pill on cream. Segmented monthly/yearly and organic/paid toggles are twin pills in a bordered pill track. Cards: pricing cards on cream with 1px hairline border, ~12px radius, flat (no shadow); the featured plan gets a solid orange fill with white text and a "MOST POPULAR" black tag. Metric/stat tiles are flat muted-color rounded blocks (lavender, olive, sage, gray) with tiny uppercase labels and huge numerals. Product screenshots sit inside rounded panels tinted lavender/green/black. FAQ is an accordion list on a lavender band with hairline dividers and circled plus icons. Newsletter input: simple rectangular field with a small filled submit button inside the dark footer. Eyebrow labels everywhere: small black circle-number + uppercase letterspaced microtext.

## Signature
- Edge-to-edge marquee serif headlines with italic serif connector words ("perks", "our", "the") repeating past the viewport edges.
- Hard alternation of full-bleed bands: cream, near-black, lavender #B4A7EC, chartreuse #E9F04B, sage — no gradients anywhere.
- Black pill CTAs prefixed with a bullet dot, plus numbered-dot eyebrow badges opening every section.
- Black-and-white portrait photography (team grid, founder quote clipped into a blob/arch shape) mixed with dark dashboard and Instagram-DM UI mockups.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set headlines in a high-contrast serif at extreme sizes, letting them clip off the left and right edges of the viewport.
- Alternate solid full-bleed background bands (cream/black/lavender/yellow/sage) to segment the page instead of dividers.
- Use black pill buttons with a leading "•" and keep secondary actions as outlined pills.
- Desaturate people photography to black-and-white; reserve color for UI mockups and stat blocks.
- Open each section with a numbered circle badge + tiny uppercase eyebrow label.

Don't:
- Add gradients, glassmorphism, or drop shadows — every surface here is flat with hairline borders at most.
- Use blue or teal; the palette is strictly warm cream/black plus lavender, orange, chartreuse, sage.
- Round cards beyond ~14px or square off the buttons — pills are for actions, medium radius for containers.
- Set body copy in a serif; the serif is display-only, all supporting text stays in a plain grotesque.
- Center-stack feature sections; feature rows keep the text-left / screenshot-panel-right split.
