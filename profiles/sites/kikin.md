## Overview
A national-park outfitter aesthetic applied to B2B invoice financing: hand-drawn wilderness illustration (mountains, canoes, pine forests, log piles) over a deep forest-green canvas, alternating with warm cream sections. Massive all-caps black headlines carry the message while embroidered-patch-style circular badges scatter around them like merit badges. The single bright signal color is an electric grass green used only for CTAs and headline highlights.

## Layout
Minimal top nav: wordmark left, LOG IN (outline) + GET FUNDING (green fill) right, no menu links visible. Full-bleed illustrated hero with centered stacked headline (cream line over green line), short centered paragraph, single centered CTA. Sections alternate backgrounds in bold horizontal bands: dark green → cream → dark green (calculator) → photo band → cyan 3-column strip → cream badge field → cream FAQ → dark green illustrated footer. Feature rows pair left-aligned text (numbered 01/02/03 in small green circles) with product-UI screenshots set on photographic nature backgrounds. Generous vertical whitespace; content column is narrow and centered for statements, two-column for features.

## Components
Buttons: solid #56DC49 rectangles with medium (~10px) radius, dark green all-caps label, small and compact relative to the huge type; secondary button is a thin-outlined rounded rect on dark. Cards: product screenshots float on rounded-corner photo panels; the funding calculator is a dark green panel with sliders (green track dots), line-item rows, and a huge green price figure. Circular badge stickers with contrasting ring colors (green, orange, yellow, blue, salmon) act as decorative components. FAQ: full-width rows with left category rail, all-caps questions, plus-icon expanders, hairline dividers. A cyan strip presents three lettered (A/B/C) columns with thin vertical dividers.

## Signature
- Embroidered scout-patch badges ("CHANGE THE WORLD", "BORROW WISELY", "1% FOR THE PLANET") scattered as floating decoration
- Detailed line-art wilderness panoramas in green/orange/blue/cream on near-black forest green, framing hero and footer
- Ultra-heavy all-caps headlines with two-tone stacking (cream + electric green, or dark green on cream)
- Strict alternation of only three surface colors: forest green #122216, cream #F4ECE3, and one cyan #73D3EC interlude

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve #56DC49 strictly for CTAs, numbered step dots, and headline highlight lines
- Set every headline in all-caps black weight at very large sizes; keep body copy short and centered under it
- Use the dark forest green as both hero/footer canvas and as ink on cream sections (same #122216 both ways)
- Decorate with circular patch/sticker motifs and hand-drawn outdoor illustration, keeping product UI screenshots crisp and flat by contrast
- Keep buttons small and understated; let type scale do the selling

Don't:
- Don't introduce gradients, glassmorphism, or drop shadows — surfaces are flat matte blocks
- Don't use pure white or pure black; the palette warms everything (cream #F4ECE3, green-black #122216)
- Don't use pill buttons or large radii — corners stay modestly rounded
- Don't set headlines in mixed case or light weights; the voice is all-caps and heavy everywhere
- Don't spread the accent brights (orange, cyan, yellow) into UI controls — they live in illustration and badges only
