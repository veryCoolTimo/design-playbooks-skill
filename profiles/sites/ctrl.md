## Overview
Warm off-white minimalism punctuated by flat candy-pastel color blocks and huge black grotesque type. The page reads mostly monochrome — cream canvas, near-black text — until pastel blue/yellow/pink cards and a bright green CTA inject a playful toy-like energy. Illustrations are flat, sticker-like shapes with thin black outlines, echoing the checkerboard Ctrl logo mark.

## Layout
Sticky top bar: logo left, a centered pill-shaped nav capsule with 3-4 links separated by thin dividers, and a solid black "Download" pill on the right. Heroes are centered, dominated by one enormous headline ("Take Ctrl.", "Security", "$XDEFI") often with an inline pastel badge or logo glyph inside the headline itself. Sections alternate between the cream canvas and soft gray-green gradient bands. Content sits in a narrow centered column with big vertical breathing room; feature storytelling uses a 3-column rhythm (pastel caption card | phone mockup | plain text) or stacked full-width white cards. Every page ends the same way: newsletter input, a giant left-aligned "Level up." sign-off headline, then footer link columns and a green pill CTA.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, solid fills — black pill for nav Download, bright green (#35C838) pill with logo glyph for the main "Download for Chrome" CTA; small black "Subscribe" pill. No gradients, no shadows. Cards: two families — flat pastel-filled rounded rectangles (blue/yellow/pink, ~16-24px radius) holding short captions or metrics, and plain white cards with generous padding for feature/FAQ content. Security page uses enormous outlined capsule rows numbered 01-04, each with a colored outlined circle icon. Inputs: single-line email field as a thin-outlined pill with gray placeholder. FAQ: full-width white rows with plus/chevron toggles. Icons: pastel circles with 1px black strokes.

## Signature
- One oversized black grotesque headline per section, often with a pastel oval badge or logo mark embedded inline in the text
- Flat candy pastels (powder blue, lemon yellow, bubblegum pink) used as full card fills with black text, never as tints or gradients
- Numbered capsule mega-rows (01-04) as a feature list format on the security page
- The recurring "Level up." giant sign-off above the footer, and the green logo-glyph pill CTA

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas warm off-white and let black type carry the hierarchy; reserve color for card fills and the green CTA
- Use pill geometry consistently — nav capsule, buttons, inputs, even section rows
- Fill metric/caption cards with one flat pastel each and set the text in black
- Scale hero and sign-off headlines to dominate the viewport (display sizes far beyond typical h1)
- Draw icons and illustrations as flat shapes with thin black outlines in the same pastel set

Don't:
- Add drop shadows, glassmorphism, or gradients on buttons/cards — every surface here is flat (only faint gray-green section gradients exist)
- Use blue links or a blue primary button; the CTA is bright green and secondary actions are black pills
- Mix in dark sections; the site never inverts to a dark background
- Use serif or rounded-friendly typefaces; everything is one sharp grotesque family
- Crowd sections — each idea gets a full band of whitespace with a centered column
