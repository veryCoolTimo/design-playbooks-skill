## Overview
A big-type, poster-like one-pager built from full-width color bands: cool gray canvas, pure black interludes, forest green (#335b47), and one screaming acid-yellow block. Nearly every word is set in a heavy grotesque — headlines fill the entire viewport width ("WOW·PAGE", "PROCESS", "STEPS", "OUR CREDS"), and even body copy is bold. Hand-drawn scribble illustrations (globe, apple, flower, bearded PM mascot) in the same forest green cut the corporate stiffness.

## Layout
No conventional nav — just a small logo mark top-left and a live "Berlin, 16:24" timestamp top-right; the page opens with a quiet gray manifesto block and a "Keep scrolling" cue. Rhythm is a stack of alternating full-bleed color bands (gray → black → gray → green → gray → white → green), each band a self-contained chapter announced by a viewport-wide headline. Content sits in chunky rectangular tiles arranged in loose bento grids with thin gaps; giant single-word section labels ("PROCESS", "STEPS") bleed off the band edges, sometimes in a barely-visible tonal ghost ("OUR CREDS" in pale gray on white). Whitespace is generous between bands, dense inside tiles.

## Components
Buttons are solid pill shapes filled with forest green #335b47 and white bold uppercase labels ("I'M IN", "NEXT", "GET ACCESS"); secondary "BACK" is a gray-outlined pill ghost. The email capture is a single white pill input with the green pill CTA docked inside its right end. The lead form is styled as a chat card: large-radius white/gray container, rounded white inputs with gray placeholder text, a progress bar, and the hand-drawn "Mirco PM" mascot beside it. Cards split two ways: hard-cornered info tiles (pricing, "INCLUDES" block, reasons grid with thin green keylines) versus large-radius rounded feature chips ("MONOLINGUAL", "REDUCE TIME TO MARKET", the 4-weeks yellow tile). No shadows anywhere — flat fills and 1px borders only.

## Signature
- Viewport-filling black/white headlines that behave like section dividers, including ghost-tone words layered behind content
- Strict four-color world: cool gray, black, forest green, acid yellow — the yellow used exactly once per stretch as a jolt
- Green marker-scribble illustrations and doodle icons pasted into an otherwise rigid grid
- Personality furniture: city-and-time stamp in the header/footer, "Keep scrolling" cue, chat-style lead form with a drawn PM character, "WITH LOVE, FROM BERLIN" sign-off

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Set section titles at true viewport scale (10rem+) in a heavy grotesque, uppercase, tight tracking, and let them touch or bleed past edges
- Alternate full-bleed background bands (gray / black / green / white) instead of using cards on one continuous background
- Keep every CTA a solid #335b47 pill with white bold uppercase text; reserve #e7f760 for one attention tile per screen, never for buttons
- Use forest green as the text color on light backgrounds — body copy, headings, and labels alike, in bold weight
- Mix sharp-cornered data tiles with large-radius rounded accent chips inside the same grid

**Don't**
- Don't add drop shadows, gradients, or glassmorphism — everything here is flat fill plus occasional 1px keyline
- Don't introduce a sticky nav bar or menu; the page is a linear scroll story with only a logo and timestamp up top
- Don't use regular or light font weights for paragraphs — thin text breaks the poster voice
- Don't render icons as clean geometric glyphs; illustrations must look hand-scribbled with marker texture
- Don't expand the palette with blues, reds, or neutral-warm tones; the cool gray #dae0e4 is the only "white" on most bands
