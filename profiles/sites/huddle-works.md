## Overview
Warm minimal-light marketplace site built on a putty-gray canvas with pure-white content sections and a restrained set of muted pastels (lavender, dusty rose, ochre, sage-teal). Near-black ink and a solid black pill CTA carry all the contrast; color is reserved for illustrative product-UI mockup cards. The mood is friendly-editorial rather than corporate SaaS: inline avatar photos inside the headline, small-caps step labels, and playful faux-app cards.

## Layout
Sparse top nav: wordmark left, single "Sign in →" text link right, on the gray canvas with a hairline divider. Hero is a split layout — large left-aligned headline with inline avatar cutouts, short paragraph, dual CTA (black pill + text link) on the left; a scrolling column of pastel project-status cards on the right. Below, the page becomes a giant white rounded panel holding a 4-step sequence: each step is a two-column row with eyebrow label ("STEP 1 · THE BRIEF"), medium heading, one-line description, a small gray testimonial quote card with avatar, and a large pastel mockup card on the right. Rows alternate generous vertical whitespace; section breaks use rounded corners where panels meet. Page ends with a dusty-rose marquee ticker strip, then a two-up split CTA band (ochre "I'm a Founder" / sage-teal "I'm a Builder"), then a light multi-column footer.

## Components
- Buttons: solid near-black pill with white label and trailing arrow ("Get a Huddle →"); secondary is plain text link. In the split CTA band, pills recolor to tone-on-tone darks (brown-on-ochre, dark slate-on-teal).
- Cards: large-radius (24px+) flat pastel fills, no borders, no shadows; contain faux product UI — status dots, timestamps, tag chips, avatar clusters, wallet stats with mini area chart.
- Chips/tags: pill outlines or tiny dark filled pills in matching hue, small-caps or lowercase hashtag labels.
- Testimonial quotes: small light-gray rounded rectangles with tiny italic-free text and a circular avatar + name/role below.
- Marquee/ticker: full-width rose strip with inline avatars and live-activity copy.

## Signature
- Avatar photo cutouts embedded inside the hero headline parentheses.
- Faux app-UI cards in muted lavender/rose/teal acting as the sole imagery.
- Numbered small-caps eyebrow labels ("STEP 2 · THE TEAM") pacing a story-driven scroll.
- Black pill CTAs with trailing arrows as the only high-contrast accent on warm gray/white.
- Rounded white mega-panel floating on the putty canvas, with matching rounded seams between sections.

## Do
- Keep the canvas warm gray (#e5e6e1) and let content live in big white rounded panels.
- Use one solid black pill CTA per view; secondaries stay as plain text links with arrows.
- Express product/features as flat pastel mockup cards with chips, avatars, and status dots instead of screenshots or photos.
- Pace long pages with small-caps "STEP n · LABEL" eyebrows and one idea per two-column row.
- Keep pastels muted and grayed (lavender #bab1cd, rose #cd9ca1, ochre #e4b875, teal #b1bcbd) — never saturated.

## Don't
- Don't add borders, drop shadows, or gradients to cards — everything here is flat fills with big radii.
- Don't introduce a bright brand blue/purple CTA; contrast comes only from near-black on light.
- Don't use stock photography as hero imagery; the only photos are small circular avatars and one product-shot inside a card.
- Don't tighten vertical rhythm — each step row gets a full viewport-scale block of whitespace.
- Don't mix typefaces; the whole page runs on one grotesque sans differentiated by size and small-caps.
