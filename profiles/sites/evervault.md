## Overview
Near-black navy canvas washed with large luminous violet gradients that bloom from section edges — the neon element is a soft purple glow rather than hard neon lines. Content sits in dark rounded panels with hairline borders; the main CTA is a stark white pill that pops against everything. Use-case subpages invert to a white canvas with black pill CTAs, keeping the same violet accent and geometry.

## Layout
Centered pill-shaped nav bar floating at the top (logo left, links in a rounded capsule, Log in + white "Get started" pill right). Home hero: left-aligned display headline over a full-width purple gradient field, two pill CTAs, followed by a monochrome logo strip. Sections alternate between short centered section titles ("Encrypt", "Process", "Share") and large rounded feature panels — often a purple-glow panel paired with a dark code-editor panel in a 2-column split. Blog is a strict 2-column card grid with a full-width feature row on top. Pricing is a 3-column card row plus a hairline-divided comparison table. Whitespace is generous; sections breathe with 150px+ vertical gaps.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped; primary is solid white fill with near-black text; secondary is dark/transparent pill with thin light border ("Get in touch", "Contact Sales"). Small pill badges ("Beta", "Popular") with hairline borders. Cards: 10-14px radius, fill slightly lighter than canvas (#12131C-ish), 1px low-contrast borders, no drop shadows on dark pages — depth comes from gradient glow inside the card. Inputs (shown in product mockups): white cards with light-gray bordered fields, small radius. Tables: hairline row dividers, check icons in circles, no zebra striping. Code panels: dark editor chrome with syntax highlighting in purple/green.

## Signature
- Massive soft violet gradient blooms (#5B2EEA → #B9A6FF) that fade into black, used as hero backdrops and inside feature cards
- White pill CTA as the single high-contrast action element on an otherwise dark, low-contrast page
- Floating capsule nav centered in the header
- Real code editors and product UI mockups embedded inside rounded panels as the primary imagery; playful engraved-illustration art on blog cards

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Use one dominant violet gradient per hero/section, blurred and fading into the #0A0A12 canvas
- Reserve solid white pills for primary CTAs; make secondary actions bordered transparent pills
- Frame features and code samples inside dark rounded panels with 1px hairline borders
- Keep section headers short (one or two words) and centered between long scroll gaps
- Use hairline dividers and circled check icons for pricing/comparison tables

Don't:
- Add drop shadows or elevation on dark sections — depth comes from internal glow only
- Use saturated multi-color accents; everything non-neutral stays in the violet family
- Use sharp-cornered or small-radius buttons; every button and badge is a pill
- Fill the layout densely — the design relies on large empty dark stretches between sections
- Put colored fills behind body text; long copy always sits in muted gray on near-black (or dark gray on white for inverted pages)
