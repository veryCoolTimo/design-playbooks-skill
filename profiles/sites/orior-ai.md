## Overview
An extremely restrained, warm-paper landing page: a single black grotesque headline floating on an off-white canvas with almost nothing else on screen. Interface chrome is reduced to a tiny black flower-mark logo, three quiet text links, and one pill CTA. The photorealistic character imagery (per the site's premise) is given the entire viewport as a stage, with UI elements appearing as small floating pill badges rather than framed sections.

## Layout
Top bar with logo mark far left and right-aligned nav (Community, Pricing, Blog) plus a pill CTA; no visible borders or background on the nav. Hero is a centered stack: one large sentence-case headline ending with a period, one line of gray subtext beneath it, and vast empty space below reserved for imagery/animation. Scroll states show scroll-driven staging: floating status pills ("Creating your character", "Face matched") appear over the canvas, connected by hairline leader lines with dot terminals, like annotations on a photo. No card grids or dense sections visible; rhythm is one idea per viewport with enormous whitespace.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped; the main CTA "Start creating" is a light warm-gray fill (#D9D6D0) with near-black semibold text — no border, no shadow. Nav links are plain gray text, no underline. Status/progress chips: white pill badges with a soft diffuse drop shadow, containing a small spinner or checkmark icon plus gray label text. Annotation lines: 1px light-gray leaders with a small dot endpoint linking a chip to a point on the canvas. No inputs, cards, or tables visible in these captures.

## Signature
- One bold black grotesque headline in sentence case, terminated with a period, centered on warm off-white
- Monochrome warm-neutral palette with no accent color anywhere — the CTA is just a slightly darker gray pill
- Floating white pill chips with spinners/checkmarks and hairline leader lines, narrating an AI process over empty canvas
- Tiny hand-drawn black flower logo mark as the only graphic ornament

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas a warm paper tone (#F5F3EE), never pure white or cool gray
- Make every interactive element a pill; use fill-only buttons with no borders or heavy shadows
- Let headlines carry the page: large, bold, tight-tracked, sentence case with a closing period
- Narrate process states with small floating white chips (spinner/check + gray label) and thin leader lines
- Leave most of the viewport empty; one message per screen

Don't:
- Introduce a saturated accent or colored CTA — the system is strictly warm monochrome
- Add section dividers, boxed cards, or visible nav backgrounds/borders
- Use uppercase headings, eyebrow labels, or decorative gradients
- Crowd the hero with feature grids or multiple CTAs; there is exactly one pill CTA in the nav
- Apply hard shadows or outlined chips — shadows stay soft and diffuse, borders stay absent
