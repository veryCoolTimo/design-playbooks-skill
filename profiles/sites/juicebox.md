## Overview
Light, near-monochrome SaaS design that saves color for tiny, deliberate hits: a warm off-white canvas, near-black ink and solid black pill CTAs, with electric violet and magenta reserved for links, highlight chips, and neon wireframe illustrations. Product UI screenshots do most of the storytelling; decoration comes from perspective grid lines that converge behind hero and section content. The footer flips to full black with scanline stripes, echoing the dark neon artwork used on blog covers.

## Layout
Sticky-free slim top nav: wordmark left, sparse text links plus "Sign in" and a black pill "Try for free" right; an announcement pill chip floats above the hero headline. Hero is centered text (two-line headline, one-paragraph subhead, two pill CTAs) over a large product screenshot framed by receding 3D wireframe grid lines, followed by a grayscale logo marquee strip on a slightly darker band. Body alternates two-column feature blocks (text one side, floating UI cards the other), each with its own "Try for free" + "Request a demo" link pair, ending in centered FAQ accordions and a black footer. Pricing uses a 4-up card row where the "Team" plan is inverted to solid black, then a long bordered comparison matrix. Blog is a single centered column of stacked post cards. Whitespace is generous; content column is narrow and centered.

## Components
Buttons: solid black pill with white text as primary; secondary is white pill with thin gray border; tertiary actions are plain purple text links with arrow ("Request a demo →"). One gradient violet-to-pink pill appears in the hero. Cards: white, 8-14px radius, hairline gray border, very soft diffuse shadow; candidate-profile cards float with layered offsets. Highlighted plan card inverts to black with white text and a "Popular" chip. Chips/badges: small pill outlines, purple text or purple "20% off" tag on the billing toggle. Inputs seen as chat-style query bars inside product mockups. Checklist rows use small gray checkmarks; FAQ rows are full-width hairline-divided accordions with chevrons. Inline text highlights use pastel purple/lavender background behind key phrases.

## Signature
- Solid black pill CTAs on a warm light-gray canvas, with violet reserved for links and micro-accents only.
- Perspective wireframe grid lines radiating behind heroes and cards, light-gray on the page, neon magenta/cyan on black in blog covers and the footer.
- Floating layered candidate-profile cards and chat-style search mockups as the main feature imagery.
- Inverted all-black "Popular" pricing card breaking an otherwise white card row.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas warm off-white (#F5F5F4) and make primary CTAs solid black pills; pair every CTA with a purple "Request a demo →" text link.
- Use hairline gray borders plus a barely-there shadow on white cards; float profile cards at staggered offsets.
- Draw thin converging perspective grid lines as section decoration; switch them to neon magenta/cyan on black for dark art panels.
- Highlight key phrases inside body copy and mock chat responses with pastel lavender/purple text backgrounds.
- Invert one emphasized card (pricing "Team", nav CTA) to full black for contrast.

Don't:
- Don't flood sections with purple fills — violet is for links, chips, and one gradient button, never for large backgrounds outside blog cover art.
- Don't round cards to pill-level radii or square off buttons; buttons are pills, cards stay at modest 8-14px.
- Don't use heavy drop shadows, glassmorphism, or colored borders; depth comes from hairlines and faint diffuse shadows.
- Don't set oversized ultra-bold display type; headlines stay medium/semibold at moderate sizes with tight, calm spacing.
- Don't introduce warm accent colors (orange/green) into the UI; the only chromatic range is violet-magenta-indigo plus neon cyan line work.
