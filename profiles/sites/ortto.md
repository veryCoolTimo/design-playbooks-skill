## Overview
Warm minimal-light SaaS design on a putty off-white canvas (#f5f3f1) with near-black navy ink. Restraint everywhere except one trick: key words inside headlines are colorized (teal, burnt orange, muted gold) and followed by small inline pill/arrow glyphs, turning typography itself into the brand graphic. Product UI screenshots and journey-builder flowcharts do the heavy illustrative lifting; a single vivid blue (#2765fd) is reserved for CTAs.

## Layout
Slim top nav: wordmark left, five text links, right-aligned "Contact sales / Sign in / Try Ortto free" — no boxed nav bar, it sits directly on the canvas. Hero is left-aligned oversized headline + one pill CTA + text link, immediately followed by a large layered product-screenshot collage (dashboard behind, journey-builder modal in front). Long single column of alternating full-width bands: off-white → pure white → off-white, each a two-column split (product visual one side, short heading + 1-2 sentence copy + arrow text-link the other side, alternating left/right). Mid-page features section uses a small-caps eyebrow ("ALL FEATURES") over a 3-column icon grid with tiny icons, bold micro-headings, and "Learn more →" links. Generous vertical whitespace; content held to a moderate max-width. Dark navy footer block contains the final CTA pair plus a dense multi-column sitemap.

## Components
Buttons: solid #2765fd pill, white label, no shadow, no border; secondary action is a plain underlined/arrow text link, not a ghost button. Cards: white with medium (8-14px) rounded corners and very soft diffuse shadows — used for embedded product-UI panels, flowchart nodes, and template thumbnails; template cards sit on the tinted canvas with a thin light border. Journey-builder nodes are small white rounded cards with a colored circular icon (teal, purple, orange) and YES/NO branch labels. Feature-grid items are borderless: icon, bold label, small gray body, blue "Learn more →". Testimonial is a full-width teal-tinted rounded panel with a photo, large quote, and a white pill button. Logo bar shows full-color customer logos, not grayscaled.

## Signature
- Headline words highlighted in teal / burnt orange / muted gold with inline pill-shaped glyph icons trailing each colored word ("customer data ●●●, messaging ▶▶")
- Warm putty #f5f3f1 canvas alternating with pure-white bands instead of gray-on-white
- Real product UI — dashboards and node-based journey flowcharts with YES/NO branches — as the only imagery in the top half; no abstract 3D or stock art
- One saturated blue used exclusively for pill CTAs against an otherwise muted earth-tone accent set

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve #2765fd strictly for pill-shaped CTA buttons; keep every secondary action as an arrow text link
- Colorize individual keywords inside black headlines using the teal/orange/gold trio, with small inline glyphs
- Alternate #f5f3f1 and #ffffff section backgrounds to create rhythm without rules or dividers
- Show layered, realistic product-UI screenshots and flowchart node cards with soft shadows as section visuals
- End the page with a dark #1b1e2a footer band that carries the closing CTA and sitemap

Don't:
- Don't use pure white as the page canvas — the warm putty tint is the base, white is the alternate
- Don't add gradients, glassmorphism, or heavy drop shadows; shadows stay barely-there and diffuse
- Don't introduce ghost/outlined primary buttons — the hierarchy is solid blue pill vs. plain text link
- Don't use the accent teal/orange/gold for buttons or backgrounds at full bleed; they live in headline words, icons, and small chart marks
- Don't grayscale the customer logo bar or replace product screenshots with abstract illustration
