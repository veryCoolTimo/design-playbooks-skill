## Overview
A warm, hand-drawn take on a SaaS landing page: near-white gray canvas, near-black headings, and bursts of multicolor marker-doodle crowds (waving hands, smiling faces) as the only imagery besides product UI. Monochrome structure carries the layout; color lives almost exclusively in the illustrations and small icon chips. Thin cursive script eyebrows ("Listen!", "Open Letter", "Waitlist") set a personal, letter-from-the-founder tone.

## Layout
Minimal top nav: logo mark centered-left, only "Blog" plus a black pill "Sign up to Waitlist" at right. Hero is centered text over a tilted floating widget card, flanked left and right by doodle-crowd illustrations, with a faint gray grid texture on the canvas. Sections alternate rhythm: centered hero, then a two-column "open letter" (headline left, long gray prose right) linked by a dashed path with small circular icon nodes, then a full-bleed dark charcoal (#292929) rounded band holding a 2x2 card grid, then centered feature/pricing/waitlist sections. Every section opens with a script eyebrow above the heading. Generous whitespace throughout; content column stays narrow and centered.

## Components
Buttons: solid near-black (#292929) pill with white label, no border or shadow; disabled state is a flat light-gray pill with gray text ("Coming soon"). Cards: white, large radius (~20px), whisper-light shadow; the hero widget card is playfully rotated a few degrees with a stacked duplicate behind it. Dark-section cards are lighter charcoal (#3a3a3a) on the #292929 band, radius ~20px, each led by a solid color circle icon chip (blue #0aa4ec, red #f0433c, orange, green). Pricing: two white cards, the premium one capped with a black rounded header lip; green circular checkmarks and orange serif "Coming soon" flags on list items. Widget mockup shows numbered 1-5 rating squares, plain bordered inputs, and a small dark send button.

## Signature
- Multicolor marker-doodle crowds of faces and waving hands (blue, pink, yellow, green, red) on a gray-white canvas — the brand's entire color story.
- Thin cursive script eyebrow above every section heading ("Listen!", "Hear it out", "Pricing", "Waitlist").
- Grayscale-first UI: black pill CTAs, gray prose, charcoal dark band — color reserved for doodles and icon chips.
- Dashed connector path with floating circular icon nodes guiding the scroll between sections.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep structure monochrome (near-black on #fafafa) and let hand-drawn doodles supply all the color.
- Use solid black pill buttons for every CTA, including the nav.
- Open each section with a small script-font eyebrow, then a bold grotesque heading.
- Rotate/stack the hero widget card slightly for a playful, sticker-like feel.
- Use one full-width rounded charcoal band mid-page for the "why" card grid.

Don't:
- Don't introduce a saturated brand color for buttons or links — CTAs stay near-black.
- Don't use photography or 3D renders; imagery is line-doodle illustration and flat product screenshots only.
- Don't put heavy borders or strong shadows on cards; keep them white with barely-there elevation.
- Don't use pure white (#ffffff) as the page background — the canvas is off-white with a faint grid texture.
- Don't set body copy in full black; paragraphs run mid-gray against near-black headings.
