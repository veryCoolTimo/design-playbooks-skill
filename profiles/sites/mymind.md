## Overview
Soft-focus gradient meshes (peach, pink, lavender, periwinkle) glow behind an otherwise calm, paper-like off-white canvas. Enormous light serif headlines set an editorial, almost literary tone, while UI chrome stays minimal: tiny sans labels, pill buttons, white cards. A single vivid vermilion orange carries all emphasis - CTA pill, "NO" list markers, full-bleed statement sections, and blog cover cards.

## Layout
Sticky-less top bar: small logo left, sparse text nav center (each item prefixed with a tiny colored dot), one orange pill CTA right. Heroes are centered: a radial gradient orb or full mesh wash behind a 2-line serif headline, then a product masonry collage. Sections alternate long single-column reading measures (~600px, sometimes left-offset like an essay) with full-width color blocks (solid orange or gradient) used as tonal breaks. The how page stacks alternating persona cards (text left, screenshot right) in one column. Blog page uses a 3-column masonry card grid. Footer is a white rounded card floating on gradient, with 4 small link columns and a boxed "See how it works" callout.

## Components
Buttons: small pill, orange fill with white text for primary (Register / Sign In, CREATE YOUR NEW MIND appears as white/outline pill on orange); tiny uppercase sans labels inside. Cards: white, 8-14px radius, very soft diffuse shadow, generous padding; blog cards contain full-bleed orange covers with duotone portraits arched inside. Chat-bubble style testimonial pills with heavy rounding. List rows (article links) are white pills with small book icons. Search inputs render as the product itself: "Search my mind..." in italic serif inside a white card. Small uppercase letterspaced kicker labels (orange or gray) precede headings.

## Signature
- Blurred gradient-mesh orbs (peach-pink-lavender-blue) bleeding from page edges behind a quiet gray-white canvas.
- Giant light editorial serif headlines ("Remember everything. Organize nothing.", "Why?") paired with tiny grotesque body text.
- One saturated vermilion orange doing every job: CTA pill, section backgrounds, blog card covers, "NO" markers.
- Product shown as masonry collages of real saved cards (images, quotes, notes) rather than framed device mockups.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas a near-neutral warm gray-white and let gradients glow only at section edges and heroes.
- Set headlines in a light serif at extreme sizes; drop to a small neutral sans for everything else.
- Use the orange sparingly on light sections, then flip it: full orange sections with white/cream serif type as rhythm breaks.
- Round buttons to full pills and keep them small; cards get modest 8-14px corners with soft shadows.
- Prefix nav items and kickers with tiny dots or uppercase letterspaced micro-labels.

Don't:
- Add hard borders, dark outlines, or strong drop shadows - depth comes from blur and soft diffusion.
- Introduce a second accent hue for CTAs; blues/pinks live only inside gradients, never as UI colors.
- Use bold or condensed display type; the serif stays light even at 120px+.
- Frame product shots in browser chrome or devices; show loose masonry collages of content cards.
- Fill sections edge-to-edge with text; reading columns stay narrow (~600px) with essay-like whitespace.
