## Overview
A near-monochrome white/black canvas punctuated by 3D-rendered characters in vivid purple puffer jackets. The hero sits on a pale lavender 3D floor grid (#cccfe3) with an oversized extended-caps MORFLAX wordmark behind the avatar; the rest of the page alternates stark white and pure black full-bleed bands. Illustration style is glossy real-time 3D renders (avatars, headphones, sunglasses), not flat vectors.

## Layout
Slim top nav: wordmark left, small text links right, single solid-white pill "Explore" CTA. Hero is a full-viewport 3D scene with giant background typography, a short subhead, arrow-prefixed list links, and two ghost pill buttons at the bottom edge. Sections alternate white / black backgrounds in wide horizontal bands; each product section is a two-column split (big two-line headline + short paragraph on one side, layered UI-mockup or 3D render on the other), sides swapping section to section. Small uppercase pill badges ("STUDIO", "EXPERIMENTS") label sections. Generous vertical whitespace; a centered 3-line editorial statement acts as an interstitial. Footer is black with a 4-column sitemap and a faint perspective grid graphic above it.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, mostly ghost style — transparent fill with a thin 1px light or dark border, small label, often a northeast arrow glyph; the one solid button is the white nav "Explore" pill with black text. Cards: white or black rounded rectangles (~12px) with hairline borders and no visible shadows, containing screenshots or renders; the three-up feature row mixes two light cards with one black card. UI mockups are shown as stacked, slightly offset floating windows. No form inputs visible. Section labels are tiny black pill badges with an icon.

## Signature
- Glossy 3D avatar characters (bucket hat, sunglasses, iridescent purple puffer jacket) as the sole source of saturated color on a black/white page.
- Giant background wordmark typography behind the hero character, with thin crop-mark/frame rules.
- Strict white ↔ black full-bleed section alternation with pill ghost buttons everywhere.
- Mixed-weight headlines: one bold word + the rest light within the same line ("**Ecosystem** of / 3D design tools").

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the palette monochrome and let 3D renders carry all the color; purple (#a932ec) appears only inside imagery, never as UI chrome.
- Use pill buttons with hairline borders and transparent fills; reserve a solid white pill for the single top-level CTA.
- Alternate pure-white and pure-black sections and swap the text/image columns each time.
- Mix bold and light weights inside one headline, and use tiny uppercase pill badges to label sections.
- Show product UI as layered floating window mockups with rounded corners and hairline borders.

Don't:
- Don't use purple (or any saturated color) for buttons, links, or backgrounds — the UI itself stays black/white/lavender-gray.
- Don't add drop shadows or heavy card elevation; separation comes from background flips and hairline borders.
- Don't use serif or humanist type; everything is one squared grotesque family at contrasting weights.
- Don't fill sections edge-to-edge with text; each band holds one short headline, one short paragraph, and one visual.
- Don't square off buttons — every actionable element is a full pill.
