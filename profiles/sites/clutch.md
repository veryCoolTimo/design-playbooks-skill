## Overview
A stark black-on-white technical-minimal language for a non-human identity security platform. Nearly the entire site is monochrome — white canvas, near-black ink, hairline rules — punctuated by a single electric blue (#204DFE) that appears as light-beam gradient streaks and occasional CTAs. Isometric line-art illustrations of machines, keys, and network nodes on faint isometric grids give it an engineering-blueprint feel.

## Layout
Slim sticky top nav: wordmark left, pipe-separated dropdown links center, black pill "Book a demo" right. Heroes are centered: large tight-tracked headline, short subline, then an isometric illustration or a blue beam graphic converging into the page. Content sits in a bordered central column; sections are separated by hairline rules and framed boxes rather than background-color shifts (everything stays white). Rhythm alternates centered section headlines with 2-4 column card grids and label-left / mockup-right feature rows (platform page). Whitespace is generous; density comes from thin borders, not color.

## Components
Buttons: black pill with white text is the default CTA at all sizes ("Book a demo", "Book a Demo →"); blue pill (#204DFE) used sparingly for emphasis ("See Workforce Attribution Demo"); small text links with arrow suffix ("LEARN MORE →", tiny uppercase). Cards: white, square or barely-rounded corners, 1px light-gray borders, no shadows; monochrome line icons top-left. Product mockups are rendered as flat white UI tables with hairline dividers and small status pills (green "Active", orange/red severity chips). Tabs on home are bordered rectangles in a row, active tab underlined/bordered darker. Blog cards: 3-column grid, illustration thumbnail, small uppercase category tag pill, title, author row with circular avatar. Newsletter input: single hairline-underline field with inline "Sign up →".

## Signature
- One electric-blue "light beam" gradient streak cutting diagonally across otherwise pure black-and-white pages
- Isometric 3D line-art illustrations (machines, keys, stacked panels) on faint isometric grid backdrops
- Hairline-bordered sectioning everywhere — the page reads like a technical schematic, no colored section backgrounds
- Black pill buttons against white, with blue reserved as a rare accent

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas pure white and use 1px light-gray borders/rules to structure sections instead of background tints
- Reserve #204DFE for one beam graphic or one emphasized CTA per page; everything else stays black/white/gray
- Use black pill buttons for primary CTAs and tiny uppercase arrow-links ("LEARN MORE →") for secondary actions
- Illustrate concepts with monochrome isometric line art and flat white UI-table mockups with status chips
- Center hero headlines with tight tracking and keep sublines short and small

Don't:
- Don't add drop shadows, colored card backgrounds, or gradients beyond the single blue beam motif
- Don't round card corners past ~4px — only buttons and tag chips are pills
- Don't introduce secondary accent hues; the only non-neutral color in the system is electric blue (plus tiny status chips inside mockups)
- Don't use photographic imagery outside of small circular team avatars
- Don't fill sections edge-to-edge; content lives in a bordered central column with wide margins
