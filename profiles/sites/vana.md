## Overview
A playful pop-art data-economy brand: clean white canvas punctuated by full-width blocks of neon chartreuse, lavender, and red-orange, with dense comic-psychedelic illustration clusters (hands, keychains, collaged gadgets, checkerboard pixels). Headlines are short declarative sentences that always end with a period. The mood is loud and friendly consumer-tech, not corporate crypto.

## Layout
Sticky-less slim top nav: wordmark left, one text link plus a single blue CTA button right. Home hero is centered stacked text + inline email-capture row, sitting above a huge horizontal illustration collage. Sections alternate: white canvas with 3-column feature rows, then a full-bleed or inset rounded color band (chartreuse FAQ block, lavender stats band, orange slogan banner) as a rhythm break. "How it works" runs as alternating text/illustration rows with numbered chip labels (01 Store, 02 Control). About page uses a 4-column team grid and 3-column logo/press tiles. Footer is chartreuse with a giant cropped lowercase "vana" wordmark bleeding off the bottom edge.

## Components
- Primary button: solid #0075FF fill, white bold label, rounded 10-14px corners; used identically in nav, hero email row, and footer newsletter ("Sign up").
- Email capture: white pill-ish input with thin border paired inline with the blue button.
- FAQ accordion: white 24px-radius cards with bold question text and a plain down-arrow glyph, stacked on the chartreuse block with visible gaps.
- Team cards: borderless — square photo, name, colored role chip (small pill tags like "Cofounder & CEO" in yellow/pink/blue), LinkedIn/GitHub glyphs, then small bio text.
- Stat band: lavender #EEBCFF rounded block, oversized numerals and headings in blue.
- Press/investor tiles: flat light-gray or chartreuse rounded rectangles with centered logos, no shadows anywhere on the site.

## Signature
- Neon chartreuse #DBFD00 as the loudest brand surface — FAQ block, footer, press cards — always paired with black text.
- Maximalist comic-collage illustrations (red-orange hands, keychain vaults, sticker-covered objects) in a fixed navy/red-orange/chartreuse/blue ink palette, dropped into circles or floated free.
- Giant cropped black "vana" wordmark filling the footer bottom edge.
- Sentence-case headlines that end with a period ("Data, welcome home."), set in a chunky rounded grotesque; serif only for long-form narrative and pull quotes.
- Checkerboard/pixel-dither motifs trimming color banners and photo edges.

## Do
- End every headline with a period and keep it a short declarative sentence in sentence case.
- Break long white pages with inset rounded full-width color blocks (chartreuse, lavender, orange) rather than shadows or dividers.
- Reserve #0075FF strictly for buttons and interactive emphasis; let chartreuse and lavender carry decorative surfaces.
- Use the old-style serif for storytelling paragraphs and testimonial quotes to contrast the rounded sans headings.
- Keep everything flat: color fills, thin borders, and radius do all the separation work.

## Don't
- Don't add drop shadows, gradients, or glassmorphism — the site is 100% flat fills.
- Don't use chartreuse for text or on white text; it only works as a background under black.
- Don't render illustrations in soft pastel or 3D styles; they must stay bold-outline comic collage in the navy/red-orange/chartreuse ink set.
- Don't use title case or exclamation marks in headings.
- Don't shrink the footer wordmark into a normal logo lockup — it should crop off the page edge at massive scale.
