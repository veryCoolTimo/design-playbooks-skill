## Overview
Near-white minimal canvas where almost everything is typography: big bold black sans headlines carry the page, and color appears only as electric indigo/blue text highlights and inside product imagery. CTAs are solid black pills, giving a monochrome ink-on-paper feel punctuated by saturated purple media panels and a grainy violet gradient banner near the footer. Density is extremely low — single narrow text column, huge vertical gaps, one idea per screen.

## Layout
Sticky-simple top nav: wordmark + small blue "Beta" chip on the left, black pill "Get started free" and white bordered "Login" pill on the right — no center nav links. Home hero is a centered short headline ("One home for everything.") ringed by small floating app icons, followed by a stacked copy block with inline colored links, then pill CTAs. Sections alternate: centered headline → short gray subcopy → large rounded screenshot/media card. The about page is even sparser: a left-aligned single column of large statement paragraphs (manifesto style) with one engraving image up top and one black media card. A vertical list of ghost-gray use-case headlines with a single line highlighted in color acts as a scroll-through menu. Four-column link footer on white.

## Components
Buttons: solid black pills with white bold labels (primary); white pills with hairline gray border for secondary (Login, banner "Get started" inverts to white-on-purple). A composite "Watch intro" pill combines a thumbnail, text, and a YouTube play badge. Cards: large-radius (16-24px) rounded rectangles — white screenshot frames with soft diffuse shadows, near-black (#2E2E2E) media panels, and a purple-glow halo around featured dark cards. Search input mock: white pill with soft shadow and gray placeholder. Links: underlined, set in saturated blue/indigo within body copy.

## Signature
- Manifesto typography: oversized bold black statements as the main content, with single words or lines switched to electric indigo/blue for emphasis
- Black pill CTAs on a near-white page — color is reserved for text accents and imagery, never for buttons
- Ghost-gray stacked headline list where only the active line is colored
- Grainy purple-to-black gradient panels (banner, "multiplayer OS" card) as the dark counterpoint to the white page

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas near-white (#FEFEFE) and let bold black type do the visual work
- Use solid black pill buttons for every primary action; white hairline pills for secondary
- Emphasize inside sentences with saturated indigo/blue text (#3F21BC, #2D3BD1) or underlined blue links
- Give screenshots and media large rounded corners, soft shadows, and occasional purple-glow halos
- Leave very large vertical whitespace between sections — one statement or one artifact per screen

Don't:
- Don't color the CTA buttons blue or purple — brand color lives in type and imagery only
- Don't add nav link rows or mega-menus; the header stays wordmark + two pills
- Don't use borders or visible card outlines on white cards — shadows and radius define them
- Don't introduce warm hues beyond incidental illustration accents; the palette is black/white/indigo
- Don't tighten section spacing or run multi-column text; body copy stays a narrow centered or left column
