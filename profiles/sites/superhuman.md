## Overview
Warm cream canvas punctuated by deep jewel-toned full-bleed bands (indigo-violet, dark teal, oxblood maroon) and dreamy photo/gradient imagery — sunset skies, silhouetted people, pink-to-blue washes with floating UI chips composited on top. Type is restrained: one grotesque family at medium weight doing nearly all the work, with generous size jumps between hero display and small body. The result is calm, premium, and photographic rather than illustrative.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on white/cream: logo left, sparse links (Product, Enterprise, Pricing, Contact us), Log in + filled purple pill-ish CTA right. Home hero is a full-bleed photo (woman in profile against sunset sky) with centered white display text and floating product-UI chat cards layered over the photograph. Below: centered "trusted by" logo strip in bordered cells, then a tabbed product-suite section of alternating two-column feature cards (text left, screenshot-on-gradient right) inside a single cream container. Section rhythm alternates light cream blocks with saturated dark bands (indigo "Experience in building trust," teal "Becoming Superhuman," lavender testimonial band). Every page ends with a dark maroon footer featuring a giant clipped ghost "SUPERHUMAN" wordmark. Blog flips to a full dark near-black theme with a 2-column masonry card grid.

## Components
Buttons: filled violet (#6C3BD9) rectangles with small radius and white label; secondary buttons are white with a thin gray border; hero CTA on home adds an arrow chip. Cards: cream or white, hairline borders, medium radius; product screenshots sit on soft photo/gradient fills inside rounded frames. Pricing: four white plan cards with thin borders, the highlighted Business plan gets a dark header bar and filled purple CTA while others use outlined CTAs. FAQ accordions: full-width white rows, 1px border, chevron right. Compliance matrix on enterprise: dark indigo table with circular check icons. Feature checklists use small circular bullet markers. Shadows are nearly absent — separation comes from borders and background shifts.

## Signature
- Full-bleed dusk/sunset photography with silhouetted human subjects and floating translucent product-UI cards composited over it
- Cream base alternating with deep jewel-tone bands: indigo-violet, dark teal, oxblood maroon
- Giant ghost "SUPERHUMAN" wordmark bleeding off the bottom of the dark maroon footer on every page
- Screenshots always framed on soft pink-blue gradient or nature-photo backdrops, never on flat white

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Use a warm cream (#F5F3EE) page background, not pure white; reserve white for cards and nav
- Alternate cream sections with full-bleed deep indigo, teal, or maroon bands to pace long pages
- Composite floating UI snippets over atmospheric photography in heroes
- Keep buttons small-radius filled violet with quiet white-outlined secondaries
- End pages with the dark footer and oversized clipped wordmark

Don't:
- Add drop shadows or heavy elevation — this system separates with hairline borders and background color shifts
- Use bright saturated accents (no cyans, oranges, or neons); accents stay deep and muted
- Show product screenshots on plain flat backgrounds — always seat them on gradient or photo fills
- Mix in more than one heading weight or decorative display face; the grotesque stays uniform and medium-weight
- Center-align long body copy outside of hero/section intros; feature blocks are left-aligned two-column
