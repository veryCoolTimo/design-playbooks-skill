## Overview
Warm pastel playfulness held together by a single near-black plum ink. Each product page owns a pastel theme color — lavender for email, powder blue for store, orange/tan for website, peach for migration — while the home page sits on warm cream. Sections flow into each other through huge convex/concave arc dividers, and small dark confetti shapes (hexagons, blobs, triangles, squares) float in every colored band. Product UI is shown as friendly dark-navy mockup cards, not real screenshots.

## Layout
Slim single-row top nav on the canvas color: wordmark left, five text links, Login plus a dark pill "Sign up free" at right. Heroes are either centered (home, pricing, switch) or split left-text/right-mockup (email, store, website), always sitting on the page's theme color with confetti shapes, then curving down into a white band via a full-width arc. Section rhythm alternates: white explainer band → tinted feature band → white → full-bleed testimonial band with an edge-to-edge cutout portrait photo → template-gallery masonry grid → FAQ (left heading, right accordion list) → rounded white CTA card floating on a tinted footer band → dark-ink-on-canvas footer with 5-column link lists. Feature lists run as two- or three-column checkmark grids. Generous whitespace; content column is modest (~1100px) inside full-bleed color.

## Components
Buttons: solid near-black (#231F35) fill, white label, 6-10px corner radius, often with a small "›" arrow; secondary actions are plain text links with arrows. Cards: white, large radius (16-24px), borderless and essentially shadowless — separation comes from the tinted background behind them. Pricing plan cards are fully color-filled (orange and lavender) with dark checkmark bullets. Product mockups are dark-navy rounded panels with white/lavender UI chrome. FAQ rows use a filled dark circle "+" icon. Comparison table on pricing is plain white rows with checkmarks, zebra-light. Inputs barely appear; the one select on pricing is a simple white rounded field.

## Signature
- Giant arc section dividers — colored bands curve into white bands with a full-width ellipse edge, top and bottom.
- Per-page pastel identity: lavender / powder blue / orange / peach themes, all anchored by the same near-black plum ink and CTA.
- Dark confetti: small scattered hexagons, blobs, and triangles in ink and accent colors decorating every colored band.
- Full-bleed testimonial bands pairing a cutout portrait photo with a large quote and a dark CTA button.

## Do
- Keep exactly one dark ink color doing all the work: text, CTAs, icons, footer — everything else stays pastel.
- Give each major page or section its own pastel theme color, then return to white for explanatory content.
- Separate sections with sweeping arc curves rather than straight edges or hard rules.
- Render product UI as stylized dark rounded mockup panels instead of literal screenshots.
- Use checkmark-bullet grids (2-3 columns) for feature enumeration.

## Don't
- Don't introduce saturated brights or gradients — the palette is muted pastels plus one dark plum.
- Don't use borders or drop shadows on cards; rely on background-color contrast for separation.
- Don't color the primary CTA in an accent hue — it is always the dark ink fill.
- Don't leave colored bands empty; sprinkle the small geometric confetti shapes.
- Don't mix typefaces — headings and body are the same grotesque, differentiated only by size and weight.
