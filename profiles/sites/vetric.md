## Overview
A white-canvas SaaS site that pairs bookish serif headlines with a candy-pastel CMYK-ish accent system (magenta, royal blue, green, yellow). Gradient-mesh energy comes from soft airbrushed rainbow blooms behind heroes and CTAs, plus halftone-dot rainbow ribbons and playful line-art mascots (crowned cat, gem, puzzle pieces). The result is friendly and toy-like on top of an otherwise restrained, editorial layout.

## Layout
Slim top nav: small logo left, tiny text links right, no nav CTA button. Heroes are centered (home, products use-case) or left-aligned (careers, products) with a large 2-line serif headline, one short sans paragraph, and a single small dark CTA; a pastel gradient bloom or dotted rainbow band sits behind or below. Section rhythm: serif center-aligned H2 → content block, with lots of white air between sections. Recurring patterns: logo marquee under the hero, 2x2 pastel stat tiles with oversized colored numerals, alternating text/pastel-illustration rows (products), numbered step rows on dotted connector lines, a radial "What Makes It Tick" diagram with color-coded chips orbiting the logo, and a big serif CTA headline over a gradient bloom before the footer. Footer is light with the cat mascot peeking in.

## Components
Buttons: small, near-black (#101018) slightly rounded rectangles with tiny white sans label ("Book an Intro Call", "Join the Team"); secondary is the same shape in white with a hairline border. Cards: white with 1px light-gray border, 12-16px radius, no visible shadow (feature bullets on home) — or borderless flat pastel fills (#FDE7F3, #E8EFFC, #E9F6E6, #FBF4D9) at 16-20px radius for stat tiles, value blocks, and product illustrations. Chips/labels: white rounded-rect pills with hairline borders and colored text, used as floating annotations. Icons: thin blue line icons in rounded-square outlines. Carousels use minimal arrow glyphs and dot/dash pagination. Careers job list is plain hairline-divided table rows with "Apply →" text links.

## Signature
- Serif display headlines on pure white, with all UI chrome in a tiny sans — a strong editorial/typographic contrast.
- Soft airbrushed rainbow gradient blooms and halftone-dot rainbow ribbons as the only backgrounds; color never appears as solid section fills behind text-heavy areas.
- Four-color pastel accent system (magenta/blue/green/yellow) rotated systematically across stat numerals, value-card titles, and chips.
- Line-art mascots and motifs: crowned cat with yarn ball, faceted gem logo rendered as a pastel 3D gradient, halftone puzzle pieces.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Set every headline (hero, H2, closing CTA) in the serif; keep body and UI text in a small neutral sans.
- Use pastel tinted panels (pink/blue/green/yellow at ~10% saturation) for stat tiles and illustration cards, with the saturated version of the same hue for numerals and titles.
- Keep CTAs small, dark, and singular — one primary action per hero/section, near-black fill with white text.
- Confine gradients to blurred mesh blooms and dotted halftone bands at hero tops and pre-footer CTAs.
- Rotate the four accent hues in sequence across repeated items (steps 1-2-3-4, chips, category cards) rather than picking one at random.

Don't:
- Don't use large saturated color blocks or dark sections — the canvas stays white end to end.
- Don't add drop shadows or heavy elevation; separation is done with hairline borders and pastel fills.
- Don't make buttons pill-shaped or oversized; the site's buttons are compact with modest radius.
- Don't set headings in bold sans — the serif display face is the identity.
- Don't render illustrations photorealistically; stick to flat pastel shapes, halftone dot textures, and thin-line mascots (photos appear only for team/culture content).
