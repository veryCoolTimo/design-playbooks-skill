## Overview
Warm paper-cream canvas with near-black ink, punctuated by full-bleed pastel color-block sections (pink, sky blue, yellow, green, lavender) and torn-edge orange bands. Hand-drawn fruit-character doodles and marker-style annotations run through every page, giving a friendly zine/illustrated feel layered over an otherwise tidy SaaS layout. Serif display headings against grotesque body copy; the single hot-orange CTA color cuts through the pastels.

## Layout
Sticky-feel top nav on cream: wordmark left, dropdown links center-right, ghost "Log in" plus bordered "Start for free" pill-adjacent button. Heroes are centered, doodle-topped serif headlines with a short sub and one wide orange CTA. Sections alternate: cream section, then a full-width pastel or orange band with rough torn/angled top and bottom edges. Feature walkthroughs are stacked full-width color blocks (one pastel per step, numbered). Cards sit in 2-4 column grids with generous whitespace; testimonial "Wall of Love" and team grids use tight, even gutters. Footer is near-black with cream wordmark and multi-column links.

## Components
Buttons: flat orange (#F2845C) fill, thin dark border, small 4-6px radius, dark label with trailing arrow; secondary buttons are cream with the same thin dark border. Cards: cream or pastel fill, 1px dark or subtle border, 8-14px radius, small hard offset shadow on some (e.g. the "10 Keys" booklet card); blog cards pair a pastel image block with cream text area. Tag/filter chips: small bordered cream rectangles. Team avatars are circles with tiny blue role-label chips. FAQ rows are bordered cream accordions with chevrons. Inputs barely appear; job-listing rows are cream cards on the orange band with bordered "Learn more" buttons.

## Signature
- Hand-drawn passionfruit mascot doodles (ink line art) scattered across heroes, section corners, and a full marching-crowd illustration strip
- Torn-paper / rough-edge transitions into solid orange bands between sections
- Full-bleed pastel color-block sections (pink, blue, yellow, green) as the step-by-step rhythm
- Marker-highlight and strikethrough text effects ("Wall of ~~Fame~~ Froots", handwritten eyebrow labels like "PARTNER NETWORK")

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep canvas warm cream (#F3EEE3), never pure white, with near-black ink
- Reserve the orange fill for primary CTAs and full-bleed divider bands; give every button a thin dark border
- Alternate cream sections with saturated pastel blocks to pace long pages
- Use line-art doodles and handwritten/marker accents to soften SaaS content
- Set headlines in the soft serif and all UI/body text in the grotesque sans

Don't:
- Use gradients, glassmorphism, or heavy soft shadows — surfaces are flat with hard 1px borders
- Round buttons into pills or oversize card radii; corners stay modest
- Introduce cool grays or a dark theme mid-page; the only dark surface is the footer
- Add photography outside small circular avatars and testimonial headshots
- Let multiple pastel accents compete inside one section — each color block owns one hue
