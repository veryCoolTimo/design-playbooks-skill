## Overview
Near-black canvas (#0F0E13) punctuated by warm cream (#FFFAEA) section panels, with data rendered as playful "confetti" grids of multicolored rounded pills — an equalizer/waveform motif that doubles as brand art. Type is restrained: big regular-weight grotesque headlines, tiny uppercase monospace labels. The one loud fill is a mint-green pill CTA; everything else is hairlines and whitespace. Reads as developer-serious with a toy-like, tactile visualization layer.

## Layout
Slim dark top nav: wordmark left, uppercase mono links center, outlined pill "Open Dashboard" right. Hero is centered — H1, short subline, two pill CTAs — followed by a full-width equalizer built from colored pill dots, then a customer logo strip divided by vertical hairlines. Page rhythm alternates full-bleed dark and cream sections; the cream panels enter with an offset/stepped corner rather than a straight edge. Dark sections favor table-like rows separated by full-width hairlines (feature lists, FAQ) over boxed cards; two-column splits pair a left headline/copy block with a right visualization (a flat cyan #4DCAFA image panel with workflow-node chips). Huge bare stat counters (23,992,341 / 100K+) sit in their own band.

## Components
Buttons: all pills, uppercase monospace labels — mint #63F5B6 fill for the primary CTA, cream #FFFAEA fill for secondary ("Talk to Vapi", "Case study"), thin-outlined transparent pills for tertiary ("Learn more", "Try it now"); no shadows or gradients. Tabs: a full-width row split in halves, active tab a solid cyan pill spanning its half. Cards: on cream sections, dark #0F0E13 cards with ~12px radius, small icon badge top-left, title + short body; on cream feature lists, rows with a colored rounded-square icon chip (lilac, orange, cyan, green, yellow) + hairline separators. FAQ: plain rows with plus toggles and hairlines. Workflow-node chips: small-radius dark pills with status-dot icons, stacked and connected by thin arrows.

## Signature
- Multicolored rounded-pill dot grids forming equalizers, globes, and a padlock — data-as-confetti on both dark and cream backgrounds
- Warm cream #FFFAEA panels with stepped/offset corners slicing into the dark canvas (nothing is pure white)
- Uppercase monospace micro-typography on every button, eyebrow, and nav item
- Mint #63F5B6 pill as the single saturated CTA color, with flat cyan #4DCAFA reserved for image panels and active tabs

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Build illustrations from grids of small rounded multicolor pills (mint, cyan, lilac, orange, yellow) instead of photos or 3D renders
- Set every CTA, label, and eyebrow in uppercase monospace inside pill shapes
- Alternate near-black and warm cream full-bleed sections; give cream panels an offset stepped edge
- Use full-width hairline dividers to structure feature rows, logo strips, and FAQs on dark sections
- Let headlines stay regular-weight and large, with two-sentence rhythm ("Feels human. Moves the needle.")

Don't:
- Use gradients, glows, or glassmorphism — every surface here is flat
- Substitute pure white for the cream #FFFAEA (backgrounds, secondary buttons, and heading text are all warm-tinted)
- Box dark-section content into cards; dark sections use hairline rows, cards live only on cream
- Spread the mint green beyond CTAs — large color fields are the flat cyan panels only
- Add drop shadows or heavy borders to the pill buttons; contrast comes from fill vs. thin outline
