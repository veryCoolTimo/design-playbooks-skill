## Overview
High-contrast neon manifesto: near-black textured canvas alternating with full-bleed chartreuse (#CDEE00) band sections, bold grotesque headlines, and irreverent hand-drawn sticker illustrations (birds, water guns, road signs). Everything is loud on purpose — giant ghosted numerals (1948, 203, 71) sit behind section content as decorative type. Copy tone is cheeky ("Holla at your next designer!", "My designer did friendly fire?") and the visuals match it.

## Layout
Sticky dark nav: wordmark left, few links center, two pill buttons right (outline + neon fill). Hero is left-aligned headline over a faint diagonal grid texture, with a stacked testimonial card floating top-right; single neon pill CTA below. Page rhythm strictly alternates black and chartreuse full-width bands. Sections are mostly single-column, center-aligned headlines with generous vertical padding; a 3-4 column strip of illustrated value props on yellow; 4-up designer profile cards; 2-up feature cards and 2-up pricing cards. Logo strip of client marks sits below the hero.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped; primary is neon chartreuse fill with black bold text, secondary is thin white outline on dark. Cards: dark charcoal (#2E2E2E) with large 16-24px radius, no visible borders or shadows — separation comes purely from lightness contrast against black or from sitting on the yellow band. Pricing cards: dark card on yellow, white heading, gray checklist with circled check icons, full-width neon pill CTA at bottom. Testimonial blocks: gray rounded card with small circular avatar. Newsletter input: small dark field with adjacent neon pill submit.

## Signature
- Electric chartreuse (#DBFE08) as both CTA color and full-section background — the whole page pulses black/neon/black
- Giant ghosted background numerals in ultra-bold type behind section headings
- Naive red/blue/chartreuse sticker-style doodle illustrations scattered through sections
- Subtle noise/grain and faint diagonal grid texture on the black canvas, keeping flat areas from feeling empty

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Alternate full-bleed black and chartreuse sections; never fade between them — hard cuts only
- Keep every button a pill: neon fill + black text for primary, white outline for secondary on dark
- Set headlines in heavy grotesque with tight tracking; let one line be witty rather than corporate
- Add grain/noise texture to large black areas and oversized ghost numerals or type as backdrop
- Use hand-drawn multicolor doodles (red/blue accents) as section art instead of photos

**Don't**
- Don't use the chartreuse for body text or long paragraphs — it's for fills, bands, and CTAs only
- Don't add borders, gradients, or drop shadows to cards; rely on charcoal-on-black lightness steps
- Don't introduce serif type or thin light weights — the system is one bold grotesque family
- Don't use stock photography; the only imagery is illustration and small circular avatars
- Don't center the hero — the headline is left-anchored with the CTA directly beneath it
