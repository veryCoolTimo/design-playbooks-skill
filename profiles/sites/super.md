## Overview
A two-mode brand system: product pages (home, Super+) are dark-neon — near-black purple canvases washed with magenta gradients, neon signage illustrations, and glowing pink UI — while corporate pages (about, careers, blog, press) flip to white/blush-pink canvases with the same hot-pink accents. The constant thread is a bold high-contrast serif for every headline, a small geometric sans for everything else, hot-magenta pill CTAs, and a playful pink lightning-bolt mascot woven into custom illustration.

## Layout
Slim sticky top nav: logo left, 4-5 center links, "Log in" outline pill + solid magenta "Get the app" pill right. Heroes are either full-bleed dark illustrated scenes with centered serif headline and stacked CTA, or light split heroes (serif headline left, image/illustration right). Sections alternate background color blocks (dark purple → magenta gradient → dark; or white → blush pink → lavender) rather than relying on whitespace alone. Recurring horizontal stat-pill marquee ("Over 30M users", "$2B+ total sales") scrolling edge-to-edge. Blog uses a 1-large + 2-column-list grid per category with thin divider rules. Footer is consistent site-wide: QR code, app-store badges, two link columns, dense legal fine print, thin magenta bottom strip.

## Components
Buttons: fully pill-shaped; primary is solid hot magenta (#E90D74) with white label; on dark sections a pale-pink pill (#F5AFD2) with dark text is used; secondary is a thin outline pill. Buttons are small and compact, not oversized. Cards: white cards with soft shadow and ~10px radius on light pages (benefits, values, job listings with a colored left edge bar in cyan/purple/magenta); on dark pages cards are dark panels with thin pink borders (pricing pills, FAQ accordion rows, tarot-card step graphics). Stat chips: white pill capsules with a magenta number + small dark label. Category eyebrows in all-caps magenta. FAQ: full-width dark rounded rows with chevron toggles. Search input: simple rounded outline field.

## Signature
- Bold high-contrast serif headlines (GT Super feel) paired with a tiny geometric sans body — an unusual serif-led fintech voice.
- Hot magenta (#E90D74) used everywhere: CTAs, eyebrows, stats, borders, the lightning-bolt logo mark, and the round pink mascot character.
- Dark product pages staged as illustrated neon night scenes (phone booth, neon "easy street" signs, magic 8-ball, tarot cards) with superhero/magic copywriting.
- Scrolling stat-pill marquees and flat editorial character illustration recurring across light pages.

## Do
- Set every headline in a bold high-contrast serif; keep body copy in a small (~14px) geometric sans.
- Keep all buttons pill-shaped and compact; solid magenta on light, pale-pink fill with dark text on dark backgrounds.
- Alternate full-width background blocks (near-black purple, magenta gradients, white, blush #FDE7F1, lavender) to create section rhythm.
- Express numbers as white pill chips with magenta figures; run them as a horizontal marquee.
- Use custom flat illustration with the pink mascot rather than stock-photo-only sections.

## Don't
- Don't use blue/green corporate fintech palettes — the only strong hues are magenta, purple, cyan, and yellow as accents.
- Don't set headlines in a sans or a light-weight face; the serif contrast is the brand voice.
- Don't square off buttons or use large sharp-cornered CTAs.
- Don't keep the dark-neon treatment on corporate pages (about/careers/blog/press are light with pink accents).
- Don't play the tone straight — copy leans on superhero/magic jokes ("no potions or phone booths required"), so avoid dry enterprise language.
