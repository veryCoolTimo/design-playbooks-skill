## Overview
Near-black canvas with white extra-bold headlines where one phrase per heading is recolored in coral or blue for emphasis. The page reads as a product demo reel: large centered media blocks (video stills, before/after images, app screenshots) carry the visual weight while chrome stays minimal. Occasional saturated full-color panels (electric blue, taxi yellow) punch through the dark field.

## Layout
Top nav is a slim dark bar: logo left, sparse links right (Apps dropdown, Pricing, Blog) plus a white pill "Sign-up for free". Hero is fully centered: two-line headline with colored second line, short subline, a text link plus a small blue pill CTA, then a large rounded video thumbnail. Sections repeat one rhythm — centered bold heading with a colored keyword, one-line description, then a big media card — separated by generous vertical whitespace. A use-cases section uses a left tab list (dark card per tab) next to a large image panel; FAQ is a plain accordion list with thin dividers; footer is a four-column dark card with rounded top corners.

## Components
Buttons: small pill CTAs — solid blue fill with white text for primary ("Try for free", "Get started with the ClipDrop API"), white pill for the nav signup; secondary actions are plain arrow-prefixed text links in blue. Cards: dark charcoal (#1c1c1e) panels with medium radius and no visible border or shadow, used for feature callouts, testimonial quotes, and the footer. Media blocks: large rounded-corner images/videos, sometimes with in-image captions in huge bold type. Tool selector rendered as small pill chips with brand icons (Photoshop, Figma, iOS, Android), active chip filled light. Accordion rows use chevron icons and hairline separators; a small blue "New" pill badge labels new sections.

## Signature
- Two-tone headlines: white bold type with a coral (#f66e63) or blue highlighted phrase, always centered.
- Product-as-hero: oversized rounded screenshots and demo videos with giant bold captions baked into the imagery ("remove objects", "Remove background").
- Dark charcoal cards floating on near-black, differentiated by value shift alone — no borders, no glows, no gradients.
- Sudden full-saturation color fields (electric blue sneaker panel, yellow API collage) as section-level accents.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep everything center-aligned per section: heading, subline, CTA, media, in that order.
- Recolor exactly one phrase per headline in coral or blue; leave the rest pure white.
- Use small pill buttons with solid blue fill for primary CTAs and arrow text links for secondary actions.
- Let large rounded media cards do the selling; keep surrounding copy to one or two short lines.
- Separate dark surfaces by lightness steps (#0d0d0d canvas vs #1c1c1e cards), not by borders or shadows.

**Don't**
- Don't add gradients, glows, or neon outlines — the dark-tech look here is flat and matte.
- Don't oversize the CTA buttons; they stay compact pills even in the hero.
- Don't left-align hero or section headers; nothing on this page breaks the centered axis except the use-case tabs and footer columns.
- Don't introduce more than one saturated accent panel per stretch of page — the blue and yellow fields work because the rest stays near-black.
- Don't use thin or light heading weights; display type is consistently extra-bold with tight spacing.
