## Overview
A near-white, softly shadowed canvas treated like a physical desk: invoices, polaroids, sticky notes, name tags, binder clips, and washi tape are scattered as skeuomorphic props with realistic drop shadows. Type is a clean black grotesque set at large sizes with short, joke-forward copy. Full-bleed color band sections (deep green graph-paper footer, navy letter section) break the light rhythm and carry the crypto/payments accent stickers (token badges, iMessage-style chat bubbles).

## Layout
Slim centered nav bar in a rounded light strip: logo left, links center-left, "Log in" plus a dark pill CTA right. Heroes are centered stacks — big 3-4 line headline, one-line subhead, single pill CTA — with props collaged around and behind them at rotated angles bleeding off the edges. Sections alternate: narrow centered text column (about page reads like an essay with left-aligned blocks in a centered column), then a wide collage or card row. Blog is a clean 2-column card grid. Pricing is a 4-column tier row. Every page ends with the same green graph-paper CTA band ("Love you, pay me") littered with stickers.

## Components
Buttons: solid near-black (#2B2B2B) pill with white text, subtle inner highlight; white/light pill secondary on dark bands. Cards: white, 8-14px radius, hairline border or very soft shadow; blog thumbnails are rounded illustrated scenes on green/navy/dark backgrounds. Pricing cards are flat white with thin borders, checkmark lists, and a dark pill CTA per tier. Skeuomorphic props double as components: paper invoice mockups with realistic shadows, yellow sticky notes with handwriting, red "HELLO" name tags, rotated polaroids with handwritten captions, chat-bubble notifications. Product UI screenshots are embedded flat with fine borders.

## Signature
- Physical-desk collage: sticky notes, binder clips, tape, polaroids, and paper invoices scattered at angles with real shadows over a soft off-white canvas
- Deep-green graph-paper CTA band with the "Love you, pay me" sign-off closing every page
- Playful, self-aware headline copy ("Voted most likely to start a payments company in high school") in big black grotesque
- Handwritten script and monospace-caps annotations layered on photos and notes; crypto token badges (USDC, ETH, TRON) as sticker accents

## Do
- Keep the base near-white and calm; concentrate all color in props, stickers, and the two dark band colors (deep green, navy)
- Rotate collage elements 3-15 degrees and give them soft realistic drop shadows so they read as physical objects
- Use one dark pill button as the sole CTA style everywhere, including inside the nav
- Write headlines as short jokes or letters, sentence case, 2-4 lines, centered
- Repeat the green graph-paper footer band on every page for continuity

## Don't
- Don't use bright color as a page background outside the green/navy band sections
- Don't render buttons or links in blue or green — brand color lives in decorations, not controls
- Don't align collage props to the grid; perfectly straight stickers kill the desk metaphor
- Don't add heavy borders or dark shadows to content cards — they stay hairline-light while props carry the depth
- Don't use serif or display faces; everything is one grotesque plus handwriting/mono accents
