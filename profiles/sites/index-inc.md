## Overview
A dark-neon SaaS landing built on a near-black navy canvas with a cinematic purple-to-coral aurora skyline in the hero and a glowing planet-horizon in the footer. The single warm accent — an orange-coral gradient pill CTA with a soft outer glow — pops against an otherwise cool violet/blue palette. Dense, realistic dark-mode app screenshots in large rounded frames carry most of the visual weight; typography stays quiet and neutral.

## Layout
Sticky-less minimal top nav: wordmark left, "Sign in" text link plus one glowing pill CTA right — nothing else. Hero is centered: small pill badge ("Launch / Introducing Index"), two-line H1, short subhead, single CTA with "No credit card required" microcopy, then a full-width app screenshot bleeding over the aurora backdrop. Section rhythm alternates centered headline blocks with 2–3 column bento card grids; a logo strip and a glowing red-tinted testimonial panel break up feature sections. Interior pages (Linear integration) switch to a narrow single-column doc layout (~640px) with H2s, bullet lists, and one framed screenshot. Generous vertical whitespace between sections; footer is a sparse 4-column link grid over the horizon glow.

## Components
- Buttons: primary is a pill with an orange-to-coral vertical gradient (#FF5A36 range), subtle lighter inner rim and warm outer glow; secondary is a dark slate pill (#2A2F4A-ish) with light text, no border glow.
- Cards: large radius (16-20px), fill barely lighter than canvas (#12152B-ish), 1px low-contrast border; bento cells hold mini app-UI mockups with colored status chips and tags.
- App-screenshot frames: extra-large radius, thin light border, faint drop glow; screenshots are real dark-mode product UI, not illustrations.
- Chips/badges: small pill tags in saturated hue fills (teal, blue, purple, green) used inside mock UI; hero badge is a two-segment dark pill.
- Links: light blue (#4E7BFF range) inline text links in doc pages.

## Signature
- Aurora/mountain gradient horizon (violet → magenta → coral) framing the top of the page and a planetary glow at the footer — space-sunset bookends.
- One warm coral-orange glowing pill CTA as the only hot color on a cool dark canvas.
- Bento grids filled with genuine dense dark-mode app UI and colorful status chips instead of abstract illustration.
- Testimonial section washed in a red/ember radial glow behind centered white quote text.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the canvas near-black navy (#0A0D1F) everywhere; let gradients live in hero/footer imagery, not section backgrounds.
- Reserve the coral-orange gradient exclusively for the primary CTA and its glow; use cool violets/blues for everything decorative.
- Use pill buttons and 16px+ card radii with 1px low-contrast borders; card fills only a step lighter than canvas.
- Show real, information-dense dark-mode product UI inside rounded glowing frames as the main imagery.
- Center hero and section headlines; keep nav to wordmark + sign-in + one CTA.

Don't:
- Don't introduce a light/white section — the whole site stays dark, including doc pages.
- Don't use hard drop shadows or heavy borders; depth comes from soft glows and subtle 1px strokes.
- Don't add multiple competing CTAs or colored buttons; secondary actions are muted slate pills or text links.
- Don't use illustration or stock photography; product screenshots and star-field/aurora gradients are the only imagery.
- Don't make typography loud — headings are plain semibold grotesque with no gradient text or display serifs.
