## Overview
Ultra-restrained, Apple-adjacent minimalism for a wearable AI startup: vast white space, a single neutral grotesque at low weights, and one electric-blue accent doing all the interactive work. The page alternates between white, pale-gray (#EDEDED-ish), and near-black (#101012) full-bleed bands, with photography (a soft-fade human profile, a floating black sensor puck) as the only ornament. Text frequently sits at very low opacity, fading in on scroll — mystery over explanation.

## Layout
- Fixed, centered pill nav floating over content: circular starburst-logo button on the left, then five text links (Wearable, Neural, Programs, Updates, Search) inside one light-gray rounded capsule; a secondary announcement pill ("Discover the future in every update") hangs beneath it in the hero.
- Hero: enormous whitespace, image centered, brand + tagline ("Beyond Humanware.") bottom-left in modest size — not a giant display headline. Tag-pill links ("Explore A¹ Sense / B¹ Eye / A¹ Neuro") under the tagline; a small circular scroll-down arrow bottom-left.
- Section rhythm: a two-column editorial grid — narrow left rail with numbered kicker labels ("0.1 Our Mission", "Our Progress", "Our Aim") in small gray type, wide right column with the statement text. Thin 1px hairline dividers between rows.
- Mid-page dark act: long near-black passages listing mission/vision/ambition as stacked headlines that dim progressively (opacity stepping), plus a 3-column fact row (Industries / Core Business / Domain) with tiny blue tick marks above labels.
- Giant blue outlined-ish display words ("Invisible Approach") layered behind a 3D product photo as a section transition.

## Components
- Nav: light-gray capsule (#EDEDED-range) with plain dark text links, no underlines; stays identical over light and dark sections.
- Buttons/links: pill-shaped tags with 1px light-blue border, white fill, blue label (A¹ Sense, B¹ Eye); text links in blue preceded by a small outlined arrow-in-oval icon ("Research Insight", "Go to Updates", "Go to Programs"). No heavy solid buttons anywhere; the closest to a filled element is a small solid electric-blue rectangle holding the starburst mark.
- No cards, no shadows, no inputs visible; structure comes from hairlines, column alignment, and background-color bands.
- State language: opacity — inactive/upcoming text renders at 20–50% opacity; active statements at full ink.

## Signature
- One accent color (electric blue) used only for interaction and superscript product codes (A¹, B¹, [Pha¹] [ICT²] [AIWC³] bracket glossary).
- Floating pill nav with a circular starburst-logo chip, persistent over every band.
- White → gray → black full-bleed band sequencing with scroll-fade text (low-opacity headlines that resolve as you scroll).
- Numbered left-rail kickers ("0.1 Our Mission") paired with wide right-column statements and hairline rules.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep type weight regular/medium max; let size and whitespace, not boldness, create hierarchy.
- Reserve blue strictly for links, pill tags, tick marks, and superscript codes — everything else stays grayscale.
- Use the numbered kicker + wide statement two-column grid with 1px hairlines for content sections.
- Alternate full-bleed white, light-gray, and near-black bands to pace the scroll.
- Use pill geometry for every interactive element (nav capsule, tag buttons, arrow-oval icons).

**Don't**
- Don't add solid filled CTAs, drop shadows, or card containers — the site has none.
- Don't use a second accent color or gradients; the palette is blue + grayscale only.
- Don't set a huge hero headline; the hero keeps the tagline small and lets imagery and emptiness dominate.
- Don't render all text at full opacity — dimmed/stepped opacity is part of the language.
- Don't introduce a serif or display face; everything is one neutral grotesque.
