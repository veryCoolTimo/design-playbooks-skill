## Overview
Warm cream canvas with near-black ink and a chunky, friendly serif for every headline. Playfulness comes from pastel confetti (squiggles, dots, lightning bolts), highlighter-mint marks behind key words, and a cartoon speech-bubble mascot wearing pink headphones. Despite the whimsy, layout discipline is strictly minimal-light: centered single-column sections, white cards, lots of air.

## Layout
Sticky-feeling top nav: wordmark left, 6 plain text links right, one mint pill "Book a Call" CTA. Hero is centered stacked text (serif headline with mint highlight on the key phrase) over paired CTAs (mint primary + white outline secondary), then a full-width illustration or product visual. Sections alternate between raw cream background and large white or pastel rounded panels (mint "We believe", orange callout banner, giant sky-blue "Let's Chat!" footer block). Grids are simple: 2-3 column card grids, masonry testimonial columns, horizontal logo strips and pill-tag marquees. Generous vertical rhythm; decorative confetti fills the margins instead of imagery.

## Components
Buttons: pill shape, thin 1px black border on both variants; primary is mint (#A7EDC3) fill with black text, secondary is white/cream fill with black text. Cards: white fill, 8-14px radius, subtle border, many with a pastel accent edge (colored bottom border or tinted offset shadow — pink, blue, orange, mint rotate per card). Pricing cards add a mint price pill and green checkmark lists. Testimonials are borderless white cards with small circular avatars. Tags/labels are pastel pill chips in blue/pink/purple/mint. Toggle switch (monthly/annual) is a small dark pill. Phone-frame video cards get thick pastel borders.

## Signature
- Heavy rounded serif headlines with a highlighter-mint block or hand-drawn underline behind one key word or phrase.
- Speech-bubble mascot with pink headphones (blue, pink, or mint variants) popping into section corners with radiating tick marks.
- Confetti garnish: tiny pastel squiggles, arcs, dots, and lightning bolts scattered around headings on the cream canvas.
- Full-width rounded sky-blue "Let's Chat!" footer panel with underlined black serif and the mascot, repeated on every page.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the canvas warm cream (#F4F1EA) and reserve pure white for cards and section panels.
- Give every headline the serif treatment and highlight exactly one word/phrase with the mint marker.
- Outline all buttons in black regardless of fill; keep primary CTAs mint and pill-shaped.
- Rotate pastel accents (blue, pink, orange, purple) across card edges, tags, and phone frames so no single accent dominates.
- End every page with the rounded blue "Let's Chat!" block and mascot.

**Don't**
- Don't use saturated or dark brand colors — every accent stays pastel; black is the only heavy tone.
- Don't set headlines in a sans; the serif/sans split (serif display, grotesque body) is the core identity.
- Don't add photographic hero imagery; people appear only inside video thumbnails and avatar circles.
- Don't use hard drop shadows or gradients — depth comes from thin borders and pastel offset edges.
- Don't crowd sections; decorative confetti replaces density, and each section holds one idea.
