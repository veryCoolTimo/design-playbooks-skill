## Overview
A warm cream-paper canvas carried entirely by a two-tone identity: near-black forest green ink and acid butter yellow, punctuated by hand-drawn line illustrations of people, pencils, and scribbles. Everything not green or yellow is a sticky-note pastel (lilac, mint, rust, teal) used in playful collage blocks. The effect is a friendly, analog "notebook" take on an AI meeting tool — minimal structure, maximal doodle personality.

## Layout
Sticky top nav on cream: yellow scribble logo left, three center links, outlined Log In + solid dark-green CTA right. Hero is centered stacked type over a typewriter-style brief mockup flanked by line-art illustrated people. Sections alternate: centered headline blocks, two-column feature rows pairing product screenshots with sticky-note callout cards, and full-bleed color slabs (rust orange, pale mint) for testimonial/persona moments. Each section opens with a tiny monospace tag pill. Pricing is a classic 3-card row (outlined / yellow / dark green) plus a wide outlined Enterprise bar. Every page ends with the same signature footer: newsletter row, then a viewport-spanning giant "SayBriefly" wordmark sitting on a frieze of hand-drawn characters.

## Components
- Buttons: small-radius (~4-6px) rectangles; primary is solid #163300 with yellow text, secondary is 1px dark-green outline on cream; compact padding.
- Cards: 1px dark-green hairline borders on cream, ~12px radius (pricing); featured tiers get solid yellow or solid dark-green fills. Blog cards are borderless pastel-collage thumbnails with title text baked into the image plus an arrow-out icon.
- Tag pills: tiny monospace uppercase labels in yellow chips with a small icon, used as section eyebrows.
- Inputs: minimal underline/hairline email fields paired with a small dark-green Subscribe button.
- FAQ: full-width hairline-divided accordion rows with plus icons.

## Signature
- Giant footer wordmark "SayBriefly" in extra-bold green spanning the full viewport width, entangled with doodle illustrations of people and flying papers.
- Two-tone core (forest green #163300 on cream #FBFAF5) with acid yellow #FCEC5B as the only saturated brand color; pastels appear only as sticky-note props.
- Monospace eyebrow tags and typewriter-styled document mockups referencing "the brief" artifact.
- Hand-drawn single-weight line illustrations and marker scribbles/underlines woven through headings and section breaks.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the palette disciplined: green ink + yellow accent on cream; reserve lilac/mint/rust/teal for illustrative sticky-note blocks, never for UI chrome.
- Use 1px dark-green hairlines for card and accordion borders instead of shadows — the site is essentially flat.
- Open sections with a small monospace tag pill and a centered extra-bold headline ending in a period.
- Let hand-drawn scribbles, underlines, and line-art people do the decorating; keep the underlying grid plain and centered.
- Highlight one pricing tier by flooding it with brand yellow and another with solid dark green.

Don't:
- Add drop shadows, gradients, or glassmorphism — nothing here is dimensional.
- Use blue links or generic SaaS blue anywhere; even links stay in the green/yellow system.
- Round buttons into pills; corners stay tight (4-6px) even though cards are softer.
- Use photographic imagery outside product screenshots — humans are always line-drawn.
- Let the footer be quiet; the oversized wordmark + illustration frieze is a fixed page-ending ritual.
