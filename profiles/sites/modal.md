## Overview
Near-black canvas punctuated by an electric lime-green identity color and grainy, iridescent 3D renders of translucent stacked cubes and slabs. Headlines are white with one key phrase colored green ("Run anything in the cloud", "magical as our product"), a device repeated on every page. The overall feel is terminal-adjacent developer tech elevated by lush generative artwork — dark, precise, and quietly playful.

## Layout
Floating pill-shaped nav bar (slightly lighter than the canvas) with center links and a green Sign Up pill at right. Heroes are left-aligned text blocks with a large 3D render floating at right; two buttons (green primary + outlined secondary) below the subhead. Sections alternate: dense bordered card grids (customer quotes, feature lists), thin-hairline feature rows with small green icons, and full-bleed gradient interludes — the pricing page drops plan cards over a lime/amber bar-chart texture. Every page closes with the same "Ship your first app in minutes." CTA band featuring a stacked-cube render. Footer is multi-column link lists on pure black.

## Components
Primary CTA: solid lime-green pill with black text ("Get Started", "Sign Up"); secondary is a transparent pill with a thin gray outline. Filter chips and tag pills use the same outlined-pill grammar, sometimes with a small green arrow-circle inside. Cards are near-flat dark panels (#141614-ish) with 1px darker-gray hairline borders, small radius, no shadows; highlighted cards (e.g. Tidbyt announcement) swap the border to bright green. Pricing comparison table uses hairline row dividers with green check / red cross dots. Code blocks and dashboard mockups appear as embedded dark terminal windows with syntax-highlighted green/blue text. Testimonial grids are uniform bordered cells with logo, quote, and attribution.

## Signature
- Grainy iridescent 3D renders — translucent green/lime/amber cubes, slabs, and exploded stacks — as the sole illustration language on every page.
- One green-highlighted phrase inside otherwise-white headlines, applied consistently sitewide.
- Electric lime (#7FEE64–#D7FF3F range) as the single functional accent: CTAs, links, icons, active nav states, and card highlight borders.
- Hairline-bordered dark cells arranged in strict grids, giving a spreadsheet-like density to testimonials, features, and pricing tables.

## Do / Don't
**Do**
- Keep the canvas at true near-black and reserve lime green for CTAs, keyword highlights, and small icons.
- Color exactly one phrase per headline green; leave the rest white.
- Use 1px hairline borders on dark cards instead of shadows or elevated fills.
- Render buttons as pills — solid green with black text for primary, thin gray outline for secondary — while cards stay barely rounded.
- Use noisy, gradient-mapped 3D artwork (cubes, stacks, layered slabs) for heroes and CTA bands rather than photography or flat icons.

**Don't**
- Don't introduce blue, purple, or corporate SaaS gradients — the accent family is strictly lime-to-yellow-green.
- Don't add drop shadows, glassmorphism blur, or heavy card elevation; surfaces are flat with hairline edges.
- Don't use bold, heavy display weights — headlines run light/regular even at very large sizes.
- Don't fill whole sections with green; bright color is confined to artwork, small controls, and highlighted words.
- Don't round cards or tables into soft blobs — pill shape belongs to buttons and chips only.
