## Overview
A minimal light page built on one loud move: electric lime (#C1FA6C) against white and near-black green ink. Everything else stays quiet — cool off-white hero wash (#F5F7FB), gray body text, white cards — so the lime CTA, badge pill, and two giant lime section slabs carry all the energy. Product screenshots of the notebook UI do most of the storytelling.

## Layout
Slim top nav: mark + wordmark left, one text link, right-aligned "Templates" text link and a white pill "Login" button. Hero is centered and stacked: lime announcement pill ("WE RAISED A $5M SEED ROUND") → huge two-line H1 → short gray subhead → single lime pill CTA → tab pills ("Financial model / Quarterly business report / Offer letter") switching a large embedded product screenshot with floating UI fragments spilling off its edges. Sections alternate: centered claim headline on white → numbered feature grid (white cards on a pale panel, green circled numbers 1-4) → split text/screenshot row → full-bleed lime rounded slab with a 3x2 grid of white example cards → spreadsheet-vs-notebook comparison → closing lime slab with logo mark and "Start making better decisions." Generous vertical whitespace between sections; content column stays narrow and centered.

## Components
Buttons: pill-shaped, flat lime fill #C1FA6C with very dark green text (#042800), no border, no shadow; secondary "Login" is a white pill with subtle border. Announcement badge: small lime pill with monospace uppercase text and arrow. Cards: pure white, 16-24px radius, effectively shadowless, emoji as icon, black question-style heading, gray body, monospace uppercase author byline with tiny avatar. Tabs: white pill for active state, plain gray text for inactive. Product-UI fragments (inputs, sliders, formula chips, sticky notes) rendered as small white/green rounded widgets with soft shadows. Inline text highlight: the word "team" gets a light periwinkle selection background.

## Signature
- Single-accent discipline: electric lime #C1FA6C is the CTA fill, badge, and two full-bleed rounded section backgrounds — no competing hues.
- Monospace all-caps micro-labels (eyebrows, bylines, badge copy) against big tight grotesque headlines.
- Notebook UI shown as exploded collage: screenshot plus floating formula chips, sliders, and sticky notes around it.
- Question-formatted card headlines with emoji icons ("Should I upgrade to Github's Enterprise plan?").

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Reserve #C1FA6C exclusively for CTAs, the announcement pill, and full-bleed section slabs; keep everything between them white/off-white.
- Pair lime fills with near-black green text (#042800), never white text on lime.
- Use monospace uppercase, letter-spaced eyebrow labels above every section headline.
- Round generously: pill buttons, 16-24px cards, 40px+ on colored section containers.
- Let real product screenshots with annotated floating widgets replace illustration.

Don't:
- Add drop shadows or borders to buttons and cards — flat fills on contrast do the separation.
- Introduce a second saturated color; blues/purples appear only as faint tints (hero wash, text highlight).
- Use pure black body text — body copy stays mid-gray (#62656D); only headlines go near-black.
- Loosen headline tracking or drop weight — display type is heavy and tightly set.
- Run lime sections edge-to-edge square; the colored slabs always float with big rounded corners and white margins.
