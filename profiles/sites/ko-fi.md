## Overview
A warm, playful minimal-light system built on a cream paper canvas, near-black serif display type, and a rotating cast of pastel accent colors (periwinkle blue, peach, butter yellow, lavender). Hand-drawn cartoon mascots — anthropomorphic blobs, hearts, and stars with faces — appear on nearly every page and carry the brand's friendliness. Product UI is shown as flat illustrated mockups inside rounded, offset-shadow frames rather than real screenshots.

## Layout
Sticky-feeling top nav: logo left, sparse text links right, black pill "Sign up free" as the only nav CTA. Heroes are simple: big serif headline (left-aligned on feature pages, centered on home), one sentence of sans body, single blue pill CTA, then a large illustrated device/profile mockup. Section rhythm alternates: cream canvas → white rounded content card → full-bleed pastel color band (FAQ sections sit on solid blue/peach/yellow/lavender backgrounds that change per page) → cream closing CTA with the "ko-fi.com/Name" claim input. Generous vertical whitespace; content column stays narrow (~700-900px). Each feature page ends with the same footer: white rounded card holding a 4-column link grid.

## Components
- Buttons: pill shape; primary is periwinkle blue (#77A4EE) with dark ink text (not white); secondary/nav CTA is solid black pill with white text; "Follow" appears as white pill with subtle border.
- Cards: white, large radius, thin 1px dark outline OR borderless with an offset duplicate-layer shadow (a flat card "stacked" behind, like a sticker). Testimonial cards are outlined white boxes with small round avatars and bold names.
- FAQ accordions: fully rounded pill/stadium rows, tinted a lighter shade of the section's background color, dark text, +/arrow toggle at right.
- Inputs: the recurring URL-claim field is a white pill with prefixed "ko-fi.com/" label and a black pill "Claim" button nested inside the right edge.
- Pricing: two-column plan grid ($0 vs $12/mo) inside a bordered card; checklist rows with small colorful doodle icons.

## Signature
- Cream #F3EEE7 canvas everywhere — never stark white pages; white is reserved for cards.
- Chunky warm serif headlines paired with a plain geometric sans body.
- Cartoon blob/heart/star mascots with faces, drawn in thin black outline with spot color, tucked around section edges.
- Per-page accent theming: each feature page recolors its mockup frame and FAQ band (blue for commissions, peach for membership, yellow for shop, lavender for pricing) while the rest of the system stays constant.
- Offset "sticker stack" shadows on big cards and device mockups instead of soft blurs.

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep the cream canvas and put content in white rounded cards; use one saturated pastel per section as a full-bleed band.
- Set headlines in a heavy warm serif and everything else in a quiet sans; headlines do the personality work.
- Use pill geometry consistently — buttons, FAQ rows, tags, and inputs are all stadium-shaped.
- Put dark ink text on the blue primary button, not white.
- Add one or two outlined cartoon mascots per page, positioned casually at card corners or section breaks.

Don't:
- Don't use real product screenshots — Ko-fi shows simplified flat illustrated mockups with oversized cursors.
- Don't use gradients, glassmorphism, or soft drop shadows; shadows are flat offset layers.
- Don't introduce a dark section or dark mode look; the darkest element is the black nav pill.
- Don't let the pastel accents mix within one section — each band commits to a single accent hue plus its lighter tint.
- Don't center-align long body copy; paragraphs stay left-aligned inside cards even when headlines are centered.
