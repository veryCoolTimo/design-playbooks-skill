## Overview
A minimal light SaaS site on a cool near-white canvas, punched up with playful candy accents: mint-green CTAs, lavender, magenta-pink, and cyan. Deep indigo-navy headings carry the weight; color arrives via highlighter strokes behind words, colored keywords inside headlines, and translucent geometric "glass" shapes (parallelograms, isometric slabs). Product screenshots on dark or hot-pink app chrome sit directly on the light page as the main imagery.

## Layout
Sticky light top nav: left logo, center dropdown links (Product, Developers, Community, Resources), right "Log in" text link plus a solid mint pill-ish CTA. Sometimes a slim dark announcement bar above the nav. Heroes are left-aligned text blocks (headline, one short paragraph, primary + secondary button pair) with a large product screenshot or isometric 3D illustration to the right or below. Sections alternate white and pale blue-gray bands in a steady rhythm; feature rows zigzag image/text left-right. Generous whitespace, wide margins, centered section headings with one accent-colored word. A signature near-footer sequence repeats across pages: light "Stay in the loop" newsletter block with isometric illustration, then a full-width dark indigo band ("Designers and Developers love Penpot") with dual CTAs, then a light multi-column footer.

## Components
Buttons: flat solid mint-green fill with dark text for primary; secondary is a white/transparent button with a thin dark 1px border; both nearly square with 2-4px corners, no shadows, compact padding. On the dark band the secondary button inverts to a thin light outline. Cards: white with 1px light-gray borders or very soft shadows, 6-10px corners — used for testimonial quotes (with oversized colored quote-mark glyphs and avatar rows), template tiles with thumbnail + download icon, and bordered outline boxes for feature bullets. Inputs: plain white rectangle with thin border, square corners, paired with a small checkbox consent line and a mint submit button. Text links are underlined or arrow-suffixed ("See features →").

## Signature
- Bright mint-green (#2EE5A8) flat CTA against deep indigo headline text — the core brand pairing on every page.
- Hand-highlighter marks: translucent lavender/pink/cyan strokes swiped behind or through headline words, plus single words recolored in accent hues.
- Translucent isometric geometric shapes (parallelograms, extruded slabs, character illustrations built from them) in mint/lavender/pink floating at section edges.
- Real product UI screenshots framed in dark charcoal or hot-pink app chrome as hero/section imagery.
- Community "Wall of Love": masonry grid of white testimonial cards over a pink-to-lavender gradient wash.

## Do
- Keep the canvas cool near-white and reserve saturated color for CTAs, highlighter marks, and illustration shapes.
- Use one flat mint-green primary button per hero, paired with a thin-outline secondary ("Self-host install" style).
- Set headlines in heavy dark-indigo grotesque and color exactly one or two words in an accent hue.
- Alternate white and pale-gray section bands, and close pages with the dark indigo CTA band before the footer.
- Frame product screenshots in dark or bold-colored app chrome so they pop off the light page.

## Don't
- Don't round buttons past ~6px or use pill shapes — Penpot buttons are nearly square.
- Don't add gradients or shadows to buttons; fills are flat and borders are thin 1px.
- Don't use warm/cream backgrounds — the palette is cool (blue-gray whites, indigo ink).
- Don't render illustrations as flat 2D clipart; the language is translucent isometric geometry with layered transparency.
- Don't let body copy run wide — text columns stay narrow beside large imagery, with lots of surrounding whitespace.
