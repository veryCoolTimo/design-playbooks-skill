## Overview
Airy blue-on-white SaaS minimalism: near-pure white canvas, dark slate-navy ink, and one bright sky blue doing all CTA work. Heroes alternate between saturated royal-blue panels (Enrich, Reveal) and soft pastel gradient washes — lavender-blue, purple, and peach — that fade into white. Product UI is shown as floating, softly shadowed mock cards rather than raw screenshots.

## Layout
Fixed slim top nav: logo left, center text links, Login + solid blue "Try for free" pill-ish button right; nav sits directly on the hero color. Heroes are either left-aligned text over a full-bleed blue block with a floating UI collage (product pages) or centered headline over a gradient haze (home, pricing, partners). Below the fold, sections alternate text-left/visual-right in a two-column rhythm on wide whitespace; centered section headlines punctuate each theme. Recurring furniture: grayscale customer-logo strip near the top, a floating "Ready to chat?" stacked-card contact form before the footer, an FAQ accordion of hairline-divided rows, and a dense multi-column white footer.

## Components
Buttons: small solid-blue rectangles with ~4-6px radius and white 13-14px labels; secondary buttons are white with a light gray border (pricing "Create a Free Account", "Get Information"). Cards: white, 10-14px radius, very soft diffuse shadows, thin or no borders — used for pricing tiers, customer stories (image top, logo, text, "Read story ›" link), and partner-agency cards with colored brand headers. Inputs in the contact form are light-gray-bordered rounded fields on a white card. Small blue pill "tab" chips (Create Demand / Capture Intent / Convert Pipeline) toggle between an active blue fill and outline states. Checkmark feature lists use small green ticks. Logo tiles float as tiny white squares with soft shadows in integration grids.

## Signature
- One bright blue (#2E90FA) owns every CTA; everything else stays white, gray, and slate
- Floating fake-UI cards with soft blurry shadows and slight offsets as the primary illustration language
- Pastel gradient hazes (blue-violet, peach) bleeding into white behind centered headlines
- Stacked-card "Ready to chat?" mini form repeated at the bottom of nearly every page
- Grayscale partner/customer logo rows as social proof directly under heroes

## Do / Don't
Do:
- Keep one saturated blue for all primary buttons and reuse it for links and active chips
- Illustrate product value with layered mock UI cards (profile panels, tables, score widgets) floating on soft shadows
- Use full-bleed royal-blue hero blocks for product pages and pastel gradient washes for brand/marketing pages
- Center section headlines in bold tight grotesque, then alternate text/visual columns beneath
- Desaturate all third-party logos to gray in proof strips

Don't:
- Add heavy borders or dark shadows — elevation here is always soft, diffuse, and light
- Introduce a second competing CTA color; secondary actions are white/outlined, never another hue
- Use photography as hero art — people photos appear only inside testimonial and case-study cards
- Make buttons large or fully pill-shaped; controls stay compact with modest 4-6px rounding
- Fill sections edge-to-edge with color mid-page; interior sections stay white with gradient glows only at section seams
