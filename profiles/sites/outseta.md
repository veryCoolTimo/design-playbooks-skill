## Overview
White pages washed at the top with a soft pink-to-yellow gradient, punctuated by full-bleed deep-plum (#2B0B2E) bands with big rounded corners. Headlines are heavy, dark-plum display type; the single loud element is a hot-magenta CTA. Hand-drawn pink script annotations with arrows and squiggle doodles add a playful, founder-made feel over an otherwise tidy SaaS layout.

## Layout
Centered pill-shaped floating nav bar (logo left, links center, Log in + magenta Sign up right) with a small "Migrating from X?" ribbon tucked beneath it. Heroes are centered: mono uppercase eyebrow, 2-3 line display headline, one magenta CTA (paired with a ghost secondary on home). Section rhythm alternates white content blocks with dark-plum rounded panels; a recurring pre-footer CTA band shows a tilted, perspective-skewed dashboard screenshot bleeding off the edge. Content sits in 2-4 column card grids or, on Learn, a narrow single-column article with floating brand logos in the margins. Blog is a dense borderless list of title rows with mono category tags and dates.

## Components
Primary buttons: pill, solid #E32C9B fill, white label; secondary buttons are dark-plum pills or thin-outline ghosts. Pricing "Get started" buttons are full-width dark-plum pills (magenta on the highlighted tier). Cards: white, 8-14px radius, 1px light-gray border, subtle or no shadow; the featured pricing card lifts with a soft drop shadow and slight overlap. Testimonials are plain bordered cards with small round avatars. Toggles (monthly/yearly) are pill segmented controls with a yellow "Save" badge. Eyebrows and metadata use letterspaced uppercase mono. FAQ rows are thin-ruled accordions with plus icons. Blog list rows are hairline-separated, no cards.

## Signature
- Soft pink→yellow gradient wash fading down from the top of hero sections into white.
- Hot-magenta pill CTA as the only saturated action color against plum-on-white type.
- Deep-plum rounded full-bleed panels containing a tilted 3D-perspective product screenshot.
- Handwritten pink script annotations with arrows and looping squiggle doodles.
- Uppercase letterspaced mono for eyebrows, tags, and table headers.

## Do
- Reserve #E32C9B strictly for CTAs and small accents; keep everything else plum, white, and gray.
- Open hero sections with the pink-to-yellow gradient fog at the top edge, dissolving into white.
- Use dark-plum (#2B0B2E) rounded bands for CTA and feature moments, with an angled dashboard screenshot bleeding off-frame.
- Mix type registers deliberately: heavy display sans for headlines, serif for lead paragraphs, mono caps for labels.
- Add sparse handwritten pink callouts with arrows to point at proof (customer examples, first-yes moments).

## Don't
- Don't add heavy shadows or gradients to cards; they stay flat with hairline borders.
- Don't use blue or generic SaaS palettes; competitor logos are the only place foreign brand colors appear.
- Don't square off buttons — actions are always pills.
- Don't fill dark sections with long body copy; they hold one headline, short subtext, one CTA, and a screenshot.
- Don't overuse the doodles; one or two annotations per page, never inside content cards.
